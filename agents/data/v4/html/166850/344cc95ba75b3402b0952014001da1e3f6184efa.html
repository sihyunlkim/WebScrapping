Welcome to the Center for Teaching & Learning Innovation, Development & Design (CTIDE)

The Center for Teaching and Learning Innovation, Development and Design (CTIDE) collaborates with Merrimack College faculty to design, build and innovate transformative educational experiences. We accomplish this through the intentional integration of academic technology and strong pedagogical practices.

Self-Serve Teaching & Learning Tools

CTIDE's Two Key Units

  • The Teaching and Learning Hub provides workshops, seminars and other ways for faculty to connect on best practices around classroom instructions, curriculum enhancement and course assessments.
  • The Development and Design Lab features tools and resources for faculty to create engaging learning environments online and in the classroom. Our new studio space provides a dedicated area for faculty to record content or lectures, create innovative student engagement opportunities and host small group discussions.

Self-Serve Teaching & Learning Resources

FIND IT FAST

Learn more about our popular teaching and learning tools, designed to support your success at Merrimack.

TEACH

Website

Teaching Excellence Advancement Challenge (TEACH)is an Office of the Provost initiative supporting ongoing innovative pedagogy and curriculum development at Merrimack College. TEACH utilizes a cohort model to incentivize faculty collaboration to achieve pedagogical and curricular innovation.

TEACHING STUDIO

Studio for Faculty

Merrimack’s Teaching Studio provides a technologically advanced environment for faculty members to deliver their content in a visually appealing and pedagogically effective manner.

COURSES & WORKSHOPS

Learn More

With our Courses & Workshops, you can enroll in a CTIDE self-paced course or watch recorded streams of CTIDE Learning Community Events & Learning Community Days. Revisit valuable insights from each session and discover fresh perspectives from your Merrimack colleagues.

CANVAS & RESPONDUS

Learning Mgt. System
& LockDown Browser

Canvas is a powerful learning management system built to make teaching and learning easier for everyone. Respondus LockDown Browser helps maintain exam integrity and ensures a level playing field.

PANOPTO

Record & Share Videos

Panoptois Merrimacks lecture capture tool that allows instructors to record and share videos with their students. It can be accessed within all Canvas courses and can also be used by students to submit video course assignments.

EXAMSOFT

Exam Management

ExamSoftis an exam management and proctoring platform. It is designed to streamline the process of creating, administering and grading exams in a secure and efficient manner.
