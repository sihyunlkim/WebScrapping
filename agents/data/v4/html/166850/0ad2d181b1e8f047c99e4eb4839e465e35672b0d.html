Privacy / Terms

Information gathering

Merrimack College uses your IP address to help diagnose problems with our server and to administer our website by identifying (1) which parts of our site are most heavily used, and (2) we do not link IP addresses to anything personally identifiable. This means user sessions will be tracked, but the users will remain anonymous.

Merrimack College staff occasionally monitors search terms users enter into the sites Google Search Engine, but this tracking is never associated with individual users.

Completing a submission form will serve as an official signature, providing consent to representatives of Merrimack College contacting me about educational opportunities via text, email, mail, phone, including my mobile phone if provided, and those calls may be placed using an automatic dialer. I understand my consent is not a requirement for enrollment.

When registering, I understand I may be asked to enter my name, e-mail address, or phone number.

Use of information

The Merrimack College Web Services team uses the information gathered above to tailor site content to user needs, and to generate aggregate statistical reports.

Security

This site has security measures in place to protect the loss, misuse and alteration of the information under our control.

Cookies

This site may use cookies. Cookies are small files a website or its service provider transfers to your computers hard drive through your web browser. These files enable the sites or service providers system to recognize your browser, so the sites can capture and remember certain information. You can opt out of allowing cookies in your browser settings.

This site uses cookies to compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interaction. This helps us offer better site experiences and tools in the future. We may contract with third-party service providers to assist us in better understanding our site visitors. These service providers are not permitted to use the information collected on our behalf except to help us conduct and improve our business. This may include utilizing cookies and third-party service providers to serve ads based on prior visits to the Merrimack College website.
