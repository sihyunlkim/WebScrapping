Disclaimer

Stockton University and the Richard E. Bjork Library do not pay for, technologically support, or endorse the use of any vendor or product listed on this page. This list is for informational purposes only. 

Credit

This list has been informed in part from Rutgers University's School of Communication and Information's (SC&I) Dr. Joyce Kasman Valenza and her Generative AI Tools: A Petting Zoo

This list is just a sample of the available AI technologies, with new GPT models and bots emerging daily.

General Tools and ChatBots

Chatbots
  • ChatGPT
    OpenAI's most current ChatGPT.
  • Claude
    AI assistant by Anthropic.
  • Google Gemini
    Google's AI model (formerly Bard).
  • Meta AI
    Generative AI assistant by Meta.
  • Microsoft Copilot
    Microsoft's AI companion.
  • Perplexity
    AI-powered answer engine.
  • Poe
    Poe currently supports ChatGPT, GPT-4, GPT-4o and DALL-E 3 from OpenAI, Claude 2, Claude 3, and Claude 3.5 from Anthropic,
    more ... less...
    Stable Diffusion 3, Stable Diffusion 3 Turbo from Stability AI, PaLM, Gemini 1.5 Pro, Gemini 1.5 Flash, and Gemini 1.5 Pro Search from Google, Llama 2 and Llama 3 from Meta, Ideogram by Ideogram, Playground-v2.5 by Playground, Mistral-Medium and Mistral Large by Mistral, Command R Plus from Cohere, and millions of unique bots created by the community.
  • xAI
    Generative assistant by X (formerly Twitter).
General Tools
  • AI Test Kitchen
    Experiment with AI tools by Google. Generative assistants available: VideoFX, ImageFX, MusicFX, and TextFX.
  • DeepAI
    Create prompts to generate text, images, videos, and music.
  • There's an AI for That
    An AI aggregator, updated daily.

Images, Videos, and Design Tools

  • Adobe Firefly
    Firefly models and services power generative AI features in Adobe creative apps.
  • Canva AI Image Generator
    Free online AI image generator through Canva.
  • DALL-E 3
    Available in ChatGPT Plus.
  • ImageFX
    Google's AI image generator.
  • Lexica
    Search for free AI images or generate your own. You must pay for a subscription account to create images.
  • Midjourney
    Requires a subscription account.
  • Pebblely
    Generate 40 free AI product photos per month.
  • This Person Does Not Exist
    Random face image generator.
  • Veo
    Coming Soon! Generative video models by Google DeepMind.

Lifestyle Tools

  • AI Dungeon
    A text-based adventure-story game you direct (and star in) while the AI brings it to life.
  • Be My AI (Coming Soon)
    Be My Eyes Virtual Volunteer, powered by GPT-4. Be My AI will give users a detailed description of pictures taken on their devices with the ability to chat and ask further questions.
  • ChefBot
    An AI-powered cooking companion that allows you to enter your ingredients, dietary restrictions, and preferred cooking style to generate a unique recipe just for you.
  • GoblinTools
    goblin.tools is a collection of small, simple, single-task tools, mostly designed to help neurodivergent people with tasks they find overwhelming or difficult.
  • Mitsuku (Kuki)
    An award-winning chatbot that can engage in fun and interesting conversations
  • Replika
    An AI companion that offers safe space for expressing feelings and helps alleviate loneliness.
  • Tallo
    An AI-driven platform that helps individuals explore and recommend personalized career paths and develop skills based on strengths and interests.
  • Youper
    An AI-powered mental health assistant. Free app with premium in-app subscription options.

Presentation Tools

  • Gamma
    Generates presentations and slide-based websites based on text prompts.
  • SlidesAI
    AI-powered text to presentation. Available for Google Slides.
  • SlidesGPT
    AI-generated PowerPoint presentations.

Search and Research Tools

  • Consensus
    Academic search engine powered by AI.
  • Elicit
    An AI academic research assistant.
  • JSTOR Text Analyzer
    Use your own document to search for articles and books.
  • LitMaps
    Litmaps automates literature discovery by finding the most relevant papers and authors on your topic using the citation network.
  • Research Rabbit
    Search for papers and authors, monitor new literature, visualize research landscapes, and collaborate with colleagues.
  • ScholarAI
    GPT widget available for ChatGPT Plus.
  • Semantic Scholar
    A free, AI-powered research tool for scientific literature

Speech and Language Tools

  • Adobe Podcast AI
    Clean up audio for free with Adobe Podcast AI.
  • DeepL Translator
    An AI-powered text translator.
  • Lovo AI
    A hyper realistic AI voice generator.
  • Microsoft Speaker Coach
    Rehearse your slide show with Speaker Coach.
  • Otter.AI
    AI meeting transcriptions and assistance.
  • Speechify
    Text-to-speech, AI voice generator. Free and paid-subscription content is available.

Writing Tools

  • Grammarly
    AI writing assistant.
  • Jenni.AI
    Academic writing and citation tools. Free and subscription accounts available.
