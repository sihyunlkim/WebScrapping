      Course Creation Reimagined - Leveraging GenAI for Streamlined Learning

      In many ways, generative AI has made faculty members' work far more challenging. In this workshop, we will explore how it may assist you. Are you revising a course or working on a new one? Discover how generative AI can support and streamline your course design process, from planning to assessment. In this workshop, you’ll explore AI tools and strategies for creating syllabi, course maps, learning objectives, FAQ pages, and announcement or email templates. Learn how to generate or enhance lecture materials, develop case studies and custom scenarios, and design collaborative student activities. Explore AI-assisted approaches to rubric creation and quiz question development or revision. You’ll walk away with practical tools, templates, and ideas designed to help you put what you’ve learned into action right away.You are invited to join us for a virtual workshop on Course Creation Reimagined: Leveraging GenAI for Streamlined Learning.

      Online on Tuesday, November 4, 11:30 – 12:30 via Team’s Meeting Link. Please register for the workshop on the My Scranton site, using the Employee CTE Events Card. The event is listed under Brightspace (D2L) Workshops. Reimagine your course design process with the power of generative AI in this workshop focused on streamlining every step from planning to assessment.

      Workshop Highlights:

      • Streamline Course Design: Learn how to use GenAI to efficiently create syllabi, course maps, objectives, and communication templates.

      • Enhance Instructional Materials: Generate or refine lecture content, case studies, scenarios, and student engagement activities using AI tools.

      • Create Smarter Assessments: Explore AI-assisted strategies for developing rubrics and quiz questions that align with your learning goals.
