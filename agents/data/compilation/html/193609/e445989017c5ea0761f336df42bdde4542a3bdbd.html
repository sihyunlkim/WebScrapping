Open Houses & Tours

Open Houses - join us!

Join us for one of our upcoming Open House events and discover why The Neighborhood Playhouse has been a cornerstone of professional actor training since 1928. More than an introduction, these events offer a rare opportunity to step inside the tradition of conservatory training—an immersive, hands-on approach to the craft of acting that cannot be replicated anywhere else. Whether you’re just beginning your artistic journey or seeking to deepen your skills, this is your chance to experience our renowned Two-Year Professional Actor Training Program and meet the master teachers who bring it to life.

**Bonus: All Open House attendees will receive a waiver for the $75 application fee.**

School Tours

Tours of the school are given by recent alumni and are given on weekdays between 10am-3pm throughout the year. Come to New York City and visit the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre! To set up a tour please call 212.688.3770 or email admissions@neighborhoodplayhouse.org. Reservations in advance are required and walk-in/same-day tours are not permitted. 

THE LUNCH HOUR

Our Admissions Team meets on select Fridays on Zoom for The Lunch Hour. It is a casual time for interested applicants to chat with recent graduates about life at The Playhouse, our application process, acting--whatever you'd like to chat about! Please email admissions@neighborhoodplayhouse.org to receive the Zoom information for our next Lunch Hour.

Come learn about the legendary training for actors available at The Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre.
