Living and Training in New York City

These resources are provided for informational purposes only. The Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre does not endorse any specific housing or rental organization, yet we find that these are a good place to start for applicants and students seeking housing in New York City.

Students who are fully enrolled can contact us to be invited to our private electronic bulletin board for finding apartments, rooommates, and shares directly through the Neighborhood Playhouse family. If you are enrolled and would lilke to participate, please contact Lizz Dow at edow@neighborhoodplayhouse.org.

Location information

As you secure housing, please keep in mind that The Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theatre is located at 340 East 54th Street New York, NY 10022.  We are within walking distance of the E/F/M/N/R/W/4/5/6 Subway lines.
