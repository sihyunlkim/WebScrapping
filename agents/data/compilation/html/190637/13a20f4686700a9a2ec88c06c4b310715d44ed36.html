B.S. in Data Science & AI

Join a Growing, Future-Forward Industry    

Go beyond buzzwords and prepare for a career using AI. Lehman’s Data Science and AI degree is leading the way in teaching how artificial intelligence and large data sets can be used in a variety of professions that impact everyday life; from Web 3.0 development to healthcare.    

In this program, you’ll learn the skills needed to navigate, analyze, and leverage data, turning it into actionable insights that can drive strategic planning, decision-making and create meaningful change.  

Apply Now

Master Cutting-Edge Data Techniques

Our comprehensive curriculum covers the full spectrum of data science, from data collection and cleaning to advanced analytics and machine learning. Use cutting-edge tools and technologies to process and interpret complex datasets with precision. Choose from specialized concentrations such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Data Science Theory, and Spatial Data Science.  

View Program Courses and Requirements

 

 

Multidisciplinary Major 

Learn how data science and AI can be applied across a wide variety of disciplines such as earth science, computer science, sociology, business, and math. 

Specialized Concentrations 

Choose from areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Data Science Theory, and Spatial Data Science. 

Real-World Applications 

Students will be prepared immediately apply their skills to solve practical problems in various sectors. 


Career Opportunities

Data Science and Artificial Intelligence touches practically every industry, allowing graduates to explore a wide variety of career paths and fields. In today’s job market, data science professionals are highly sought after across various industries such as:  

  • Energy
  • Environmental Management 
  • Finance 
  • Government  
  • Healthcare 
  • Manufacturing 
  • Technology 
  • Urban Planning 

 

Those interested in academic research, teaching, or highly specialized industry roles, can pursue a graduate degree (Master's or Ph.D.) in Artificial Intelligence, Computer Science, Data Science, Machine Learning, Operations Research, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, and related fields.
