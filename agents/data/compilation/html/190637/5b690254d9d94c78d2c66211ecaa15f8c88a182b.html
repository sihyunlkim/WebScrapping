Step-by-Step Instructional Videos

You can log into Lehman Navigate and follow the prompts to schedule an appointment. You can also watch our ‘How to schedule an appointment’ video below for a detailed step by step.

Some students have one or more advisors assigned to them in Lehman Navigate. You can find your Success Team members by logging into Lehman Navigate, clicking ‘Appointments’, then clicking on ‘My Team’. For step by step guidance, watch our Success Teams video.

Sometimes your advisor will invite you to make an appointment. When this happens, you will receive an email invitation with a unique link to sign up. You can also access the invitation by logging into Lehman Navigate, clicking  ‘Appointments’, and looking under ‘Appointment Invitation’. Watch the video 'How to Respond to an Appointment Invitation' for step by step guidance.

You can log into Lehman Navigate and follow the prompts to cancel your appointment, watch How to Cancel My Appointment in Lehman Navigate for a step-by-step guide. If you won’t make it, please take the time to cancel so that your advisor can see other students.
