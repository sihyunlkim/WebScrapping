Class Schedule

Register Online

Join our transformative summer workshop series, "Generative AI Foundations for Enhanced Productivity," designed to catapult your skills into the future of technology through the mastery of leading Generative AI tools, including Microsoft Copilot, OpenAI ChatGPT, and DALL-E.

This comprehensive workshop series starts with an introduction to the foundational concepts of Generative AI, followed by a deep dive into the functionalities and capabilities of each tool.

This workshop series meets from 6-8:30 pm at CUNY on the Concourse. Register today with our introductory price!

July 18
Boost Productivity with Generative AI: An Interactive Beginner's Course 
Get started with Generative AI (GenAI)! This opening class provides a solid foundation for the entire workshop series, guiding beginners with no prior AI knowledge.  
July 25
Unleash the Power of Generative AI with ChatGPT 
This session dives deep into ChatGPT, one of the most powerful Generative AI tools available. In this practical session you will begin to leverage ChatGPT's potential.  
August 1
Supercharge Your Workflow: Mastering Microsoft Copilot
Dive into Microsoft Copilot, a next-level Generative AI designed to supercharge your professional workflow.  This session unpacks Copilot's inner workings, contrasting its strengths with tools like ChatGPT. 
August 8
AI for Creatives: Generate Like a Pro with Whisper, DALL-E & Sora
Unleash your creativity with this exploration of specialized Generative AI tools!  We'll delve into Whisper, DALL-E, and Sora, unpacking their functionalities and the AI that fuels them.
August 15
The Future of You & AI: Project Showcase, Q&A, & Implementation Strategies
We'll delve into practical implementation strategies for both professional and personal endeavors, solidifying your learning and empowering you to keep exploring these powerful tools on your own.
FREE EVENT
July 11, 6-8 pm
Navigating the AI Wave: A Panel Discussion with Local Businesses
Are you wondering about Artificial Intelligence (AI)? Come and find out how local entrepreneurs are using AI in their small businesses. Get a basic understanding of the applications for business purposes. This panel will be followed by a 5-session course introducing you to the most common applications and uses of AI (remote and in person) starting July 18 from 6-8:30pm.
