Everyday AI: Shaping Policy, Health, and Education Together

download flyer
  • share
add to calendar
OCT
6
2025

Time: 9:00 a.m. - 1:30 p.m.

Location: Music Building, Faculty Dining Room &amp; East Dining Room

Audience: Alumni, Faculty, Friend of Lehman, Students, Visitors

Admission: Space is limited&mdash;please RSVP ASAP!

Tickets: Click here

Contact Organizer

Jasmin Clavasquin

718-960-8111 president.events.@lehman.cuny.edu

This half-day program will bring together policymakers, educators, healthcare leaders, and industry experts to explore the real-world impact of artificial intelligence. 

Highlights include:

  • Panels on AI’s Impact in healthcare, business, education, workforce development, and policy.
  • Interactive AI Expo featuring hands-on demos and student projects.
  • Sponsored Research Opportunity for students.
  • Complimentary breakfast and networking.
