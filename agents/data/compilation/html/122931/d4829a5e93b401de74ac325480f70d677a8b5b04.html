Outside Resources

See what other scholars and professionals are doing:

  • ACM Ethics
    The Committee on Professional Ethics (COPE) promotes ethical conduct among computing professionals by interpreting and publicizing the ACM Code of Ethics, supporting ethics education, and recommending updates to the Code. COPE also reviews and adjudicates complaints involving violations of the Code of Ethics and ACM’s Anti-Harassment Policy.
  • AI Now
    AI Now develops policy strategies to redirect away from the current trajectory: unbridled commercial surveillance, consolidation of power in very few companies, and a lack of public accountability.
  • The Alan Turing Institute
  • Ethics of AI Lab
    The Ethics of AI Lab at the University of Toronto’s Centre for Ethics has fostered academic and public dialogue about Ethics of AI in Context—the normative dimensions of artificial intelligence and related phenomena in all aspects of private, public, and political life.
  • Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence Institute
    The Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI (HAI) is an interdisciplinary institute established in 2019 to advance AI research, education, policy, and practice. Stanford HAI brings together thought leaders from academia, industry, government, and civil society to shape the development and responsible deployment of AI.
  • MIT Media Lab
    The Berkman Klein Center and the MIT Media Lab, as anchor institutions of the Ethics and Governance of Artificial Intelligence Fund, are conducting evidence-based research to provide guidance to decision-makers in the private and public sectors, and to engage in impact-oriented pilot projects to bolster the use of AI for the public good, while also building an institutional knowledge base on the ethics and governance of AI, fostering human capacity, and strengthening interfaces with industry and policy-makers.

Check Out These Podcasts

  • The AI Podcast by NVIDIA
    Explore how the latest technologies are shaping our world, from groundbreaking discoveries to transformative sustainability efforts. The NVIDIA AI Podcast shines a light on the stories and solutions behind the most innovative changes, helping to inspire and educate listeners.
  • Ethics in AI by the University of Oxford
    Oxford’s new Institute for AI Ethics fosters interdisciplinary dialogue on real-world AI challenges, including bias, privacy, and social impact. Ethics in AI seminars support this by uniting students and experts to explore AI's ethical dimensions collaboratively.
