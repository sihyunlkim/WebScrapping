ChatGPT Edu for FAS Faculty, Students, and Staff

Banner image created by ChatGPT

Faculty, students, and staff in the Faculty of Arts and Sciences now have access to OpenAI ChatGPT Edu as part of a pilot program. OpenAI ChatGPT Edu is an advanced version of the ChatGPT tool with enhanced security and privacy protections. This tool is designed for producing a wide array of outputs including text, images, and executable code from inputs that include text, files, or images and can be used for chatbot customization. 

Information and resources are available in the "Getting started with ChatGPT Edu" article in the HUIT IT Help portal.

Where can I get help?

  • If you need help with account creation or access, please contact the HUIT Service Desk.
  • The Bok Center and HUIT are collaborating on providing support for using ChatGPT Edu in your courses or creating custom GPTs. If you have questions or need help, contact HUIT’s Academic Technology team at atg@fas.harvard.edu and they will triage your request.
