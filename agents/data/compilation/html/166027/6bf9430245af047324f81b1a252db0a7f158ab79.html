ChatGPT Edu

Get AI support for writing, coding, research, and more

ChatGPT Edu is an enhanced version of OpenAI’s AI chatbot, available to eligible HMS users. With robust privacy protections and approval for use with Level 3 data, the tool supports writing, programming, academic research, and productivity tasks.

Access is by request and requires training and form submission to ensure compliance with approved use cases.  

Request access to ChatGPT Edu

Eligibility

Access is limited to HMS Quad-based users with qualified use cases.
Faculty Students

HMS Quad-based faculty and staff, as well as students with needs not met by the Harvard AI Sandbox, may request access to ChatGPT Edu. Applicants must describe their intended use in a form that demonstrates alignment with approved use cases.

Security

Approved for up to Level 3 data with HarvardKey sign in.
Data Level 3 HarvardKey

ChatGPT Edu is approved for use with data classified as Level 3 and below according to University risk classifications. Users must sign in with HarvardKey, though the platform is transitioning to single sign-on (SSO) to streamline access.

Access and use

Available by request with training and form submission.
Request Training

Access to ChatGPT Edu is not automatic. Eligible users must complete a use case form to demonstrate their need for the service. Training is required prior to activation.

After approval, users can access the tool at chat.openai.com or through the HUIT service catalog.

Cost

HMS IT covers $96 annual cost per user.
HMS-paid Annual fee

The service has an annual cost of $96 per user, this fee is covered by HMS IT. Individual users do not need to pay to access or maintain the service.
