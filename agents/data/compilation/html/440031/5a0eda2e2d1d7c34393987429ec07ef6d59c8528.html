Meet Keck Graduate Institute (KGI) at the UC Berkeley Fall Graduate & Law School Fair! Explore STEM-focused grad options—MBS, MBM, PharmD, MSMDE, MSBPE, MSPHS, MSGDA, MSGC, PPA, and DMSc. Get quick 1:1 advising on prerequisites, timelines, scholarships, and STEM-OPT. Bring your résumé and questions. All majors welcome!
