About the AI Office

Responsible advancement and use of AI in support of the University of Utah’s mission

Contact Us

Our Mission

The University of Utah is committed to the responsible advancement and use of artificial intelligence to support research, education, and positive societal impact. We view AI not just as a tool, but as essential infrastructure—encompassing data, models, systems, and hardware—that fosters innovation and makes AI ready and reliable for everyone.

Goals

icon

Lead AI strategy, policy, and governance across the University of Utah.

icon

Facilitate the responsible advancement and use of AI.

icon

Manage the broad and consistent communication of AI opportunities, policies, practices, and responsibilities.

 

Our Vision

A responsible AI innovation ecosystem catalyzes discoveries, drives innovations, and benefits society.
