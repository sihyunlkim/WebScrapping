Responsible Use of AI in Research

Go back to URES Events Calendar >>

Join us for a thought-provoking session on the responsible use of artificial intelligence (AI) in research. Explore ethical considerations, data integrity, and the evolving role of AI in shaping research practices across disciplines. This presentation aims to equip undergraduate researchers with the awareness and tools to engage with AI responsibly and effectively.

Presenters:

  • Dr. Penny Atkins, Director of Research & Science, Scientific Computing and Imaging Institute
  • Dr. Caren Frost, Associate VP for Research Integrity & Compliance, and Research Professor, Social Work

Presentation resources:

  • Presentation slides
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI) at the U
  • One-U Responsible AI Initiative
  • NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute University Ambassador Program
  • America’s AI Action Plan
  • NOT-OD-25-132: Supporting Fairness and Originality in NIH Research Applications
  • University Shares Guidelines for AI in Research and Teaching - Artificial Intelligence​
