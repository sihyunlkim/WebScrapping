AI Policy Special Interest Group

Vision and Mission

The Policy Special Interest Group seeks to inform the creation and refinement of policy to promote responsible artificial intelligence, for commercial, governmental, and academic entities.

Initial Objectives and Activities

  • Catalog, understand, and track existing and emerging AI policy frameworks
  • Seeking to create:
    • A General Model Policy (or Strategy)
    • A Healthcare Model Policy
    • Data Standards for Interfacing State/Company/Organization/Entity
