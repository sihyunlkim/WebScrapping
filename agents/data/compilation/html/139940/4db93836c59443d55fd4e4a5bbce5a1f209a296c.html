    CONTACT ACADEMIC INTEGRITY

    Please note that this form shouldnot be used to make a formal report/charge of academic dishonesty. Please visit this link at the Dean of Students website to access the “Notice of Academic Dishonesty” form, located in the lower left corner of the page.

    You must enter your @gsu.edu email address to submit your question; the “Submit” button will not appear at the bottom of the screen if you do not use your university email address.

    Contact Form

    • Please enter your @gsu.edu email. The submit button will not appear unless you use your university email address.
    • Please enter your work title/academic rank.
    • Enter your GSU department.
    • What's your question? Let us know. Please note that this form is not intended to replace the university form for reporting academic dishonesty. A link to the form is available in the website menu bar above. (Max. 500 characters)

    • The submit button will not appear until you enter an @gsu.edu email address above.
