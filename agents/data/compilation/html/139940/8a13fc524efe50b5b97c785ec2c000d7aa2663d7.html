    Using AI With Intention: Do's, Don'ts and Data Types

    USING AI WITH INTENTION

    Do's, Don'ts and Data Types

    While the generative AI space continues to evolve, we want to help the university community adopt emerging technologies, while maintaining as a focus data integrity, privacy and security as well as accountability, transparency and protection of the university community when selecting and using new tools.

    AI DO'S

    • DOdisclose the use of AI tools for relevant activities and methods and scope of use.
    • DOcooperate with a risk review through the universitys contract review process for new AI and technical tools.
    • DO verify outputs of AI.

    Review the AI policy and related links for more AI guidance.

    AI DON'TS

    • DON'Tselect an AI tool without first considering the type of data being entered and made available to the tool and whether the tool has been reviewed for that use.
    • DONTuse AI tools thatdo not meet university security standards with protected data.

    See the AI by Data Types table below,AI Tools List, andAI Tool FAQs for information on tool use guidelines, university licensed tools, and tool review.
