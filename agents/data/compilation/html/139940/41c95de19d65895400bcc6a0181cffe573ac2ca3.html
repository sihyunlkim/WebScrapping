    University Policies

    University Policies

    The Georgia State policy library houses the official collection of institutional policies that affect the members of the university community across all colleges and divisions. The colleges, schools, divisions and departments of the university may have additional policies that govern their operations, and such policies are not housed in the Georgia State policy library.

    The Office of Institutional Effectiveness is responsible for overseeing the policy management process. Additional information and resources for navigating university policies can be found at https://policies.oie.gsu.edu/ .
