Mission, Vision, Strategic Directions and Values Statement

FY26-29

Mission Statement

Students are at the center of everything we do.
We empower students to change their lives through comprehensive educational opportunities and holistic support services that promote equitable outcomes. We provide innovative, quality education that responds to workforce and community needs while cultivating a safe learning and working environment, leveraging partnerships, and strengthening Delaware’s economy. We embrace the diversity of all individuals by nurturing an inclusive environment that prioritizes access and equity for student success.

Mission Goals

To fulfill the Mission, Delaware Tech will:

  1. Prepare students for transition to employment, career success, and/or continued academic opportunities through comprehensive educational offerings and holistic advising support.
  2. Cultivate learning and student success through high-quality programs and comprehensive support services that promote equitable outcomes.
  3. Strengthen programs and services through partnerships and community vibrancy.
  4. Promote a culture of inclusion, belonging, and respect that advances diversity and equity.
  5. Expand and strategically allocate public and private resources to support student success.

Vision Statement

Delaware Technical Community College will be an innovative leader in offering educational opportunities that prepare students for success and empower them to become engaged citizens and contributing members of their communities. We will strengthen and expand partnerships, foster inclusive environments, and remain responsive to the ever-changing needs of our students, industry, and the State of Delaware.

Strategic Pillars and Directions

The Strategic Pillars are the core areas that provide a solid base for strategic initiatives. They represent key areas of focus and priorities that Delaware Tech strives to achieve. The College’s Strategic Directions provide a clear roadmap for Delaware Tech to follow, ensuring alignment across all divisions and initiatives. These Strategic Directions respond to trends in the external environment that will impact accomplishment of the College’s Mission and Vision. The Strategic Directions and Goals serve as the foundation for developing strategic objectives in the College Plan.

  1. Partnerships and Community Vibrancy – Strengthen collaborative relationships with PK-12 schools, colleges, and the community to expand educational resources, support workforce development, and promote economic mobility.
  2. Data–Informed Decision Making – Optimize data analysis and usage for continuous improvement.
  3. Excellence in Teaching, Learning, and Support – Advance high-quality, student-centered experiences and services.
  4. Institutional Agility and Innovation – Foster a culture that enables adaptation in response to internal and external shifts.
  5. Technology, Innovation, and Digital Equity – Leverage emerging technologies and digital tools to enhance operations.
  6. Strategic Execution and Accountability – Institutionalize the alignment of resources and actions to be consistent with the College’s Mission, Mission Goals, and Vision through intentional planning and assessment.
