Student Support

Academic support and student resources are available to help with your academic success. Applicants and enrolled students can access staff, information, resources, and more. On this webpage you will find student support contact information and hours of availability.

Student support is closed during college holidays. Hours may be modified based on college programming (Spring Break, etc.).

Advising

We recommend current students work with their program advisor. If you’re not sure who your advisor is, please locate your advisor via MyDTCC (requires login).

We encourage new students to come to campus for initial advising. No appointment is necessary. If you cannot come to campus you may schedule the appointment by using the contact information below; appointments are limited and must be scheduled a few days in advance.

Tutoring Center

The Virtual Tutoring Center is your one-stop shop for online academic support. Online tutoring is available for math, writing, science, computers, business, ESL, and Spanish. No appointment is necessary.

In-person tutoring is also available at each campus location for one-on-one help for writing and math only. No appointment is necessary.

Have questions? Contact e-tutor@dtcc.edu.
