MISSION STATEMENT

We exist to engage, equip, and empower authentic Biblical Christ-followers.

INSTITUTIONAL INFORMATION

Arlington Baptist University is accredited by the Association for Biblical Higher Education Commission on Accreditation (5850 T. G. Lee Blvd., Ste. 130, Orlando, FL 32822, 407.207.0808) to grant certificates and degrees at the Associate, Baccalaureate, and Master’s levels.

  • At ABU, students will gain a strong foundation in Biblical competence, including a solid understanding of biblical facts and principles. They will learn to apply biblical principles to real-life situations, ensuring a practical and relevant understanding of their faith.

    LEARN MORE

  • ABU's institutional performance reflects strong student satisfaction and development. At least 94% of graduates reported that their program helped them grow spiritually and biblically.

    LEARN MORE

  • ABU is committed to fostering a transformative educational experience that supports both academic and spiritual growth. Through rigorous courses and a supportive environment, the institution consistently empowers students to develop intellectually, morally, and socially.
