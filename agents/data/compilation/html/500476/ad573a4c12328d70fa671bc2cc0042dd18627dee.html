Student parking permits are available for the Grand parking garage. 2025-2026 Student permits are $600 per term - $600 for Fall and $600 for Spring or $1,440 including summer. The permits are available on the ASU parking permit portal.

Students may purchase a U-Pass for unlimited rides on all LA Metro services on their ASU Transportation Account. Select "California Center" as your campus. After purchasing, please contact Jared Barlow to pick up your U-Pass sticker to attach to your ASU ID card (aka Sun Card). If you do not have a physical ID card, a Metro ID can be printed after you email your ASU mobile ID picture.

ASU University Housing
 
Broadway Palace is a luxury apartment in Downtown Los Angeles, featuring a unique design based on old Hollywood and is 0.4 miles from the ASU Grand campus.
 
Offering a range of amenities such as fitness centers, an indoor basketball and volleyball courts, 24/7 state-of-the-art gym and sauna with complimentary Peloton and yoga classes, as well as a rooftop pool and a second resort-style pool. Additionally, they have a karaoke lounge, theater, study libraries, private conference rooms, and package lockers. The community also has 24/7 on-site security, as well as a mobile patrol app that allows door monitoring.
 
For additional information please contact University Housing.
