Discover a Suite of AI Tools at Your Fingertips 

Whether you're a student, faculty, or staff member, you're invited to explore and experiment with a variety of generative AI tools and services designed to support your work, learning, and creativity.

The tables below outline the AI tools and services available and the types of data that they can be used with. Access each by selecting the link on the tool or service name. 

Please note that this is not an exhaustive list of the software and applications used or available for purchase throughout the university. For a complete list of university offerings, please visit the IT software list. If neither list contains your desired AI tool, OR you would like to purchase a paid license for an AI tool NOT provided by Drexel, OR your use case warrants a data classification level higher than what is listed, please visit and complete a Third Party Risk Assessment to determine what level of data is safe for you to use with that tool and use case.

Drexel Provided Tools & Services For full university software offerings, visit the IT software list. To assess AI tools, use cases, and data classification levels not listed, reach out to Third Party Risk Assessment. Both are linked above.

Examples of Popular No Cost Tools Faculty, staff, and students are strongly encouraged to exhaust the use of Drexel-provided tools (see table above), which are supported by IT and have appropriate data protections, before turning to the use of additional free or popular no cost tools. For full university software offerings, visit the IT software list (linked above). The tools below are NOT supported or serviced by Drexel University IT and should ONLY be used with data classified as LOW RISK. Please note that whatever data you share with these tools may be used for model training. To assess AI tools, use cases, and data classification levels not listed, reach out to Third Party Risk Assessment (linked above).
Tool/Service Name Brief Description Available To Tool/Service Usable With
OpenAI ChatGPT ChatGPT is an AI assistant uses OpenAI's most recent GPT language model (GPT-5 as of Aug '25) to assist with writing, answer questions, and support a wide range of tasks using natural language. By default, ChatGPT captures user data to improve future AI models. Users who prefer not to contribute their data can change their settings to opt out of model training Staff & Students Low Risk Data
Google Gemini Google Gemini is Google's AI assistant that helps with writing, planning, and organizing. It works across Google apps like Calendar and Keep, supports voice and text input, and offers real-time, multimodal, and personalized assistance. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
Claude.ai Claude.ai is a conversational AI developed by Anthropic. It uses advanced language models to assist with writing, data analysis, coding, image interpretation, and multilingual tasks. It’s designed for accuracy, long document handling, and ease of use across personal, business, and developer contexts. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
Meta.ai Meta.ai is Meta's conversational AI assistant built on the open-source Llama 3.2 model. It supports text generation, image editing, and voice interaction across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. Notable features include real-time image editing, voice mode, Ray-Ban smart glasses integration, and availability on web and mobile. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
Perplexity AI Perplexity AI is a search assistant that combines conversational language models with real-time web access to provide cited answers. It includes tools for quick lookups, in-depth research, content organization, article creation, voice search, incognito mode for privacy, and developer APIs. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
Consensus Consensus AI is a research-focused assistant that uses AI to scan peer-reviewed papers and provide evidence-based answers. It offers summaries, natural language search, and tools to support literature reviews and highlight scientific consensus. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
OpenEvidence OpenEvidence is a clinical assistant that uses AI to deliver evidence-based medical summaries, guideline overviews, and recommendations from peer-reviewed literature to support faster, more accurate decision-making. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
Midjourney Midjourney is an AI-powered text to image generation tool that offers customizable styles and iterative editing capability through its open-source platform. By default, images created with this tool are accessible to the public and may be retained for model training. Faculty, Staff, & Students Low Risk Data
Goblin Tools Goblin Tools is an AI-powered toolkit that offers a collection of small, single-task agents to break down complex tasks, adjust tone in writing, estimate time, and organize thoughts. It is designed for simplicity and accessibility.  Faculty, Staff, & Students  Low Risk Data
