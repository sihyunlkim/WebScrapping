Center for Teaching and Learning Virtual Webinar

Writing an AI Policy: Assignments & Syllabi

 

Presented by Sarah Moore, Provost’s Teaching Fellow & Kathryn Lookadoo, Associate Professor of Instruction, Management

This workshop guides faculty in drafting AI policies for assignments and syllabi, balancing responsible use, effective assessment, and innovation. Participants will explore evidence-informed practices, real examples, and practical implementation strategies. This is a hands-on online workshop as part of the Week of AI.

Thursday, April 3, 2025

3:00 p.m. – 3:45 p.m.

Microsoft Teams

Register Here

Faculty, staff, graduate students, and post-doctoral researchers are welcome to join.

Participating in this session may fulfill part of the requirements for the Graduate Teaching Certificate and Postdoctoral Teaching Certificate programs.

CTL policy prohibits use of AI tools or bots for notetaking or recording during online sessions.
