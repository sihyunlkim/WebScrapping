Artificial Intelligence (AI)

 

By clicking on the links to the left, you will find information about AI, specifically GenAI, and its use in the field of education. These materials are available at the Eugene McDermott Library and online. They will be of use to education students, K-12 teachers, professors, and scholars.

To learn more about GenAI, watch the above short video from Oracle. Another great source of information on AI is the LibGuide Artificial Intelligence (AI), by Alexander Rodriguez. library liaison to the Erik Jonsson School of Engineering & Computer Science (ECS) and Open Educational Resources.
