Welcome to the Institute for Scholarship, Pedagogy, Innovation, and Research Excellence (InSPIRE) at UTEP. The InSPIRE team benefits from a rich history of faculty leaders who have set the foundation for a center dedicated to faculty success. It also works with the Provost's Office to align with strategic priorities and respond to faculty-expressed needs and interests. We are committed to supporting the personal and professional growth of all members of our campus community.

TEACHING AND LEARNING

Our curated collection of teaching and learning resources is designed to help you enhance your students’ educational experience. Take your teaching to new heights with our thoughtfully selected and rigorously vetted resources.
