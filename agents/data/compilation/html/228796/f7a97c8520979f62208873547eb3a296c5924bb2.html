Artificial Intelligence

This program equips students with cutting-edge AI skills, including machine learning, deep learning and decision-making. The curriculum emphasizes responsible, real-world solutions that meet the evolving needs of industry and government. Graduates are prepared for impactful careers in technology, research and public service, building AI systems that drive innovation and benefit society.

First program of its kind in Texas and one of a handful of programs in the nation

Focus on developing algorithms and models that enable machines to learn, reason, and make decisions

Multiple scholarships, research, and professional development opportunities are available to support students

Preparing AI Leaders to Serve Society

AI, rooted in computer science, enables systems to learn, reason, and make decisions from data. Transformative and responsible AI technologies are essential to solving complex challenges across sectors, from education and healthcare to infrastructure and sustainability. The BSAI program equips students with strong technical expertise and practical experience to develop solutions that drive innovation and create meaningful societal impact.

Dr. Shahriar Hossain
Interim Chair, Department of Computer Science

Career Opportunities

B.S. in Artificial Intelligence graduates are equipped to work in a wide variety of industries, including technology and software development, autonomous systems, biotechnology, finance and fintech, e-commerce, and government.
