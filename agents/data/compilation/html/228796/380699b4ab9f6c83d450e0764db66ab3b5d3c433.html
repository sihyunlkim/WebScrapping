Pioneering real-world AI solutions, building talent, and driving regional transformation with global impact.

MOTIVATION

AI is rapidly reshaping societies and economies, yet it is often disconnected from real-world complexities. Integrating practical, contextual, and ethical factors are essential to ensure responsible and impactful innovation.

 

WHO WE ARE

Established in 2025 with support from the UT Regents’ Research Excellence Program,  AAII brings together researchers and experts from public and private sectors to work on deploying and refining AI in complex and culturally rich settings.

 

HOW WE OPERATE

AAII unites public and private partners and interdisciplinary researchers to address challenges through collaborative innovation. It supports AI-driven research, strengthens labs/centers, and promotes AI adoption through expertise,  resources, infrastructure.
