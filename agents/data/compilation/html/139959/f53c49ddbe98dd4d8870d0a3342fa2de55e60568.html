The availability and awareness of tools that make use of generative AI has grown substantially over the last few years – bringing with them an array of both challenges and opportunities in the teaching and learning space. Join attendees for this exploration of the basics of generative AI and teaching. Eplore the options and consider the role of AI literacy both for instructors and for students, gaining the skills and knowledge to effectively navigate an AI-integrated world.
