Students can enroll in a training module to explore generative AI

Article Date
February 11, 2025

Do you want to explore the world of generative AI and enhance your academic journey? In collaboration with the CWRU AI Task Force, Kelvin Smith Library and [U]Tech's Teaching and Learning Technologies (TLT) will introduce the “Getting Started with Generative AI in Education for Students” Canvas training opportunity for CWRU students.

This self-paced training was developed to offer a concise foundation in generative AI for all students, regardless of their discipline, allowing them to work at their own pace, with an expected total completion time of three to four hours.

The training goal is to develop students’ ability to work with AI in organizing, analyzing and expanding their understanding of complex concepts to support learning.

Enroll in the Canvas training module.
