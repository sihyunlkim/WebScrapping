UTech and Google Monthly AI Office Hours
January 30, 2026
In collaboration with Google, University Technology is to host a monthly AI Open Office Hours Series wherein attendees will get a chance to learn more about the latest AI innovations from Google and the newest AI updates from University Technology. If you have a GCP Microgrant and need assistance, please attend to have any of your questions answered by a Google Engineer.

In the first session on January 30, we will be covering how to apply for the GCP Microgrant Program, how to get access to Google Cloud Platform at CWRU, and the latest AI news from Google, including Google Workspace Studio and the newest features added to NotebookLM. Additionally, there will be a general Q&A/support session from UTech and Google.
