Academic Integrity

Students are expected to uphold standards of academic integrity. If faculty suspect a graduate student may have violated academic integrity standards, submit an Academic Integrity Violation Report with supporting evidence to Senior Associate Dean Lynmarie Hamel. Further action will be determined after the report is reviewed by Grad Studies.

Faculty can use anti-plagiarism tools iThenticate and Turnitin to ensure a student's thesis or dissertation has been properly cited.

Academic integrity violations including (but are not limited to):

  • plagiarism
  • falsifying research results
  • cheating on exams
  • submitting duplicate work to fulfill assignments in more than one course
  • forging or altering University documents
