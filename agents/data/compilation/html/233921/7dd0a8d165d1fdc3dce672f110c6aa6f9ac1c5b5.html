How to Effectively Utilize AI Tools

  • Share This: Share How to Effectively Utilize AI Tools on FacebookShare How to Effectively Utilize AI Tools on LinkedInShare How to Effectively Utilize AI Tools on X

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a tool that can be used to optimize the internship or job search process. Examples of AI that can be used when looking for an experience include ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Copilot. Leveraging AI can help streamline your search and find specific information quickly. However, not all information generated by AI is accurate. Be sure to fact check. Additionally, AI should not replace your original work. Instead, it should be used as a tool to supplement your writing, research, etc.

  • Assist with building your resume or cover letter
  • Researching a company or field of interest
  • Exploring a major or career
  • Practice interviewing for a specific experience
  • Ask for key skills and qualifications from job description
  • Check for grammar or professional language
  • And more!

  • Beware of AI hallucination
  • Make sure not to input any personal information or private data
  • AI should be used as a tool. It shouldnâ€™t generate a whole document or piece of work for you (should not replace your original work)
  • Check for inaccuracies
  • Be specific with what you ask AI
  • Ask follow up questions for more information or clarification
  • Some companies are explicit in their job description about not using AI

Below are sample prompts that can be used with your generative AI platform of choice for a variety of job searching and career development topics. Remember that these prompts should be a starting point in your research. If you choose to use one of the prompts below, make sure to add any specific details that might be missing.

  • Suggest ways to add these specific keywords [list keywords] into my existing resume bullet points: [paste bullet statements]
  • Provide strong action verbs to enhance the impact of my responsibilities
  • Summarize the most important keywords and skills from this job description
  • Check my resume for any grammatical errors and suggest corrections (Make sure to remove any personal information from your resume)

  • Write a 3-paragraph cover letter in a formal tone for a [major] student applying for a [type] internship at [company]. Highlight the following skills and experiences: [insert skills/experiences]. (Remember this should be used as a guide, not replace your original work)
  • What key points should I include in a cover letter for a [insert position] opportunity?

  • Summarize the most important keywords and skills from this job description.

Exploring careers
  • Provide a list of 10 careers and job roles well suited to a [insert degree name].
  • Based on my major/program in [insert major] and experience on my resume [attach or provide link], can you suggest potential careers and job roles for me? (Make sure to remove any personal information from your resume)
