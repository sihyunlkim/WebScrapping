Date & Time: Self paced

Location: Online Only

Presented By: Technology Enhanced Learning & Online strategies (TLOS)

Overview:

Building on your AI literacy and prompting skills from the first two modules in the AI Essentials for Teaching and Learning learning journey, this module guides you through an analysis of your current teaching workflows. You will identify opportunities to strategically integrate generative AI into routine tasks, such as content development and assessment design.

Learning Objectives:

  • Analyze current teaching processes to identify opportunities for generative AI integration.
  • Identify common ethical and responsible generative AI use cases and workflows for streamlining instructional tasks.
  • Create a simple AI-assisted workflow to improve instructional efficiency.

About the Learning Journey & Module Prerequisites:
This module is part of the 'AI Essentials for Teaching and Learning' learning journey:

    Module 0: Intro to Generative AI
    Module 1: Foundational AI Literacy for Educators
    Module 2: Intro to AI Prompting for Faculty
    Module 3: Integrating AI into Teaching Workflows
    Module 4: AI-Conscious Course and Assignment Design (forthcoming)

To get the most out of 'Integrating AI into Teaching Workflows,' we strongly encourage participants to have taken topics 0-2 prior to the session or have an equivalent foundational understanding of how generative AI works, responsible and ethical use of AI, and strategies for secure and effective prompting.
