Information Technology program

Create new ways forward

If you thrive on understanding why things work and enjoy the challenge of making them better, the Sullivan University IT program is designed for you. Dive deep into the inner workings of technology through hands-on experience and real-world applications. You’ll learn by doing, testing your knowledge in an online environment enhanced with virtual resources. No matter which program path you choose, you’ll find a straightforward road to success, with direct access to experienced professors who are dedicated to your learning.
