Information Technology

Get the experience and skills you need to meet the management challenges of the ever-changing IT landscape. Sullivan’s Master of Science in Managing Information Technology degree paves the way for advancement in expanded management positions. You’ll increase your technical knowledge, build a conceptual understanding of emerging IT issues, and gain the ability to conduct independent research to support IT project management decisions.

Explore IT

Cybersecurity

Take your cybersecurity career further with a Master of Science in Cybersecurity. You’ll hone your analytical and research skills, expand your technical knowledge, and gain practical skills in combating real and simulated cyber attacks. Ultimately, you’ll be prepared to lead and work in various capacities to protect information infrastructures.

Explore Cybersecurity

Business Administration

Give yourself a competitive edge with an advanced business degree from Sullivan University. You’ll develop the leadership, communication, managerial, and problem-solving skills required to effectively handle the ever-evolving challenges and opportunities of modern business.

Explore Business

Human Resource Management

Develop your strategic, organizational, managerial, and analytical skills while preparing for SHRM certification. The HR program at Sullivan University has been recognized by the national Society for Human Resource Management (SHRM) as a Top 10 SHRM Learning System.

Explore HR

Pharmacy

Become a crucial presence in healthcare as the go-to expert on medications. Our accelerated Doctor of Pharmacy will help you become a dynamic leader in the field, with personalized support from our faculty to ensure you’re prepared to make a meaningful impact in your community.

Explore Pharmacy

Physician Assistant

Start your healthcare career with a Master of Physician Assistant, where you’ll learn to provide compassionate, ethical care that truly makes a difference. With hands-on training and clinical experience, you’ll be ready to excel in any healthcare setting and pass the Physician Assistant National Certification Exam.
