Public safety

Sullivan University Public Safety is here to keep our campus safe and secure. If you ever need someone to walk you to your car, jump-start your battery, or unlock your car if you’ve locked the keys inside, we’ve got you covered. You can reach us through the Student Services Office or the campus receptionist in the guest lobby. For 24/7 assistance, call us at 502-413-8888 (Louisville) or 859-514-3600 (Lexington).
