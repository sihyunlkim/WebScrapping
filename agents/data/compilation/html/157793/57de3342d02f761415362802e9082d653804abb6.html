A.S. Information Technology

The Associate’s Degree in Information Technology is designed to turn you into a versatile tech professional ready to meet the needs of today’s employers. This IT degree offers hands-on learning experiences, with industry experts teaching you to solve real-world problems and create practical business solutions. You’ll graduate with a broad skill set that ensures you’re prepared for a variety of IT roles.
