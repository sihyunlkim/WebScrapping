Online learning

Sullivan University makes it easy to earn a degree on your terms, with flexible and affordable online programs that fit right into your busy life. Whether you’re looking to advance in your current career or start fresh, you’ll get the same top-notch education and support as our on-campus students, with courses designed to meet the demands of today’s job market.

Benefits of online learning

We offer flexible, affordable online degrees that bring our career-focused education directly to you.

Convenient

Learn anytime, anywhere. As long as you have an internet connection, you can access your courses on your terms.

Flexible

You’ll have the flexibility to make your own schedule, with timely access to professors whenever you need.

Savings

With an online degree, you’ll save time and money.

Personalized service

You’ll have a dedicated admissions advisor and financial planning coordinator to help you every step of the way.

Resource access

Online students get full access to the same resources as on-campus students, including 24/7 tutoring, career services, counseling, and more.

Campus resources

From counseling to career services and so much more, Sullivan University has programs and resources to support your experience both in and outside the classroom.

Sullivan University is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) to award associate, baccalaureate, masters and doctorate degrees. Questions about the accreditation of Sullivan University may be directed in writing to the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges at 1866 Southern Lane, Decatur, GA 30033-4097, by calling (404) 679-4500, or by using information available on SACSCOC’s website (www.sacscoc.org).
