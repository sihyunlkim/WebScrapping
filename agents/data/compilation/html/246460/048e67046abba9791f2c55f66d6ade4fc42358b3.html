About Us

Our Story

The story of Central Texas Beauty College started with a local family that has owned and operated the beauty schools for over 30 years. Our story, our ties to our communities, and our care for students have driven this school to success for many years. Central Texas Beauty College was established over 60 years ago in Waco, Texas. Mr. Robert Painter was the Director and Owner of Central Texas Beauty College since 1983. Mr. Painter opened a campus in Temple, Texas in 1984 and in Round Rock, Texas in 1991. He was in the beauty business since 1940 and his family continues to run Central Texas Beauty College today.

Our schools have been around a long time, but that doesnt make us old. It means weve learned the right way to educate and train our students for a lifetime of success in the field of cosmetology. Our educators stay on the cutting edge of all the latest styles & trends.

Our Standards

Central Texas Beauty College offers the Cosmetology and Manicuring courses. The courses at both campuses have been approved by the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation. Central Texas Beauty College is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts & Sciences (NACCAS) and approved by the U.S. Department of Education.

We are proud to be aMilady RISE Partner School. We commit to cover critical topics with our students such as Domestic Abuse and Human Trafficking Awareness. All of our students will leave here certified in Client Well-Being and Safety, ready to protect public health, make an impact on the community, and give a voice to the most vulnerable.

99+% State Board Pass Rate*

How do we do it?

Its simple – and really hard! Our students pass the state exams because they are prepared. We train our students so they know the content, and we test our students internally to make sure they know the state requirements before heading in to that room to take their tests. That means our students take their tests with confidence, knowing they are ready to pass their exams and move on to their professional careers.

We Accept All Transfer Hours**

Sometimes the first school isnt the right fit, or life simply gets in the way. If you started your training elsewhere and couldnt complete it, were here to help you move forward. Were confident in our ability to support your success, which is why we accept all transfer hours that have been officially released by your previous school. Our goal is simple and aligned with yours: to help you graduate, earn your license, and build a rewarding career in the beauty industry.
