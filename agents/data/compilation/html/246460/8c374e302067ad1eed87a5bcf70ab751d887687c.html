Cosmetology and Manicuring Schools in Round Rock and Temple

Central Texas Beauty College has been training students in cosmetology and manicuring courses at our Beauty Schools in Round Rock, Temple, and the Greater Austin area for over 40 years. We live here, were local, and we care about our students and our community. Our goal is for our students to receive the highest quality training possible at a fair price and graduate without the debt of school loans.

99+% State Board Pass Rate* in our Programs

At Central Texas Beauty College (or CTBC), this isnt just a statistic, its a legacy. We are dedicated to our students and take the time to prepare them to pass all their state cosmetology exams, barber exams and manicuring exams. Our students are ready, and they pass, because they understand the content, they are educated, they are professionals!

* From 2012-2021
