Round Rock Campus

Central Texas Beauty College is located in the heart of Central Texas. Austin, the Live Music Capital of the World, is just minutes away from our Round Rock campus. According the Austin Chamber of Commerce, Austin has earned a reputation as one of the best places to live in the nation, and Round Rock is the northern suburban star of the Central Texas region.

Calendar and Class Schedule

Central Texas Beauty College offers Cosmetology and Manicuring courses at least six times during each calendar year at our Round Rock beauty school campus, and any eligible person may enroll at any time for the next available class. Classes begin at 8:30 and end at 5:00 Monday through Friday. Central Texas Beauty College observes most state and federal holidays, and the school is closed for Spring Break and a Winter break during the last two weeks every year.

The Team

Our educators want you to be ready for the salon, but above all, confident! Each member of our academic team has extensive salon experience and wants you to know what to expect when you walk into a salon as a professional stylist. Our instructors are able to provide the one-on-one attention necessary to identify your natural talents and fine-tune every aspect of your skill set. They not only love to teach, but they also love to learn, attending seminars and clinics to keep abreast of fashion-forward styles and cutting edge methods of teaching.

Facility

At Central Texas Beauty College, we are vested in empowering individuals to become dedicated, well-educated professionals who are leaders in the cosmetology field creatively and financially. The Round Rock campus building consists of approximately 11,000 square feet built by the Painter family on the banks of Brushy Creek, just north of Austin, one block east of IH-35.

Central Texas Beauty College was designed to simulate the salon environment, and is fully-equipped with:

  • 76 Working Stations and Hydraulic Chairs
  • 6 Shampoo Stations
  • 5 Dryer Stations
  • 7 Mannequin Tables & Shelves
  • Marcel Stove Irons

Our nail room contains:

  • 12 Manicuring Stations
  • 6 Mobile Manicuring Tables
  • 4 Pedicuring Stations
  • 4 Gel Light Systems
  • Steri-dent Dry Heat Sterilizer
  • Airbrush System
  • Paraffin Wax Machine
  • 1 Nail Drill
  • 2 Waxing Stations

Our facial room is equipped with:

  • Facial Machine
  • Towel Warming Cabinet
  • Other Supplies for Advanced Training

The Round Rock campus also maintains:

  • Two large classrooms
  • Stage for platform artists and special demonstrations
  • Whiteboards, Flipcharts, Television, DVD Players, Projectors, and Streaming Devices
  • A Computer for research & State Board exam preparation

Our large student lounge contains:

  • Microwave
  • 2 Refrigerators
  • Blender & Coffee Maker
  • Vending machines with drinks and snacks
  • The student lounge also contains lockers that are available for students to store their personal belongings.
