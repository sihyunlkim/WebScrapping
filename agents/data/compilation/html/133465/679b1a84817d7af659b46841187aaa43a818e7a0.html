    South University, West Palm Beach

    Providing excellent student services since 1987, the beautiful West Palm Beach Campus, with its landmark Clock Tower, is ideally located in the Palm Beaches of Florida.  Regardless of pursuing an associate degree, baccalaureate, masters, or doctorate, our campus offers something for all. 

    Request Information

    overview

    The West Palm Beach campus of South University has been serving the Palm Beach area since 1987. The campus is a short distance away from bus route 43 and the I-95 interstate, with the beaches only a thirty-minute drive away. West Palm Beach is also close to the Palm Beach International Airport, the Norton Museum of Art, and the South Florida Fair.

    The West Palm Beach campus blends both the downtown life and the beach aesthetic Florida is renowned for. The campus is small enough that our students are known personally by our faculty. Together, they help West Palm Beach stand as a pillar of education in the community. To continue to support our students, we offer hybrid* classes in some of our programs and strive to deliver the best student experience possible. As with all South University’s campuses, our knowledgeable and educated faculty** and staff are eager to support you as you pursue your educational goals.

    Some of West Palm Beach’s programs are taught entirely online, hybrid format (online and in-person), and several programs are taught entirely in-person at the campus. Regardless of the modality of the program, you will have complete access to all the amenities at the campus and full support of the faculty and staff throughout your time with South, no matter what you choose. The West Palm Beach campus offers degree programs in the areas of Business & Technology, Counseling & Psychology, Criminal Justice and Legal Studies, Healthcare, Nursing, and Public Health. See below for more information about the specific degree programs available.

    *Although hybrid offered courses are available in-person or online, this program still requires Clinical courses to be conducted in person and on-site. We cannot guarantee flexibility in these components of the program.

    **Faculty credentials and experience may vary.

    FAQs

    Frequently Asked Questions

    What makes the South University, West Palm Beach campus unique?

    South University’s West Palm Beach campus has been providing excellent student services since 1987. Known for its landmark Clock Tower and beautiful setting in the Palm Beaches, the campus offers a personalized and supportive learning environment. The faculty know students by name and are often experts who work in their fields*. The campus takes a student-first approach for education.

    *Faculty credentials and experience may vary.

    What attractions are near the campus?

    Located in Royal Palm Beach, the campus is just a short drive from downtown West Palm Beach and the Atlantic coast. Students can enjoy nearby attractions such as the Palm Beach Outlets, Mounts Botanical Garden, and the Norton Museum of Art. The area also offers easy access to beaches, golf courses, and year-round outdoor activities.

    How close is the campus to downtown West Palm Beach or local landmarks?

    South University’s West Palm Beach campus is approximately 20 minutes west of downtown West Palm Beach. It’s conveniently situated near major roadways like Southern Boulevard and Florida’s Turnpike, making it easily accessible from surrounding areas.

    Is parking available?

    Yes, parking is available on-site for students, faculty, and visitors.

    What public transportation options exist?

    Public transportation in Palm Beach County is provided by Palm Tran (https://www.palmtran.org/ ). Several routes serve the Royal Palm Beach area, and students can use the Palm Tran app for real-time tracking and trip planning. Additionally, the Tri-Rail commuter train connects West Palm Beach to other parts of South Florida.

    Where is the campus located?

    The South University, West Palm Beach campus is located at University Centre 9801 Belvedere Road, Royal Palm Beach, FL 33411.

     

    What student support services are available on campus?

    The West Palm Beach campus offers a wide range of student support services, including:

    • Federal Work Study programs are available on and off campus
    • Career Services for job search support, resume building, and interview prep
    • Counseling services for academics
    • Disability services and accommodations
    • Access to digital textbooks and learning platforms

    Does the campus have a library, lounge, or tutoring center?

    Yes. The West Palm Beach campus has an on-campus and virtual library, a student lounge where students can relax and study, and various resources for academic and career support.

    Are internships available through local businesses?

    While none of the programs at the West Palm Beach campus have any paid internships, the director of the university works with local businesses and healthcare centers to find partnerships. Federal Work Study is available for students and South encourages students to apply.

    Some of the partnerships in the area include:

    • Wellington Regional Medical Center
    • Local Walgreens
    • Action Physical Therapy
    • Jupiter Medical Center
    • Integrated Healthcare Systems
    • Florida Mental Health Counseling Association

    Is the campus wheelchair accessible?

    Yes. South University’s West Palm Beach campus is fully wheelchair accessible and complies with ADA standards. Students needing accommodations can work with the campus’ Disability Services office.

    What safety measures are in place on campus?

    The West Palm Beach campus follows South University’s safety protocols, including:

    • Annual security reports
    • On-site security
    • Emergency response procedures
    • Campus access controls
    • Surveillance camera
    • Panic Button from local Sheriff’s dept.
    • Drug and alcohol policies
    • Defibrillators located on campus
    • Sexual offense prevention programs

    More information is available in the Student Support | South University section of the South University catalog.

    How can I contact the admissions office?

    You can contact the admissions office by calling (866) 242-1840, or the front desk at (561) 273-6500. Office Hours are Monday through Thursday, 8:30 a.m. – 7:00 p.m. and Friday, 8:30 a.m. – 5:00 p.m.

    What housing options are available?

    While South University does not offer on-campus housing, students can explore nearby rental options using the following resources:

    • https://www.apartments.com/royal-palm-beach-fl/student-housing/
    • https://offcampushousing.fau.edu/housing/neighborhood-West+Palm+Beach_q610cky
    • https://www.apartmentfinder.com/Student-Housing/Florida/Royal-Palm-Beach

    These platforms offer a variety of affordable housing options close to campus.

    What dining options are available?

    South University does not offer on-campus dining. The West Palm Beach campus has two break rooms, one in each building, with vending machines and fresh foods available. The Royal Palm Beach area also offers a wide range of dining options with local cafés and Latin cuisine, fast-casual chains, and other international restaurants nearby.

    South University is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) to award associate, bachelor, master, and doctorate degrees. South University also may offer credentials such as certificates and diplomas at approved degree levels. Questions about the accreditation of South University may be directed in writing to the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges at 1866 Southern Lane, Decatur, GA 30033-4097, by calling (404) 679-4500, or by using information available on SACSCOC’s website (www.sacscoc.org).
