Technology Services

Contact Us

HELP DESK

  • (734) 432-5800
  • helpdesk@madonna.edu

Technology Services provides the technology tools, training, and support to assist you in achieving your educational and career goals throughout your college experience.

IT Help Desk

The IT Help Desk is the one point of contact for technology support at Madonna University providing resources, workshops, and support for Blackboard, student email, MY Portal, and other University supported technologies. Tutorials for Blackboard, student email, MY Portal navigation, mobile devices, and Microsoft Office products can be found under the Student Help tab in MY Portal.

For assistance, contact the IT Help Desk by:

  • Submitting a ticket, at madonna.topdesk.net
  • Emailing helpdesk@madonna.edu, or
  • Calling 734-432-5800.

MY Portal

MY Portal gives you personalized access to campus information and services including online registration, textbook orders, access to MY Schedule, MY Grades, MY Housing, MY Finances, and other Student Resources.

To access MY Portal, go to the MY Portal link in the upper right corner of any page.

Student User ID, Email, and Passwords

Student ID number, email address, and passwords that are needed to access Madonna University resources will be given to you 3 to 5 business days after you are admitted to the university.

Information will be delivered to your personal email address.

Student Email – my.madonna.edu

Every student receives a Madonna University Microsoft Office 365 email account. This account is the official email communication between you and the faculty and with student service departments including Registration, Financial Aid, Student Accounts, and Student Life. You are responsible for checking your "my.madonna.edu" account regularly.

Blackboard

Madonna University uses the Blackboard learning management system for online classes and to supplement traditional face-to-face classes. "How to Thrive in an Online Course" is a free two-hour workshop offered prior to and at the beginning of each semester offering hands-on instruction using the Blackboard program. Click the "Student Help" tab on MY Portal for a schedule of the workshops.

To take an online course, you need to have access to an Internet connection; an up-to-date web browser; and a Madonna email address (yourname@my.madonna.edu).

Mobile Devices and Wireless

Wireless access for mobile devices is available in the dorm rooms, classrooms, and throughout the campus. The password to the wireless network changes each semester; the current passwords can be found by clicking the "Student" tab on MY Portal under "WiFi Passwords".

Walk-in Access to Desktop Computers

Library

Located in Room #2300 in the Administration Building, there are available workstations for all students. Please visit the Library Hours page for availability.

IT Help Desk

Located in Room #1724 in the Administration Building are several available workstations. Hours are Monday to Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. and Friday, 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

IT Help Desk hours may very depending on holidays and events on campus.

Classroom Technology

All classrooms, labs, conference rooms, and lecture halls are supplied with equipment and teaching technologies that promote active student learning and engagement. Over 100 learning spaces on main campus and at campus centers have computer and multimedia display for instruction and presentations with some learning environments designed for distance learning, video conferencing, and digital recording.

Production Facilities

The Franciscan Center houses a state of the art production facility which includes a TV Studio, Radio Studio, Editing Lab, and Edit Suites for the production of films, photography, instructional videos, videos for the web, and television programs. Our multi-camera TV studio is the home of "Celebrate Michigan," a public service show crewed by Broadcast and Cinema Arts students that airs weekly on WMYD-TV 20 in Detroit. The hands-on approach in the Broadcast and Cinema Arts program gives students the opportunity to produce full length feature films, music videos, news programs, artistic pieces, and to collaborate on a variety of production projects with Sign Language Studies, the Writing Center, Recruitment, and Marketing.
