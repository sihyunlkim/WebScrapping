Library

If you need more help finding resources at the library or would like to talk with a librarian, please use our 24/7 Chat with a Librarian service. You can also email ref@madonna.edu, call (734) 432-5703, or book an in-person or virtual research appointment here.

Writing Center

The Madonna Writing Center is located in the library. You can work with a tutor to help with your paper at any stage of the writing process.

  • Writing Center Information
  • Appointment Scheduler
  • Online Writing Lab (handouts and presentations on writing topics created by the Writing Center).
  • You can contact the Writing Center by email (writingcenter@madonna.edu) or calling 734-432-5304. 

CPI/TRiO

The CPI/TRiO office is located in the library. It offers resources and tutoring for students in various academic courses at no cost.
