Web and Privacy Policy

Information on the Madonna University server may not be used in any manner prohibited by law or disallowed by licenses, contracts, copyrights, copyrighted material or college policies and regulations. Madonna University reserves the right to remove any page from its servers at any time, without prior notice to the creator. However, the University does not monitor the content of unofficial material posted on University-owned computers.

Madonna University Privacy Policy

Madonna University is committed to protecting your privacy. Because of this, we pledge never to sell any information you voluntarily provide to us, such as your email address, name or phone number, to other colleges, universities, non-profit organizations, or businesses without your consent.

Information We Gather Automatically

Unless you take steps to browse the Internet anonymously, Madonna University, like most institutions and organizations, tracks web browsing patterns to inform us about how our websites are being used.  We do this through the use of “cookies”, which are text files placed on your computer. This helps us improve our web content and how we provide access to it.  You may refuse the use of cookies by selecting the appropriate settings on your browser, however, doing so may prevent access to the full functionality of some of our websites.

Madonna University uses both Ping and Google Analytics on some section of our websites.  Ping is an analytics service that allows us to track remote web access across all Madonna University websites, and correlate that access within our admissions platform. Ping data is aggregated every hour, and web page access is matched to records in our admissions platform every night.  This allows us to better understand and meet the needs of prospective students and others who access our website. Google Analytics is a similar, but separate, web analytics service provided by Google, Inc., which also uses cookies, and helps us to analyze how users use our websites. The information in the Google Analytics cookie includes your IP address, and will be transmitted to Google to determine coarse geo-location information (such as the user's country and city) and is then immediately discarded by Google's servers. Google uses this information for evaluating your use of the website, compile reports on our website activity, and providing other services related to Internet usage.

Please note that Ping or Google may also transfer the information collected to third parties where required to do so by law, or because third parties are processing information on behalf of them.  Google will not associate your IP address with any other data they hold.  By using our websites, you consent to the processing of data about you by Ping and Google in this manner.  Please see Ping's terms of use and privacy policy for more information on Ping, and see Google Analytics terms of use and privacy practices for more information.

The Madonna University IT department is responsible for monitoring the security of all Madonna University systems and networks.  As part of that monitoring, they will use standard industry practices to look for signs of vulnerability, attack or compromise.

External Links

Some Madonna University websites may contain links to external websites not owned by, or officially affiliated with, Madonna University in any way. Madonna University is not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of external websites.

Personal Websites

Madonna University recognizes the value and potential of electronic publishing on the Internet and encourages students, student organizations, staff and faculty to produce personal websites. The University administration, departments, organizations, faculty, staff and students are encouraged to distribute information consistent with the mission and policies of Madonna University. The information and opinions contained in personal websites reflect individual thoughts, tastes and activities of the author and do not necessarily reflect official policies or positions of Madonna University and do not in any way constitute official Madonna University content; the University does not accept responsibility for the contents of said websites.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy statement or the practices of this website, you may contact:
Madonna University Marketing Department, marketing@madonna.edu
