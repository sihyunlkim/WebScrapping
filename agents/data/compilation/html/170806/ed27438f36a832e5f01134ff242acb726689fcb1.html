  • Articulation Agreements and Transfer Guides

Making your transition easier!

 If you are planning to transfer to Madonna University, please contact the Office of Undergraduate Admissions at 734-432-5339. We will connect you to a specific Transfer Admissions Officer who can help you with the transfer process, answer questions, and discuss specific program transfer guides and articulation agreements.

Madonna University is proud to have partnered with the following colleges to increase the transfer mobility of students through articulation agreements. The articulation agreements are reviewed every two to three years and may receive frequent revisions as curriculums and courses are updated. We look forward to assisting you throughout the admission and enrollment process.

If you click on a school below and do not see the articulation agreement or transfer guide you are looking for, please be patient.
