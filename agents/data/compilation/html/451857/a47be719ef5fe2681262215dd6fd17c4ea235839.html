Our Mission at Remington College

The mission of Remington College is to contribute to the public good by providing relevant, career-focused education designed and intended to help students acquire the knowledge and develop the skills and abilities necessary for successful entry into their chosen career fields.

Remington College believes that a well-educated workforce contributes to the economic and social vibrancy of the diverse communities in which its students, faculty, and staff live.

To accomplish its mission, Remington College is committed to providing a caring learning environment in which a technically rich, intellectually stimulating, quality education is delivered by a dedicated faculty and staff.

Programmatic Accreditation

Depending on your field of study, you may want to find out whether the program you are considering has programmatic accreditation. Programmatic accreditation refers to accreditation for a specific degree or diploma program offered within the college.

Remington College offers the following programs with programmatic accreditation:

Dental Hygiene Associate Degree

The Dental Hygiene program is accredited by the Commission on Dental Accreditation. The commission is a specialized accrediting body recognized by the United States Department of Education. The Commission on Dental Accreditation can be contacted at (321) 440-4653, or at 211 East Chicago Avenue, Chicago, IL 60611. The Commissions web address is: https://www.ada.org/100.aspx

Pharmacy Technician Programs

The pharmacy technician programs conducted by Remington College in Dallas, TX, Lafayette, LA, Memphis, TN and Shreveport, LA are accredited by the American Society of Health System Pharmacists (ASHP) and the Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education.
