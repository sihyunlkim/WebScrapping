Who We Are

We’re UITS! We are the technology division at Kennesaw State University working behind the scenes to support all Owls by ensuring you have seamless access to technology. Whether you’re logging in with your NetID, confirming your identity with Microsoft Authenticator, navigating your courses on D2L, sending emails through KSUmail, or collaborating on Teams while connected to KSU Wi-Fi, UITS is here to support every step of your tech journey.

Learn more about UITS

 

KSU’s Chief Information Officer (CIO), Aaron Streimish, oversees the strategic direction of our department and ensures that our technology initiatives align with the goals of KSU. He is a strong advocate for leveraging technology to enhance the learning experience, streamline operations and foster a collaborative campus community. Under his leadership, our division is dedicated to providing the services to support all Owls to achieve academic excellence, innovative research, and be able to collaborate across divisions and departments. 

Learn more about Our CIO

 

The Information Technology Advisory Committee (ITAC) at Kennesaw State University serves as a collaborative forum that connects academic, administrative, and technical perspectives to guide the university’s strategic direction in information technology. The committee advises on policies, priorities, and initiatives that enhance the effectiveness, security, and innovation of KSU’s technology environment.
