Construction

The construction trades continue to be Polytech Adult Educations most popular program area and offer many opportunities for new employment and professional advancement. Polytech provides Registered Apprenticeship programming and continuing education opportunities in the areas of: Electrical, HVAC-R, Plumbing, Electronic Systems, Construction Laborer, and Welding and Fabrication.

Learn More

Customized Training

Polytech Adult Education offers a variety of customized and contract training options that align with each of the program areas offered (Maintenance, Construction, Healthcare, Logistics, and Operations). This option allows employers to design a specific curriculum, schedule, and delivery model that works best for their unique needs.
