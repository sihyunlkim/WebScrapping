Research, Study, and Academic Support


Library-Learning Commons

Located in the main building near the LRSC gymnasium and the New Horizons 24/7 computer lab, the Library & Learning Commons is your central space for research, study, and academic support.

The library offers print, video, audio, and electronic resources, with a reference librarian available to answer questions. Study tables include power stations, making it easy to research, study, and charge your devices. The Library also houses the LRSC Testing Center, which provides placement, academic, and professional exams.

Research & Study Resources

Research Resources

  • CINAHL Complete allows users to get fast and easy full-text access to top journals, evidence-based care sheets, quick lessons, and more. For remote learners, use this link.
     
  • MLA and APA Format Help will give you a better understanding of how to cite sources and format your writing. 

Online Tutoring Resources

  • Khan Academy offers free educational videos and exercises for all learners. With over 2,600 videos covering subjects like math, science, finance, and history, you can learn at your own pace anytime.
     
  • NetTutor provides 24/7 on-demand tutoring in 300+ subjects. Students can connect with live tutors and use tools like a virtual whiteboard, screen sharing, and audio/video chat. NetTutor services are free to LRSC students.
