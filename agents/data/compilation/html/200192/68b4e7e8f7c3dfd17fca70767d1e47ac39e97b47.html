FAQ

Have questions? You're not alone. Find quick answers to LRSCâ€™s most common questions here.

Admissions

How do I apply?

Applications are open year-round. Whether you are starting fresh or continuing your education, you can apply anytime.

Click here to get started.

Can I earn a scholarship?

Yes. The LRSC Community College Foundation awards over $540,000 in scholarships each year. One application matches you with all scholarships you are eligible for.

Learn more about scholarships.

Is there a different application process for international students?

Yes. International students follow a slightly different process. Our International Coordinator is available to help with any questions.

Learn about the international application process.

Life at LRSC

Are there opportunities to work on campus?

Yes. Students can work in a variety of roles, including Office Assistant, Library Aide, Tutor, Barista, Dietary Aide, Mascot, Marketing Assistant, Photographer, Athletic Student Manager, and more.

Learn more about student employment.

What activities are available for students on campus?

Resident hall advisors plan events throughout the year, including art sessions, sports tournaments, and themed activities like laser tag nights. The Liquid Bean downtown also hosts student-only events such as comedy shows and puzzle nights, with complimentary drinks provided.

Explore student life and organizations.

What if I have extensive dietary restrictions?

Our dining staff works to accommodate a wide range of dietary needs and preferences. Substitutions and customized meals are available whenever possible. If you have specific restrictions, the team is open to working with you to find solutions.

Learn more about dining options.

Current Students

Do I need to reapply for housing each year?

Yes. Students must reapply for housing annually through Campus Connection. Returning students may also apply to become Resident Hall Assistants (RAs).

Learn more about housing.

Do I need to reapply for scholarships every year?

Yes. A new application is required each year. The scholarship application opens October 1 for the next academic year.

Learn more about scholarships.

What is the process for preparing for graduation and receiving my degree?

LRSC holds one commencement ceremony each May. Students must submit a graduation application and a commencement participation form for graduation. The Registrarâ€™s Office can help confirm requirements and guide students through the process.

Learn more about graduation.

We appreciate staying connected with our alumni, which requires up-to-date contact details. Please update your information by completing the Contact Information Update Form.
