Generative AI Survey for UB Instructors

The image above was created using generative AI software.

By JEREMY COOPER

Updated January 13, 2026

As the integration of artificial intelligence in education continues to evolve, we are seeking the input of instructors at UB to better understand how these tools are being utilized.

We recognize the potential of AI to transform teaching and learning. However, to fully harness this potential, we need to understand how AI is currently being utilized by our faculty and what challenges or opportunities it presents in the classroom.

To that end, we are launching a survey aimed at gathering insights from faculty across all departments. Whether you are actively using AI tools like ChatGPT, MidJourney, or Grammarly in your teaching, considering their implementation, or simply curious about their possibilities, your perspective is invaluable.

This survey will remain open throughout the 2025-26 academic year. As your usage of generative AI changes, please consider resubmitting the survey so that we can develop an improved sense of how generative AI is impacting the UB learning community.
