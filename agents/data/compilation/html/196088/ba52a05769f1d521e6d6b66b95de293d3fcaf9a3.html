AI Workshops for Departments

Great teaching thrives on exploration, collaboration, and innovation. CATT is excited to offer custom, department-specific workshops designed to help faculty confidently engage with Generative AI in teaching and learning.

We’ll bring the tools and expertise to you:
Our fleet of 20 high-performance iPad Pros comes preloaded with paid licenses to ChatGPT, Grammarly, Midjourney, and UB’s enterprise solution, CoPilot.

Whether you’d like to select from our growing portfolio of ready-to-run workshops or collaborate on something custom, we’re here to help—and all trainings are free of charge.

If your department is ready to host a hands-on, AI-powered faculty development session, simply fill out the form below and we’ll take care of the rest.
