Why choose Lincoln Tech?

At Lincoln Tech, you don't just imagine your future. You build it. FAQS

Students attending Lincoln Tech need to be a high school graduate or possess a state-approved high school equivalency assessment including, but not limited to: a GED, HiSET or TASC examination; or possess an associate’s degree or higher from an accredited institution.

Financing your education is possible through a variety of financial assistance programs and options. In order to qualify for financial aid, you must apply and qualify for financial aid. Financial help is granted on a case-by-case basis and is not guaranteed.

Applying to Lincoln Tech is easy! Simply follow the steps on our Apply Now form, or visit the Lincoln Tech campus nearest you.

There are various ways to finance a Lincoln Tech education, and this includes the possibility of applying for loans, grants, scholarships. Additional information can be found on our Resources page.

Lincoln Tech has a Career Services team at every Lincoln campus, ready to help the upcoming program graduate find employment. However, Lincoln Tech does not guarantee employment. Please contact the campus nearest you to speak with a career placement counselor.
