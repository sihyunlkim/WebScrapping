Life at Lincoln Tech as an Employee

What Our Employees Are Saying

Whether you’re pursuing a career as an Instructor, Administrator, Sales Rep, Financial Aid Expert, Career Services Team Member, Executive Assistant, Coordinator, Campus President, Accountant, Receptionist, or Marketing Manager – you will be serving our wonderful, diverse, and inspired students who are making the commitment to transform their lives.

Ken Young - Automotive Instructor, Melrose Park IL Campus - One of our most admired and respected instructors, Ken Young put it best: “I don’t work for Lincoln Tech. I work for the students who come here, whose lives we’re all trying to change”. This video with Ken exemplifies his drive and passion for teaching.

 

 

A.J. Wade - 2nd Lead Welding Instructor, East Windsor CT Campus - A.J. explains his drive to help the community through teaching:

"Service over self. For years my fiancée and I discussed how my working schedule didn't allow me to better serve my community, she knew I wanted more out of life and I did too. Last April I left the manufacturing industry to pursue a career in education, it was scary, it was new, I had no idea what the future held for myself, my family and my community and... for the first time in my life I was the expert in the room.

Fast forward a year later, I accepted the Department Lead position. I realized that I could do more, offer more support, help institute new policies that have the student's best interest in mind and ultimately better serve my community. I love every moment of it. Today I wouldn't be who I am if it wasn't for all the support, guidance and mentorship I have received.

The organization at Lincoln Tech has done nothing but support me to be the best I can be. Every day I wake up and look forward to helping others make their dreams come true. Thank you to ALL that have supported and continue to do so! I look forward to doing the same with future generations!"

Samantha Colon – South Plainfield campus - “As a new hire, it has been an amazing experience. Everyone is very helpful and welcoming. Coming to work each day feels very rewarding.”

Employer Partnership Testimonials

There is not a single stage of the student experience where Lincoln does not strive to provide value for the student by engaging employers in the process.”

Kenneth Thomas – “As a graduate of Lincoln Tech, Columbia, I can say that employers are getting quality candidates from all the programs that are offered. I have hired quite a few graduates!”
