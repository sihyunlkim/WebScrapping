Future students:
Your CCN can help you get started at CLC

Your College and Career Navigator (CCN) is  your "go-to person," guiding and supporting you to become a CLC Lancer. Connect with your CCN to:

  • complete the admission process
  • choose an academic program
  • enroll in first-semester classes
  • get access to helpful resources

In case of an emergency:

  • dial 9-1-1 from any campus or public phone
  • dial 5555 from any campus phone
  • Emergency call boxes (PDF) are located in the parking lots 1 through 5, 7, 8, University Center and the baseball field. Operational instructions are located on the exterior of the box.
