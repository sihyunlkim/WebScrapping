What else can you achieve at CLC?

Discover the many other ways we support learners of all types and ages.

  • CLC helps you establish your college transcript before you even start college. Gain confidence in your ability to succeed while reducing the time it takes to complete your degree.

    Explore early college credit options for high school students

  • Gain new skills, earn industry credentials, prep for a certification exam or stay current in your career through professional development programs.

    Get skills to level up

  • Explore your interests and make new friends through noncredit, community education classes, youth camps and more.

    Programs for all ages

  • Take free English as a Second Language (ESL) class to improve your speaking, listening, reading and writing skills.

    Take ESL classes

  • Prepare for a high school equivalency exam, such as the GED.

    Earn your high school diploma

  • Learn important life skills, explore careers and set goals on your own terms through the Lancer Explore Institute (LEI).

    Join our inclusive community

  • Defensive driving, family parenting and more offered for the 19th Judicial Circuit Court.

    Enroll in a Court-ordered Program
