our departments

Experience & Innovation

With over a decade of experience advancing Apprenticeship Degrees through direct practice and partnership, Reach is uniquely organized to lead the large-scale adoption of job-to-degree programs across America. With our partners, we are testing new models founded on best practices. 

Reach Colleges & Centers

Reach University is home to several colleges and centers, each with a unique angle on transforming higher education and leading the widespread adoption of the Apprenticeship Degree. Coming soon: Behavioral Health.
Reach Teachers College

In partnership with school districts and Local Education Agencies, Reach Teachers College offers paraprofessionals, other school staff, and licensed educators the opportunity to earn a job-embedded degree to begin or grow their teaching career.

Learn More
Classical Liberal Arts, Science & Society 

The Institute for Classical Liberal Arts, Science & Society (CLASS) provides work-embedded programming in applied liberal arts for graduate and undergraduate candidates across the university. Formerly the College of Arts & Sciences, the CLASS Institute remains dedicated to upholding tutorial-based practices of inquiry and dialogue, embracing viewpoint diversity and robust discourse in the pursuit of truth.  

Learn More
NCAD

The National Center for the Apprenticeship Degree partners with employers, state agencies, and the nation’s most forward-thinking institutions of higher education to spearhead the systematic adoption of the Apprenticeship Degree.

Learn More

Academic Departments

Committed to learning through discourse and conversation, Reach University’s faculty is organized into subject area teams, including fields such as social science, math, science, and literacy. Reach is also home to a large computer science faculty and is uniquely committed to computer science and AI fluency as a core component of teacher preparation.
