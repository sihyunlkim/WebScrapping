Digital Learning Lab

The Digital Learning Lab is a non-quiet space in the library for study and collaboration. In addition to numerous study and work areas, there are 5 desktop stations with access to programs like Adobe, Accordance, Videoscribe, and more.

Need help with digital tools or a digital project? Drop-in Support for digital tools is available Monday through Friday, from 2-6 pm. You’re welcome to stop by the Digital Learning Lab during those hours for in-person support.If you’re remote or prefer a scheduled meeting time, you can also make an appointment with us!

Book an Appointment

Writing Assistance


In-person appointments for Writing Assistance are available in the Digital Learning Lab. Whether you have questions about how to formulate your thesis, or how you can improve your writing, the writing assistants are here to help. Student writing assistants can help you understand a writing prompt, brainstorm ideas, create a thesis, and improve writing structure and clarity.

Directions

The Digital Learning Lab is located on the third floor of the library, in the North Wing. If you take the main staircase or main elevator and either hallway away from the bookshelves, you will reach the North Wing area. The Digital Learning Lab is room 3132.

Swipe your ID card to access the room. If you have trouble accessing the space, email digital.learning@ptsem.edu for help. We’ll do our best to get your access sorted out within one business day.
