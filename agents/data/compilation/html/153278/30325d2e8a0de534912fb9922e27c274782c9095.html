Privacy Policy

University of Dubuque values your privacy. 

Founded in 1852, University of Dubuque is a private university affiliated with the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.). All of the University's interactions with current and prospective students, parents, alumni, vendors, business affiliates, and the general public carry this Christian focus. As a hospitable community, in the pursuit of excellence in scholarship, we follow a tradition of honesty, and care and concern for those around us. In keeping with our Christian values, University of Dubuque is dedicated to protecting your privacy and handling any personal information we obtain from you with care and respect. 

University of Dubuque Privacy Standards:

  1. We will not rent, lease or sell the personal information of any prospective or current student, or alumni.
  2. We will comply with the responsibilities set forth in the CAN Spam act.
  3. We will never knowingly infect your computer with software that obtains data and sensitive information. 
  4. We will follow the principle of treating others as we want to be treated.
