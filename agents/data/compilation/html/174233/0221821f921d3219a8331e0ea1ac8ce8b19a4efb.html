ITSS's Academic Technology and Course Design Team shares updates and programming with our faculty and student community every week. Each month during the academic year, we are writing and exploring awesome ideas and concepts in our monthly blog. #3ThingsWorthSharing. Explore the entries below or share your own ideas!

What do we talk about in posts? Everything including engagement and communication, AI, 3D Printing, Universal Design, assessments, active learning strategy, scaling courses for large enrollment, challenges in delivering hybrid lectures, VR & AR headsets, video capture tools… we could go on and on and on!
