Jan 27, 2025

Discover the advantages and challenges of 2 University supported tools in our upcoming workshops.

ITSS Academic Technology is hosting hour-long workshops on the following university supported Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools: 

  • Microsoft Copilot with Enterprise Data Protections
  • Zoom AI Companion

These sessions are designed to help faculty and staff explore the benefits and limitations of AI tools for enhanced productivity and collaboration.

Our Hands-On Sessions Will Demonstrate

  • Microsoft Copilot's with Enterprise Data Protection ability to summarize documents and assist with idea generation and text generation,
  • Zoom AI Companion's capacity to summarize discussions, respond to questions, and create meeting notes
  • A need for editing, fact-checking, and data security when using Generative AI tools.

Schedule

Sessions are approximately 1 hour and are scheduled at noon on:

  • February 11
  • February 13
  • February 25
  • February 27

All events will be hosted on Zoom with AI Companion enabled.
