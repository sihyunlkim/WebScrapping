Schedule a Free Campus Tour

Thank you for your interest in visiting UEI! Campus tours are led by an admissions representative who will review our programs in more detail, resources, and student life. Campus tours will last approximately one-hour.

During Your Visit We'll:

 

Discuss Your Program Options
Already know what program youre interested in? Great! Or are you not sure?
Thats ok too! Well go through the programs we offer and help you find the one that fits your career goals.

 

Learn About Your Goals
Well go through and learn more about you and your goals. This will help us know you better and understand how to get you on your path to success!

 

Tour the Campus
Well look at the program labs, career services center, student learning center, and common areas. We are excited to show you the places where your future could begin!

 

Discuss Class Schedule Options
Well go through our hybrid learning schedules and our day and evening class options, so you can see the choices and flexibility you will have to get your training completed.

 

Meet with Financial Aid
We know paying for your education can seem daunting. We do not want that to get in the way of your career, so youll have the opportunity to meet with a financial aid representative to discuss your options. They will explain everything from scholarships and grants to loans. Financial aid is available for those who qualify.

 

Answer Your Questions
We know this is a big step! We are here to answer your questions, so you have the information you need to make a decision on your career path.
