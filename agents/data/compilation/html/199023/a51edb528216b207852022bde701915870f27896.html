Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA)

Need funding to pay for college or training? The Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act (WIOA) can help!

WIOA Programs

  • Adult (ages 18+): Designed to assist economically disadvantaged adults, who are underemployed.
  • Dislocated Worker: Serves customers who have been separated from employment due to lay-off, business closure, or displaced homemakers.
  • Youth (ages 16-24): Designed to help low-income youth and young adults with additional barriers achieve educational and employment goals.

WIOA Program Requirements

  • Monthly attendance
  • Monthly check-in with your career advisor
  • Course syllabus each semester
