Ottawa University in Kansas City and Online

Getting your degree as an adult is a big deal. Ottawa University knows that, which is why we center our programs around you. Courses are offered in person at the Kansas City (Overland Park) campus and online. We know that you’re footing the bill and doing the hard work while also maintaining a busy work and life schedule. To get the most out of your investment of time and money, you want an accelerated path to your degree from a school with a long history and proven track record.

Rich History and Heritage

Ottawa University was established in 1865 and was one of the first to embrace a growing demand for programs specifically tailored to the needs and learning styles of adult students. A pioneer in adult education in the United States, OU opened its first adult campus in Kansas City in 1974. Today, Ottawa University is conveniently located in Overland Park, Kansas, and is easily accessible from Kansas City, Missouri, and Kansas City, Kansas. From our founding, we have offered working professionals the hallmarks of a quality higher education – flexibility, excellence, affordability, a wide range of accelerated programs, and comprehensive support. 

Flexible Schedules

Ottawa University in Kansas City and Online offers all courses in 8-week terms, so you can move through your program more quickly. Want to fast-track? You can take multiple courses at the same time. Want to slow it down? You can take one course per term. Our 8-week terms allow you to go to school year-round or take a term off any time you need. You can even take a face-to-face course and an online course at the same time. You decide what works best for you. At Ottawa University Kansas City, you get the opportunity to “do education” your way. You have choices at Ottawa University to accomplish your degree goals based on your unique needs. Students in the PA Program and the BSN Prelicensure Program attend classes in person at the Kansas City (Overland Park) campus.  

Advance Your Career

All of Ottawa University’s degree programs are rooted in the liberal arts, which provides growth for the total person and equips you to make a lifelong impact in the communities where you live and work. For many students, one of the school’s greatest benefits is its atmosphere of free and open inquiry into all aspects of knowledge, with an emphasis on teaching you how to think, not what to think. Employers are looking for such critical thinking skills in a marketplace that is changing at warp speed. 

Accelerated Degree Programs

Our Kansas campus offers a wide range of accelerated programs that are relevant in today’s ever-changing career landscape. You can choose from in-demand undergraduate degree programs, ranging from Accounting, Human Services, Nursing, and Health Care Management to Business Administration, Education, and Addiction Counseling. If you are in pursuit of a graduate degree, our Kansas City and Missouri area campus offers programs, including MBA, Executive MBA, Human Resources, Educational Leadership, Nursing, Physician Assistant Studies and more!

Earn Your Online Degree 

As an accredited University conveniently located in the Kansas City, Missouri/Kansas metropolitan area, Ottawa University is committed to providing you these hallmarks of a quality higher education. We are dedicated to preparing you for a life of greater significance as you earn and apply your degree knowledge in your workplace and community. We would love to answer your questions personally and help you decide if Ottawa University at Kansas City is the right school choice for you.

Comprehensive Support

Ottawa University in Overland Park offers caring, student-centered support throughout your learning experience. We start by giving you a student success team, which often consists of community development executives as well as enrollment, academic, and financial advisors who are uniquely qualified to advise you on your college admissions requirements, academic progress, and financial assistance. You can also utilize OU’s 24/7 help desk, the student portal with real-time access to student services, and online tutoring services using Net Tutor. Most importantly, faculty, staff, and advisors have an unparalleled commitment to helping you achieve your goals.
