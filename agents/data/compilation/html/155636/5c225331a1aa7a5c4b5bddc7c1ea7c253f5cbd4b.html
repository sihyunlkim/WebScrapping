IDAT Help

  1. IDAT Help

Instructional Design and Academic Technology is happy to help our users with their needs related to Blackboard, the Learning Management System at Ottawa University. Faculty can submit a Blackboard Course Copy Request, a Faculty Course Issue Report, a Course Development and Revision Request or just a general inquiry by emailing ouhelp@ottawa.edu. Chat and phone support are available to all Ottawa University users (students, staff, and faculty) through OU Help.  Students may complete the Student Course Issue Report form or seek help through OU Help.

Help and Support

In order to submit a Blackboard support request, please go to OU Help (https://myottawa.ottawa.edu/ouhelp) and select OU Help Requests. From there select Blackboard Support.
