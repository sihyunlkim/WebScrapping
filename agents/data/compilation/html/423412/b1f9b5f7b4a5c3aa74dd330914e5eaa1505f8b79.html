    Frequently Asked Questions

    What are the barbering program requirements?

    In most states, a high school diploma or GED is required to enter barber school. Specific programs may have additional requirements like age minimums.

    How long is barber school?

    Barber programs typically range from 1,500 to 2,000 hours, depending on the state. This can translate to roughly 9-18 months of full-time study.

    Is barber school different from cosmetology school?

    Yes. Barber schools focus specifically on men’s haircuts, beard grooming, and traditional barbering services. Cosmetology schools offer a broader range of training, including hairstyling, chemical services, and aesthetics.

    Do I need a barber license to work as a barber?

    Yes, all barbers require a state license to practice legally. Barber school programs prepare you for the licensing exam.
