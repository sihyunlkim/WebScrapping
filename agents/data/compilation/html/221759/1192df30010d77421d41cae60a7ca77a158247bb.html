Academic Integrity Resources

We want students to be successful in maintaining academic integrity in all of their coursework. There are many available campus resources to aid student academic success.


Academic Integrity Seminar

The Academic Integrity Seminar is a Canvas course designed to help students understand the consequences of academic misconduct, learn from their own experiences, and find resources to avoid academic misconduct in the future. This course uses an educational approach to teach students about citing sources, time management, and other academic topics for their academic success. Students may request access to this course by emailing studentconduct@utk.edu.


Studying and Academic Support

Vol Study Center

The Vol Study Center is a space where students can study, get supplemental instruction, and even free printing! The Academic Success Center’s website has more specific information about the Vol Study Center’s around campus.

The Writing Center

The Writing Center is a great resource on campus to help students with their academic success in all types of writing. The Writing Center offers services for both undergraduate and graduate students, with experiences tailored to the type of writing assistance needed.

AI Tools and Writing

The Writing Center has a webpage dedicated to sharing information about the use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in academic work. Remember, it is always best practice to know what policies your instructor has for the use of AI in coursework.

The Math Place

Located in Hodges Library North Commons, on the second floor, the Math Place is open to all students taking 100- or 200-level math courses. The Math Place is here to help you learn, grow, get your questions answered, do your homework and prepare for exams. Come and interact with other Vols that are taking the same course as you, while being supported by undergraduates who have been successful in their math classes.

Tips for Success

Academic Success Strategies

The Academic Success Center has compiled a list of academic success strategies to help students be successful in their academic courses. Please visit the Academic Success Center page to learn more about these strategies and how to get connected.

Vol Success Tips

The Division of Student Success has created Vol Success Tips to help students be successful in their academic courses. These tips cover everything from organization to communication. Check out the Vol Success Tips page for more information and to watch the video series.
