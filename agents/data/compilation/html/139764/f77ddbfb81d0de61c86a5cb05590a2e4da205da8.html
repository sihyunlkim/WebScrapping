Institutional Effectiveness and Planning

The Office of Institutional Effectiveness and Planning facilitates university-wide assessment and planning. Specifically, the office coordinates compliance with the accreditation requirements of the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACSCOC), with federal regulations, and with University System of Georgia (USG) requirements and initiatives.
