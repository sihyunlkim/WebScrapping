LLU’s Commitment to the Ethical Conduct of Research

Loma Linda University and its affiliates are committed to the ethical conduct of research. All members of the LLU community and its affiliates who are actively engaged in research are expected to conduct their research with integrity and intellectual honesty at all times.  Read More

Contact Research Affairs - Human Research & Compliance to schedule a consultation or ask a question about:

  • Quality Improvement of research practice
  • Compliance Hot line
  • Investigations of research misconduct, subject complaints, and related areas
  • Research Education and Training
  • Research Conflict of Interest
  • Research Export Control Program
  • Guidance on Imports
  • Other Research Compliance areas, including: 
    • Research with Select Agents and Controlled Substances
    • ClinicalTrials.gov Registration
    • PubMed Central Requirement for NIH-funded Research
