MyLLU Help


Problems with the myLLU Portal


If you are having difficulty logging into myLLU, using the 'Workflow Summary', or if you have any technical questions about myLLU, contact the helpdesk at lluhelpdesk@llu.edu or Ext. 48611 on campus or (909) 558-1000 Ext. 48611 off campus.

Forms for University Records Office


If you need assistance filling out forms for the Records office, contact University Records at registrar@llu.edu.
