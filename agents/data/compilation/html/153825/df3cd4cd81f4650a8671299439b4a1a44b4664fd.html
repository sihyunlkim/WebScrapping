Student Handbook

The Loras College Student Handbook is an information resource to help navigate your student experience. Some items you will find of interest are the code of conduct, policies and procedures, community living, and campus groups and organizations.

Think of the handbook as a road map. It contains the rules and procedures that will guide students while they live and grow at Loras College.
