Who May Borrow Library Materials?

Who can get a card?

  • Loras College students, faculty and staff
  • Loras College alumni and retirees
  • Members of the clergy of the Archdiocese of Dubuque.
  • Students, faculty, and staff at Iowa-based academic members of DALINC (Dubuque Area Library Information Consortium) such as
    • Clarke University
    • Divine Word College
    • Emmaus Bible College
    • University of Dubuque
    • Northeast Iowa Community College
    • Wartburg Seminary
  • Public library patrons in Iowa courtesy of the Enrich Iowa program.
  • Academic library patrons from libraries participating in the Iowa Open Access program.

How do I sign up for a card?

  • Loras College Students Faculty and Staff
    • Bring in your Loras ID, you’re already in our system
  • All others:
    • Fill out the Request a Library Account form
    • Present your library card and picture identification the first time you come to the Library.

What services can I access?

  • Loras College students, faculty, and staff
    • Check out any of the materials in our collection
    • Request materials from other libraries and pick them up at Loras College
    • Use Library databases on and off campus
  • All others
    • Check out any of the materials in our collection, EXCEPT reserve items.
    • Use Library databases ON campus.

May I renew my materials?

May I renew my library materials?

Yes, but with the following exceptions:

  • Reserve items many not be renewed
  • Government publications may only be renewed once
  • Interlibrary Loan renewal requests should be emailed to ill@loras.edu

To renew your materials, go to the Library's home page (http://library.loras.edu/)

  1. Hover over My Personal Library
  2. Select My Library Account
  3. Enter your network username and password (the same combination you use to log into your computer)
  4. Click the renew button next to the items you want to renew
  5. A note will appear above the box letting you know if you were successful or not

How do I renew materials if I'm not a Loras community member?

  • Call (563-588-7189) or
  • Email the Library (library@loras.edu)

If you have any questions, please call 588-7189 for assistance.
