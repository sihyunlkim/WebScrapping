Tools

UTSW is promoting AI literacy at all levels. These resources are intended for the UTSW community and may require VPN for intranet access.

Request AI Tools

If you wish to request access to UTSW AI tools, please visit the AI Tool Request page (VPN required). You must complete Responsible Use of AI at UTSW course before requesting a tool. Formal tool requests will be available based on the UTSW AI Implementation timeline.

Generative AI Tools at UT Southwestern

To promote responsible and productive use of AI at UTSW, we have partnered with a range of generative AI vendors to offer safe and effective tools for clinicians, researchers and administrators across the institution. The UT System overall has established a system-wide partnership with Microsoft, enabling wide access to cutting-edge technologies. Additional expansion of the UTSW and OpenAI collaboration will facilitate the availability of secure, HIPAA-compliant ChatGPT solutions across campus.

Accessing the Intranet

UTSW employees must go to MyUTSW to access more AI implementation details, Taleo and Percipio training, some applications, and policies. For intranet access, please use the GlobalProtect VPN client. Clinical users should use Citrix Workspace.

If you have questions about VPN or intranet access, please ServiceDesk@UTSouthwestern.edu the Service Desk or call 214-648-7600.
