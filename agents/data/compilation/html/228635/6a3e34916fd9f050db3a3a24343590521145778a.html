Research

We build and integrate clinically useful AI for radiology:

    NLP & LLMs for Radiology

    NLP & LLMs 

    We develop report-aware assistants, EMR mining, follow-up tools, and safety guardrails for LLM use in clinical reporting. Focus areas: factuality, uncertainty handling, and bias mitigation. Clinical integration is co-designed with radiologists and IT.

    Workflow Automation & Integration

    Workflow Automation & Integration

    Tools for automated protocoling, imaging-transfer verification, prioritization, and structured-impression support—implemented within clinical systems and evaluated for ROI.

    Imaging AI & Quality

    Imaging AI & Quality

    Detection, triage, and error-reduction models with prospective validation and post-deployment monitoring; emphasis on robustness across scanners and patient populations, with feedback loops for model-performance drift.

    Predictive Analytics (Multimodal)

    Predictive Analytics (Multimodal)

    Outcome and risk models that combine images + text (reports/EMR) for disease management and care pathways.
