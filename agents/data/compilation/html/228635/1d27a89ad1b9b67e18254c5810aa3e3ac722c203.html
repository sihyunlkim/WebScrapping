Discover Our Projects

At our lab, we are dedicated to developing advanced virtual reality simulators specifically designed for surgical training and medical education. Our focus extends to automating surgical video segmentation, identifying anatomical structures with precision, recognizing surgical tools in real-time, and providing comprehensive skills assessments. These innovations work together to enhance the learning experience and improve surgical proficiency.
