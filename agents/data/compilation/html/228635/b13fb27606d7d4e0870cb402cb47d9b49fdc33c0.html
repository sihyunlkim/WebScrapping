Training

Required Training Before Requesting AI Tools

Please take the Responsible Use of AI at UTSW course before requesting AI tools.

UTSW partners with Percipio to provide general AI training modules. To determine your current level of AI Competency, you can take the AI Knowledge Benchmark to measure your level of familiarity with AI concepts. 

AI Foundational Training Videos

The curated AI playlist in Percipio covers foundational knowledge of Generative AI and takes less than 30 minutes to complete. To learn more, go to the Foundational AI Playlist or take any module ad-hoc by clicking on the individual links below.

  1. Principles of Responsible AI Use (7:09 min)

  2. Ethical Use of AI (3:07 min)

  3. AI Use Cases at Your Office (8:47 min)

  4. Effective Prompt Writing for ChatGPT (2:33 min)

  5. Being an AI Advocate and Innovator in Your Organization (4:03 min)

  6. Impact of AI Models on Human Rights and Dignity (2:35 min)

  7. How to Protect Company, Personal, and IP Data While Using AI Models (4:14 min)

  8. Roles of Government, Industry, and Society in Regulating AI Models (3:43 min)

Accessing the Intranet

UTSW employees must go to MyUTSW to access more AI implementation details, Taleo and Percipio training, some applications, and policies. For intranet access, please use the GlobalProtect VPN client. Clinical users should use Citrix Workspace.

If you have questions about VPN or intranet access, please ServiceDesk@UTSouthwestern.edu the Service Desk or call 214-648-7600.
