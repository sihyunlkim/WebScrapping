Office of the Provost

The Office of the Provost advances the academic mission of the institution's four degree-granting schools, is responsible for more than $700 million in funding for biomedical research, and leads full-time faculty of more than 3,200.

About the Provost Office

Provost W. P. Andrew Lee, M.D., and our Vice Provosts lead institutional efforts across education, basic and clinical research, and faculty affairs.

We empower UT Southwestern faculty to pursue innovative research and train the next generation of professionals who will advance discoveries in science, medicine, health professions, and public health.
