This page provides access to Saint Leo Universitys Faculty AI Use Policy along with supporting resources. In addition to the full policy, youll find a quick guide that highlights key points and a webinar that offers deeper explanation of the policys purpose, guidelines, and impact on teaching and learning. Together, these materials support responsible, ethical, and innovative use of AI across the University community.
