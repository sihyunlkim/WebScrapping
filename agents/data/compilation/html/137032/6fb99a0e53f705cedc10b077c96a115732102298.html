Suggestions for lesson that promote thinking about AI with students

  • Play with and discuss an AI Automatic grading tool

  • Create a company and give it a values statement

    • Discuss Privacy, ethics and customer privacy and data

      • How could it impact their company?

  • Create a product

    • Review each other’s products for Ethics problems

      • Is it unique? Does it infringe on copyright, trademark, intellectual property rights of a different company?

  • Edit product(s) based on reviews from peers

  • Imagine a path to an AI job

    • Ethics Officer

    • Prompt engineer

    • Hallucination/Quality Assurance specialist

    • Future-think - what new positions might be needed in a company using AI?
