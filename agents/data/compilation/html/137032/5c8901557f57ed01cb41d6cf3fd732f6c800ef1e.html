On this resource page, you’ll find a comprehensive collection of scholarly articles, videos, tutorials, and practical guides focused on the use of AI in higher education. Topics include AI in teaching, enhancing course design with AI, AI and writing, AI in scholarship, and discipline-specific applications. Whether you’re exploring AI for the first time or looking to deepen your expertise, these resources are designed to support your work in thoughtful and meaningful ways.
