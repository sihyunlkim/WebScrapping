Explore Our Artificial Intelligence Degrees

Saint Leo University's Artificial Intelligence program equips students with advanced skills to thrive in a rapidly evolving tech landscape. The curriculum covers machine learning, deep learning, algorithm design, and natural language processing, providing hands-on experience solving real-world challenges using AI technologies.


Delivered fully online, the program offers flexibility for working professionals while maintaining rigorous academic standards. Courses are led by expert faculty from Saint Leo’s College of Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, and Data Science (CARDS), fostering a collaborative and future-focused learning environment.


Graduates are prepared for roles such as AI Engineer, Machine Learning Specialist, or Data Analyst. With a strong foundation in both theory and applied practice, students are empowered to drive innovation and contribute meaningfully to the future of artificial intelligence.

Online University Experience

Saint Leo University is a pioneer in providing cutting-edge online university experiences. As one of the first universities in the United States to offer online degree programs, Saint Leo is proud to continue leading and innovating the way online university programs are constructed and taught to educate our students.

  • Earn an online degree from one of the first universities to teach online. Saint Leo University has been offering online degree programs for more than 25 years.
  • Receive an education rooted in Catholic core values. Every course offered at the university incorporates one of the university’s six core values.
  • Learn at your own pace. Our flexible online degree programs allow you to complete coursework and engage in class discussions when it’s convenient for you.
