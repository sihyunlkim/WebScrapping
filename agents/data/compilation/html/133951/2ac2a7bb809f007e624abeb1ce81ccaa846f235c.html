Full course description

A webinar discussing Artificial Intelligence (AI), Tech Integrity, and its impact on our teaching and learning environments at FIU.

 

This webinar brings leaders from key University departments to discuss AI and its impact on privacy, security, teaching, academic integrity, and the student learning experience as well as the university-supported tech tools available for your classroom.  This interdepartmental lens will focus on the challenges and opportunities available using these technologies in this new age.
