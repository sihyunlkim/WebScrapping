Teaching & Learning with AI

Faculty Support for AI

Welcome! Our goal is to support and inspire you in creating engaging, innovative, and dynamic learning environments by integrating artificial intelligence (AI) effectively into your courses. We are here to partner with you in building students' critical AI skills relevant to today's rapidly evolving technological landscape while maintaining the intellectual rigor and integrity of your curriculum. 

CAT is committed to building meaningful relationships grounded in trust, collaboration, and continuous learning. We serve as your resource and ally in achieving teaching excellence in AI by providing specialized programming, tools, resources, and professional development opportunities designed to encourage pedagogical innovation and thoughtful reflection. 

For support creating something that works for you, schedule a consultation with a member of the CAT Team.  In addition to CAT's AI-team, we have AI Faculty Fellows who have specific knowledge in areas of pedagogical application of AI in curricula, AI agent creation, AI Rubric creation, and more!  You may also sign up one of CAT's many professional development opportunities which include AI programming and other workshops to broaden your pedagogical expertise, experiment with diverse teaching strategies, and implement these approaches to meaningfully enhance student learning and achievement.

How exactly does someone teach with Generative AI? How do we incorporate these powerful tools into our teaching and learning strategies?

Use tools like ChatGPT,  Copilot and Elicit to increase student engage with your course content. Examples include: 

  • Critical analysis, research skills, and content literacy are challenged by verifying AI-generated responses
  • Presentations of student projects identify accurate AI-generated information while discounting and correcting inaccuracies or oversimplifications
  • Responding to AI-generated real-world scenarios build creative thinking and problem-solving skills  
  • Learn academic argumentation by producing counterarguments to AI-generated positions and anticipating opposing viewpoints

Save time and expand your creative portfolio by using AI to:

  • Create rubric and syllabi creation
  • Develop learning outcomes
  • Conduct extensive research
  • Develop multiple choice and free response questions
  • Perform literature reviews and images
  • Brainstorm new assessments, and
  • Write programs without knowing how to code
