Florida AI Policy Summit

The Florida AI Policy Summit

The inaugural Florida AI Policy Summit, hosted by the Jack D. Gordon Institute for Public Policy at Florida International University, is a pioneering event dedicated to advancing the understanding and application of artificial intelligence in various domains. Set against the vibrant backdrop of Miami, Florida, this summit took place on January 19th, 2024.

Our mission is to bring together thought leaders, innovators, policymakers, and industry experts to foster collaborative discussions, share cutting-edge research, and explore the future of AI. The summit aims to address the critical aspects of AI policy, its impact on society, and the ethical considerations surrounding its deployment.

The Florida AI Policy Summit will feature a rich program including keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities. It will cover a wide range of topics from AI's role in healthcare, education, and finance, to its implications in privacy, security, and governance. Attendees will have the chance to engage with AI professionals from various sectors, contributing to a multidisciplinary dialogue that shapes the future of AI policy and practice.

This event is a unique opportunity for professionals, academics, students, and AI enthusiasts to gain insights, form collaborations, and be at the forefront of this rapidly evolving field. Join us at the Florida AI Policy Summit to be part of a conversation that shapes the future of AI in Florida and beyond.

Who Should Attend

 The Florida AI Policy Summit is a crucial gathering for individuals and organizations at the forefront of shaping AI policy and its impact on society. This summit is tailored for:

Policymakers and Government Officials

Engage with policymakers and government representatives who play a pivotal role in crafting regulations and policies related to artificial intelligence. Gain insights into the latest advancements and discuss strategies to foster responsible and inclusive AI development.

Industry Leaders and Private Sector Professionals

Connect with executives, entrepreneurs, and professionals from the private sector who are driving innovation in AI technologies. Explore collaborative opportunities, share industry perspectives, and contribute to discussions on the ethical and legal aspects of AI implementation.

Academic and Research Communities

Join leading academics and researchers in the field of artificial intelligence. Participate in discussions on cutting-edge research, share knowledge, and contribute to the development of policies that balance technological progress with ethical considerations.

Legal and Ethical Experts

Delve into the legal and ethical dimensions of AI with experts who specialize in navigating the complex landscape of AI regulations. Discuss the challenges and opportunities in creating frameworks that ensure responsible AI deployment.

Nonprofit Organizations and Advocacy Groups

Collaborate with representatives from nonprofit organizations and advocacy groups dedicated to promoting ethical AI practices. Explore strategies for addressing societal concerns, promoting transparency, and advocating for the responsible development and deployment of AI technologies.

Technology and Innovation Enthusiasts

Whether you're an AI enthusiast, developer, or technologist, this summit provides a platform to stay informed about the latest trends, innovations, and policy developments in the AI landscape.

Startups and Entrepreneurs

Connect with startups and entrepreneurs driving AI innovation. Explore opportunities for growth, navigate regulatory landscapes, and contribute to discussions on fostering a dynamic and responsible AI ecosystem.

Join us at the Florida AI Policy Summit to be part of the conversation shaping the future of AI policy and its profound impact on our society.

About Our Collaborators

 

 

Launched in 2008, the Steven J. Green School of International and Public Affairs at FIU educated the leaders and changemakers of tomorrow through innovative teaching that advances global understanding, contributes to policy solutions and promotes international dialogue. One of the leading schools of its kind in the world, the Green School is a full member of the prestigious Association of Professional Schools of International Affairs (APSIA), one of only 26 in the U.S. and only 38 in the world. The Green School is the only APSIA member school in Florida. With more than 5,000 students and 360 faculty, the Green School offers 38 interdisciplinary degree programs at the bachelor's, master's and doctoral levels, as well as more than 50 undergraduate and graduate certificate programs. The Green School encompasses eight departments: Criminology and Criminal Justice, Economics, Global and Sociocultural Studies, History, Modern Languages, Politics and International Relations, Public Policy and Administration and Religious Studies and is home to 17 of the university's most prominent international centers, institutes, and programs.

 

 

The Florida Center for Cybersecurity, also known as Cyber Florida, was created by the Florida State Legislature in 2014 to help Florida become a national leader in cybersecurity education, academic and practical research, and community outreach and engagement. While the University of South Florida serves as our host institution, providing administrative and budget support and oversight, Cyber Florida operates on behalf of the State of Florida at large, engaging a large and growing list of public and private partners to achieve our mission.

 

Lab22c stands as a preeminent full-service consulting agency, serving as a trusted thought partner for companies, funders, founders, and institutions aspiring to amplify their impact within Miami's dynamic innovation ecosystem. The agency is dedicated to guiding clients through the nuances of the #FutureofWork, adeptly integrating technological advancements into their business models. Led by some of Miami's most prominent connectors, innovators, and strategists, the Lab22c leadership injects a touch of humor with occasional Dad Jokes, fostering a collaborative journey towards success. The team combines years of collective knowledge, extensive experience, strategic acumen, top-tier talent, and boundless creativity to ensure clients and partners thrive in South Florida. Lab22c is committed to being unwavering 'ride or dies' for its clients in navigating the intricacies of the market.

 


Miami Dade College is the most diverse institution in the nation. There are 167 nations and 63 languages represented in its student body. The college's eight campuses and outreach centers offer more than 300 distinct degree pathways including associate and baccalaureate degrees, career certificates and apprenticeships. Baccalaureate degree offerings include biological sciences, engineering, data analytics, information systems technology, education, public safety, supervision and management, nursing, physician assistant studies, film and others. MDC is the recipient of many top national awards including the Aspen Prize. As Democracy's College, MDC changes lives through accessible, high-quality teaching and learning experiences. It is the home of the Miami Culinary Institute, the Miami Animation & Gaming International Complex, the Miami Fashion Institute, the Eig-Watson School of Aviation, The Idea Center, the Cybersecurity Center of the Americas, the Cloud Computing Center, the Center for Learning, Innovation and Simulation, the School for Advanced Studies, and the New World School of Arts, to name a few of its most innovative programs.
