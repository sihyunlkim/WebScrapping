Jan. 29, 2026
Tina L. Johnson
Office of Responsible AI newsletter  | Responsible AI use across the University of Arizona

December 10, 2025

A Message from David Ebert, Chief AI and Data Science Officer

As we wrap up the year, we want to thank you for your incredible engagement this semester and for helping us shape a bold vision for responsible AI at the University of Arizona. Your ideas, energy, and collaboration have already sparked exciting initiatives—many of which you’ll see highlighted below. 

But this is just the beginning. The Spring semester brings new tools, programs, and opportunities to lead in responsible AI—and we want you to be part of it. Share your ideas, your needs, and your vision for innovation. Whether it’s a challenge you’re facing, a project you’re dreaming about, or a partnership you’d like to explore, we’re here to make it happen together.

Let’s keep building something extraordinary. The future of responsible AI leadership at the University of Arizona starts with all of us—so join the conversation, get involved, and help shape what’s next!
