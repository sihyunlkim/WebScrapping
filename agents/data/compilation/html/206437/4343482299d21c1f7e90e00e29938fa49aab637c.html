With nearly 100 programs, you'll find your academic niche at Walsh University. Whether you're a traditional undergraduate student looking to obtain a bachelor's degree, a college graduate seeking a master's degree or doctorate, or a busy adult who wants to finish your degree in an accelerated time frame, Walsh has a program for you. Many of our programs are even available online.

Not sure which academic path to take?

Our General Education curriculum allows you to explore a variety of disciplines that ignite passion and cultivate a habit of curiosity and a lifetime of learning. Your advisor will work with you to find a program that fits your interests and skill sets.

Our experienced faculty comes from all over the world to offer an international perspective, which is always rooted in faith. With a 15 to 1 student/teacher ratio, we foster an environment that encourages students to interact closely with faculty members.

All of our academic programs are designed to educate the whole student—mind, body and spirit— through hands-on, experiential learning that goes beyond the books. Our students gain insights outside the classroom through internships, on-site training, student teaching opportunities, and service learning.

At Walsh, we believe that leaders are created through service to others. That's why volunteer work is not only encouraged, it's a requirement of our core curriculum.

The University provides its students a higher education that fosters critical thinking, effective communication, spiritual growth, and personal, professional and cultural development.
