Microsoft AI for Business Leaders

  4. Microsoft AI for Business Leaders

Microsoft AI for Business Leaders is a workforce upskilling program designed to equip leaders with essential technology skills to drive efficiency, collaboration, and data-driven decision-making. As businesses navigate an increasingly digital landscape, the ability to manage teams, projects, and operations effectively within a technology-first environment is critical.

Through hands-on training, participants will gain practical experience with Microsoft 365 applications, including Teams, Planner, Power BI, and Power Automate, ensuring they can confidently integrate these tools into their leadership strategies.

Participants will develop essential skills to:

  • Enhance team collaboration and productivity with Microsoft Teams, OneNote, and Planner.
  • Leverage data-driven insights using Power BI and Microsoft Forms for strategic decision-making.
  • Streamline workflows and automate processes with Power Automate and SharePoint for improved efficiency and compliance.
  • Harness AI and automation with Microsoft Copilot to optimize decision-making and drive innovation.
  • Lead digital transformation by implementing future-ready strategies that enhance agility and organizational performance.

This program is ideal for business leaders, project managers, and professionals looking to integrate AI and automation tools into their daily workflows. By the end of the program, participants will have the technical proficiency to improve operations, make smarter decisions, and build a more agile, high-performing organization.
