To Register/Self-Enroll in a Course on TaskStream:

1. Login to TaskStream through your MyWalsh account. The link can be found under the MyEducation tab.

2. Enter the program code: gened17. (If you are not enrolling in a GenEd course, you need to register going through my.walsh.edu)

3. Click self-enroll. Congrats! You are now enrolled in this year's program. To submit work, please see below.

See the attached file on Task Stream registration for additional assistance and screenshots.

**If you are trying to submit on the same day you enroll, you will have to wait at least 6 hours from the time you enroll before you can fully submit your work.

 

 

To attach an artifact in TaskStream:

1. Login to TaskStream through your MyWalsh account. The link can be found under the MyEducation tab.

2. Click on the link for GenEd 2016-2017 and find your course in the list of courses* located on the left hand side of the page.

*Note: Courses may not be in alphabetical order.

3. Click on the "+" sign located next to your course to expand. Then select on the requirement* you are submitting your artifact to. 

*Examples: Diversity, Creative Thinking, Speaking, etc.

4. Click on the "Attachment" button located at the bottom of the screen to upload your document/artifact.

5. Click "Save and Return."

6. Click the "Submit Work" button located towards the top right of the page. After clicking submit, you should receive confirmation of your submission. If you do not receive confirmation, please go back and submit again.

For any issues, please contact the IT Help Desk for further assistance, (330)244-4357.
