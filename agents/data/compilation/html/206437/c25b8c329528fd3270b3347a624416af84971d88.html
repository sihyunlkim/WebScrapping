Research Help

  • Library databases from A to Z
    The Library subscribes to over 150 research databases, containing citations, abstracts and full-text of periodical publications including newspapers, magazines and journals. Specialized databases also include company financial data, directory information and statistics.
  • We Deliver! - Interlibrary Loan
    Books, articles and videos not available from Walsh or OhioLink can be requested from other libraries around the country. To request an item, please fill out this form.
  • OhioLink resources
    Can't find the book or video you want at the Br. Edmond Drouin Library? OhioLINK gives you access to over 12 million items in Ohio's academic libraries.
  • OhioLINK Open Access Publishing for Authors
    OhioLINK has negotiated five read and publish agreements for its institutionsâ€™ authors. These publishing agreements allow authors to publish open access at no cost to the authors or additional cost to their institutions, in addition to providing access to a broad range of high-quality research articles from these publishers.

Textbooks on Reserve

You can put a copy of a textbook you use on reserve in the library. Reserve books can be borrowed by your students for two hours.

To place a book on reserve or see if the library can purchase one, please email the library or call us at 330.490.7185
