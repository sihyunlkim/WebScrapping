Introduction

The NIH has a new scientific data sharing policy which takes effect on January 25, 2023.  The policy will require all applications for NIH funding to submit a data sharing plan which the project will adhere to should the application be successful.  This guide will explain the data management and sharing plan, offer resources for finding data storage repositories, discuss budgeting for data management and sharing, go over considerations related to sharing human data, and list possible limitations on data sharing.

UTMB Resources

  • Research Data Management
    A guide from UTMB Research on the NIH data management and sharing requirements. Includes an overview of the requirements, the components of a data management and sharing plan, tips for budgeting, and suggested resources.
  • Center for Large Data Research & Data Sharing in Rehabilitation
    The Center's mission is to build capacity in rehabilitation and disability outcomes research using large datasets and in data sharing and archiving completed rehabilitation research studies. Includes news updates on data sharing, education and training materials on archiving and sharing data, and resources for large datasets in rehabilitation studies.
  • Electronic Lab Notebooks
    The use of electronic lab notebooks (ELNs) can help with data management and sharing. This page from UTMB Research reviews recommended ELNs.
