UTMB Galveston first in Texas to use artificial intelligence for routine cancer diagnosis

KPRC, Dec. 12, 2024

“A UTMB Galveston pathologist can now use AI to help diagnose prostate cancer,” reporter Haley Hernandez tells KPRC viewers. KPRC featured UTMB’s first in the state AI assisted prostate diagnoses process on their news broadcast. The next step, Hernandez reports, is using the tool for breast biopsies.
