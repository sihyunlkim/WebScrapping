UTMB AI Learning Hub

The UTMB AI Learning Hub is UTMB’s centralized destination for learning about artificial intelligence, data, and digital skills. The Hub is powered by Skillsoft Percipio, UTMB’s enterprise learning platform, and is available at no cost to all UTMB employees.

Through the AI Learning Hub, faculty, staff, learners, and leaders can access a rich library of on-demand content covering:

  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning

  • Generative AI and large language models

  • Data literacy and analytics

  • Digital, leadership, and professional skills

The platform supports self-paced learning, custom learning journeys, and digital badges, allowing individuals and teams to build skills progressively and demonstrate competency over time.

Whether you are brand new to AI or looking to deepen your expertise, the AI Learning Hub provides flexible, role-appropriate learning paths to help you engage with AI safely, responsibly, and effectively in your work at UTMB:

 

Recommended Learning Journeys

To get started, we recommend the following two learning journeys for all UTMB employees:

AI for Frontline End-Users

Designed for clinicians, staff, and operational teams who interact with AI-enabled tools in their daily work. This journey focuses on:

  • What AI is (and is not)

  • How AI is used in healthcare and operations

  • Interpreting AI outputs responsibly

  • Understanding limitations, bias, and human oversight

This is the best starting point for anyone who wants practical, real-world AI understanding without a technical background.


Generative AI: Introduction and Overview


An accessible introduction to generative AI technologies such as large language models and AI copilots. This journey covers:

  • How generative AI works at a high level

  • Common use cases and productivity applications

  • Risks, governance, and responsible use

  • What generative AI can and cannot safely do in an academic medical center

This journey provides essential context for engaging with generative AI tools thoughtfully and appropriately at UTMB.
