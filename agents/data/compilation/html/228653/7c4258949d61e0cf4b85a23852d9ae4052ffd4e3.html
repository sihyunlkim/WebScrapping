  • Endsley Named Chair, Microbiology and Immunology

    February 18, 2025 • 10:08 a.m.

    Janice Jones Endsley, PhD, has been named the next Chair of the Department of Microbiology and Immunology, effective March 1, 2025. An accomplished microbiologist, educator, and academic leader, Dr. Endsley has been an asset to the department for nearly two decades. She has the vision and experience necessary to lead its next chapter.

  • SPPH Names Inaugural Chair, Population Health and Health Disparities

    December 16, 2024 • 4:46 p.m.

    Mandy Hill, DrPH, MPH, has joined School of Public and Population Health as the inaugural Chair, Department of Population Health and Health Disparities. She has more than 20 years of research and experience in public health practice and a long history of NIH funding with a current R34 from the National Institutes of Mental Health.

  • Announcing UTMB's AI Council and Chief AI Officer

    October 17, 2024 • 9:40 a.m.

    We are excited to announce two significant developments in our institution's commitment to leveraging artificial intelligence (AI) for the advancement of healthcare: the launch of UTMB's Artificial Intelligence Council and the appointment of Peter McCaffrey, MD, as our inaugural Chief AI Officer.

  • Extending Thanks to Chair ad interim Funston

    October 15, 2024 • 2:27 p.m.

    Please join me in extending appreciation to J. Sean Funston, MD, for his service as Chair ad interim of the Department of Anesthesiology since July 2023.

  • UTMB to Welcome Vidal Melo, New Anesthesiology Chair

    October 15, 2024 • 2:17 p.m.

    Marcos Vidal Melo, MD, PhD, will join UTMB as Chair of the Department of Anesthesiology, effective Jan. 10, 2025. Dr. Vidal Melo is an accomplished physician-scientist with more than 23 years of cardiothoracic anesthesiology experience at major academic medical centers.

  • Recognizing Radiation Oncology Chair Hatch for Distinguished Service

    August 27, 2024 • 9:46 a.m.

    We extend thanks to Sandra “Sunny” Hatch, MD, FAACS, FACCS, FACR, for her distinguished service as Chair of the Department of Radiation Oncology.

  • Parvathaneni Named Chair of Radiation Oncology

    August 27, 2024 • 9:35 a.m.

    Upendra Parvathaneni, M.B., B.S., FRANZCR, will join UTMB as Chair of the Department of Radiation Oncology, effective Nov. 1. He will hold the John Sealy Distinguished Centennial Chair in Radiation Oncology.

  • Recognizing Longtime Pediatrics Chair Richardson for 50-Plus Years of Service

    August 15, 2024 • 2:03 p.m.

    We extend immense gratitude to C. Joan Richardson, MD, following 50-plus years of outstanding service to UTMB and the Department of Pediatrics, as she completes her service as Chair.

  • Tuli Will Lead Department of Pediatrics as Next Chair

    August 15, 2024 • 1:51 p.m.

    Sanjeev Tuli, MD, MEd, FAAP, will join UTMB as Professor and Chair of the Department of Pediatrics, effective Oct. 1, 2024. He will hold the John Sealy Distinguished Chair in Pediatrics.

  • Bianco Named Vice President and Vice Provost for Research, Chief Research Officer

    July 17, 2024 • 8:56 a.m.

    Antonio Bianco, MD, PhD, a renowned physician-scientist, scholar, and research leader, will join UTMB as Vice President and Vice Provost, Research, and Chief Research Officer, effective Sept. 1.

  • Office of Faculty Affairs Names Associate Vice Provost for Leadership Development

    June 18, 2024 • 8:47 a.m.

    The Office of Faculty Affairs shares that Kimberly Skarupski, PhD, MPH, will join UTMB as Associate Vice Provost, Leadership Development, effective Aug. 19, 2024.

  • UTMB Educators Take Top Honors at TEACH-S Educational Symposium

    June 21, 2023 • 8:00 a.m.

    UTMB was well represented at the TEACH-S Educational Symposium, with three UTMB posters receiving the top awards. This event provides a forum for showcasing scholarly innovations of the regional community of educators.

  • Peek Named SVP and Dean, School of Public and Population Health

    June 1, 2023 • 8:00 a.m.

    M. Kristen Peek, PhD, has been named Senior Vice President and Dean of the School of Public and Population Health (SPPH), effective June 1. Dr. Peek has provided outstanding leadership as Dean ad interim of the school since October 2021.

  • Lyons Named Chair, Department of Nutrition, Metabolism, and Rehabilitation Sciences

    May 30, 2023 • 8:00 a.m.

    Elizabeth Lyons, PhD, MPH, has been named Chair of the Department of Nutrition, Metabolism, and Rehabilitation Sciences, effective June 1. Dr. Lyons, who has been at UTMB since 2011, has served as interim chair for the past year.

  • Jamal to Serve as Chair, Department of Otolaryngology

    May 17, 2023 • 8:00 a.m.

    Nausheen Jamal, MD, will serve as Chair of the Department of Otolaryngology at the John Sealy School of Medicine, effective this summer. Dr. Jamal joins UTMB from the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley School of Medicine.

  • Two Receive Roberto Logroño, MD, Award for Professionalism in Health Care

    May 15, 2023 • 8:00 a.m.

    Congratulations to two individuals who received the 2023 Roberto Logroño, MD, Award for Professionalism in Health Care: Christen Sadler, MSN, RN, CNM, CNE; and Pooja Agrawal. Both awardees were recognized May 15, 2023.
