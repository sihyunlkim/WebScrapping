Academic Enterprise Announcements

Exciting things are happening in UTMB's Academic Enterprise every day.

We are committed to keeping our UTMB community informed about news from the AE - from new leadership appointments and awards to development and learning opportunities for our faculty, staff, and students.

Read announcements from members of our AE leadership and other important news items from across the AE.

  • UTMB Receives Joint Accreditation for Interprofessional Continuing Education; McGoff to Serve Full-Time as IPCE Director

    January 6, 2026 • 8:47 a.m.

    UTMB has received Joint Accreditation for Interprofessional Continuing Education, placing us among a select group of organizations that meet the highest standards for team-based education. Dr. Erin McGoff has transitioned to serve full-time in her role as Director of IPCE.

  • Starnes-Ott to Serve as Dean ad interim, School of Nursing

    December 23, 2025 • 11:54 a.m.

    I am delighted to announce Kristen Starnes-Ott, PhD, CRNA, FAANA, FNAP, will serve as Dean ad interim of the UTMB School of Nursing (SON), effective January 17, 2026.

  • Julie Griffith Named AE Director of Wellness

    December 18, 2025 • 8:38 a.m.

    I am excited to share that we have named a Director of Wellness for the Academic Enterprise. Please join me in welcoming Julie Griffith, MPH, who will begin her new role at UTMB on Jan. 20, 2026.

  • David Vines to Serve as SHP Senior VP and Dean

    December 17, 2025 • 3:17 p.m.

    David L. Vines, PhD, RRT, FAARC, FCCP, will serve as Senior Vice President and Dean, holding the Catherine L. Scholl, MD Distinguished Chair in Health Professions, effective Jan. 20, 2026.

  • SON Senior VP and Dean to Depart, Accepts President Role at MGH Institute of Health Professions

    December 3, 2025 • 10:05 a.m.

    Deborah J. Jones, PhD, MSN, RN, FAAN, will depart UTMB to take on an exciting and significant new role as President of the Massachusetts General Hospital (MGH) Institute of Health Professions. Her last day at UTMB will be Jan. 16, 2026.

  • Berenson named Vice Dean for Research, JSSOM

    November 21, 2025 • 1:53 p.m.

    I am pleased to announce that Abbey Berenson, MD, PhD, has accepted the new role of Vice Dean for Research at the John Sealy School of Medicine (JSSOM), effective December 1, 2025.

  • Dr. Susan Chubinskaya named Senior VP, Provost, and Chief Academic Officer

    November 20, 2025 • 10:14 a.m.

    Susan Chubinskaya, PhD, MS, has been appointed Senior Vice President, Provost, and Chief Academic Officer of The University of Texas Medical Branch.

  • Gugala Named Associate Dean for Faculty Affairs and Career Advising, JSSOM

    November 5, 2025 • 9:05 a.m.

    I am pleased to announce that Zbigniew Gugala, MD, PhD, has accepted the new role of Associate Dean for Faculty Affairs and Career Advising at the John Sealy School of Medicine (JSSOM), effective Nov. 1, 2025.

  • Celebrating New Accreditations for the Health Education Center

    October 17, 2025 • 10:35 a.m.

    I am elated to congratulate our Health Education Center (HEC) team on achieving additional levels of accreditation in Human Simulation and Research from the Society for Simulation in Healthcare (SSH). These new distinctions build upon the HEC’s 2024 SSH accreditations in Assessment and Teaching/Education, further highlighting the HEC’s commitment to excellence.

  • Recognizing Sealy Institute for Vaccine Sciences Director for Three Decades of Service

    September 26, 2025 • 9:00 a.m.

    We are honored to announce the retirement of Alan Barrett, PhD, DSc (Hon), Professor and Director of the Sealy Institute for Vaccine Sciences in the John Sealy School of Medicine at UTMB. Dr. Barrett has served UTMB with a distinguished career for 32 years, in the areas of research, education and administration.
