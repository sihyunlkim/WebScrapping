Please call our Service Desk at (409) 772-5200 for assistance.

The ITS Service Desk and Onsite Desktop Support provides support for students' computers and laptops. Services provided include spyware/virus removal, software installations, upgrades, and internet configuration. Please call the Service Desk at 409-772-5200 to initiate a support request.
