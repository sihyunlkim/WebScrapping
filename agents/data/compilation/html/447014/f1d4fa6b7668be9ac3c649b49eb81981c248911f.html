Daytona College is here to help you learn it, love it and live it! Learn more about how we can take you from here to career!

Daytona College is a private college located in the beautiful and historic city of Ormond Beach, Florida, just north of Daytona Beach. We offer a range of Diploma and Degree programs designed to prepare students to enter new careers in their chosen fields.

Whether youre just starting to think about a career, or looking to change your current work situation, Daytona College offers a focused career education to get you trained and ready to start a new career.

Real-World Experience

We work directly with employers to determine the best possible curriculum for our students.

We are dedicated to you. Waking up every day to see our students graduate and go to work is what we live for! We offer stimulating programs in the fields of Healthcare and Beauty and Wellness. We offer a blended approach of classroom instruction, online study, and on-campus lab and clinic practice.
