Why do I need to schedule an on-site tour before applying?

During the tour, you‚Äôll have the opportunity to explore our campus, meet our instructors, and see our classrooms and student salon in action. This experience allows you to ask questions, get personalized guidance, and ensure that our programs align with your goals. It‚Äôs an important step to help you feel confident and informed as you begin your journey in the beauty industry.

No, we no longer offer pedicures in our salon. However, we provide a variety of other nail services. You can learn more about our student salon services here.

The cost of our manicuring program, along with enrollment requirements and additional details, can be found here. Financial aid options may also be available for eligible students.

Our Esthetics program covers skincare, makeup, and spa services. You can find comprehensive information about costs, enrollment requirements, and course details here.

What are the enrollment requirements?

To enroll at Jenks Beauty College, you‚Äôll need the following:

  • Enrollment Fee: $100
  • State-Issued ID
  • Social Security Card
  • High School Diploma or GED
  • Three Personal References
  • $10 Money Order for the Oklahoma State Board of Cosmetology

Are your programs available online, or are they in-person?

All of our programs are conducted in-person at Jenks Beauty College. Attendance on campus is required for training and hands-on experience.

Are the programs free?

Our programs are not free, but many of our students qualify for financial aid. Tuition costs vary depending on the program you choose. Contact us for more information on financial aid eligibility.

Do you offer nail services in your salon?

Yes, our student salon provides a range of nail services. Please note that we no longer offer pedicures. Learn more about our services and pricing here.

What is the $10 money order from the state for?

The $10 money order is a state-mandated processing fee required for licensure or program-related submissions. If you have specific questions about this fee, please contact our admissions office.

When do classes start?

We start new classes every Monday! This allows flexibility for students to begin their journey toward a beauty career at a convenient time.

Are students allowed vacation time?

Students may miss up to 10% of their program hours without incurring additional fees. However, absences exceeding 14 consecutive days are not permitted. Any missed hours must be made up to meet the program requirements.

Does Oklahoma Promise cover tuition for your programs?

Unfortunately, Oklahoma Promise does not cover tuition for beauty college programs. However, there may be other financial aid options available to assist you.

Will I receive hands-on training during the program?

Absolutely! Students work in our on-campus student salon, providing real services to clients under the supervision of licensed instructors.

Can I work while attending the Cosmetology program?

Yes, we offer flexible schedules to accommodate students who wish to work while attending classes. Discuss your availability with our admissions team to find the right fit for you.

Do you assist with job placement after graduation?

Yes, we provide career services to help graduates find job opportunities. This includes resume assistance, interview preparation, and connections with local salons and spas.

What is the flexible schedule option?

Our flexible schedule allows you to customize your class hours to fit your lifestyle. Classes are available Monday through Thursday from 8:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m., and on Fridays from 8:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. You choose the hours that work best for you within these timeframes.

  • Part-time students must complete 23 hours per week.
  • Full-time students must complete 30 hours per week.

  • Monday & Tuesday: Focus on theory-based learning and foundational knowledge.
  • Wednesday, Thursday & Friday: Engage in hands-on practice, including working on mannequins, collaborating with peers, and providing salon services to the community.

Yes! Salon services and hands-on practice are scheduled on Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays. These sessions include working on mannequins, peers, or real clients from the community.

What happens if I miss a scheduled day?

If you miss a day, you can make up the missed hours during the remaining operating hours that week. This flexibility helps ensure you stay on track.
