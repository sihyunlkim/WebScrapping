Financial Aid: Bridging Your Ambitions at Jenks Beauty College

Financial Aid Options for Students

Financial aid is available to those who qualify. At Jenks Beauty College, we understand that the cost of education can be a challenge for many individuals and their families. Our Financial Aid Office is dedicated to assisting students in identifying opportunities and guiding them through the process of applying for financial aid.
