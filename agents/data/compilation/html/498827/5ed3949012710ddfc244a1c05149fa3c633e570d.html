Welcome to JT’s Cuts Academy

We are a family owned barber academy. Join our growing family and learn the classic and modern techniques of cut, style and hair health for both men and women.

Request Information

WHY CHOOSE JT’S CUTS ACADEMY?

At our academy, your experience will include:

  • Motivating mentors to show you what you can do
  • Study groups to help you pass state board exams and get certified
  • A complete barber stylist toolkit, mannequin and practice material
  • Close supervision while working with real customers
  • Hitting the clinic floor fast and with confidence
  • Working with instructors who care about you and your goals

Our goal is to make you an indispensable asset in our modern, ever-changing beauty industry.

Keep up with trends. Become a skilled artist and build your own business.

GET STARTED TODAY

Our program is 10-12 months long, a total of 1200 clock hours. We accommodate full and part time students.

You will easily make your own schedule if you are a parent, have a job or take other classes. Being located right off of I-95, we proudly accept students from all over South Florida.

Our barber and styling program is all-inclusive, starting from basic technique, covering certifications, health standards, sanitation and state board exams.Ensuring that when you graduate, you are well prepared to continue your learning journey on your own.

  • Flexibility – flexible scheduling options that include full-time or part-time

  • All inclusive– everything you need is included

  • Convenient – very close to I-95

OUR VALUES

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to educate, empower, and enable students to become expert professionals in the growing field of barbering and styling. At JTs we are committed tonot only the highest standards of education but helping our students go on to be successful in the work force.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Your success is our success. We challenge you in many ways to help you learn and encourage success through hands-on instruction that allows for individual differences and learning styles.

OUR COMMITMENT

Our educators will provide the tools and knowledge you need to help you reach your goals and create a strong foundation for your new career. With our flexible schedules, all you need is to make the commitment and let us dedicate ourselves to your achievements.

OUR PROMISE

We at JTs Cuts Academy promise to advance the field of barbering and styling through world-class education, fostering creativity, and inspiring the innate talents of each individual student.

OUR VALUES

We care for each one of our customers and students. We lead by example. In addition to excellent technical skills, you will learn to nurture the well-being of each client. Our students stand out by representing our family values for customer care.
