Current students, faculty, and staff at Andrews automatically receive weekly Agenda emails.

Anyone else can sign up to receive our weekly Agenda email by emailing agenda@andrews.edu and requesting a subscription.

Teaching Enhanced: Integrating AI in the Classroom
   School of Distance Education
   Tue, October 7, 2025 @ 04:00 pm - 04:50 pm
    Zoom

(Photo by MART PRODUCTION)

Explore evidence-based approaches to incorporating AI into your teaching practice. We'll showcase successful classroom implementations, discuss pedagogical frameworks and share practical examples of AI-enhanced lesson planning, content creation and student engagement. Learn how to leverage AI while maintaining your teaching philosophy and student learning outcomes. Sign up here.
