Proposal for Presentation

My research proposal focuses on identifying and analyzing the best practices for utilizing Artificial Intelligence (AI) by research professors. It aims to explore how AI can enhance research quality, efficiency, and innovation across various academic disciplines. The study will examine effective AI tools and methodologies, their integration into research processes, and their impact on data analysis, hypothesis testing, and scholarly communication. By gathering insights from leading AI applications in academia, this proposal provides actionable guidelines for research professors seeking to leverage AI technology to advance their work, foster interdisciplinary collaboration, and contribute to the academic community's evolution.
