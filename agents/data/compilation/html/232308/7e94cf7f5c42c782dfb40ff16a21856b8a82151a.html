New to Moodle?

Here are some resources to get you started:

Look for a Moodle session as part of faculty orientation or early in the semester. I do at least one hands-on workshop each semester to help faculty with Moodle.

If you have never used a Learning Management System (LMS) before or are transitioning from a different one and are unsure where to begin, you may want to start with the Moodle basics short videos created by Moodle and hosted on their YouTube channel. The playlist relevant to our current version of Moodle can be found here.
