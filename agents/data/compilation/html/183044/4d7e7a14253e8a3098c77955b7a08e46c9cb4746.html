Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence at UNH

UNH Professional Development & Training offers a wide range of non-credit, continuing education programs in computer and technology skills, with a growing emphasis on Artificial Intelligence (AI). Designed for business professionals, these programs provide practical training in AI applications to help you stay competitive in today's evolving digital landscape. Flexible enrollment options are available year-round, including one-day workshops, multi-day certificate programs, immersive boot camps, and online courses.

AI Essentials Certificate
Live Online

The AI Essentials Certificate Program will provide you with the skills necessary to effectively navigate the changing world of Artificial Intelligence (AI). Through live online sessions with expert instructors, you will gain skills in AI-driven marketing, decision making, customer experience, automation, and ethics and compliance. This certificate program is ideal for small and medium-sized business owners and managers, non-profit leaders, government agency professionals, and any individual seeking to advance their career through AI expertise.
