AI Masterclass: ChatGPT & Microsoft Copilot for Professionals

AI Masterclass: ChatGPT & Microsoft Copilot for Professionals

This full-day, hands-on workshop is designed for professionals who want to work smarter, not harder. You’ll learn how to use ChatGPT and Microsoft Copilot to
handle everyday tasks faster and more efficiently—without sacrificing quality.
Both of these tools are far more powerful than you may realize.
We’ll walk through real-world examples and live demonstrations, showing you how AI can take the busywork off your plate so you can focus on what really matters. Whether it’s writing a compelling email, organizing your notes, or summarizing a long report in seconds, this workshop will give you practical tools you can use right away.
What You’ll Learn:
✅ How to cut writing time in half: Get AI to draft emails, reports, and summaries instantly
✅ Smarter decision-making: Use AI to organize and analyze information in seconds
✅ Less busywork, more impact: Automate repetitive tasks so you can focus on big-picture goals
✅ Hands-on practice: Work through real-life scenarios and get comfortable using AI tools
✅ Quick wins, lasting benefits: Walk away with strategies you can apply immediately
By the end of the day, you’ll feel more confident, more efficient, and ready to take advantage of AI tools that make your job easier.
