Data Science and Artificial Intelligence Steering Committee

Data science and artificial intelligence (DS/AI) represent the backbone of modern innovation, cutting across disciplines to unlock discoveries and efficiencies in everything from healthcare to environmental sustainability.

Drawing on its existing strengths, including leadership in interdisciplinary research collaboration, Northwestern University has launched an initiative to integrate and elevate its many DS/AI capabilities, recognizing the transformative potential of these fields to lead in generating solutions to global challenges and to enhance the University's research brand on the world stage.

Championed by Northwestern’s President, Provost and Vice President for Research, this project focuses on the University priority “Harness the Power of Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence” as a broad-reaching platform to fuel research excellence and innovation across all our schools and units.

The DS/AI Steering Committee’s mission is to propose a vision and roadmap for elevating the quality and impact of Northwestern’s research and educational activities in data science and artificial intelligence.
