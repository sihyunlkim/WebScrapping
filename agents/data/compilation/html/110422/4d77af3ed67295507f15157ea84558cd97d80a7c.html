Online Courses

What is Generative AI?

All professions will be affected by generative AI. In this course, generative AI expert Pinar Seyhan Demirdag covers the basics of generative AI, with topics including what it is, how it works, how to create your own content, different types of models, future predictions, and ethical implications.

Getting Started with Prompt Engineering

Prompt engineering is the process of designing and crafting prompts that guide generative AI models to produce the desired output. You will start this course by learning how you can leverage prompt engineering to improve your day-to-day and work-related tasks.

Creative engaging training with Microsoft and AI

This course is designed for trainers and instructional designers looking to enhance their skills and know-how with developing impactful content.
