Campus Administrative Policies

Welcome to Cal Poly’s Campus Administrative Policy (CAP) website


CAP is managed by the Administration and Finance Division (AFD) and serves as the central repository for all active campus policies that have been officially approved through the steps in our Administrative Policy Process. This site is intended to serve as a resource for Cal Poly’s students, faculty, and staff to research policies that have university-wide application. Division, department and college policies, procedures, and guidelines that are specific to those areas will not be listed on this site.

As a note, Academic Senate policies are not managed by AFD and have their own separate approval process. For questions regarding Academic Senate policies, please reach out to the Academic Senate.
