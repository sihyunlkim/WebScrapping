The first private college in the state of Georgia. Over 50 areas of study. A 13:1 student-to-faculty ratio. At LaGrange College, you’ll discover this and more – because we believe YOU ARE MORE. More than a name, and more than enough. Here, you’ll find faculty and staff dedicated to your becoming and eager to help you discover the MORE you dream of.

Visit LaGrange

Visiting campus is the best way to get a real sense of the LaGrange experience. We offer all types of options – individual tours (our favorite!), group visits, special Admission events and virtual tours. We personalize visits to make sure you see areas of specific interest, and we schedule visits all year long. Welcome to your Home on the Hill!

Undergraduate costs & aid

At LaGrange College, we believe that you are MORE – but your tuition bill doesn’t have to be. U.S. News & World Report labels LaGrange “a great school at a great price,” and more than 94% of our undergrads receive financial aid. Let’s work together to create an affordability plan that works for you.
