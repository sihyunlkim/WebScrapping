Be real-world ready

Students and alumni will tell you: LaGrange College is the place to be for a dynamic educational experience. Our faculty can’t wait to guide your growth through mentorship, personalized learning and engaging classroom discussions. Prepare to thrive in your career by developing critical thinking skills and a sense of social responsibility to take with you wherever you go.

From accounting to the arts and exercise science to engineering, LaGrange College offers MORE than 50 areas of study. Find your passion at the crossroads of what interests you and who you want to become.

Put your talent on display

As a LaGrange student you’ll prepare an electronic portfolio that reflects the work you’ve done during your time on campus. In addition to sharing with friends and family, you can provide your e-portfolio to potential employers as evidence of your skills.

It’s all part of Ethos, the college’s recently adopted common curriculum, designed to help you build the skills to reach your greatest potential in life. Whatever path you choose, you’ll know that you’re ready for the challenge.
