  • Student Academic Services

The college has many resources to help you on your academic journeys.

The Panther Academic Center for Excellence offers expert coaching outside of class, as well as weekly study halls in math, science, English and other subjects, all free and all staffed by LaGrange faculty. PACE academic coaches are available to help you explore new academic paths, set goals, work on time management or plan successful study strategies.

The Frank and Laura Lewis Library includes more than 200,000 printed and electronic books, an excellent reference collection, a large DVD and CD collection and numerous full-text databases for all academic disciplines.

If you are struggling with your coursework, the Tutoring Center, located in the 24 hour study area of Lewis Library, is a place where you can get help from students who have succeeded in those courses.  They are ready and willing to help you study and understand the material.

Need help with a writing assignment? The Writing Center, housed in Lewis Library, has coaches available to teach you how to research, arrange evidence and prepare your work.

The Registrar’s Office handles all transcript requests, and everything can now be handled online.
