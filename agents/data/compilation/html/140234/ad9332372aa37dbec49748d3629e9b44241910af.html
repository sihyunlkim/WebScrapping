Build your computer skills at LaGrange College

Don't fall behind in the Digital Age:

  • Keep up with your family and friends through email and social media
  • Gather information and research on the World Wide Web
  • Enhance your career skills and your résumé

I. INTRODUCTION TO COMPUTERS
October 25
In our first course, you will learn the most basic computer skills, computer terms and definitions, keyboard and mouse usage, Internet searches, use of Windows, how to set up your own email account and more. Handouts will be provided.

II. INTERMEDIATE COMPUTER SKILLS
November 8
In this mid-level course, you will learn about word processing, email etiquette, opening and closing files, copying and pasting files, Internet research, understanding YouTube, social media and more. Handouts will be provided.

III. ADVANCED COMPUTER SKILLS
November 15
In our advanced course, you will acquire an introduction to Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, spreadsheets and more.

  • Price: $49 per course; $119 for all three
  • Times: All classes begin promptly at 9 a.m. and end at noon.
  • Location: LaGrange College campus, Smith Hall, 3rd floor, Room 322
  • Dress: Casual
  • Parking: Park in the lots adjacent to the pedestrian bridge on Vernon Street; use the bridge to enter campus. Signs will direct you to Smith Hall. Please contact us if you have mobility issues or will need handicap parking.
