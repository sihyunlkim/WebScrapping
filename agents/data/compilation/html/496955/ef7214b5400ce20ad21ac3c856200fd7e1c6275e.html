LAUNCH A NEW CAREER WITH THE HELP OF UEI COLLEGE

UEI trade schools in Arizona cater to the evolving needs of the job market by providing students with focused, hands-on education tailored to specific trades and technical professions. Unlike traditional academic pathways, UEI Colleges emphasize swift, skill-based learning, enabling students to quickly transition from classroom instruction to fulfilling careers.

As vocational schools in Phoenix and Mesa, UEI College has a longstanding reputation for helping students reach their goals by offering high-quality training programs in rewarding fields. UEI College gives students in The Grand Canyon State a chance at success in a new career. Originally founded in 1982, UEI believes that anyone can improve their career opportunities with guidance in the right direction.

UEI College Phoenix and Mesa are accredited by the Accrediting Council for Continuing Education and Training (ACCET). UEI is a premier college in Arizona, offering daytime and evening classes Monday to Friday. Arizona campuses are equipped with computer labs, study rooms, outdoor areas, learning resources, and more to give students the tools they need to learn and grow in their new careers. Instruction includes hands-on learning, advice from industry professionals, and materials to excel in a new trade.

LEARN A NEW TRADE IN THE MEDICAL FIELD OR HVAC AT UEI COLLEGE

Being one of the top trade schools in Phoenix and Mesa, UEI College offers programs that complement jobs in rewarding fields. Students can earn a diploma in trades such as HVAC, Electrician Technician, and Automotive Technician. If you're more interested in healthcare, explore Medical Billing and Coding, Dental Assistant, or Medical Assistant programs in Phoenix and Mesa.

Local Attractions and Other Nearby Locations

Our technical colleges in Arizona lie in the heart of two of the state's most vibrant cities-Mesa and Phoenix.

Located in the culturally rich city of Mesa, this campus is surrounded by a blend of history, art, and nature. Students are a short drive away from the iconic Mesa Arts Center, which showcases the city's passion for arts and culture. For those looking to relax after a long day of studying, the nearby Usery Mountain Regional Park offers scenic trails and panoramic views. Proximity to major highways ensures the campus is easily accessible.

Our Phoenix campus offers students a chance to immerse themselves in the urban charm of the state's capital. The Desert Botanical Garden is a popular attraction for students and locals alike. However, if you prefer shopping in your free time, you may enjoy the Biltmore Fashion Park, an outdoor retail and dining mall. Its location near the I-10 ensures seamless connectivity, making commutes hassle-free for students traveling from various parts of the city.

FAQs

How much does trade school cost in Arizona? Collapse

The cost of attending a vocational school in Arizona can vary by the program and its duration. A short diploma program will likely cost less than a more in-depth two-year program. However, we understand that tuition can be a significant barrier and concern, so we offer several financial aid options. Financial Aid is available to those who qualify.

What are the admission requirements for UEI Colleges in Arizona? Expand

All trade schools in Arizona have admissions requirements. At UEI College, prospective students must have completed high school or its equivalent. As such, they're required to show proof of a high school diploma or GED. Additionally, applicants must be at least 17 years of age or older to ensure maturity and readiness for the chosen program. If you meet all of the requirements, you can begin the application process.

We recommend taking our Program Match Quiz to determine which program may be right for you. Then, you can start familiarizing yourself with the programs we have available.

To get more information, you can fill out our contact form or call us to speak to an admissions representative. From there, you can schedule a campus tour and sit in on a live class. Since we offer ongoing enrollment, you can enroll the same day and begin classes on the next available start date.

How long are trade schools in Arizona? Expand

Arizona trade school programs can vary in duration. At UEI College, students can find a range of programs with lengths tailored to the specific skills they need to enter the workforce. Most of our programs allow students to complete their training in as little as ten months.

A program focusing on basic technical skills might be shorter, while one that offers in-depth training in healthcare or advanced technological fields may require a longer commitment.

What career opportunities are available after completing a trade school program? Expand

Trade schools in Arizona offer focused training, directly preparing students for specific professions. Those who attend trade school can choose from a variety of programs that prepare them for jobs in the following fields:

  • Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC)

  • Dental assistant

  • Medical assistant

  • Medical billing and insurance coding

We understand that completing a program is just the beginning of someone's professional journey. Our dedicated career services team provides support after graduation, such as:

  • Assistance with resume creation

  • Interview tips and practice

  • Career fairs and special events
