Driving Directions Expand

The UEI Mesa Campus is conveniently located, ensuring students from diverse locations can benefit from its programs.

From Scottsdale

A drive down the AZ-101 Loop South will lead you directly to our campus. This journey is straightforward, offering scenic views of the cityscape.

  1. Drive on AZ-101 Loop S from E Indian School Road.
  2. Follow AZ-101 Loop S to S Price Road.
  3. Take exit 54 from AZ-101 Loop S.
  4. Take E Southern Avenue until you reach UEI College Mesa. Our campus will be prominently situated, ready to welcome you.

From Chandler

The commute from Chandler is about 15 to 20 minutes, and you can take the AZ-87 North directly to our campus.

  1. Take N Colorado St to N Arizona Avenue.
  2. Follow N Arizona Avenue to W Baseline Road in Mesa.
  3. Take S Ext Road and W Southern Avenue. Our campus will be on your right.

Local Attractions and Other Nearby Locations

Our Mesa trade school is strategically located among Mesa's notable landmarks. The campus is just a stone's throw away from Riverview Park, allowing students to have tranquil green space to unwind and rejuvenate. For those who enjoy a blend of nature and urban amenities, the serene banks of the Salt River are just a short drive away. History and culture enthusiasts will find the nearby Arizona Museum of Natural History a treasure trove, offering insights into the region's rich heritage. Our campus is also well-connected via major highways, with the AZ-202 Loop facilitating easy commutes from various parts of the city.

Mesa Trade School FAQs

What are the benefits of attending a trade school in Mesa, AZ? Collapse

Attending a trade school in Meza, AZ, offers advantages that align with the rapidly changing job market and the specific needs of students. Here's a breakdown of the benefits of going to UEI Mesa:

  • Focused training: Technical schools in Mesa, AZ, provide specialized training tailored to specific industries and jobs. This allows students to acquire the skills directly relevant to their chosen profession.
  • Shorter duration: Unlike traditional colleges, most trade school programs can be completed in a shorter timeâ€” anywhere from ten months to two years. This allows students to enter the workforce and start earning much faster.
  • Affordability: Trade schools in Mesa, AZ, are more affordable than traditional colleges and universities. Students often graduate with less debt and can start their careers right away.
  • Smaller class sizes: Mesa trade schools typically have smaller class sizes than large universities. This ensures personalized attention, fostering a more intimate learning environment where students can get direct feedback and support.
  • Career services: Many trade schools have dedicated career services teams that assist students in job placements, resume building, and interview preparation, giving them an edge in the job market upon graduation.

Our Mesa trade school is suitable for any individual, particularly hands-on learners, immediate job seekers, and career switchers. So whether you're looking to change careers, eager to enter the workforce, or have an interest in a specific trade, UEI College might be right for you.

How long does it take to complete a vocational program in Mesa? Expand

The duration for completing a vocational program in Mesa varies based on the program. Many of our programs can be completed in as few as ten months. However, other programs may be as long as two years. Our courses are designed to be concise, with students finishing their studies and entering the workforce quickly.

How much is tuition at UEI Mesa? Expand

The tuition at UEI Mesa can vary based on the program. Some specialized courses may have higher tuition fees than shorter, more direct programs. This cost variation reflects the resources, faculty expertise, and equipment required for each specific field of study.

However, we understand that tuition can be a significant investment, so we offer several financial aid options to assist students. Financial aid is available for those who qualify.

We encourage all prospective students to consult with our financial aid department to explore the available options and determine the best course of action.

How can I pick a vocational program at UEI College Expand

Selecting the right vocational program at UEI College is a crucial step toward a successful career. Here are some tips to guide you in making an informed decision:

  • Self-assess: Begin by understanding your passions and strengths. Think about the jobs or roles that have always interested you. A career that aligns with your interests will likely bring both success and satisfaction.
  • Research job markets: Look into industries that are currently in demand and projected to grow in the future. A program that leads to a high-demand career might offer better job opportunities upon completion.
  • Visit the campus: Spend a day at UEI College to learn about the environment. Speak with instructors and current students to gain insights into the programs you're considering.
  • Take our Program Match Quiz: Use our Program Match Quiz to help narrow down your choices. The quiz will analyze your interests and strengths and match them with suitable programs, providing a tailored recommendation.
