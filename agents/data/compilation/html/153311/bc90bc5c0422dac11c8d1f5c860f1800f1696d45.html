Artificial intelligence is no longer optional. It is a practical way to cut costs, work smarter, and move faster. This fully online certificate shows you what AI can do today, how it actually works, and how to build a plan that delivers real value for your organization. 

Explore how AI reduces repetitive tasks, improves accuracy, strengthens decision-making, and frees teams to focus on higher-priority work. Then move into core AI concepts and finish by creating a strategy you can use at work. 

Walk away with the clarity and confidence to lead AI adoption where you work. 

What You Learn 

  • How AI improves efficiency, reduces human error, and supports better decisions. 
  • How machine learning, natural language processing, and neural networks function. 
  • How to use modern AI tools to increase productivity. 
  • How to evaluate where AI fits in your workplace. 
  • How to develop an actionable AI plan and strategy. 

Program Format

Fully online

100% Online 24/7

4.8 CEUs

Earn 4.8 Continuing Education Credits

48 Hours

$495

Total cost $495

In this certificate program, you’ll complete three courses in the following order: 

Learn what modern AI can do and how tools like ChatGPT boost productivity. Explore real-world uses, practice applying AI to everyday tasks, and understand why these tools are reshaping how work gets done. 

Go deeper into the mechanics of AI. Explore machine learning algorithms, neural networks, natural language processing, and common AI frameworks. Build a practical understanding of how these systems learn, respond, and improve.

Pull everything together. Use worksheets to adjust account balances, prepare financial statements, and evaluate financial performance. Learn how to close your books properly and get ready for the next accounting period.
