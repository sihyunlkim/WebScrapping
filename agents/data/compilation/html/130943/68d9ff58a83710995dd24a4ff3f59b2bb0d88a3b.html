Artificial Intelligence in Teaching and Learning

The University of Delaware, already a leader in cutting-edge AI research, is now leading the conversation about AI in the context of teaching and learning. Through theAI for Teaching and Learning Working Group, 26 faculty and staff members from units across the University are developing innovative teaching practices and policies in relation to emerging AI tools, beginning with a yearlong seminar series. The Working Group also complements the work of UDsData Science Instituteand theArtificial Intelligence Center of Excellence(AICoE), as well as the work of a broad range of faculty and graduate students already engaged in AI research.
