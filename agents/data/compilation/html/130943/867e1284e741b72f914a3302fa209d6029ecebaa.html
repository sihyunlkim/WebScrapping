Generative AI and your Research

Generative AI is quickly becoming a part of search engines and research tools everywhere. It's important that you understand how these tools work, and how to use them effectively and ethically in your work. Follow the AI tutorial to get an introduction to common AI questions, and guidance on the best way to implement these tools.

NOTE: The next button will navigate you away from this guide. You can open the tutorial in a new window to keep this page open.

Introduction

AI literacy is a term that describes skills associated with the ability to comprehend the basic principles, concepts, and applications of artificial intelligence, as well as the implications, limitations, and ethical considerations associated with artificial intelligence. In this tutorial, you'll learn the basics of how AI technology works, ways to incorporate AI that maintain your authentic voice and ideas, effective prompting skills, and explore complicated issues that surround AI such as copyright, bias, and transparency.

You'll navigate through 9 pages and a review quiz. The whole tutorial should take about 1 hour.
