EDUC 439/639: ChatGPT & AI Chatbots

Welcome to the Web site for EDUC 439/639: ChatGPT & AI Chatbots. When taken as EDUC 439, this course is an elective at the undergraduate level. It counts, for example, toward the undergraduate Minor in Educational Technology. When taken at the graduate level as EDUC 639, this course fulfills requirements in the Master of Education in Educational Technology program at the University of Delaware. With permission of your advisor, EDUC 639 can be taken as an elective in the Doctor of Education program.

You will be happy to discover that this course uses a hybrid format that enables you to learn online as well as in optional face-to-face help sessions.

To submit answers to assignments in this course, you log on to Canvas, which is the University of Delaware's instructional management system.

For the official word about the course content, see the course description.
