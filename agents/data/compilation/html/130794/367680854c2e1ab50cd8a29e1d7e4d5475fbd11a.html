Guidance

Review a growing collection of resources.

  • Cybersecurity - Use AI the Smart Way

    View Cybersecurity’s AI episode in the Know Your Risk series to understand data classifications that guide where your data should (and shouldn’t) go. Yale log in required.  

  • AI and Privacy

    Guidance on privacy considerations for AI systems as well as for AI assistants (ex: notetaking and transcription AI apps).

  • Data Governance Guidance for Staff

    Yale’s guidelines help new AI users protect data and intellectual property while ensuring ethical and contractual compliance.

  • Guidelines for the Use of Generative AI Tools

    Guidance to all members of the Yale community regarding the responsible use of generative AI. 

  • Learn about AI

    Find videos and key AI terms to help you start using AI chat tools, plus learning courses to deepen your understanding.

  • Using AI in Research

    Yale Library can help you get started with AI, evaluate AI outputs, and provide tailored advice on effectively integrating AI tools into your research, teaching, and learning.

  • AI Guidance for Teachers

    In consultation with instructors and technology experts at Yale and beyond, the Poorvu Center offers guidance on exploring how generative AI works.

  • AI Guidance for Students

    As a hub for faculty and student collaboration, the Poorvu Center has engaged in many conversations about the impact of generative AI.

  • IT Foundations: Artificial Intelligence Course

    This 45-minute Workday Learning course introduces AI terms, concepts, and case studies, aimed at those new to AI or seeking to understand its campus applications.
