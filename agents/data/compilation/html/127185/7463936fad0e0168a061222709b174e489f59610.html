Center for Teaching & Learning

Fort Lewis College Administrative Offices Center for Teaching & Learning

Equitable and innovative learning at Fort Lewis College

The Center for Teaching & Learning is dedicated to fostering equity in learning and driving innovation in higher education. Guided by the belief that all students can succeed, we offer comprehensive support to FLC faculty through:

  • Inclusive and accessible learning design resources to meet the diverse needs of faculty.
  • Data-driven insights to enhance teaching practices and support student success.
  • Cross-disciplinary approaches to digital learning and distance education.
  • Canvas support to improve accessibility, consistency, and usability.

Visit us in person or contact us via email to explore CTL resources or schedule a 1:1 consultation.

  • Read CTL's 2023-24 Impact Report

Teaching tools and opportunities through CTL


  Teaching development

Participate in professional development opportunities through CTL to enhance your course design or teaching.

  Distance Education

Create high-quality online, hybrid, and HyFlex courses that are supportive, inclusive, and foster collaboration from anywhere.

  Teaching Toolkit

Explore pedagogical strategies related to identity pedagogies, equity-minded teaching, supporting first-year students, and evidence-based, data-driven approaches.

  Teaching Effectiveness at FLC

Use the teaching effectiveness framework and the multiple voices of feedback to inform teaching growth as you apply for promotion and tenure.

Contact Info: mathompson@fortlewis.edu

  • Create faculty training and resources to support the implementation of a learning analytics platform in Canvas.
