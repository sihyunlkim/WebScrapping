Technology at FLC

At FLC, we believe in the importance of technology in our lives. To support personalized and hands-on education, we ask students to have a laptop and specific software.

We understand that not everyone has equal access to technology. That’s why we offer a limited number of free or affordable tech packages on a first-come, first-served basis. Our goal is to ensure that as many students as possible have the tools they need to keep up with the digital world.

UPDATE: All laptops have been rented for the Fall Semester. Please check back Spring 2025 Semester for availability.

Internet

FLC provides free wifi Internet access in all areas of campus. Use the Fort Lewis College network for the most secure and fastest network connection.

Through a grant with the US Department of Education, $100 per semester scholarships are available to supplement off campus internet access.

Eligiblity requirements are:

  • 150% of Pell Eligibility
  • Enrolled in a Distance ED (hybrid, online, HyFlex) course for the current term
  • I will be able to provide a receipt for off-campus internet access

Apply for Internet Assistance

Laptops

Rent a laptop

Laptop rental includes:

  • Dell Latitude 7440/ laptop with Windows 11, 16 GB memory, 512 GB SSD storage

Rental period

Semester rental periods run through the following break if you’re registered for the following semester. We open up renewals about 3 weeks before the end of the semester.

  • May-December
  • December-May

Rent your tech

Rental agreement:

When you reserve your tech bundle, we'll send you a link to the rental agreement that explains what happens if you don't return or damage the equipment.

*If you bring your own device, be sure it meets these minimum requirements:

Windows 10                                                   

  • Intel I5 or equivalent                         
  • 8GB RAM                               
  • 100 GB disk space available       

Mac                                               

  • Intel Quad Core CPU or faster processor
  • 8GB or greater of RAM

Not recommended

  • Chromebooks are not recommended
  • Tablets are not recommended
Printers

Our shared campus printer program will help you save time and money. Using the printer program is less expensive than bringing your own, as you won't have the upfront cost of a printer or the ongoing costs of paper, ink, and repairs. We don't recommend you bring a printer to the residence halls, especially wireless printers, as they cause interference with the wireless network.

You have a $4.00 /semester printing and copier allowance with unused amounts rolling over to the next semester. Printers are located in all residence halls and computer labs. If you use all of your printer allowances, you can use Skycash.

Learn about lab and resident hall printing and copying

Whether you bring your laptop or rent one from us, all your essential software is free to use while you're a student at FLC. This includes Office 365 programs like Word, PowerPoint, and Outlook, among other applications. What's more, watch our training tutorials to use this software like a pro.
