THE FLC CATALOG

The Fort Lewis College Catalog of Courses is issued every Academic Year, which begins at the start of the Fall term (e.g. the fall of 2025) and runs through the end of the following summer terms (in this example, the summer of 2026). Your Catalog Year is the Academic Year you first enrolled at Fort Lewis College.

Your Catalog Year remains the same until you elect to change it. Contact your Skyhawk Station Advisor or Faculty Advisor to petition to change catalog years.

You may also declare Minors and Certificates on different catalog years. For instance, you could be on the 2023-2024 Catalog Year as a Psychology Major, and you could have a certificate in Regenerative Food Systems from the 2024-2025 catalog. This flexibility allows students to add minors and certificates from newer catalogs without changing their major requirements. Talk to your Skyhawk Station Advisor or Faculty Advisor if you want to learn more.

This Page Links to the FLC Academic Policy Library, Student Handbook, and Academic Integrity Policy

The FLC Policy Library and Student Handbook are maintained outside the Catalog of Courses. For the most up-to-date information on each, follow the links below:

Academic Handbook

The Academic Handbook is your guide to all the Academic rules, policies, and procedures relevant to your studies.

Student Handbook

The Student Handbook is packed with essential information on safety, conduct, housing, and support services. It’s like the Academic Handbook, but for all the stuff you need to know outside Academics.

Academic Integrity Policy

The Academic Integrity Policy outlines policies and procedures surrounding cheating, falsification, or plagiarism. Students must also refrain from unethical behavior that affects grades, academic placement, or academic standings. These standards apply to all courses taken on or off campus, and all modes of delivery.

Academic Policy Library

The Academic Policy Library is the full list of all rules and regulations governing everything at FLC, from students to faculty to staff.
