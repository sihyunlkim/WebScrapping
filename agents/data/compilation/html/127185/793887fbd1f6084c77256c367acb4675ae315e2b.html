Explore, learn, and lead with AI

You can learn at your own pace or with support from across campus. Whether you’re new to AI or ready to go deeper, Fort Lewis College offers a range of professional development opportunities—from live workshops and asynchronous training to resource libraries and peer support networks.

How faculty and staff are using AI

AI is already transforming work at FLC. Faculty are using AI for grading assistance, content creation, instructional planning, and guiding students in using AI to create personalized learning experiences. Staff are streamlining communication, organizing data, and automating repetitive tasks. No matter your role, AI can help—when used thoughtfully and responsibly. Training is designed to help you:

  • Integrate AI tools into your workflow
  • Stay current with evolving best practices
  • Navigate ethical, data, and accessibility considerations

Campus training and support
