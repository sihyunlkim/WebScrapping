We are thrilled to announce the 2025 UT System AI Symposium in Healthcare.

Imagine a brighter future for Texas. Cultivate a spirit of collaboration across the UT System, demonstrating how our unified efforts can drive extraordinary progress in patient care and well-being. 

This event will highlight the latest breakthroughs in Artificial Intelligence (AI) across research, education, and clinical care, presented by UT System. 

The UT System AI Symposium in Health Care aims to bridge the gap between cutting-edge methodology and current day applications by bringing together researchers, clinicians, educators, students and industry leaders.

Join us for an enlightening event where knowledge converges, ideas flourish, and the future of AI-enhanced health care takes shape.
