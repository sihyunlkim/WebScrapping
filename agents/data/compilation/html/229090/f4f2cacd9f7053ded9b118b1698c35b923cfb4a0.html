The mission of the Office of Technology and Information Services (OTIS) is to:

Support UT System Administration staff and functions by providing proactive, customer focused information management and technology services and ensuring a secure technology infrastructure. Coordinate compliance with State and Federal legislation and University of Texas policy related to electronic and information resources across UT System Administration information technology teams. This includes security, risk assessment, disaster recovery, accessibility and reporting.
