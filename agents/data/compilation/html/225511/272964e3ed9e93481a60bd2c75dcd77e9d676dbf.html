Hobby School Survey: Parker Holds Commanding Lead in Democratic Primary for Harris County Judge

Former Houston Mayor Annise Parker is leading her closest challenger in the Democratic primary for Harris County judge by a margin of almost two-to-one, with the support of 46% of likely primary voters. Former Houston City Council member Letitia Plummer drew support from 25%.

Hobby School Survey: Mealer Holds Eight-Point Lead Over Cain in 9th Congressional District GOP Primary

February 16, 2026

Hobby School Survey: Mealer Holds Eight-Point Lead Over Cain in 9th Congressional District GOP Primary

Alex Mealer, who ran for Harris County judge in 2022, is leading a closely watched Republican primary for the newly redrawn 9th Congressional District, with support from 34% of likely voters. State Rep. Briscoe Cain follows with the support of 26% of voters, while businessman Dan Mims trails with 10%.

Hobby School Survey: Menefee Leads Green by 24 Points in 18th Congressional District Primary

Hobby School Survey: Menefee Leads Green by 24 Points in 18th Congressional District Primary

Fresh off winning a special election to represent the 18th congressional district, newly minted U.S. Rep. Christian Menefee is leading challenger U.S. Rep. Al Green by 24 points ahead of the March 3 Democratic primary.
