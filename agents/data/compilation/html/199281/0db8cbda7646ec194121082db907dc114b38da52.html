The Student Conduct office at UNC Pembroke is committed to fostering a respectful, supportive, and safe campus environment where all members can grow academically, socially, and personally. Grounded in the values of respect, responsibility, integrity, and fairness, we aim to help students learn from their experiences, repair harm when it occurs and strengthen our Brave community.

Code of Student Conduct

The Code of Student Conduct provides information to students and organizations about their rights and responsibilities as members of the UNCP community.

Submit Incident Report

The UNC Pembroke Incident Reporting Form serves as a centralized mechanism for reporting various campus concerns, including criminal activities, student misconduct, and behavioral issues. Designed for use by students, faculty, staff, and campus police, the form facilitates communication with multiple university departments, such as the Office of Student Conduct, Housing and Residence Life, and the CARE Team.

Academic Honor Code Policy

All matters related to Academic Honor Code violations are under the purview of the Office of the Provost.
