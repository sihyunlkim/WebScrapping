Computer Science & Information Technology Guide

This guide is meant to support students studying computer science, technology, and similar topics. Mary Livermore Library has complied resources to assist students as they go through this program. Students needing more specific help can contact a librarian through chat or email, set up a research consultation, or visit Livermore Library on the UNCP campus.
