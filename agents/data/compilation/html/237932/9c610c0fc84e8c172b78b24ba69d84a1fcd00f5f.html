Policies

Policies:

  • To ensure the safety of electronics, please refrain from having food or beverages at computer and other electronic workspaces.  We have provided a lounge area for food and beverages on the main floor of the library.
  • Feel free to utilize the individual study rooms at your convenience.  If you wish to book a group study room, class room or tech room, please contact Necol Dunson at 304-336-8352.
  • Follow campus conduct policies:  https://westliberty.edu/conduct/
