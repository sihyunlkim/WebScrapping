Dr. Cathy Monteroso Named West Liberty University Provost

April 15, 2024 By Theresa G

Share

West Liberty, W. Va., April 10, 2024 —West Liberty University announced that Dr. Cathy Monteroso has been named Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs, following a national search this spring.

“Dr. Monteroso has a great deal of experience for the position and the strong support from campus stakeholders following the campus interviews,” said Dr. Tim Borchers, President. “I have enjoyed working with Dr. Monteroso in the Interim position and look forward to advancing important initiatives for the University with her leadership in Academic Affairs.”

Cathy Monteroso is currently Interim Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs at West Liberty University. Previously, she served as Interim President and Dean of the College of Education and Human Performance at West Liberty.

“As I step into the Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs role at West Liberty University, I am deeply honored and excited to continue advancing the university’s commitment to academic excellence and student success,” said Monteroso. “With the support of our dedicated faculty, staff, and students, I am eager to lead initiatives that support the new strategic plan commitment to Community, Excellence, Innovation, and Integrity.”

Monteroso has led efforts to establish a pilot training program at the University and the University launched its first doctorate program under her leadership. She also worked to expand dual enrollment offerings at area high schools.

She served as a faculty member at West Liberty, as the Center and Education Coordinator at Sylvan Learning Center, as a middle school teacher, as the Volleyball coach, faculty, and Chair of Physical Education at Marietta College, and as the volleyball coach and faculty at California Institute of Technology.

She holds an EDD from Northcentral University, a Master of Education from Azusa Pacific University, and a Bachelor of Science in History and Secondary Education from East Tennessee State University.

West Liberty University blends a small-town environment with a comprehensive college experience, fostering students’ aspirations through hands-on learning, personalized support, and unique academic programs at both undergraduate and graduate levels. Learn more at westliberty.edu.

WLU MISSION

West Liberty University, established in 1837, was the first institution of higher education in West Virginia. Today, we are a dynamic, inclusive, student-centered community that cultivates scholarly exploration, experiential learning, creative expression, and global citizenship.

WLU is a public university committed to providing students with a comprehensive education from undergraduate to advanced degrees while serving as the region’s leading advocate for the arts, education, research, and economic opportunity.
