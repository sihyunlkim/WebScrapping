Connected Classroom Virtual Learning Series

Thank you for your interest in the Connected Classroom Virtual Learning Series.

The Connected Classroom Series is focused on approaches, resources and tools for teaching and learning in K-16+ classrooms. Educators learn new ways to use engaging and relevant methods and technologies through participating in live fifty-minute Zoom sessions and/or viewing previously recorded fifty-minute sessions on Topper Station, West Liberty University’s virtual streaming platform.

SERIES 13.0 | June 24th – Aug 2nd, 2024 | Summer II Semester
EDUC 578: Connected Classroom CE 13.0 | CRN 3290

In addition, live Zoom sessions on new topics will be held throughout July 2024. The sessions will also recorded for later viewing on Topper Station, West Liberty University’s virtual streaming platform. Educators may register, at no cost, to participate in any of the ZOOM sessions being offered this fall. Registration closes June 25th, 2024.The

Series 13.0 session schedule will be available soon.

Graduate Credits: Educators participating in each of the Connected Classroom Virtual Learning Series have the option of receiving three graduate credits for recertification and pay increment purposes through West Liberty University. Each course requires participation in or viewing six of the Connected Classroom sessions and the completion of six reflection assignments and a final assignment. The fee for each three-credit continuing education graduate course is $180.

To start the registration process for the Connected Classroom continuing education graduate credit option, please go to https://westliberty.edu/graduate-programs/ and click on the apply button. Select Non Degree Seeking Special Student and then select Connected Classroom. Both new and returning participants need to complete the graduate application.

For additional information about the Connected Classroom graduate credit option, please contact The Office of Graduate Studies at graduatestudies@westliberty.edu or 304-336-8015.

Questions: Please contact Lou Karas, Director of the Center for Arts and Education, at lkaras@westliberty.edu or 304-336-8162 if you are interested in being a session presenter or if you have any questions or need any additional information about The Connected Classroom Virtual Learning Series.

The Connected Classroom Virtual Learning Series is a program of the Center for Arts and Education and the Masters of Arts in Education Program in the College of Education and Human Performance at West Liberty University

Thank you for your interest in participating in The Connected Classroom Virtual Learning Series 4.0. There are 16 fifty-minute virtual sessions scheduled on Wednesdays and Thursdays, 5:00 p.m. – 5:50 p.m., between October 6, 2021 and December 2, 2021. The sessions will focus on approaches, resources and tools for teaching and learning in PK-16+ classrooms.
