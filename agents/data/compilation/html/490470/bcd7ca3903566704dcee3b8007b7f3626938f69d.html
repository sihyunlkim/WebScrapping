Tuition & Fees

All tuition and fees must be paid in full to receive a diploma.

FINANCIAL AID
Pell Grants and Direct Loans are available to students who qualify. More information about these programs is available at StudentAid.gov or you may contact our Financial Aid Administrator Jacob Mathenia at 901-726-4665 or financial@themassageinstitute.com

To apply for Financial Aid, complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) by visiting FAFSA.gov Our School Code is 042465
