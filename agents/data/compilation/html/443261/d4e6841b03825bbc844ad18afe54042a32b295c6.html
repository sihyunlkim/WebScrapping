Stephanie Carnes
BS, RDMS, RVT
Diagnostic Medical Sonography – Program Director

  1. Bachelor’s degree in Health Sciences from the University of South Dakota
  2. Associate’s degree in Diagnostic Medical Sonography from Southeast Technical Institute
  3. 12 years of scanning experience in multiple different specialties

I've always known I wanted to be in the medical field, but after graduating, I was uncertain about my path—physical therapy, occupational therapy, or medical school. Eventually, I chose ultrasound, and I can’t imagine doing anything else. I truly love creating images and collaborating with a team of healthcare professionals to assist in diagnoses.After several years in the clinical setting, I was apprehensive about transitioning to education, but I realized teaching was also a passion of mine. My purpose has always been to help patients and bring a smile to their faces on their toughest days. Working with students, I’ve discovered that same sense of purpose and passion. I enjoy shaping the next generation of ultrasound technicians and feel like I'm continually learning alongside them.

David Maurice Griffin
Licensed Massage Therapist

David Maurice Griffin is a distinguished Licensed Massage Therapist and educator, whose career spans extensive experience in therapeutic modalities and client-centered wellness practices. With Board Certification in Therapeutic Massage & Bodywork and certifications in CPR and Basic Life Support (BLS), he demonstrates a rigorous commitment to health standards and advanced therapeutic techniques, including neuromuscular, trigger point, oncology, and deep tissue massage therapies. Since March 2022, David has served as a Massage Therapy Instructor at Southeastern College, where he is responsible for guiding students through a 740-hour curriculum that comprehensively prepares them for licensure as massage therapists. His instructional approach is rooted in approved curricula, instructional rigor, and constructive mentorship, fostering both professional competency and ethical practice in his students.
‍
Currently, as the Lead Massage Therapist at Hand & Stone in Harbison, David not only delivers specialized treatments but also facilitates training programs for staff development, contributing to an exemplary 87% client satisfaction rate. Additionally, he is the proprietor of EmpowerFuel Massage & Wellness LLC, a private wellness practice he founded in 2018, where he delivers individualized therapeutic services addressing diverse client health needs. David's professional background is complemented by roles in sales and administration, where he developed essential interpersonal and organizational skills through positions at Dollar General, Prairie Quest Consulting, Kratos Defense, and during his 14-year tenure with the United States Navy. His blend of expertise in therapeutic massage, coupled with a disciplined approach derived from his military background, underscores his commitment to advancing standards in massage therapy and wellness education.
