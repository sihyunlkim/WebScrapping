Master of Arts in Christian Ministries

For individuals who have earned an undergraduate degree and are currently holding or seeking opportunities as pastors, ministers, evangelists, missionaries, and teachers in churches or parachurch organizations.  The degree may be taken in one of two concentrations: Biblical Studies or Leadership.

Master of Science in Education: Curriculum & Instruction

For individuals currently holding or seeking positions as Christian school teachers and who already possess a BA or BS degree from an accredited or otherwise approved institution. This degree leads to ACSI Certification, but does not lead to California state credentialing. SBC&GS does not grant Certification or Recertification.
Program Checklist MSE CI 2023-2024

Master of Science in Education: Administration

For individuals currently holding or seeking positions as Christian school administrator, church/school business manager, principal, assistant principal, or supervisor.  Must already possess a BA or BS degree from an accredited or otherwise approved institution. This degree leads to ACSI Certification, but does not lead to California state credentialing. SBC&GS does not grant Certification or Recertification.
Program Checklist MSE A 2023-2024

ACSI Certification and Recertification*

Shasta Bible College is approved by the Association of Christian Schools International (ACSI) to offer courses leading to an ACSI Teacher/Administrator/Specialist Certificate. For those who already possess a BA or BS degree from an accredited or otherwise approved institution.  SBC&GS does not grant Certification or Recertification.  *Not a formal program, students enter as Non Degree
