About Shasta

We are a nationally accredited college located in Northern California and member of the TRACS organization. Our mission is to educate and equip Biblically committed Christian leaders who will impact the world for Jesus Christ. Our complete history and doctrinal statement can be viewed below.

A Message from The President

Located in Northern California

Our campus is close to wonderful mountains, waterfalls and lakes. Many national parks including: Lassen, Whiskeytown, Shasta and more are all located within 10 miles of campus.
