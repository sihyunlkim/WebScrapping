Staying current

In this rapidly evolving landscape, these resources will help you stay updated on new tools, terminology, and key developments.

  • Generative AI Product Tracker
    This tool is regularly updated and provides information on AI products tailored for education.
  • Glossary of artificial intelligence
    Concise definitions of key AI terms and concepts, helpful for quickly understanding the basics and beyond.
  • LinkedIn Learning
    LinkedIn Learning offers a variety of AI courses that OHSU students and staff can access for free.
  • The Algorithm
    A weekly newsletter published by MIT meant to demystify artificial intelligence.

Creating multimedia with GenAI

Unlike text-based AI chatbots, AI multimedia tools can generate images, audio, and videos, including presentation slides. It is important to know that the responsible practices outlined in this guide also apply to these tools and their use cases.

  • Creating Multimedia with AI Tools
    Learn the basics of multimedia generation in this online tutorial, including ethical issues related to bias, copyright, and deepfakes.

Ethical and environmental impacts of AI

AI raises many ethical challenges, including risks related to the environment, democracy, human rights, bias, and fairness. The following resources will help you learn more about these critical issues and the communities that are working address them. 

  • AI’s carbon footprint is bigger than you think
    The article discusses the significant and often underestimated environmental impact of AI.
  • How AI could perpetuate racism, sexism and other biases in society
    In this brief interview, Safiya Noble, an internet studies scholar and Professor of Gender Studies and African American studies at UCLA, addresses how artificial intelligence could further perpetuate biases in society.
  • Long hours and low wages: the human labour powering AI’s development
    This article discusses the labor challenges behind AI development, highlighting the long hours, low wages, and poor working conditions faced by data labelers and other workers.
  • Ethical AI groups
    A list of organizations and communities working to address and raise awareness about AI issues and challenges.

AI and healthcare

AI in healthcare presents opportunities such as enhanced diagnostics, personalized treatments, and improved patient outcomes through data-driven insights. However, there are major risks including potential biases, privacy concerns, and the challenge of integrating AI technologies into existing healthcare systems.

  • Transformative Potential of AI in Healthcare: Definitions, Applications, and Navigating the Ethical Landscape and Public Perspectives
    Provides a detailed overview of how the technology was envisioned, developed, and implemented, its current applications in the field, and predictions about its potential to transform the future of medicine.
  • The state of artificial intelligence in medicine
    In this video from Stanford Medicine, panelists explore how artificial intelligence is transforming healthcare and the ethical challenges that arise as it becomes more integrated into medical practice.
