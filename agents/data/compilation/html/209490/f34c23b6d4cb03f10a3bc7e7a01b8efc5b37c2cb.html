Consultations

Schedule a 1:1 consultation to get personalized help from OHSU librarians with literature searches, citation management, data management, publishing and more.

Rooms with virtual meeting technology are available at the BICC Library and the Graduate Learning Resource Center in the Robertson Life Sciences Building. If you want to use one of these rooms for an online appointment, please mention it when you schedule.

Workshops

We host regular workshops on searching the literature, research data management, reference management, scholarly communication, data science skills and more.

Scheduled workshops don't work for you? We can partner with your group or program to host workshops on demand. Contact us to discuss your needs.

Share and Promote Your Scholarship

We can help you select journals for publishing, navigate open access opportunities, track the impact of your work, and provide guidance on referencing your contributions in curriculum vitae and grant applications.

  • Research and Publishing
    Learn how our Publishing and Data Services staff can help with publishing and research impact, data management, and more.
  • OHSU Digital Collections
    Digital Collections provides a home for OHSU research and scholarship and offers students a citable digital portfolio of their contributions.
  • Digital Collections submission guide
    Everything you need to know about submitting works (such as theses, capstones and more) to OHSU's institutional repository.

Data Management

Good data management will help you save time and increase the impact of your work. We offer services and expertise to help faculty, staff, and students organize, describe, store, and share the data they produce.

  • Research Data and Reproducibility guide
    Guidance, tools, and resources to help OHSU faculty, students and staff manage data
  • Schedule a consultation
    Select "Scientific data management and sharing" from the appointment type menu to request a data consultation
