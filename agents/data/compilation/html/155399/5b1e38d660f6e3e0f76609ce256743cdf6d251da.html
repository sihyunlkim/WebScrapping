Office of Provost

The Office of the Provost provides administrative leadership for the university's academic enterprise, driving a culture of academic innovation and excellence in support of our Next-Gen K-State vision and goals.

Building a culture of growth and innovation

Our Next-Gen K-State plan calls us to grow our traditional and nontraditional enrollment to 30,000 by 2030. This can only be achieved through strategic enrollment practices; laser-focused retention efforts; learner-focused, industry-demanded credit and noncredit offerings; and strategic measures of faculty support to drive academic innovation.
