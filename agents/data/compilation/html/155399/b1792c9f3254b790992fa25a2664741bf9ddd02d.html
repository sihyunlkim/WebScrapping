Welcome!

Are you teaching a class? This research guide helps faculty and instructors identify appropriate K-State Libraries instruction and research resources for inclusion in their class.

In this guide, you will find pages about the following topics:

  • Library Instruction connects you to your subject specialist, helps you request a Research Guide for your course, and more. 
  • Digital Resources showcases the many resources available for faculty and students to use for various projects or activities.
