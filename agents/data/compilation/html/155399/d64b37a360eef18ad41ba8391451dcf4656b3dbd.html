Compliance Framework

K-State is committed to acting ethically and in compliance with applicable laws, regulations and university policies. The K-State community fulfills this commitment by sharing a common framework for addressing compliance and integrity across campus. This framework aligns with Chapter 8 of the Federal Sentencing Guidelines for Effective Compliance Programs DOJ (Department of Justice) guidelines for evaluating corporate compliance programs.

  • University policies and procedures
  • Compliance officer
  • Compliance network
  • Training and education
  • Communication channels
  • Internal auditing and monitoring
  • Disciplinary guidelines and procedures
  • Prompt response to problems
