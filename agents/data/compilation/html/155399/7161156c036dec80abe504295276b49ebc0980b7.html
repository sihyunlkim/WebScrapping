Let's Chat about ChatGPT: Updated room location and recording

Submitted by Karin Westman

As previously announced, from 2:30-3:30 p.m. Wednesday, March 1, faculty and staff will present a series of lightning talks on ChatGPT.

The room location in Hale Library for the event has changed to Hale 181. The Zoom registration for concurrent streaming remains the same.

In response to requests from the K-State community, the event will be recorded for later viewing. Contact english@k-state.edu after the event for the link.

"Let's Chat about ChatGPT" is sponsored by the College of Arts and Sciences' department of English and its program in expository writing, its Writing Center and its graduate track in composition and rhetoric.
