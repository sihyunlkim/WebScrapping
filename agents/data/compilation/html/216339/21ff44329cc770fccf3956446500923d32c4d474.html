Academic dishonesty undermines the university’s mission and purpose and devalues the work of all members of the Temple community.  Every member of the university community is responsible for upholding the highest standards of honesty at all times.  Students, as members of the community, are responsible for adhering to the principles of academic honesty and integrity. 

 

How can faculty report academic dishonesty?

Faculty can report academic dishonesty via My Courses:

  1. Sign-in to TUportal.
  2. Click on My Courses.
  3. Select the Term.
  4. Navigate to the student in the roster.
  5. Click on Additional Info.
  6. Click on Academic Dishonesty Charge.
  7. Complete the form and proceed with the subsequent steps.

The online process complies with the Student Code of Conduct and other policies.

 

Who should I contact about academic dishonesty charge reporting?

Instructors may contact the Office of Student Conduct and Community Standards for questions related to reporting academic dishonesty.
