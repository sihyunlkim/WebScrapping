    Turnitin AI Detector

    Tags tuhelp-migrated canvas turnitin public AI plagiarism

    Reference

    Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools are prohibited for students, unless an instructor explicitly grants permission. Turnitin’s AI detector tool is available for faculty. Faculty must request access and complete a brief asynchronous training before the tool will be enabled. 

    Faculty can request access to the Turnitin AI Detector and self-enroll in the training.

    For more detailed information on AI use within the university, visit A Faculty Guide to A.I.

    Feel free to reach out to the Center for the Advancement of Teaching if you have any questions.
