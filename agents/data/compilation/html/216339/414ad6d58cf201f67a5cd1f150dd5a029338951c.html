David Boardman

Interim Provost
Dean, Klein College of Media and Communication

Overseeing all academic initiatives, the provost is the senior academic administrator at Temple. Across campus—from admissions and curriculum to budgeting and strategic planning, from faculty recruitment and retention to research and assessment—the provost is a key leader for Temple’s academic community. The provost supports a portfolio that extends across the university’s 17 schools and colleges and encompasses several administrative units.
