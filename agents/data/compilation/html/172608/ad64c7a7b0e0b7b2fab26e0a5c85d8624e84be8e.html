Walsh College Announces Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Degree

TROY, Mich., Oct. 9, 2023 Walsh College, a top nationally ranked business and technology college, announces a new Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning degree.

Launching in the Winter 2024 semester, this new program will impart students with essential tools, skills, and strategies to take maximum advantage of the AI revolution that is currently underway.

The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is the latest degree offering in our School of Technology, providing students with an affordable, flexible, real-world education that will help unleash new potential upon the business world, said Dr. David Schippers, Chief Academic Officer and Dean of Academics at Walsh College.

Walsh Colleges entry into this space is timely with the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projecting 23% growth over the next several years and Forbes projecting over 97 million jobs involving Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning by 2025.

This is a future-ready degree that is launching in our historic 100th year serving our amazing students, said Dr. Suzy Siegle, President and CEO of Walsh College. The Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning is a unique hybrid of both business and technology, two areas where Walsh College has excelled in providing quality education for years.

For more information on the Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning degree and to apply for admission, please visit https://www.walshcollege.edu.

ABOUT WALSH

Walsh Collegeis an all-business, private, independent, not-for-profit, fully accredited college offering undergraduate, graduate and doctoral business and technology degrees, as well as certificate programs. Founded in 1922, Walsh College is one of Southeast Michigans largest graduate business schools, offering classes on campus and online. Our internationally and nationally-ranked programs integratetheory and application to prepare graduates for successful careers. Walsh College degree programs include accounting, data analytics, finance, information technology, human resources, management, marketing, taxation and other fields. For more information on the Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning degree and to apply for admission, please visit https://www.walshcollege.edu.

Walsh College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission (www.hlcommission.org)and the Accreditation Council for Business Schools & Programs (www.acbsp.org).
