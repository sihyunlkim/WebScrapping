 As artificial intelligence reshapes education, institutions confront a critical challenge: scaling personalized learning while preserving humanity through emotional resonance and human connection. In short, pedagogy must adapt and integrate AI while safeguarding human values. With AI tutors, chatbots and algorithmic nudges increasingly prevalent in classrooms, preserving humanity becomes imperative to maintaining empathy and responsiveness. Institutions can preserve humanity in AI-driven environments by (1) integrating empathy directly into AI tools, (2) empowering human educators as emotional anchors and (3) designing hybrid models that sustain meaningful human connections.
