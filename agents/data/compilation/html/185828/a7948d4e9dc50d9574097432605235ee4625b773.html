Generative AI Practitioner Microcredential

Hands-on AI certification program building real-world skills in communication, data, visuals & audio through expert-led, project-based learning.

This series of hands-on courses equips learners with the real-world executive and technical skills needed to apply generative AI across communication, visuals, data, and audio. This 35-hour, in-person or online certification program takes learners beyond theory and into hands-on practice, blending cutting-edge tools with high-touch instruction and executive coaching. Designed for workforce readiness, the program delivers live, expert-led sessions and project-based learning that mirror the demands of today’s AI-powered roles.

  • Level - Introductory and no AI experience required
  • Audience - Adults seeking practical AI fluency for today’s workforce (no technical background required)
  • Structure - 12 sessions over 4 weeks (3x/week, 3 hours per session), plus a final capstone + certification
  • Total Hours - 36 live hours
  • Format - Delivered in cohorts of participants with live instruction, hands-on labs, and peer collaboration
  • Price - $3,000
  • Includes:
    • 15 hours of executive and business skills in Microsoft Suite/Google suite, building a LinkedIn profile, resume generation, networking tips and tricks, and interview practice
    • 20 hours of AI skills training
      • Access to industry-grade tools (ChatGPT, Gemini, Midjourney, ElevenLabs)
      • A portfolio of 2 industry ready AI created projects
    • NJIT-verified Generative AI Practitioner Microcredential
    • Mastery in prompt engineering and AI-driven content creation, giving you a competitive hiring edge. These skills rank among the fastest-growing job requirements across fields including marketing, healthcare, finance, and public service.

This is a next-level program for learners ready to apply AI in real-world roles and not just learn about it. Whether you're reskilling, upskilling, or entering a digital career path for the first time, this immersive experience will prepare you to contribute confidently in AI-enhanced workplaces.
