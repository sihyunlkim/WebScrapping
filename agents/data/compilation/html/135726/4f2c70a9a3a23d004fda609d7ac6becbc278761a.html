OUR ACADEMIC MISSION

The Provost, as Executive Vice President for Academic Affairs, serves as the chief academic officer for the University of Miami. The Office of the Provost is responsible for driving the academic vision of the university, overseeing programs and research, managing colleges, schools, centers, and institutes, and collaborating closely with university leadership on strategic planning. The office also manages key administrative units, such as faculty affairs, enrollment management, research and scholarship, accreditation, and student success initiatives.
