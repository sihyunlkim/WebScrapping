Mentoring Employees in the Era of AI

  • Share This: Share Mentoring Employees in the Era of AI on FacebookShare Mentoring Employees in the Era of AI on LinkedInShare Mentoring Employees in the Era of AI on X

Instructor: Ruth Gotian

Artificial intelligence has created a lot of uncertainty in the workforce. Employees are often unclear if they will have a role in their organization moving forward, and that uncertainty can hinder professional development—which in turn can negatively affect employee retention. In this course, Dr. Ruth Gotian, a social-scientist and author, teaches you strategies for adapting and applying impactful mentorship practices to suit dynamic work environments in the era of AI. Learn the skills to mentor and retain your employees while you help them develop and prepare for the new world of leveraging AI in their work.

This course includes AI-powered Role Play. Role Play allows you to practice what you’ve learned in interactive simulations of real-world conversations.
