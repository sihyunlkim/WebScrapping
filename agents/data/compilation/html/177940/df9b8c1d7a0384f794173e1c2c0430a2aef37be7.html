Expand Your Horizons With Lincoln University Online

At LU Online, we are dedicated to advancing your educational journey and career development with our exceptional online programs. Whether aiming to excel in business, education, or cybersecurity, LU Online is your gateway to top-tier instruction and transformative learning experiences.

Our online programs include the following:

Graduate programs

  • Master of Business Administration with an emphasis in management: Develop cutting-edge business acumen and leadership skills to drive success in business firms, institutions, small businesses and other organizations.
  • Master of Arts in Higher Education (HBCU concentration): Prepare for impactful roles in higher education with a focus on Historically Black Colleges and Universities.
  • Master of Arts in Community and Economic Development: Learn the skills to lead and uplift communities through strategic planning, civic action and sustainable development.
  • Master of Education in School Administration and Supervision (K-12): Gain the expertise needed to lead and inspire in K-12 education settings.

Nursing programs

  • Master of Science in Nursing: Equips students with advanced skills in leadership, evidence-based practice and patient-centered care, preparing them for roles as clinical leaders, educators and advocates for heathcare equity. 
  • RN to BSN Program: Equips registered nurses with advanced clinical reasoning, leadership skills and evidence-based practice knowledge to excel in diverse healthcare settings. 

Teaching programs

  • Para to Pro Program: Enables paraprofessionals to gain certification, develop teaching skills and prepare for full-time teaching roles while continuing their current positions.
  • CREATE Certificate: Empowers students to uncover and develop their creative potential, engage in innovative learning processes and apply their skills to make a meaningful impact in their personal and professional lives.

Security Sciences Institute programs

  • Cybersecurity Certificate: Equip yourself with critical skills to safeguard digital information and tackle today’s cybersecurity challenges.
  • Mental Health for First Responders Certificate: Gain essential self-assessment and mitigation skills to support your own emotional and mental health (this certificate is designed to support the mental well-being of first responders, those who significantly impact others' health and well-being).
  • GIS Certificate: Gain a strong foundation in geospatial technology, data analysis and applications in various fields, such as agriculture, environmental science, public safety and emergency management.
  • EMT: Advance your career in emergency services, law enforcement, or healthcare with Lincoln University’s flexible online EMT Certificate.

Adult education, career preparedness program

  • Lincoln University Employment Academy: Prepare for employment and career advancement through this two-semester program for Missouri residents. Pursue flexible online learning with an option for real-world experience internship.
  • Career Training: Lincoln University, in partnership with ed2go, offers online open enrollment programs. These programs are non-credit bearing and are available to everyone.

Discover how LU Online can help you reach your full potential and achieve your professional goals.
