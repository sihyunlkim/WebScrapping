  • Articulation Agreements

Articulation agreements are formal agreements between organizations detailing the recognition of college credit between those organizations. Lincoln University uses articulation agreements to avoid duplication of resources and to encourage and enhance students' interest in postsecondary education. The articulation agreements listed below detail the ways in which coursework will transfer from one institution to another.
