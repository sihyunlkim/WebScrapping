Citing Generative AI

Content produced by language models or other computer algorithms is referred to as AI-generated content (ChatGPT, Google's Gemini, Jasper, etc.). Academic honesty and integrity should be adhered to when using AI-generated content.  Faculty, staff, and students are urged to review the section on ethical use. Frequently "hallucinates" (makes stuff up), particularly when it comes to scholarly citations or references. To learn more about this, see the glossary.
