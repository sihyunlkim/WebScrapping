        • About PARSE

          The Professional Activity Report and Self-Evaluation (PARSE) is the documentation of a faculty memberâ€™s accomplishments during each academic year and cumulatively, in the three principal areas of teaching, scholarly and professional growth, and service. The PARSE serves as the basis for the annual evaluation. It is also provides faculty with an instrument to present to departmental and college review committees for reappointment, tenure, and promotion.
