FLEX Lab: Faculty Learning and Experimentation Exchange

Discover Emerging Technologies and Transform Your Teaching

Welcome to the FLEX Lab located in G603, City Tech's dedicated space for faculty members to explore the forefront of emerging technologies, foster collaboration, and unlock innovative approaches to integrate technology into the classroom.

What is the FLEX Lab?

The FLEX Lab, short for Faculty Learning and Experimentation Exchange, is a dynamic environment where educators can:
  • Engage with next-generation technologies.
  • Collaborate with fellow faculty members.
  • Experiment with new pedagogical approaches.
  • Discover possibilities to revolutionize teaching.

A Collaborative Ecosystem

The FLEX Lab fosters a culture of innovation and collaboration. It is a space for faculty to connect, share ideas, and learn from each other's experiences.

Shape the Future of Education

By participating in the FLEX Lab, you can:

  • Redefine the landscape of higher education.
  • Transform teaching, learning, and research.
  • Prepare students for the future.

Get Involved

Join the FLEX Lab community and be a part of the next wave of technological advancements.
