Urshan Graduate School of Theology admits qualified men and women of the Apostolic faith to further prepare them for Christian service. The decision to admit a student rests solely with the Admissions Committee.

WELCOME!

Admissions

Urshan Graduate School offers the opportunity for students to begin taking classes as non-matriculated(guest) students. In this way, students may enroll in a short-term course or a distance-learning course to discover first-hand whether or not they want to enroll in a degree program.

Students may take up to 12 credit hours as a guest student. This allows students a glimpse into UGST Academics before deciding to become a fully matriculated student and committing to a full degree program. Regular tuition and fees apply to courses taken as a Guest student.
