Honors Program

Students who meet the following requirements are invited to apply to the Honors Program after acceptance to CIU:

  • Minimum GPA of 3.75 on a 4.0 scale
  • Standardized Test Score
    SAT: 1280
    ACT: 27
    CLT: 84


There are limited spots in the Honors program, so we recommend applying soon after receiving the invitation.

Nursing Students

Declaring Nursing as a major or being accepted into the Nursing major by CIU Admissions does not ensure admission into the Nursing cohort. The requirements to apply for the Nursing cohort are:

  • A minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 on 4.0 scale on all prerequisite coursework, earning a C or above in each science prerequisite. All science prerequisite courses must have been completed within the last 5 years
  • 70% or higher on the TEAS (Test of Essential Academic Skills)
  • International students must have a satisfactory score on the TOEFL or IELTS exam.
    TOEFL (Internet-based): 80
    TOEFL (paper-based): 570
    IELTS International Academic Course Type 2: 6.5
  • Personal interview with the BSN Admissions Committee
  • After admission into the Nursing cohort, additional requirements will apply.


Incoming freshman students may apply for priority admission into the Nursing cohort by meeting the following requirements:

  • 3.5 GPA on a 4.0 scale
  • 1110 on SAT (520 or higher in math) or 22 on ACT (20 or higher in math)
