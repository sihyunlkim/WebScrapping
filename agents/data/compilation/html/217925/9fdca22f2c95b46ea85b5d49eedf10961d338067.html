About CIU

Christ-Centered Education for Global Impact

Columbia International University is dedicated to educating students from a biblical worldview, equipping them with the knowledge, character, and skills to impact the nations with the message of Christ. Our mission drives everything we do, from academic excellence to spiritual formation.

Mission & Vision

Our History

Over 100 Years of Faithful Impact

Since 1923, Columbia International University has been preparing students for ministry, leadership, and service worldwide. With a legacy of biblical teaching and global impact, CIU continues to uphold its commitment to academic excellence and spiritual growth. See how God has worked through CIU for over a century.

Guiding with Faith & Vision

CIU’s leadership team is composed of experienced educators, ministry leaders, and professionals who are committed to guiding the university with excellence, integrity, and faithfulness to God’s calling. Meet the individuals who shape our vision and strategy.

Join a Mission-Driven Community

At CIU, we are more than a university—we are a Christ-centered community passionate about education, ministry, and global impact. If you share our mission and want to make a difference in students’ lives, explore our open positions and consider joining our team.

Global & Local Ministry Initiatives

Ben Lippen School

Ben Lippen School, a ministry of CIU, provides a Christ-centered, academically rigorous education for Pre-K through 12th grade students, fostering spiritual growth and leadership development.

CIU Korntal

CIU Korntal offers a Christ-centered, globally focused theological education in partnership with CIU, serving students in Korntal, Germany and beyond.

Kepha Institute

The Kepha Institute, accredited by the Chinese Ministry of Education, is dedicated to cultivating community leaders who are passionate about public service and guided by a pursuit of truth.

Prison Initiative

CIU’s Prison Initiative equips inmates with a fully accredited theological education, transforming lives and empowering graduates to serve as chaplains and ministry leaders within the prison system.
