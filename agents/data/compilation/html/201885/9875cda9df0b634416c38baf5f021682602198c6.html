AI for Education

A Guide for students:
Should I use AI?

Why do you want to use an AI Chatbot? I want it to ...

Help me get started on an assignment

Help me improve on what I've already done

Explain an idea in simpler terms or in a different way

Help me with my research and find facts, quotes or resources

Fully complete an assignment for me

Yes

Yes, but ...

No

Ask my teacher and check my handbook to ensure that it is acceptable

Use an appropriate AI tool and track your work

Double check your work for hallucinations and bias

Cite the use of the tool and describe how you used it

Use a generative search tool like Copilot Chat to reduce inaccuracies 

If AI is doing the work for you, you're missing the learning. Try another approach.

Verify with known information sources

KEY VOCABULARY

Hallucination:
When Gen Al tools provide incorrect or made up information.

Bias:
Gen Al tools are trained on data from the internet which is not always fair or balanced.

Citing Al:
When citing Al, provide the name of the tool(s), how you have used it, and the date utilized.
