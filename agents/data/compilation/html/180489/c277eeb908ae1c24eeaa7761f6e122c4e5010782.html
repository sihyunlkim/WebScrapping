Educational Initiatives and Innovation

Hub for Advancing Excellence in Education

Educational Initiatives and Innovation (EII) brings together diverse units dedicated to creating dynamic, accessible, and globally connected learning opportunities for a wider range of learners. By innovating in teaching, developing strategic programs, and fostering lifelong learning, we empower students, educators, and communities to thrive in a complex and changing world.

Our efforts strengthen the university’s role as a leader in innovation, access, and global education, preparing learners to meet the opportunities and challenges of a rapidly evolving future.

Learning Experience & Design

Designing and developing engaging, effective and high-quality learning experiences. 

Specialty & Extended Programming

Develop and deliver a wide range of flexible learning options; noncredit courses, digital badges and specialized training programs. 

Online Program Strategy

Utilize strategic planning and innovation to develop degrees, certificates and stackable credentials that enhance online and hybrid learning environments.

Global Engagement Office

Fostering global connections through study abroad opportunities, English language learning, international partnerships, and comprehensive support for students and scholars from around the world.

Maureen and Mike Mansfield Center

Fostering globally minded leaders of integrity through programming in ethical leadership, civic responsibility, international engagement.

UM Summer

Supporting the success of all summer learners by providing robust course offerings and experiential learning opportunities.
