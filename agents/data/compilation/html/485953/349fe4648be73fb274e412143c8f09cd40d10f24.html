Film / Video Producer

Attend TRCOA's comprehensive Video / Audio Arts Program or Video / Audio Arts Associates Degree . Or take our one-on-one, Individual classes in Salem, online via screenshare.

*all programs provided by TRCOA outside of Austin, TX are 100% distance education programs. For more information about how our one-on-one distance education programs work call 512-231-0344 or email info@trcoa.edu.

Individual Classes:

Cinematography and Video Editing

Cinematography and Editing is a one-on-one screen share class held one hour per week for 15 weeks taught by an industry professional. It introduces fundamentals of film and video production techniques and equipment used to create professional content. Concepts such as pre production, camera setting, lighting, video editing, color looks, gimbals and movement, and other film and video production basics are taught. This class assures your ability to function and communicate intelligently and confidently while in a film or video production environment.

Advanced Video Editing

Advanced video editing encompasses more complex and sophisticated techniques beyond basic cuts and transitions. It involves mastering non-linear editing software, understanding storytelling through editing, and utilizing advanced features like multi-camera editing, color correction, and complex effects to enhance visual appeal and narrative impact.

Advanced Video Audio Production

Advanced video and audio production encompasses the techniques and technologies used to create high-quality visual and auditory content, going beyond basic production skills. It involves mastering advanced techniques in areas like cinematography, sound design, editing, and post-production to achieve professional-level results. This includes using specialized equipment, software, and workflows to produce polished and impactful media experiences.

Projects and Production Expereience

Record and edit video, mix and master the audio for six projects. This class is the glue that ties all other classes together. In our profession it is essential that you be making real product for real potential clients. Here you will seek out projects, book them to shoot or receive files to edit, color correct, mix and master or do other production work. We will also place you in a local producer for experience (if you feel comfortable and there is producer availability).
*Projects and Production Experience with TRCOA is only offered when combined with supporting individual classes.

In order to be accepted into the Beginning Audio Engineering Program, applicants must:

  • Fill out the Application Form
  • Complete a personal interview with the school director
  • Provide proof of completion of secondary education (high school diploma or GED certificate)
