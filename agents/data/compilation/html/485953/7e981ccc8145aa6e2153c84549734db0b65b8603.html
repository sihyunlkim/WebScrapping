Individual Audio Engineering,
Music Production and Business Classes


All of the nine course subjects that we teach within our Audio Engineering Video and Music Production Programs can be taken individually and seven of the courses can be taken via screenshare from any place in the world. All of these individual and screenshare classes can be later transferred as credit towards our Audio Engineering, Video and Recording Arts Programs.

Although you have to come to our recording studios in Austin to complete the Video and muscic production projects part of our Video and Recording Arts Programs, you can get started on individual classes right in the city where you are now.

Our screenshare classes are like no other that exists. All of the One-On-One attention you would experience here in person at our Austin campus you will experience with our screenshare classes.

If you are not able to relocate to Austin or devote the time to the entire Audio Engineering, Video and Recording Arts Programs, then please browse through all the individual and screenshare Audio, Video and Recording classes to see which ones you would like to get started with now.
