Guidance for Researchers

As generative AI continues to evolve, its integration into our research and academic operations offers unprecedented opportunities for innovation, efficiency, and enhanced communication. At KU we are dedicated to harnessing these technologies to advance our research capabilities and academic excellence. Our commitment to collaboration, equity, excellence, integrity, and respect guides our approach to using generative AI tools. This guidance outlines how we leverage AI to support research initiatives, enhance learning experiences, and streamline administrative tasks, all while upholding our ethical standards.
