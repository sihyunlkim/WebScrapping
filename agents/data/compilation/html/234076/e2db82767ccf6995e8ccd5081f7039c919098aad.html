Selected AI Research Initiatives

LaCross Institute for Ethical AI in Business

Equips leaders with the critical insights and ethical grounding needed to thrive in the AI-driven world.

Digital Technology for Democracy Lab

Interdisciplinary collaboration to explore how digital technology can support democracy.

National Security Data and Policy Institute

Brings together academia, government, and industry to combine expertise in data science, artificial intelligence, research computing, and policy.

Department of Computer Science

Advancing the state of the art in both fundamental and new algorithms.

AI-Powered Astronomy Institute

The NSF-Simons AI Institute for Cosmic Origins allows researchers to build a new breed of astronomical tools and accelerate the pace of astronomical discovery.

Link Lab Multidisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems Research

The Link Lab at UVA Engineering is a world-class center of excellence in cyber-physical systems.

Biocomplexity Institute and Initiative

Using AI and network science to solve real-world problems by untangling complex and interacting causes.

AI at McIntire

By collaborating in multidisciplinary projects, lending their expertise to publish research in leading journals, and pursuing strategic industry engagement, McIntire faculty are advancing the understanding and application of AI in both academic and professional spheres.

AI at SEHD

Experts in UVA's School of Education and Human Development are at the forefront of integrating technology and artificial intelligence ethically, inclusively, and effectively into education and research.
