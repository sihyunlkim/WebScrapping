Generative AI in Teaching and Learning (GENAI)

Quick Guide

Guidance for Faculty & Students — Generative AI in Teaching and Learning

Guidance for faculty about talking with your students about AI, designing assignments in ways that deter inappropriate uses of AI, and incorporating AI into your teaching, and guidance for students about appropriate uses and attribution of AI tools in coursework.

genai.provost.virginia.edu

Course Development Grants

Faculty interested in incorporating AI instruction into a new or existing course can apply for a course development grant. These grants are designed to help foster the thoughtful exploration of generative AI.

cte.virginia.edu

Pedagogical Guidance About Teaching in a World with AI

Advice and curated collections from the Center for Teaching Excellence with tips for designing assignments, discipline-specific strategies for incorporating AI tools into your teaching, ethical dimensions of AI, etc.

teaching.virginia.edu

Teaching in a World with Generative AI

An online course for UVA instructors and graduate teaching assistants to learn about this technology and its pedagogical implications.

canvas.its.virginia.edu

Faculty AI Guides

This program is sponsored by the Office of the Provost and the Center for Teaching Excellence. Approximately 50 faculty from across Grounds will learn about AI in teaching and will share what they learn with colleagues during the academic year. 

cte.virginia.edu

Report of the Gen-AI in Teaching and Learning Task Force

This thoughtful and fascinating report draws on the spring 2023 town hall meetings and a survey to report faculty and student perspectives on AI; provides an high level discussion of the implications of this technology for higher education; and offers a series of recommendations to the University can adapt to this new technology.

genai.provost.virginia.edu
