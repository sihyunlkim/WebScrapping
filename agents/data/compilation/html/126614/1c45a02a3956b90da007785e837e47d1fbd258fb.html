Academic Integrity

Academic Integrity

This includes engaging in or tolerating acts of falsification, dishonesty, misrepresentation or deception in any scholarly activity.

Ways to report as a student:

  • Students who have witnessed possible incidents of academic integrity violations should share concerns directly with the appropriate instructor or program administrator.
  • Students should report incidents of alleged academic integrity violations to Student Conduct and Conflict Resolution (SCCR).

Submit a report to SCCR

Ways to report as a Faculty or Staff member:

  • Discuss your concerns with the student before you submit the academic integrity form.

Report suspected academic misconduct

Reporting at CU Boulder

If you are experiencing an emergency or in immediate danger, please call 911.  

When in doubt about whether to make a report, or who to make a report to, email report@colorado.edu for guidance
