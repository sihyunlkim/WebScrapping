Teaching, Learning & AI Community of Practice (TLAI CoP)

What is TLAI CoP?

The CTL TLAI CoP brings together colleagues from across campus who wish to explore topics related to teaching, learning, and AI in a supportive and respectful community. We are here to learn together from both emerging experts and one another. Relevant sharing and dialogue are encouraged among all participants.

TLAI CoP Mission

The TLAI CoP mission is to meet all participants where they are as they navigate the implications of generative AI in higher education. We want all campus community members to have an attainable vision for how they can be effective in their work and their teaching and learning in the age of AI.

Resource Sharing Hub
  • Contribute to our CU Boulder exclusive crowdsource document where community members can share related resources with one another to build our literacy and transform our teaching practices together.
     
  • Submit a resource you’ve developed to our Teaching, Learning & AI open repository. All contributions are published online under Creative Commons licensing which means they are available for educators to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon the material in any medium or format, so long as attribution is given to the creator — possibly you!

Why Participate in TLAI CoP?

  • Collaborate with Peers: Network with educators and professionals who share your curiosities and concerns about teaching, learning and AI.
  • Expand Your Knowledge: Learn actionable strategies to address or integrate gen AI into your discipline and work.
  • Support Continuous Growth: Benefit from ongoing discussions, reflections, and real-world applications of AI Literacy and AI applications in teaching and learning.
  • Contribute to Change: Help shape policies and programs that make CU Boulder a more AI ready community.
