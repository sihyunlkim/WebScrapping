With Ellen Kedzierski, Ameenah Razi, and August Milliken (Educational Technology Research Assistants, ASSETT)

April 29, 2025 from 12:30-1:30pm

 

Join on Zoom, no registration needed: https://cuboulder.zoom.us/j/94680757585


Are you curious about what students really think about AI and how they might or might not use it as a learning tool? In this session, we’ll discuss recently collected data from a campus-wide survey of ~3,000 undergraduate students at CU Boulder.  Students in over 80 majors across campus identified their attitudes and perspectives toward AI, how and why they use (and don’t use) AI in their academic work, and what AI policy and training they’d like to see moving forward.  The students who conducted the survey will present preliminary results and discuss the implications of the data for teaching and learning. Leave this session with insights about student perspectives that can guide you as you address AI use within your own teaching and learning domain.
