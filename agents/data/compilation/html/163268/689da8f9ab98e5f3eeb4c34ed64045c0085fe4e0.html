AI Support

Getting AI Access, Support, or Training

To request access, ask questions, or sign up for training, or otherwise receive support, select the button labeled AI Tools & Training Support.See below for more information on these services

AI Tools & Training Support

  • All students and employees have access to the free AI tools under Google Gemini and Microsoft CoPilot. For employees, if you would like a paid account, you may submit a ticket requesting access.
  • All employees may request an Amplify account, which will give them access to a variety of large language models (LLM) to provide AI support.
  • Employees can sign up for training subscriptions that we have purchased from UMBC Training Centers. Clicking the above link will take you to the form, using the pulldown associated with “I need help with:”, select “UMBC Training AI Learning Subscription.”
