Academic Success and Engagement

A college education is more than a degree – it's a journey of personal growth and discovery. We're here to provide constant, caring support and ensure students know where and how to access the resources and services needed to be engaged, healthy, and successful.  


Artificial intelligence at UToledo
Artificial Intelligence can bring significant value to a university in several ways. AI can enhance teaching and learning, advance research and innovation and optimize campus operations and the student experience.

Awards,  Recognitions, and Leadership Opportunities

UToledo is proud to recognize faculty and staff that help bring our University's mission to life. Highlighting our outstanding teachers, researchers, and advisors, and acknowledging our professor emeriti and distinguished university lecturers are just a few of the ways academic affairs acknowledges the exceptional teaching, research, and service our Rockets receive.

 

 

College Credit Plus

College Credit Plus program (CCP) is a state-funded program that allows Ohio students in grades 7-12, who meet eligibility requirements, to jump start their Rocket experience by enrolling in college courses at little to no cost.

Curriculum Development, Teaching and Learning

Find resources to help with curriculum development, teaching and learning including resources on how to access and use the Curriculum Tracking System (CIM) and new program development guidelines. The Center for Excellence in Teaching and Learning, and UToledo Online provide pedagogical expertise and resources, including syllabus templates, one-on-one consultations, and course design workshops, to enhance the educational experience.

Institutional Effectiveness

Institutional Effectiveness encompasses Accreditation, Assessment, Program Review, and Institutional Research, and provides leadership, resources and support for academic policies, state/federal compliance, strategic planning, institutional and programmatic accreditation, program review, assessment, and serves as the official clearing house for university data.


Student Affairs

The Division of Student Affairs offers a comprehensive educational student life program that engages students in holistic learning opportunities, promotes connections, and prepares change agents to be global citizens within their personal and professional endeavors.

Workforce Development
The University of Toledo welcomes opportunities to collaborate with business and industry leaders in the community to build development initiatives for the advancement of Ohio and its citizens. We offer faculty expertise, research and development, and employee training and education.
