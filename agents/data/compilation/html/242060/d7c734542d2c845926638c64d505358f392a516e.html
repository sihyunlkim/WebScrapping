Admissions

The first step to to forge your future as a bilingual professional is applying for admission to the Universidad Ana G. Méndez. Student Services officers will help you with your Admission Application, courses validations, and financial aid.

Learn More

Financial Aid

The Financial Aid Office distributes its funds to students according to their financial needs. We offer a variety options such as The Federal College Work Study program, the FSEOG Grant, and the Pell Grant. We also offer loans to help you reach your goals.

Learn More

Academic Offerings

The Universidad Ana G. Méndez includes three campuses: Recinto de Gurabo, Recinto de Cupey, and Recinto de Carolina. We operate in Puerto Rico and the United States across 22 campuses.
