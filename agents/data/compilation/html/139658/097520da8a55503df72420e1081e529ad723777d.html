Emory's AI.Humanity Initiative

The AI.Humanity Initiative brings together the full intellectual power of Emory University to shape the AI revolution to better human health, generate economic value, and promote responsible applications of emerging technologies.

Through strategic hiring, innovative education, and a focus on ethics, Emory is unlocking AI’s potential to improve the human condition and advance our world.
