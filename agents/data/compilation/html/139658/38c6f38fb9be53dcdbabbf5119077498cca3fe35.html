Practical Gen AI for Health & Wellness Coaches

NEW LEARNING - This hands-on course is designed exclusively for health and wellness coaches, introducing the core concepts and transformative potential of Generative Artificial Intelligence (Gen AI). In two focused weekend sessions, participants will gain practical knowledge to navigate the opportunities and complexities of Gen AI in their coaching roles.

Agentic AI in the Enterprise: Microsoft Copilot Studio and Beyond

NEW LEARNING - Move beyond surface-level AI use and design reliable, contextual, and secure AI agents that deliver measurable business value. This immersive 5-week, 20-hour professional training program is designed to equip learners with practical skills in building, deploying, and operating intelligent AI assistants (agents) using Microsoft Copilot Studio.

AI Essentials: A Practical Guide for Professionals

NEW LEARNING - Feeling overwhelmed by information overload and struggling to keep up with your workload? Imagine having a powerful AI assistant by your side, ready to streamline your tasks and boost your productivity. Our AI Essentials course equips you with the magic of prompt engineering, using a leading AI tool to unlock a universe of possibilities.

Practical Artificial Intelligence with Power BI

NEW LEARNING - In this program you'll dive into the fascinating world of AI, mastering the fundamentals of data analytics, machine learning, and its various applications using Power BI. Whether you're a business analyst, marketer, or someone eager to leverage AI's potential, this program provides an ideal springboard for your journey.

The Ethical Path to AI: Navigating Strategies for Innovation and Integrity

NEW LEARNING - How should modern organizations navigate the unprecedented challenges from AI? What strategies should we use to identify the risks, understand the scope, avoid the harms, and define responsibilities when designing and using AI systems? To help answer these difficult questions, the Emory University Center for Ethics is offering this online certification course.
