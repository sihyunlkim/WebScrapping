  2. Artificial Intelligence

NDSUs minor in artificial intelligence equips you with a dynamic and in-demand skill set, enhancing your understanding of AI through a well-rounded curriculum. The minor provides foundational knowledge in math and computation, a specialized course in AI and a versatile elective drawn from one of the courses offered by the Department of Computer Science, or interdisciplinary offerings, allowing you to tailor your studies to your interests and career goals. This minor is an excellent choice for those looking to deepen their expertise in AI and prepare for a future in one of todays most exciting and impactful areas of technology.
