Esthetician and Massage Therapy Training

Posted on June 25, 2025 - Posted in: Advanced Esthetician, Esthetician, Massage Therapy, San Antonio Ingram

Looking for job training in San Antonio that leads to real opportunities and a rewarding career? Milan Institute Ingram offers hands-on programs in massage therapy and esthetics, designed for adult learners ready to start a new chapter in the thriving beauty and wellness industry.

Esthetician and Massage Therapy Training in San Antonio

San Antonio is one of Texass fastest-growing cities, with expanding opportunities in wellness, healthcare, and hospitality. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, massage therapy jobs are projected to grow by 18%. Skincare specialist roles may grow by 10% from 2023 to 2033much faster than average. With San Antonios population on the rise, as shown by recent U.S. Census data, job training in these fields puts you in a strong position for success.

Career-Focused Job Training Programs

  • Massage Therapy Training: Prepare for a career in one of the most in-demand wellness professions. Our massage therapy program includes a $1,000 scholarship for new students. This can help make your job training in San Antonio even more affordable.
  • Esthetician Training: Learn the latest skincare techniques and beauty treatments. Veterans and eligible dependents can use VA education benefits for our esthetician courses. These benefits can make it easier to access quality job training in San Antonio.
  • Flexible Schedules for Adult Learners: Our programs are designed to fit your busy life, with day and evening classes available.

Financial Aid and Support: Making School Affordable

We understand that paying for school is a major concern for many students. At Milan Institute Ingram, were committed to helping you find a way forward:

  • Federal Financial Aid: As an accredited institution, we accept federal financial aid for those who qualify. This includes Pell Grants, federal student loans, and other government assistance programs that can significantly reduce your out-of-pocket costs.
  • Scholarships and Special Benefits: In addition to our $1,000 scholarship for massage therapy students, we offer guidance on applying for additional scholarships and grants that may be available to you.
  • VA Education Benefits: Veterans and eligible dependents can use their VA education benefits toward our esthetician program, helping to cover tuition and related expenses.
  • Personalized Financial Aid Counseling: Our dedicated financial aid team will walk you through the application process, explain your options, and help you build a plan that works for your budget.
  • Flexible Payment Plans: For those who need it, we offer payment plan options to spread out tuition costs over time, making job training in San Antonio more accessible than ever.

For detailed information visit our Student Financial Aid page.

Strong Job Outlook and Earning Potential

  • Competitive Salaries: Massage therapists in Texas earn a median annual wage of $57,950, while estheticians in San Antonio average about $29,948 per year, with opportunities for higher earnings with experience.
  • Diverse Career Options: Graduates find work in spas, salons, hotels, wellness centers, and medical officesor launch a business with the skills learned through our job training in San Antonio.

Start Your Journey Today

Contact Milan Institute Ingram today to learn more about job training in San Antonio for massage therapy or estheticsand discover how you can turn your passion into a profession!

Sources: Bureau of Labor Statistics, U.S. Census, San Antonio Chamber of Commerce, Milan Institute Student Financial Aid
