Become a Confident Cosmetology Professional

Cosmetology is a hands-on career that requires precision, technique and personality. At Milan Institute, we know thats how it should be taught. As a student in this program, youll step directly into a hands-on teaching environment, specifically designed to nurture your development as a beauty professional.

Youll Learn:

  • Hairstyling Services
  • Chemical Hair Services
  • Skin Care Services
  • Hair Removal and Lash and Brow Services
  • Manicuring and Pedicuring
  • Disinfection and Sanitation
  • Health and Safety Considerations
  • And more!

Why Cosmetology School with Milan Institute

Hands-On Salon Experience

As a Milan Institute student, youll have the opportunity to gain practical experience working with real clients in our on-site student salon & spa. You’ll make a smooth transition from student to aspiring professional after working one-on-one with clients in a real salon setting.

State Board Exam Preparation

Upon completion of this program, you will be fully prepared and eligible to sit for the State Board examination for licensure, and qualify for an entry-level position in Cosmetology or related career fields.

Learn from Experienced Beauty Professionals

Milan Institute instructors are all experienced professionals who know exactly what it takes to succeed on the job. Our expert instructors are dedicated to helping new professionals get the skills they need to get established in their beauty careers.

Personalized Support

At Milan Institute, we deliberately keep class sizes small to ensure you get personalized, one-on-one support from your instructors. Youll feel fully supported and able to gain the skills you need in less time through our small class model.
