Beauty & Wellness Training in Boca Raton and Parkland, Florida

Boca Beauty Academy is conveniently located in beautiful, sunny South Florida with campus locations in Boca Raton and Parkland. You will feel at ease knowing that you are studying at a safe, secure campus where students and instuctors will help you feel at home. We provide the highest-quality products and equipment at both campuses so that you can achieve the beauty & wellness training that you deserve.
