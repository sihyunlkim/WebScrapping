You’re Invited! Parkland/Coral Springs Campus Open House

Posted on October 15, 2015 - Posted in: Beauty Career, Beauty School, Blog, Esthetics, News
Tagged: boca beauty academy, campus, open house, parkland

Is the dreamer inside of you ready to break free? If the desire for a career in the beauty, health, and wellness industry resides in the back of your mind, now is the perfect time to move it to the forefront. Because Boca Beauty Academy is having an Open House on October 29th and you’re invited!

With the grand opening of our new Parkland/Coral Springs campus comes an exciting opportunity for new and transfer students alike. Beginning at 11:00 a.m., this event will allow attendees to tour our campus, meet with our Admissions staff, and learn about available scholarships and financial aid for those who qualify.

Located at 7329 North State Road 7 in Parkland (one mile north of the Sawgrass Parkway on 441 just north of Hillsboro Boulevard), our campus is nestled in a beautiful tree-lined area of South Florida.

Here at Boca Beauty Academy, our programs cover a wide range of specialties; from Cosmetology and Nail Technology to Full Specialist and Massage Therapy, we offer something for everyone.

As thrilled as we are to extend our reach in the community, we didn’t focus solely on real estate; we’ve also added some exciting new programs. The addition of our Master Fitness Coach, Personal Fitness Trainer, and Barbering programs makes it easy to see that Boca Beauty Academy isn’t your average school we’re so much more.

If that little voice inside of you has started speaking up again, it is time to listen. Why stifle your dreams when you can live them? Come down to our amazing new campus on October 29th and find out where this path could lead. Sample the possibilities of this exciting industry and find out how Boca Beauty Academy can help launch a career you’ll love.

RSVP Online or 954-866-1011. We look forward to seeing you there!
