Event Series: AI and Teaching Fall 2024

The status of ChatGPT and AI right now.

Tuesday, September 10, 2024 @ 2:00 pm - 3:00 pm

Event Navigation

  • « Teaching Observation Partnership (TOP) For Large Enrollment Course
  • Graduate Teaching Academy »

In-Person

This workshop is part of a series of workshops, discussions, and course design sessions focused on AI in higher education. Register for any or all of them below.

 

 

September 10: The status of ChatGPT and AI right now.

September 24: AI and your discipline – prepare your students to succeed.

October 8: Determine your AI philosophy & put it in your syllabus.

October 22: Where do I start? Use AI to reclaim your time. 

November 5: Generate explicit AI guidelines for assignments.

November 19: Guide students in the use of AI to help them learn. 

December 3: Design your own a classbot or virtual TA.

October 1: Course Design Session Re-evaluate your course learning outcomes in light of generative AI

November 12: Course Design Session Re-evaluate your assessment approaches in light of generative AI

December 10: Course Design Session Re-evaluate your learning activities in light of generative AI
