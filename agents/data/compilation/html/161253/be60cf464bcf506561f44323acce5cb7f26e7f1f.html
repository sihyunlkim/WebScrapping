PaperCut Student Printing

Student Printing Funds: You are issued a $16.00 credit that your student printing is charged to. You start with a balance of $0.00 and your funds have been depleted when your balance reaches -$16.00. Your printing would be charged to your Black Bear Bucks at that time. You can view your balance through eAccounts on the Campus Portal or the eAccounts App on your mobile device.

You can print to any student accessible Xerox Unit on campus with the PaperCut printing client.
Printer Locations: Bennett Hall, DPC, Ferland EEDC 1st&2nd Floor, Fogler Library, Hilltop Dining, Memorial Union 2nd Floor, Neville Hall, Nutting Hall, Shibles Hall, Williams Hall, and York Dining.
