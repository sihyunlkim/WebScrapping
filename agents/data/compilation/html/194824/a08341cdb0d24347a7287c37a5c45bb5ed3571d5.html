Artificial Intelligence

Print Degree Planner (opens a new window) | Print-Friendly Page (opens a new window)

Return to: Programs

Artificial Intelligence is quickly becoming pervasive in our lives. Study how Artificial Intelligence can benefit from concepts and ideas from cognitive science, and explore the ways in which Artificial Intelligence is changing our lives.

To complete this integrative pathway, student must choose a minimum of 12 credits as described:

Choose one of the following:

  • IHSS 1972 – AI and Society, Credit Hours: 4
  • IHSS 1140 - Minds and Machines Credit Hours: 4
  • IHSS 1235 - Are Humans Rational? Credit Hours: 4

Required:

  • COGS 2120 - Introduction to Cognitive Science Credit Hours: 4

Choose remaining credits from the following:

  • PHIL 4960 – Ethics of AI
  • COGS 4960 - Learning and Advanced Game Ai
  • PHIL 4961 - Intermediate Formal Logic & AI
  • COGS 4210 - Cognitive Modeling Credit Hours: 4
  • COGS 4410 - Programming for Cognitive Science and Artificial Intelligence Credit Hours: 4
  • COGS 4420 - Game AI Credit Hours: 4
  • COGS 4640 - Intelligent Virtual Agents Credit Hours: 4
  • COGS 4880 - Language-Endowed Intelligent Agents Credit Hours: 4
  • COGS 4960 - Topics in Cognitive Science Credit Hours: 1 to 4

  • (Learning and Advanced Game AI or Artificial Intelligent Agents)

Compatible minor:

Cognitive Science of Artificial Intelligence Minor
