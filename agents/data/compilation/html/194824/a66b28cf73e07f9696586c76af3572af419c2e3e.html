AI Literacy Toolkit

The Artificial Intelligence (AI) Literacy Toolkit includes core competencies, search tools powered by GenAI, and skill benchmarks that faculty and librarians can use to incorporate AI literacy education into their instruction.

  • GenAI Literacy Program: Foundations of AI (Free Course)
    Foundations of AI is one of three courses in the GenAI Literacy Program.
    The course is free and self-paced, and graduates will be awarded a certificate and digital badge for scoring 80% or higher on the course quiz.
    more... less...
    Upon completing this course, you will understand:
    Why familiarizing yourself with AI is important  
    The potential of GenAI, including current limitations 
    The types of GenAI tools available and the technologies that drive them
