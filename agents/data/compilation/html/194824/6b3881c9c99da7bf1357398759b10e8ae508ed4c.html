The Office of the Provost fosters a vibrant academic community by encouraging discovery and learning, inspiring innovation and entrepreneurship, and actively promoting diversity. It also provides vision and guidance to ensure excellence in academic programs, standards, and processes. The office prioritizes the hiring and support of the highest quality faculty who provide exciting and intellectually diverse academic offerings, as well as cultivating leaders at various levels of the academic enterprise.
