Beta Classroom

Beta Classroom

The Beta Classroom is located on the First Floor of the Folsom Library. The Beta Classroom is a high technology testing space for interactive learning, which can accommodate about 20 participants. The goal of this space is for faculty to pilot new or interactive technologies and pedagogical approaches that may be appropriate for rolling out in a broad range of classrooms. This space is also dedicated to educational workshops/events for faculty and staff through the TLC. 

Teaching & Learning Events

Teaching & Learning Events

Each year the Office of Undergraduate Education hosts an event focused on inspiring our faculty with ideas for innovation and interactivity in the classroom.  The event is free and open to the faculty, staff, and graduate students of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute.
