HelioCampus Spring 2026 Syllabus Available - Complete by January 26, 2026

The HelioCampus syllabus module is now available for faculty to access for the spring. Based on the requirements from the Provost's office, faculty should have their syllabi published in HelioCampus by January 26, 2026. HelioCampus will publish all syllabi automatically on February 2, 2026.

For a refresher on how to log into HelioCampus and complete your syllabus please review the quick handout here: HelioCampus Syllabus Entry Refresher.

Teaching During the Summer Semester? Please Complete Your Syllabus in HelioCampus

Just a quick reminder to please complete your syllabus in HelioCampus. Based on the requirements from the Provost's office, faculty should have their syllabi published in HelioCampus by June 2, 2025. HelioCampus will publish all syllabi automatically on June 9, 2025.

If you need assistance, contact oira@rpi.edu or speak with your department or school administrator who assists with HelioCampus.

HelioCampus Syllabus Available for Summer Term April 28

The HelioCampus syllabus module is available for faculty to access for the summer term beginning on April 28, 2025. Per requirements from the Provost's office, faculty should have their syllabi published by June 2, 2025, two weeks after the start of classes.

 

 

 

Reminder: 2025 Student Climate Survey Launched

The 2025 Student Climate Survey launched by email on March 31, 2025. Your honest opinions about campus life—how it impacts your ability to learn and grow, both academically and personally—are important. You will find your personal link to complete the survey in your email from President Schmidt. Our goal is to achieve at least a 50% response rate this year. Please submit the completely anonymous survey by April 25, 2025.

Call for Faculty Reflective Pedagogy Fellows – Applications due April 11th

A key strategic goal of the Rensselaer Forward plan is to “Create a robust and holistic learning community.” Inquiry-based learning and creativity are key elements toward achieving that goal. The Gameful and Immersive Learning Teams invite interested faculty to apply to redesign one of their courses (or an element of their course) to align with this important Institute goal.

Our governance structure includes the BOT, the President and Cabinet, Academic Leadership (Provost & Deans’ Council), the Faculty Senate, and Student Governance (Student Union, Student Senate, Undergraduate Council, & Graduate Council), along with Staff Engagement. What’s Next After Graduation?

RPI students consistently excel, with placement rates ranging from 91–95% in recent years. In 2023, among those placed, 56% secured employment, 33% enrolled in graduate school, and 11% pursued other opportunities, including military service, travel abroad, the Peace Corps, non-traditional employment, and entrepreneurship.
