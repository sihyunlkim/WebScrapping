HelioCampus (formerly AEFIS) Assessment Tool

Rensselaer uses the AEFIS tool for Course Syllabus, Assessment, and Course Evaluation purposes. The Provost requires that faculty put their course syllabus in AEFIS and students are asked to complete course evaluations in AEFIS each semester for all courses.
