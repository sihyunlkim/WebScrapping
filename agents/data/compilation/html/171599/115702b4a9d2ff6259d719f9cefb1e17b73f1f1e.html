Boldly Lead!

Engaging webinars packed with practical insights and proven strategies for courageous, authentic leadership.

About the Boldly Lead Webinar Series

The Leadership Learning Community at The University of Olivet proudly presents Boldly Lead!a free webinar series packed with actionable strategies to help you lead with authenticity, inspire courage, and empower others to Be More and Do Good, the core values of UOlivet’s leadership philosophy.

Image

Boldly Lead! Webinar Sessions

Summer 2025 Series

All sessions are held from 12 1 p.m. ET via Zoom and are free to attend. Register for as many sessions as you would like.

June - Mindful Fuel for Your Courageous Journey

Speaker: Jennifer Cousineau, Ph.D., assistant professor of Health & Human Performance, The University of Olivet

Date: June 24, 2025

About the Session: Explore how mindful movement and breath work can support courageous leadership in times of stress and uncertainty. Through a blend of experiential learning and conversation, participants will engage in simple, practical techniques to regulate their nervous systems, increase self-awareness and lead with confidence and clarity.

Whether you’re navigating high-pressure decisions or grounding your leadership in presence and purpose, this session offers tools to lead boldly from within. Bring a willingness to participate and reflect.

Register
Image

July Leadership Development for Community Empowerment and Democracy

Date:Tuesday, July 29, 2025

Speaker: Zoe Hammer, Ph.D. & Courageous Panelists, professor of social science, sociology & anthropology, The University of Olivet

About the Session: Learn the fundamentals of grassroots community organizing and the development of grassroots leaders. A panel of working organizers will provide an overview of organizing principles and strategies grounded in their own experiences building the power of everyday people to shape our shared future.

Participants will gain tools for understanding our current conjuncture and how they can get involved making the changes they want to see.

Register
Image

August The Time Closet: A strategy for reclaiming the value of your time

Date: Monday, August 25, 2025

Speaker: Kelly Corey, Ph.D., professor of business and director, Master of Arts in Leadership, The University of Olivet. Read Kelly’s bio

About the Session: Fitting grad school, a crazy work schedule, family, fun and rest can be quite a challenge. But, the challenge can be surmountable with a strategy that treats time as a commodity, sort of like closet space.

Join us for a life maintenance break while were all getting back in the groove after summer. We will learn the philosophy of putting value on our time, then we will use a simple method to allocate our time to the things that are most important to us, and identify some free time for fun, as well. When its done, we can breathe a sigh of relief that we have a plan for how to proceed when we get up in the morning. And we can focus our energy on boldly leading.

Register

The Courageous Leadership Colloquium

Lead Boldly. Live Purposefully.

Join us September 2425 for a free, two-day virtual Leadership Colloquium designed to help you grow as a courageous, values-driven leaderboth professionally and personally.

Whether youre just beginning your leadership journey or looking to sharpen your skills, this experience is built for emerging leaders who are ready to rise to the challenges of todayand tomorrow.

Guided by the core principle of Be More. Do Good., this event will empower you to lead with purpose, navigate complexity with confidence, and make meaningful change in your organization, community, and beyond.
