VCU's Technology Policies, Guidelines & Standards

Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) provides and maintains computing resources to support the University's mission critical operations of education, research, service, and administration. To ensure that these resources are used ethically, equitably, and legally by faculty, staff, and students, VCU has established certain guidelines, policies, and standards.
