CERTIFIED PIPE WELDER

The Program

The Hottest Sector In Welding

Combo pipe welding is highly in-demand and profitable for welders. The work is hard, the hours are long, and the welds require a high level of skill. Your welding school day will feel like a job site. From welding out in the elements to constantly welding out of position, instructors push you to dig deep. Welding classes are packed with industry-relevant knowledge and skills. In the end, this program provides opportunities for special alloys and weld tests, where hard work is rewarded.

CERTIFIED Pipe WELDER PROGRAM

What do Certified Pipe Welders/Fitters Do?

  • Cut, Prep, Fit, Rig, Weld Pipe
  • New Construction/ Repair and Maintenance
  • Outages, Emergencies, Turnarounds, Projects
  • Power Plant, Gas and Oil, Food Grade Stainless

What Youll Burn:

  • Carbon to Carbon pipe
  • Stainless to Carbon pipe
  • Stainless to stainless pipe
  • Specialty alloys

What Youll Learn:

  • GTAW, SMAW, FCAW, GMAW on pipe ALL Positions
  • Restricted position welding
  • Fit, prep, rigging, and welding pipe runs
  • OFC, PAC, CAC thermal processes

What Youll Earn:

  • AWS Certified Welder Card (Combo Pipe tests)
    • Monster 2- x .625 6010/7018 6G (R optional)
    • Monster 2- x .625 ER70S-2/7018 6G (R optional)
    • Monster 2- x .625 ER308 6G (R optional)
    • Optional Upgrades: Inconel, sch10 Stainless, Branch and Bellhole, Chrome-Moly
  • NCCER Certification Card (Welder, Boilermaker, Rigger, PipeFitter)
  • OSHA 10 card (option for OSHA 30 upgrade)
  • ASHI CPR/ 1st Aid Certification Card
  • Optional MSHA training
