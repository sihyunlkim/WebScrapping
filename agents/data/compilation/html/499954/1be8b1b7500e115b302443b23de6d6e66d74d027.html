READY TO GET CERTIFIED IN LESS THAN A YEAR?

Our 15:1 student to instructor ratio, combined with classroom theory, practical demonstration and student application ensures every student is reaching their maximum potential. All KWI students graduate the program with an abundance of certifications making them ready to land their first job.

CAN YOU HANDLE AN INTENSE, INDUSTRY DRIVEN PROGRAM?

KWI focuses on one thing creating elite welders. Through intense 40 hour training weeks, students get in, train hard, hone skills, and graduate quickly in order to earn maximum pay in a short amount of time.

YOUR ROADMAP TO SUCCESS

We pride ourselve on providing you with the blueprint and tools needed to thrive as soon as you graduate. Right now, there is a gap that exists between what welding education is teaching and what industry demands from a certified welder. We aim to change that.

Thats why we talked with welders, general foremen, superintendents, inspectors and designed an educational experience that has all the grit and grind of a job site. Not only that, but students get every certification, piece of equipment, and testing needed to show up to jobs with confidence!

Each one of our welding programs are tailored for a successful path into the welding industry. Check out your options today!
