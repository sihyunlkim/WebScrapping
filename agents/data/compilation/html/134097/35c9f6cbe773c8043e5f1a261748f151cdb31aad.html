Initiatives

The Office of the Provost at FSU is dedicated to accelerating the university’s growth in key areas of academic excellence through a series of strategic, forward-looking initiatives. These efforts are designed to enhance scholarship, enrich education, and expand the university’s impact across all disciplines—aligning closely with the evolving needs of our communities and industries.

Through the leadership of our faculty, the strength of our innovative centers and institutes, and a commitment to translational research and civic engagement, FSU is driving progress in six critical domains: FSU Health, Innovation, Advanced Manufacturing and Workforce Development, Doctoral Education, Quantum Science, and Civic Leadership.

FSU Health

FSU Health is a bold initiative to build a health care ecosystem that will transform health care delivery in North Florida. 

More

Academic Innovation

Academic Innovation at FSU is a strategic initiative supported by the Office of the Provost and driven by faculty innovation in teaching, research, and service. 

More

InSPIRE

FSU InSPIRE drives regional economic impact and serves as a national model for excellence in workforce development, applied research and innovation. 

More

Enhancing Doctoral Education

This initiative aims to strengthen doctoral student outcomes with a focus on scholarly and creative activity and engagement, career readiness, and teaching preparation.

More

Quantum Initiative

The FSU Quantum Initiative aims to advance research and education in quantum science and engineering to enhance capabilities and impact.

Institute for Governance and Civics

The Florida Legislature established the FSU IGC to engage students in governance, foster literacy in public policy, and promote civic leadership.
