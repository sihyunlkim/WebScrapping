Professional Development Opportunity from the Library and Coursera

Professional Development: AI and Other Learning Opportunities with Coursera

Coursera Request Form

In support of professional development around generative AI, the library is excited to announce access to Coursera for SUNY Empire faculty and staff.  You can learn more about AI or enroll in other Coursera courses to learn new skills. There are limited seats available and Coursera seats will be for two months, with renewals possible if there aren't others waiting for access.

Here’s what’s included:

  • Customized Tracks on AI and Other Topics: Beginner and intermediate tracks to help you dive into generative AI concepts at your own pace or learn other important Empire relevant topics like Power BI or Project Management.
  • Full Coursera Catalog Access: Explore a wide range of courses for more specialized knowledge in generative AI and related topics.

Interested faculty will also have access to the larger Coursera catalog, including courses and programs on more specialized and advance AI topics. Limited Licenses are available. Please take advantage of this great opportunity to gain further insight into AI in the higher education setting. To request access please fill out the Coursera request form.
