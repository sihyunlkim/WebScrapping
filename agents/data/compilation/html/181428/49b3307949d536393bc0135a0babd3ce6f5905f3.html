Academic Affairs
University of Nebraska Medical Center
    • Institutional Services Overview
      • Accreditation & Assessment

        Access resources and the most up-to-date information about our institutional accreditation and assessment initiatives.

      • Institutional Research

        View institutional data regarding students and faculty, as well as Coalition for Next Generation Life Science data.

      • Design Thinking

        Discover new ways to generate innovative solutions to complex issues by using human-centered design methods.

      • Interactive E-Learning

        Harness the power of digital learning by creating high-quality, interactive experiences for students.

      • Behavioral Health Education Center of Nebraska

        BHECN recruits and educates students in behavioral health fields and trains and retains professionals already in the workforce.

      • Campus Engagement

        Learn how UNMC is working to create a welcoming culture that empowers everyone to have a voice at the table.

      • Community Engagement

        The office builds relationships and collaborations between UNMC faculty, staff, students and the communities we serve.

      • Rural Health

        The office provides direction and support for a variety of health programs, services and initiatives across Nebraska.

      • Global Center for Health Security

        The center encompasses all biopreparedness, infectious diseases and high-consequence infections research, education and clinical care.

      • Global Engagement

        Our experts promote international diversity, foster global understanding and awareness, and advance internationalization efforts throughout.

      • iEXCEL

        Discover how we foster the practice of patient care in effective interprofessional teams in a safe and innovative learning environment.

      • Ombuds Program

        The Ombuds Team of impartial, confidential consultants can help faculty, staff, and students resolve conflicts informally.

      • Open Nebraska Textbooks

        This university-wide program helps students save money through free and reduced-cost digital course materials.

      • Academic Services

        Faculty and staff receive support and assistance in their clinical, teaching and administrative roles.

      • Noncredit Course Development

        The Office of Noncredit Learning offers faculty resources to create and administer noncredit and professional development courses.

    • Educational Services Overview
      • High School & Undergraduate Programs

        Our pathway programs for high school and undergraduate students offer the opportunity to work toward health care careers.

      • Colleges & Graduate Studies

        Learn about our allied health, dentistry, medicine, nursing, pharmacy and public health colleges, as well as our graduate programs.

      • Interprofessional Education

        IPE allows students across colleges to learn and work together to optimize their unique strengths and increase the quality of care for patients.

      • Online Programs

        Our online certificate, bachelor's, master's or doctoral programs enable you to earn an advanced degree at a time and place convenient for you.

      • Health Security Training

        Discover interprofessional academic, national or international training on biodefense, emergency preparedness, biosafety, infectious diseases and more.

      • Behavioral Health Education Center of Nebraska

        BHECN recruits and educates students in behavioral health fields and trains and retains professionals already in the workforce.

      • Noncredit and Professional Education

        If you want to advance your skills but don't need the in-depth training of a certificate or degree, take a noncredit course from NU Advance that's taught by university experts.

      • Postdoctoral Education

        We promote and facilitate outstanding training and education of postdoctoral scholars and their timely transition toward independent careers.

      • Student Success

        No matter where you are in your educational journey, we’ll help you thrive as you prepare for your career in the health sciences.

      • Financial Aid

        The Office of Financial Aid provides guidance on applying for financial aid or FAFSA, information on scholarships and loan repayment options.

      • Information for Current Students

        Access all the resources and portals you'll need as a student, including tech help, wellness resources, study space scheduling and more.

    • Faculty Affairs Overview
      • Faculty Development

        The Office of Faculty Development provides training and resources to enhance faculty’s skills in clinical and classroom teaching, research and leadership.

      • Interprofessional Academy of Educators

        Connect with a diverse community of educators who foster a supportive environment for educational innovation, collaboration and scholarship.

      • Mentoring

        We are dedicated to the mentoring of our faculty and ensuring you succeed, whether you are the one receiving or providing the guidance.

      • Leadership Training

        Established and emerging leaders can grow and enhance their leadership skills through one of our training programs.

      • Overview

        Faculty Commons is a dedicated space for faculty members where they can test ideas, ask questions, check out resources or focus on writing projects.

      • Features

        Faculty Commons includes a living room area, hoteling suites and conference room, and is closely located to a practice classroom and e-learning resources.

      • Services

        Faculty Commons offers one location where you can consult with education, career, technology and educational research experts.

      • Experts

        Meet the experts housed in Faculty Commons who are ready to consult with faculty on your professional development needs.

      • Overview

        Discover the process and guidelines for when you are submitting your achievements for promotion and tenure.

      • Promotion & Tenure Forms

        Access all the UNMC forms you’ll need when applying for promotion and tenure, as well as college specific guidelines.

      • Promotion & Tenure Toolkit

        Learn what is needed to create an impactful teaching portfolio from individuals who have successfully navigated the promotion and tenure process.

      • Resource Center

        Discover professional development opportunities, options for independent learning, network and support communities, and more.

      • Education Awards

        The Impact in Education Awards are the highest education-related awards conferred by UNMC and recognize the dedication of our faculty.

      • ConnectED Portal

        Learn independently through our open-access portal with educational resources, infographics, videos and more from local and national experts.

    • Compliance Overview
    • Areas

      Discover all the areas who provide expertise, resources and training on laws and regulations related to research and health science education.

    • Training

      Mandatory compliance training for employees, students and visitors is a critical component of the UNMC Compliance Program.

    • Conflict of Interest

      You can submit a conflict of interest disclosure through COI-SMART, and check faculty payments in the CMS Open Payments Database.

    • Research Integrity

      UNMC is committed to maintaining an academic environment based on honesty, integrity and ethical conduct.

    • Additional Resources

      Access other compliance services that provide compliance information, guidance and/or regulations.

    • Report a Compliance Issue

      If you have questions or concerns about conduct that may violate requirements, there are several options available to you, including anonymous reporting.
