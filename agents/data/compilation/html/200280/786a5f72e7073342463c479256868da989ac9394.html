Publisher and Journal Policies

Many publishers and journals have their own specific policies regarding AI use. It is recommended to review the submission guidelines of the particular journal you are targeting or contact the editor directly. The links provided below direct you to policies from major scholarly publishers, illustrating different approaches and perspectives. Be sure to review the policy carefully to ensure you meet the publisher's expectations.
