Engage with CTLI

Engage with CTLI to share insights, explore ideas, and drive educational excellence at MSU.

At CTLI, our mission is to help individuals, units, and departments at MSU achieve their teaching and learning goals through fellowships, grants, workshops, and consultations tailored to your needs.
