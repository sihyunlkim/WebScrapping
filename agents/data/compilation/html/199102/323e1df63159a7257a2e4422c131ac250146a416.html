Office of the Provost

North Carolina A&T proudly offers a broad selection of undergraduate, graduate, distance learning and continuing education programs spanning diverse subjects from agriculture to nanoengineering.

In the Office of the Provost, it is our responsibility to manage and direct all academic programs at nine A&T colleges as well as, A&T’s Joint School of Nanoscience and Nanoengineering, Library Services, and the Graduate College.

The provost and executive vice chancellor for the Division of Academic Affairs is the chief academic officer for North Carolina A&T with responsibility for reviewing, evaluating, and recommending changes in academic policies and programs. The provost relies on the support of an administrative staff of vice provosts and associate vice provosts of academic affairs to supervise a wide range of N.C. A&T programs:
