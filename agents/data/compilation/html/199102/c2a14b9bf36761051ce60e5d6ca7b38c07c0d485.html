AI Technology Tools

AI is reshaping how we work, teach, and collaborate. This page highlights institutionally supported AI tools that are approved for use across campus. These tools help automate tasks, generate insights, and enhance creativity—empowering faculty, staff, and students to work smarter and explore what’s possible.

 

Enterprise AI Tools

The AI tools listed below are institutionally licensed and available to the entire campus community. Whether you're streamlining tasks, enhancing communication, or exploring creative solutions, these tools are ready to support your work—no additional purchase required.

 

Microsoft Copilot

Copilot Chat is an AI-powered assistant that helps you research, summarize, brainstorm, and get quick answers—all in a conversational interface. Available to all faculty, staff, and students through our Microsoft enterprise license.

Access it at copilot.microsoft.com or within Word, Excel, Outlook, and more in the web-based Microsoft 365 apps—just sign in with your university account.

Adobe Express Firefly

Adobe Express now includes Firefly, Adobe’s AI-powered design engine that helps you generate images, text effects, and creative assets with just a few words. Whether you're creating flyers, social posts, or presentations, Firefly makes design fast, fun, and accessible to everyone.

Available to all faculty, staff, and students—just sign in at adobe.com/express with your university account.

Zoom AI Companion

Zoom AI Companion is built into Zoom to help you work smarter with AI-powered features like meeting summaries, action item tracking, and message drafting.

Available to all faculty and staff through our enterprise Zoom license. No setup required—just sign into Zoom and start using it.

Scite_

Scite_ is an AI-powered research tool that helps you discover, evaluate, and understand scientific literature more effectively. With Smart Citations, Scite_ shows how a publication has been cited—whether it supports, contrasts, or simply mentions the original work—so you can quickly assess research reliability and context.

Available to all faculty, staff, and students—just sign in at scite.ai with your university account.

Approved AI Tools—Available Only Through Institutional Licences

These subscription-based AI tools are approved for use at NC A&T only when purchased through the University’s institutional contracts. Personal accounts or outside purchases are not approved for University use. Departments may purchase licenses for employees who would benefit from access, with enterprise-level data security and compliance built in. Details on availability, access procedures, and training opportunities will be shared as licensing agreements are finalized.
