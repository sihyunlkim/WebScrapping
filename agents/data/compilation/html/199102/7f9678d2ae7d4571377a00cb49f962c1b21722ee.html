Full Conference Schedule

The 2025 Artificial Intelligence Conference features a dynamic schedule designed to foster deep engagement and interdisciplinary collaboration. Each breakout session spans just over an hour and typically includes a hands-on workshop along with two sets of 30-minute oral or panel presentations, with up to three presentation options available in each set. This format allows attendees to explore a wide range of AI topics—from ethical considerations and instructional tools to cutting-edge research and practical applications—through interactive and thought-provoking discussions.

Use the schedule below to plan your conference experience and make the most of the diverse topics and sessions offered.
