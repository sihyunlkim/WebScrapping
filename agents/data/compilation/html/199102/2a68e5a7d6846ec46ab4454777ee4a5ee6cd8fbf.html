Policies

The University prohibits sex-based harassment and discrimination, including, sexual assault, domestic violence, dating violence, and stalking, collectively “Sex-Based Misconduct” or “Prohibited Conduct.” The University will promptly take steps to fairly eliminate such conduct, prevent recurrence, and remedy its effects. The University conducts ongoing prevention, awareness, and training programs for employees and students.

  • UNIVERSITY POLICY 204, EQUAL OPPORTUNITY AND NONDISCRIMINATION  
  • UNIVERSITY POLICY 205, IMPROPER RELATIONSHIPS BETWEEN STUDENTS AND EMPLOYEES  
  • UNIVERSITY POLICY 206, PREGNANCY AND RELATED MEDICAL CONDITIONS
  • APPENDIX A TO UNIVERSITY POLICY 206, PROCEDURES TO REQUEST ACCOMMODATIONS FOR PREGNANCY AND RELATED MEDICAL CONDITIONS
  • UNIVERSITY POLICY 207, TITLE IX SEX-BASED MISCONDUCT   
  • UNIVERSITY POLICY 209, UNLAWFUL HARASSMENT AND DISCRIMINATION
