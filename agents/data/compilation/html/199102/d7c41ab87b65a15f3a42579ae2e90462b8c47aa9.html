Research Compliance and Ethics

The Office of Research Compliance and Ethics is committed to achieving the highest standards of excellence and integrity in all its research endeavors and promoting the ethical conduct of research.

The primary goal of the Office of Research Compliance and Ethics is to educate the North Carolina A&T research community about regulatory requirements and ethical standards governing research. Faculty and students are responsible for ensuring compliance with all applicable regulations and policies.

Contact the Research Compliance Office for assistance with applying for approval though the compliance committees, interpretation of regulatory guidelines, Export Control guidance, Research Misconduct process and Conflict of Interest concerns.
