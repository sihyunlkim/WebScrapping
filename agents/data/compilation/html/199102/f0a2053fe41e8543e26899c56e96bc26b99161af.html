AI Trainings

The AI Accelerator Initiative is transforming the way our Aggie community learns, works, and leads with artificial intelligence. This training hub brings together live sessions, on-demand videos, and in-depth courses to help faculty and staff build confidence in using AI responsibly and effectively. From foundational literacy to advanced applications, each offering provides practical, university-approved guidance designed to boost productivity, streamline workflows, and spark new possibilities in teaching, research, and operations.

With ongoing support and secure access to enterprise-grade tools, the AI Accelerator Initiative ensures that every member of our campus community is equipped to integrate AI thoughtfully into their professional practice. Explore the trainings below and take the next step in raising your AIQ—because at North Carolina A&T, we are preparing today’s leaders to thrive in the AI era.
