Facilitator: Marc Watkins

Panelists: Emily Pitts Donahoe, Wendy Goldberg, Brian Young

Location: Zoom (virtual)

 

Description: Please join the Academic Innovations Group on Zoom for a lively panel discussion about academic integrity in the age of generative AI. We’ll cover timely topics like AI detection, offer practical advice for openly disclosing the usage of AI, how to talk with students when they misuse AI, and how to navigate using AI in your own work.

 

This event is open to all University of Mississippi-affiliated faculty/staff and graduate students.
