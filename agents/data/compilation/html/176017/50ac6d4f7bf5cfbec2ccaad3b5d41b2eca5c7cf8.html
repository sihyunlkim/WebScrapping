Generative AI on the Syllabus

Authors

Derek Bruff, University of Mississippi
Robert Cummings, University of Mississippi

Document Type

Conference Proceeding

Publication Date

8-11-2023

Abstract

Date: Friday, August 11 Time: 10:00 am to 11:00 am Location: Zoom Facilitators: Derek Bruff, visiting associate director, CETL, and Robert Cummings, executive director, Academic Innovations Group What policies on using ChatGPT and other generative AI tools should you have in your fall courses? Should you attempt to ban them? Embrace them? Explore them with your students? And how will you talk with your students about AI tools through your syllabus and class conversations? In this session, sponsored by CETL and the Academic Innovations Group, we will explore these questions and suggest policy options and potential syllabus language for instructors to use.
