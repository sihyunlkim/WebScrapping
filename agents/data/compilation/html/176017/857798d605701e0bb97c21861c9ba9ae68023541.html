Academic Integrity

Academic integrity is essential to the values upon which the University is founded.  Croft students must therefore observe academic honesty in all aspects of their work.  A Croft student with a documented case of plagiarism or some other form of academic dishonesty in a course approved for the International Studies major will face the possibility of receiving the grade of F for the course and being asked to leave the International Studies major.  Working with the University's administration, the individual faculty member will determine the specific consequences of the behavior violating the official regulations and spirit of academic integrity.

It is your responsibility to become familiar with the University of Mississippi's official policy on academic integrity and to abide by it.
