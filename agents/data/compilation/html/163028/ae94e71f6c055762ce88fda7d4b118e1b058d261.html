Automotive Service Technology Class Starts (Morning, Afternoon, & Evening)

03/17/2026

Automotive Service Technology training helps prepare students to pursue entry-level employment opportunities as technicians at dealerships, independent shops, service centers, fleet maintenance departments and more. Time to get started!

Electrical & Electronic Systems Technology Class Starts (Morning, Afternoon, & Evening)

03/17/2026

Through Lincoln’s partnership with Johnson Controls, our students can get connected to a top employer in the field. Johnson Controls facilities across the country have hiring needs to be filled, and they’ve partnered with us to provide skills training to help develop their workforce! Get additional information from our Admissions Team.

HVAC Class Starts (Morning, Afternoon, & Evening)

03/17/2026

Our HVAC program provides students with the hands-on training needed to succeed in an all-season field. Students will learn to install, maintain, and repair essential temperature control equipment found in homes and businesses. Train for an essential career today!

Medical Assistant Class Start (Morning, Afternoon, & Evening)

03/17/2026

Our Medical Assistant program can lead to an exciting career as an in-demand healthcare support professional. Students train in our classrooms and labs which simulate professional healthcare offices from reception to exam rooms. Become an invaluable part of any healthcare team!

Welding & Metal Fabrication Technology Class Start (Evening)

03/17/2026

Our Welding students learn to master the most-used welding processes utilized in the majority of applications used in the industry. When they graduate, they’ll also qualify to test for Level One certification from the National Center for Construction Education and Research (NCCER).
