What is AI Literacy? 

"AI literacy is the ability to understand, use, and think critically about AI technologies and their impact on society, ethics, and everyday life."

- Lo, L. S. (2025). AI Literacy: A Guide for Academic Libraries. College & Research Libraries News, 86(3), Article 3. https://doi.org/10.5860/crln.86.3.120
