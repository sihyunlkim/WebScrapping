Search the Lewis Library for Information on Artificial Intelligence

Example Keywords to Input in Search

Add different keywords and combinations depending on your AI related topic when doing search.  Examples:

  • artificial intelligence education
  • artificial intelligence society
  • AI jobs
  • artificial intelligence ethics

On a search results page, you can always click on 'Available online' on the left of the screen to get immediate access to articles. 

Also, you can refine the Publication Date to the last few years to see the most recent, relevant research into the topic.
