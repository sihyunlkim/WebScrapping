Welcome

By including library resources directly into your courses in the LMS, Brightspace, it will increase student usage of scholarly sources. The LibApps LTI makes embedding library content a simple process. 

LibApps LTI

Available Content Types to Embed in Brightspace:

Guides & Guide Content

  • Full LibGuide – entire guide loads in Brightspace frame
  • Single Page – a single page of a guide of your choosing loads within the Brightspace content area
  • Content Box – a single box from a guide displays in Brightspace content area

A-Z Databases

  • All A-Z Databases – library’s A-Z databases page loads within Brightspace content area.
  • Databases for Specific Subject + Subject Specialists – loads subject database page, including liaison librarian picture and contact information
  • Databases for Specific Subject (list only) – loads list of databases related to the subject you choose
  • Specific A-Z Database – provides a link to the database you choose
  • New/Trial Databases – displays list of New/Trial databases

Creating Links to Articles and Database Content

You can add links to specific articles from the Library databases in your Brightspace course, so all your students can read the same resource. You can also provide links to videos from Swank, Kanopy, and Films On Demand. The key to creating this link is using the permanent link or permalink to the specific record.
