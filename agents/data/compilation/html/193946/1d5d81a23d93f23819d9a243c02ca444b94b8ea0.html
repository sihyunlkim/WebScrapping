Two unique locations, One strong community

SUNY Niagara is located in Sanborn, NY and offers more than 50 degree and certificate programs. The Niagara Falls Culinary Institute, a division of SUNY Niagara, is situated in the heart of downtown Niagara Falls and houses our culinary, baking and hospitality programs.
