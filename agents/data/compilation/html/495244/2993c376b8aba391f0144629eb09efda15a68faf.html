    Pearlands Innovative School of Beauty is based in Pearland, TX and welcomes students from the surrounding areas of Pearland, Friendswood, and Alvin area. Our institution aims to provide a quality education to our students in the field of cosmetology. We provide opportunities for our students to not only express and expand their creativity, but also learn the mastery of cosmetology. Our mission is to prepare our students for a bright and successful future. Both full and part-time classes are available.
