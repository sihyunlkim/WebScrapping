Welcome!

Welcome to "Chatting Up ChatGPT," your guide to understanding and using generative AI tools like ChatGPT in health science research. This guide will walk you through the essentials of generative AI, provide tips for creating effective prompts, and introduce tools that help you train AI to get the best results. You'll also learn about the challenges of misinformation, AI hallucinations, and deepfakes, and discover strategies to use AI responsibly in your research.

Whether you're a researcher, student, or educator, this guide offers resources to help you navigate the evolving world of AI-powered tools in academic and health sciences.
