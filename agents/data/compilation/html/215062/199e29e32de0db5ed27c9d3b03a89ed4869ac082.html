AI @ Penn

Researchers across Penn are building the foundations and developing new applications of AI to accelerate advancements in engineering, society, health, science, business, and more.

Explore AI @ Penn
AI Foundations

The mathematical and algorithmic foundations of AI underpin its remarkably broad applications. Penn researchers are exploring why AI methods work when they do, why they fail, and discovering the fundamental principles that will enable the next generation of AI algorithms.

 AI + Society

How is AI reshaping society? Penn researchers are building trustworthy algorithms, evaluating AI's societal impact, and using AI to help understand the evolution of the human condition.

 AI + Health

Our fundamental understanding of biological systems and the applications of modern medicine is enhanced by AI. Penn scientists are using AI for genetic predictors of disease, optimizing healthcare practice, understanding population health dynamics, developing personalized healthcare, and more.

 AI + Science

AI is helping us understand how the human mind reasons, processes language, and conceptualizes the world around us. Penn scientists are applying AI to discover new materials, learn the structure of proteins, model global climate, and unravel the mysteries of dark matter and dark energy.

 AI + Business

What will the future of industry look like as AI advances in capability, enhances productivity, and potentially changes the nature of our relationship with work itself?

AI Education @ Penn

Penn is transforming AI education through multidisciplinary programs that equip tomorrows professionals with the ability to navigate AI's opportunities and risks. As a leader in innovative pedagogy, Penns programs at all levels ensure that students are ready for the rapidly evolving landscape of AI across fields and industries.
