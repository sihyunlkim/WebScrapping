Penn AI

About

Launched in 2025, Penn AI is dedicated to advancing responsible artificial intelligence and translating groundbreaking research into real-world impact. The initiative is built around a vision of shaping AI to serve society, ensuring innovation is aligned with ethical principles and human needs. Penn AI is committed to bringing together scholars, practitioners, policymakers, students, and community members across disciplines to address the most pressing challenges and opportunities in AI.
