    Effective January 1, 2025, Policy 217 Student Academic Misconduct was replaced by Policy 216 Student Academic Integrity. The policy, associated procedures, and forms have changed. All faculty and students must follow the new policy. For more information, see the Academic Integrity website: https://www.tntech.edu/provost/academicintegrity/.
