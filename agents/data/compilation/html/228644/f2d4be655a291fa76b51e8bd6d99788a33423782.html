ERMS

Transforming Research Management

ERMS is UT Health San Antonio’s implementation of the Huron Research Suite, a comprehensive software solution designed to relieve administrative burden and free up time for mission-focused activities. ERMS assists in transforming business processes to facilitate exceptional research administration service delivery, increase transparency, and automate workflows.

When implementation is completed, the following modules will be available on a single platform:

  • Agreements (e.g., contracts, MOUs, data use agreements)
  • Conflict of Interest & Conflict of Commitment
  • Institutional Review Board
  • Grants Management
  • Institutional Animal Care & Use Committee (IACUC)
  • Laboratory Animal Management

  • COI & COC Module (Live October 2023)

    Conflict of Interest and Conflict of Commitment module will replace the current iDisclose application. With a modern user interface, this module will improve compliance while reducing the administrative burden. 

    Effective October 26, 2023, all disclosures must be submitted through ERMS COI&COC at https://erms1.uthscsa.edu. 

    If your area or department would like to have representatives speak to use of ERMS COI & COC, please reach out to coi@uthscsa.edu or compliance@uthscsa.edu.

  • IACUC Module (Live October 2024)

    Key Dates to Take Note Of

    • September 6th, 2024
      • Communication of Key Dates
    • Beginning October 14, 2024
      • All IACUC related items will be submitted within ERMS-IACUC.
    • September 10th until October 11th
      • The ARC Office will finalize any outstanding tasks in the current ORCA system.
      • All active IACUC studies involving animals will be migrated to the new ERMS-IACUC module for access by study teams upon launch.
      • Current ORCA Delegates will be assigned as Proxies in ERMS-IACUC with full access and editing privileges available at launch.
    • September 20th
      • Final date for submitting progress reports/triennial reviews and amendments in ORCA.
    • September 10th and October 11th
      • If you have a new study submission, contact the ARC office directly for assistance in entering your study in ERMS prior to launch.
    • Prior to September 20th
      • Any ORCA business items currently in DRAFT status must be submitted.
      • Submissions in DRAFT status will not be transferred to ERMS. Please contact ARC office for assistance.

  • Grants Module (Live October 2025)

    The Enterprise Research Management System – Grants (ERMS-Grants) officially launched on October 1, 2025. This new module streamlines the workflow for managing grant activity—from proposal submission to award management—while serving as the central communication hub for Principal Investigators (PIs), research staff, and the Office of Sponsored Programs (OSP).

    Starting in October, proposal and award requests should be submitted to OSP through ERMS at https://erms1.uthscsa.edu.

    To support the transition, OSP has been providing institution-wide training and has built a number of instructional tools and resources found on our ERMS Grants SharePoint page.

    •  ERMS Grants SharePoint Page 

    For questions or assistance, please contact OSP at grants@uthscsa.edu

  • General FAQs

    ERMS Suite

    • What is the link to ERMS? 
      R: The web address for ERMS is https://erms1.uthscsa.edu 
       
    • Who do I contact if I cannot access ERMS?
      R: Please email vpr-it@uthscsa.edu if you are unable to access ERMS.
       
    • How do I log in if I am a student? 
      R: If you are a student, please be sure to use domainid@uthscsa.edu not @livemail.uthscs.edu and log in with your Canvas password
       
    • Who do I contact if I cannot access ERMS or for technical issues?
      R: Reach out to vpr-it@uthscsa.edu who can provide assistance. They are ready to assist and answer any questions you may have. 
       
    • Who should I contact for help when I am in a module?
      R: Each module has subject matter experts available to answer question relating to each module. Please see the below list of emails:
      ERMS Agreements: contracts@uthscsa.edu
      ERMS Grants: grants@uthscsa.edu 
      ERMS COI-COC: coi@uthscsa.edu 
      ERMS Iacuc:  arc@uthscsa.edu 
      ERMS IRB: irb@uthscsa.edu or irbreliance@uthscsa.edu 
       
    • Is there help in ERMS?
      R: In each module, there is a Help Center tab with documentation for using the ERMS product, in addition to vendor created videos.
