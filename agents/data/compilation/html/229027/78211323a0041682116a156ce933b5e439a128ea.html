Generative AI Peer Learning Network

Tuesday, July 28th
10:00 am - 11:00 am

Are you curious about how generative AI is transforming higher education? Do you want to be at the forefront of these changes at UT San Antonio?

Join the Generative AI Peer Learning Network, a vibrant community dedicated to exploring how these tools can transform teaching and learning. Since its launch in January 2023, the network has become a central hub for faculty and staff from across campus to explore, experiment, and collaborate.

As a member, you’ll have the opportunity to:

  • Discover new teaching strategies that responsibly and effectively integrate AI.
  • Stay ahead of the curve on the latest trends and applications.
  • Contribute to a growing ecosystem that is shaping the future of learning at our university.

This is more than just a network, it’s a space for you to amplify your voice and help guide UT San Antonio’s thoughtful and innovative approach to generative AI. We invite you to join us every last Tuesday of the month and help shape the future.

Creating an effective learning environment for all is important to us. To request an accommodation, please email [email protected] one week prior to your scheduled training.
