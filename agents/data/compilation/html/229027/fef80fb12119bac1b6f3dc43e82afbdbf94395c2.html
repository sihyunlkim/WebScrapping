Artificial Intelligence, Teaching and Learning

Your gateway to discovery, innovation and academic excellence

Artificial intelligence is transforming the way we teach, learn and prepare for the future. At UTSA, you’re not just observing this change—you’re part of it. Whether you’re a student curious about smart tools, a faculty member designing AI-integrated assignments or a higher ed professional seeking a replicable model, we offer the guidance, community and inspiration to help you lead the way. Use this page to find:

  • Opportunities to experiment with AI responsibly and effectively
  • Guidance to build new skills for today’s classrooms and tomorrow’s careers
  • A community of educators, students and researchers working together to do exciting things with AI

Let’s build the future together

You’re not just part of the conversation—you’re helping shape the future of higher education. Let’s discover, experiment and grow together. Have ideas? Want to explore collaboration or get support? Let’s talk.

Faculty AI Resources

When you collaborate with Academic Innovation to leverage artificial intelligence in your teaching and learning practices, you'll get access to hands-on tools and real-world examples, evolving guidance to keep pace with rapid AI developments, guidance on creative exploration and ethical use, and supportive community that encourages curiosity and responsible discovery. Whether you’re just getting started or leading the charge, we’ve got your back!
