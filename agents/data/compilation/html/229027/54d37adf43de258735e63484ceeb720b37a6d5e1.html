Generation AI Summit

Friday, September 13, 2024

9:00 a.m. – 4:00 p.m.

UTSA Main Campus
John Peace Library | Assembly Room | 4.04.22
One UTSA Circle, San Antonio, TX 78249

Please join us for the GenerationAI Summit at The University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA).

UTSA Research is honored that our campus will host the “2024 GenerationAI Summit" in April 2024 under the ORAU Innovation Partnerships Program.

The GenerationAI Summit was developed by the GenerationAI Consortium, a joint initiative between MITRE and UTSA, that aims to broaden the participation of other ORAU member institutions in the GenerationAI Consortium, fostering collaborative efforts to advance AI education across diverse academic disciplines.

The GenerationAI Summit will serve as a dynamic platform to showcase the success and impact of GenerationAI in reaching over 10,000 students since its inception in 2018. The summit will allow AI experts to share artificial intelligence practices, tools, and community. GenerationAI faculty will demonstrate their custom-designed GenerationAI lessons and participants can learn how to incorporate AI, big data analytics, and data visualization lessons into their courses.

Everyone is invited—across disciplines, across industry, across the country.

Click to Register Registration is required by August 30, 2024
