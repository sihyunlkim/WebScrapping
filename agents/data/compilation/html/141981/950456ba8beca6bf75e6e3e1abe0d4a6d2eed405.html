What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is one of several recently unleashed AI chatbots that are impacting higher education. For a baseline understanding of how AI models work, watch Debasis Bhattacharya’s Maui College faculty workshop on the topic. 

Bhattacharya provides an introduction to generative AI, large learning models, and prompt engineering. The recorded session demonstrates ways to converse with AI and includes sample queries for hands-on testing. Deborah Halbert, VP for Academic Strategy, shares syllabi sample statements to address AI content and copyright concerns, as well as other resources (below).
