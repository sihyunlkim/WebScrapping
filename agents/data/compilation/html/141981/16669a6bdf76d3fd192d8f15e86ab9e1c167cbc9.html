UH West Oʻahu Policies, Procedures and Guidelines

 

The University of Hawaiʻi – West Oʻahu adheres to UH Board of Regents policies, UH Executive policies, and UH administrative procedures, as well as federal and state laws and rules.

The UH West Oʻahu Policies Advisory Committee is charged with identifying and located campus policies, procedures, practices, and guidelines into one Policies and Procedures Manual and codifying such policies, procedures, practice, and guidelines using a consistent, uniform methodology.
