Higher Education Emergency Relief Funds (HEERF)

We understand that many of our students are experiencing additional expenses related to the disruption of campus operations as a result of COVID-19. Our Bristol, PA and Blackwood, NJ campuses each received a HEERF grant. This grant was created under the Coronavirus Aid Relief and Economic Security (CARES) Act. These funds will be distributed via a need-based points formula, which will be used to provide relief to our students, and/or their families, affected by the COVID-19 predicament. Eligibility Requirements: As of right now, all enrolled students at Pennco Tech have been placed on an Approved Leave of Absence (LOA). The HEERF provided to Pennco Tech under the CARES Act will be available to Financial Aid eligible, and enrolled (LOA), students only. These students must have a completed and processed Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) on file. If you are eligible and have incurred expenses related to the disruption of campus operations, please complete the steps below to acknowledge receipt of the emergency grant funds.
