Welcome to Pennco Tech in Blackwood, New Jersey, and Bristol, Pennsylvania

Pennco Tech offers robust career-training programs for in-demand professions in healthcare, automotive, and more. With campuses in Blackwood, New Jersey, and Bristol, Pennsylvania, we welcome students who dont see themselves attending a traditional college or university and instead seek hands-on learning for a hands-on trade career.

A Technical School in NJ & PA

Our career-mindedvocational training programs feature hands-on learning in real-world applications, equipping students with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in some of todays most in-demand fields. Led by highly skilled instructors with professional experience in these industries, our trade programs include:

Your Future Begins Today in New Jersey & Pennsylvania

When it comes to your goals for the future, you likely have a strong idea about which direction youd like to go. Yet as you explore how to achieve your ideal lifestyle, it can be difficult to know where to take the first step. Thats where Pennco Tech comes in. If youre searching for a trade school program that can help you achieve your dreams, we invite you tolearn more about Pennco Techand what we have to offer.

Established in 1973, we have been assisting students in formulating a plan for their career and providing the training and networking opportunities they need for success. What Makes Us Unique!

What Makes Us Unique

Pennco Tech is one of the few trade schools to serve as an Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) Authorized Testing Site.Other factors that separate Pennco Tech from other trade schools include:

  • Over 80% of Pennco Tech students receive financial aid
  • We have over 23,200 graduates and counting
  • We offer flexibility with day and evening class schedules
  • Our Blackwood campus was recognized by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges as a 2024-2025 school of distinction

Proud to Serve the Military and Veterans

Our trade school is committed to offering educational assistance to military members, veterans, and spouses of veterans. Through the GI Bill* and MyCAA programs, were proud to support the career dreams of our nations finest and bravest. Learn more about veteran financial aid and other assistance services available to those eligible at Pennco Tech.

*GI Bill is a registered trademark of the U.S Department of Veterans Affairs (USDVA).
