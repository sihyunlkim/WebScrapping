Vocational Training in Blackwood, NJ

Pennco Tech has been providing quality post-secondary training in Blackwood, NJ since 1973. Our programs are designed to produce experienced professionals who are ready to enter fields of high demand and thus improve their overall career readiness. Throughout their training, our students will receive individualized attention and support from our trained faculty and staff to achieve their overall career goals.

As an accredited school by the Accredited Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC), our Blackwood campus has proven itself as a quality source of education. Our warm and welcoming atmosphere ensures an ideal trade school environment for our students and has earned us the ACCSC School of Distinction Award and School of Excellence Award. Start yourself down the path to career excellence in the vocation of your choice by enrolling at Pennco Tech of Blackwood, NJ today.

Blackwood, NJ Campus Features

  • Located in Blackwood, NJ in Camden County off of Rt. 42
  • Minutes away from Rt. 295 and downtown Philadelphia
  • Situated on 12 acres with more than 124,708 sq. ft. of campus floor space
  • Daytime and evening classes are available
  • Career Advisement
  • Trained Financial Aid Advisors
  • Free parking
  • Access to public transportation

The Blackwood campus offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere matched with its convenience, accessibility, and a quality learning environment that sets Pennco Tech apart from other trade schools.
