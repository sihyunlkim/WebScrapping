Assess Students and Not ChatGPT: Strategies to Avoid Cheating

Discover how to harness Generative AI (GenAI) while safeguarding the authenticity of student assessment. *Registration is open to the public.
March 14, 2024
1:00 pm - 2:00 pm
Online
View the Recording
Share
Image

This event is open to the public.

Discover how to harness Generative AI (GenAI) while safeguarding the authenticity of student assessment. 

This presentation explores methods to assess learning outcomes effectively while incorporating GenAI use. Concrete examples and strategies for designing GenAI-powered assessments will be shared. 

Join RIDIL at the intersection of education and AI to create assessments that engage critical thinking skills to measure students' abilities connected to targeted learning outcomes.

Presented by Helen Crompton, RIDIL
