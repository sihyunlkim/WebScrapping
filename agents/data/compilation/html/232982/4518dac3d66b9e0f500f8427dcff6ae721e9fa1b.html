Reimagining Assignments in Higher Education: A Framework for the Generative AI Era

Presented by Helen Crompton, RIDIL

Generative AI has transformed the educational landscape, presenting both challenges and opportunities for higher education. Traditional assignment models often struggle to adapt to this new reality, necessitating innovative approaches that embrace AI's potential while upholding academic integrity. This webinar introduces a practical framework for designing assignments that leverage generative AI to foster critical thinking.

This event is open to the public, and registration is handled via the Zoom platform.
