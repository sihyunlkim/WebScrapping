Introducing MonarchMind: ODU's advanced, self-hosted AI interface

February 4, 2025
share

We are excited to announce the launch of MonarchMind, ODU’s AI-powered platform designed to enhance student support, improve faculty productivity, and create efficiencies across the university community – all while prioritizing ethical AI use and bias mitigation. 

What is MonarchMind? 

MonarchMind is a secure, AI-driven interface that integrates multiple large language models (LLMs), including OpenAI's ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Meta's Llama 3. By combining these models, MonarchMind offers a unified platform where users can effortlessly generate text, analyze data, and process images or files. Committed to responsible AI usage, ODU ensures that MonarchMind's insights are ethical, inclusive, and fair, with built-in safeguards for accuracy, transparency, and equity. 

Who can use MonarchMind? 

MonarchMind is now available to all faculty and staff at ODU. Simply visit monarchmind.odu.edu and log in with your MIDAS ID to get started. Faculty members interested in integrating MonarchMind into AI-related courses can apply for early access as part of our course pilot program. Request a pilot spot here. 

Here are just a few ways you can take advantage of the platform: 

  • Faculty members: Leverage the platform to design course content, create quizzes and assessments, draft syllabi or brainstorm new curriculum ideas. Prepare study guides for students to help them gain a deeper understanding of complex topics. 

  • Researchers: Streamline workflows by conducting web-based literature searches and crafting compelling presentations. Summarize academic papers, automate literature reviews, or generate research ideas and hypotheses. 

  • Advisors: Use MonarchMind to create personalized plans for students, complete with summaries for future reference. 

  • Staff, administrators, and anyone: Proofread and edit documents for clarity or interpret data and craft reports. Generate meeting summaries, task lists and drafts. 

The applications are limitless! 

Why MonarchMind Matters? 

Unlike many publicly available AI tools, MonarchMind operates within a closed system exclusive to ODU. All data is encrypted, stored in-house and never shared externally or used to train foundation models. This ensures that intellectual property and personal information remain protected while you explore AI’s full potential. 

Furthermore, our approach to AI is grounded in ethics and responsibility. We are committed to ensuring data security and fostering an AI-literate campus where technology is used thoughtfully and equitably. 

MonarchMind is more than a tool – it is an asset designed to empower the Monarch community with practical AI skills and prepare everyone on campus to thrive in an increasingly AI-driven workforce. 

Get started 

Discover how MonarchMind can transform the way you teach, research, and work at ODU. 

  • Log in to MonarchMind at monarchmind.odu.edu with your MIDAS ID. 

  • Explore resources and FAQs at odu.edu/monarchmind. 

Explore more AI resources at ODU 

Beyond MonarchMind, ODU is leading AI innovation across multiple disciplines, offering a broad range of AI research, training, and strategic initiatives.  

  • ODU AI Hub – Explore our dedicated AI website with insights into ODU’s AI strategy, ethical AI framework, and applications across teaching, research, and operations. Discover More: ODU AI Hub  

  • AI Training & Workshops – Enhance your AI literacy with faculty development programs, hands-on workshops, and specialized AI courses designed for educators, researchers, and staff. These include the AI Learning Series, which features expert-led discussions and training sessions on AI’s evolving role in higher education, research, and administrative workflows. For a full list of upcoming AI training sessions and events, visit AI Training & Events Page  

We look forward to seeing how MonarchMind empowers ODU faculty and staff to explore AI’s full potential!
