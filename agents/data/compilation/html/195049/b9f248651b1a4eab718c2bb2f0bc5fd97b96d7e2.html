Scientific Resource Centers

Many of the best tools in biology are too expensive, too complex, or simply too big to house in a single lab. To make such equipment available – and to provide the expertise needed to maximize its utility – the university’s laboratories enjoy access to more than a dozen shared resource centers that provide convenient, affordable access to key technologies and services.
