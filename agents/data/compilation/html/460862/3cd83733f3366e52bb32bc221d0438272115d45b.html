Apply Now

Click on the image to download your application. Fill out all the information and send it to the campus location of your choice.  Please note, leaving any information out will delay the process of enrollment.
 

Online Application Steps:

1. Download PDF

2. Fill in information. 

3. Save PDF 

4. Email PDF to Admissions Team or print and bring
copy with you to your appointment:

Pearl Admissions: [email protected]
Vancouver Admissions: [email protected]

5. Signatures and $20 application fee will be signed and collected in person when you come for a tour/appointment.

(This application will not be processed until
we collect the $20 application fee)

6. Set a tour/appointment with admissions.

Documents Required 

  • Completed Application
  • Copy of State or Government Issued ID
  • Copy of Social Security Card or
    Permanent Resident Card
  • Copy of High School Diploma, Official High School Transcripts, or GED Certificate (Please note - Home School Diplomas/Certificates are not accepted)
  • $20 Non-Refundable Application Fee (Check made out to Aveda Institute Portland)

Once we have received all the correct information we will contact you with more information on how to
continue the Enrollment Process.
