Our Aveda Certified Educators

Educating bright, talented, and creative Cosmetology and Esthiology students would not be possible without our experienced and passionate team of Aveda Certified Educators.  Our educators strive to create a learning environment where all students feel comfortable discovering their creative spark, all while becoming well-rounded individuals focused on connecting beauty, professionalism, and well-being.
