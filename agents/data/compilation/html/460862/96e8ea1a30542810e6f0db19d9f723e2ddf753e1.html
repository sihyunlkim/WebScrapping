Schedule a Campus Visit

Are you ready to take the first step toward your future in the beauty industry? At Aveda Institute Portland, we make it easy to explore our campuses and discover how our Portland beauty academies can help you achieve your goals. Scheduling a personalized tour at one of our locations—Portland, Oregon, or Vancouver, Washington—gives you the opportunity to see what sets us apart from other trade schools in Portland.

During your visit, you’ll meet with one of our friendly admissions representatives, who will guide you through everything you need to know about our accredited cosmetology programs and other career-focused beauty education options. You’ll get a behind-the-scenes look at our state-of-the-art facilities, learn about student life, and gain valuable insights into what it means to be part of the Aveda family.

Whether you’re curious about financial aid opportunities, program schedules, or how we integrate sustainability and eco-conscious practices into every aspect of our education, we’re here to answer all your questions. This is your chance to get a feel for our supportive campus culture and explore how we prepare students for successful careers in beauty and wellness.

We can’t wait to show you around and help you start your journey toward a career you’ll love!

Amazing careers start with Aveda

An education from Aveda Institute Portland is more than just learning—it’s about building the foundation for a rewarding career. Our alumni are making waves in the beauty industry, from thriving as salon owners and freelance artists to working in fashion, film, and more. During your campus visit, you’ll see firsthand how our career-focused beauty education can empower you to achieve success.

What to Expect During Your Visit

Embarking on a campus tour is a pivotal step in your journey toward a career in the beauty industry. During your personalized visit, you will:

  • Meet Our Admissions Team: Engage with our friendly admissions representatives who will provide detailed insights into our programs and answer any questions you may have.

  • Explore Our Facilities: Walk through our classrooms, salons, and spa areas where hands-on learning comes to life.

  • Experience Student Life: Observe current students in action and get a feel for the collaborative and vibrant community at Aveda Institute Portland.

  • Get ready to learn about Financial Aid Options: If you haven't already, fill out your FAFSA so our Finance Team can meet with you and share your financial options for schooling.

Preparing for Your Visit

To make the most of your campus tour:

  • Bring Necessary Documents: If you're ready to begin the enrollment process, consider bringing your ID, high school diploma or GED, and any relevant financial documents. This preparation can help streamline your application process.

  • Prepare Questions: Think about what matters most to you in your education and career. Preparing questions in advance ensures you gather all the information you need.

  • Consider Bringing a Guest: Feel free to bring a parent, mentor, or friend for support and to help you evaluate your options.

Why Choose Aveda Institute Portland?

At Aveda Institute Portland, we are committed to providing:

  • Accredited Cosmetology Programs: Our programs are designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge necessary for a successful career in the beauty industry.

  • Career-Focused Education: We emphasize practical experience and industry-relevant training to prepare you for real-world opportunities.

  • Eco-Conscious Practices: As part of the Aveda brand, we integrate sustainability and eco-friendly practices into our curriculum and daily operations.
