Admissions Process

1. Complete a tour with our admissions team. Schedule your tour here!

This is the first step in our process! It's a wonderful opportunity to tell us more about yourself, see our space, ask questions, and ensure that we are exactly what you are looking for in a beauty school! 

2. Complete a simple online application during your tour.

You'll want to be prepared with:

  • $20 non-refundable application fee (valid for two start dates within a year)
  • Current state or government-issued ID
  • Signed social security card
  • High school diploma, official transcript, or GED certificate

3. We encourage you to apply for financial aid through FAFSA.

Visit the FAFSA website at www.studentaid.gov using our school code: 041714. You may be eligible for more than you think! We also offer payment plans.

 
4. Meet with the financial aid and enrollment team.

You will finalize your schedule, go over FAFSA results and payment options, pay your enrollment fee ($100 for WA or $150 for OR), and save your seat in the start date of your choosing.

5. Attend orientation and begin your journey towards an exciting new career

Preparing for you visit

To make the most of your campus tour:

  • Bring Necessary Documents: If you're ready to begin the enrollment process, consider bringing your ID, high school diploma or GED, and any relevant financial documents. This preparation can help streamline your application process.

  • Prepare Questions: Think about what matters most to you in your education and career. Preparing questions in advance ensures you gather all the information you need.

  • Consider Bringing a Guest: Feel free to bring a parent, mentor, or friend for support and to help you evaluate your options.
