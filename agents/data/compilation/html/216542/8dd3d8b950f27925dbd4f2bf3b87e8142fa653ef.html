UVF Login Instructions

Ready to Start your Journey?

You should have received your UVF login credentials from your counselor.

Student Email Account

Go to Outlook to access your UVF email account.


You will be prompted for Multi-Factor-Authentication the first time you login. You will need to download the Microsoft Authenticator app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android). 


Your UVF email includes access to Microsoft Office applications, OneDrive and Teams.


Access Email

myValleyForge Portal

Log into your myValleyForge portal.


This where you can find academic information, registration, financial aid, billing, degree progress and access to many other UVF resources. 


Please note that your username for myValleyForge does not include the "@valleyforge.edu", but will use the same password as your email.


Access myValleyForge

Canvas

Canvas is our Learning Management System that allows you to access your course content, assignments, grades, and interact with faculty outside of the classroom.


Your Canvas credentials are the same as your Email login information.


Have a placement exam? Here are detailed instructions!
