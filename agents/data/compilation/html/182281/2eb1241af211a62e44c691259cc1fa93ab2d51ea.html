AI in the Classroom: A Virtual Summit - 2025

Welcome to the second annual AI in the Classroom: A Virtual Summit, hosted by UNLV and UNR. This free, virtual event brings together educators from across Nevada’s higher education community to explore the practical, creative, and ethical dimensions of artificial intelligence in teaching and learning. We’re delighted you’re here to be part of these important conversations shaping the future of education.

Please see the full schedule below.
