Office of Medical Education

The office of medical education at Albert Einstein College of Medicine develops, plans, and implements a world class undergraduate medical education curriculum developed and designed by the M.D. Curriculum Committee, Einsteins central curriculum oversight committee. We manage student assessment, program evaluation, paradigms for teaching and learning, and continuous quality improvement intitiatives to assess our progress. We also provide the resources necessary to assure the success of our teaching faculty.
