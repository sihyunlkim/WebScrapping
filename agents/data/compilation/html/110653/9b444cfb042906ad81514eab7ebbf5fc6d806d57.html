ScholarConnect

By UCI, For UCI

ScholarConnect uses ZotGPT's API to turn faculty CVs into data: data that we can use to update faculty profiles and highlight their research, teaching and service activities.

  • Keeps faculty profiles up-to-date while saving faculty time.
  • Helps people from outside of UC Irvine find and connect with our faculty.
  • Facilitates collaboration and resource allocation across departments.
Learn more

ZotDesk

By UCI, For UCI

ZotDesk is an AI-powered chatbot designed to provide 24/7 IT support for the UCI community.

  • Offers instant answers to a wide range of IT-related questions
  • Available round-the-clock for convenient assistance
  • Streamlines the process of resolving common tech issues
