Policy Central

Easy Access to Policy Changes

Policy Central is a database tool designed to provide easy access to policy changes in the NSHE Board of Regents Handbook. This searchable database gives individuals the ability to quickly identify policy modifications and additions that have been approved by the Board of Regents, as well as a reference to source documents that contain a history of changes to systemwide policies.

The information found within this application is maintained by the NSHE Office of Academic and Student Affairs. The database is updated immediately following each Board of Regents meeting and contains a history of policy changes dating back to January 2004.
