Our Mission

Our mission is to provide quality accessible education to students in pursuit of a technical career.

Our Vission

Our vision is to be recognized as an institution that prepares highly qualified students for immediate employment in technical occupations.

Ruben's Five Star Academy is an uprising institution in Central Florida with the goal to establish professionalism within our student's lives. With the help of our well-experienced and knowledgeable staff, we are sharing the latest and most trendy styles of hair and beauty. Not only will the student be trained to pass Florida's State Board Exam but to also handle clients in a place of business and grow with their abilities. The academy is the place where one determines their path of future and success in a certain field, this is where beauty specialists will see a lifestyle beyond the chair.

"Discipline is a requirement of success." _Ruben Javier

Ruben Javier

Founder

Ruben is a sponsored platform artist and educator. Skilled in artistic trades such as drawing, tattooing, and hair designs. With eight years of barbering experience, he is often invited to present at beauty shows and seminars across the country. Ruben is an award winning barber who has judged at several barber competitions.
