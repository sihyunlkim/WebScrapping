Presented by Dr. Hassan Mousaid, Principal SA at Amazon (AWS), this event is ideal for developers, data scientists, AI enthusiasts, and business leaders interested in leveraging generative AI to enhance their applications and workflows. If you're involved in creating content or managing AI-driven projects, this event will provide valuable insights into how Bedrock can simplify and accelerate your work. You'll gain a comprehensive understanding of how Bedrock can streamline the development and deployment of generative AI applications, offering both flexibility and control to meet your unique needs.
