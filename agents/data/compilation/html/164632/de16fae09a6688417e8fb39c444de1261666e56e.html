At Bay Path, we help you claim your education.

It starts with your relationship with your Bay Path professor—a highly personal, highly focused partnership where we blend the classroom (picture online, or even on the lawn of Bay Path on a sunny day) with experiences that take you out of your comfort zone.

  • Participate in a project the provides clothing for the Home for the Homeless Shelter
  • Talk with an FBI intelligence analyst who will bring you up-to-date on the latest developments in the cyber wars
  • Develop a business plan that will change how your organization or company delivers a product or service

Be prepared to take part. Your education begins here.
