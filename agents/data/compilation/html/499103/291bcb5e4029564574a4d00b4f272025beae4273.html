Dear Professor Ryan,

From this class I was able to gain a much deeper understanding of the beefits and drawbacks of relying on technology to communicate. I communicate with my peers and family through the use of technology a lot more than I was previously aware, and I realize now that although it may not seem as sincere as writing a hand-written letter, it allows me to keep up-to-date with the lives of my family members as well as keep in constant contact with those that i care about. However, in a professional setting, the information I intend to convey may not be communicated as effectively as I would have wanted. Therefore, any vitally important information that must be passed on to my peers or team, should be communicated through the form of a face-to-face meeting as much as possible as that ensures the recipient has received the information, and that I am there to clear up any misunderstandings as soon as they arise. What I have learned here will not only benefit me in my professional career, but has also taught me to consider situations from diffrent perspectives which is paramount for me to provide the best care I can as a nurse for my patients.

Thank you for bringing this to our attetion, and teaching us to think critically.

Sincerely,
