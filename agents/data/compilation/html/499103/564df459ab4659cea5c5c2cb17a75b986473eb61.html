Homestead Schools, Inc.

Homestead Schools, Inc., a private non-profit post-secondary institution formed in 1991, is an organization dedicated to meeting the educational needs of multidisciplinary groups. Homestead Schools, Inc. started offering training in vocational nursing in 2007 after getting approval from the California Board of Vocational Nursing and Psychiatric Technicians. Since then the school has added programs in medical assisting, nurse assisting, home health aide and RN-BSN. Homestead Schools, Inc. is institutionally accredited by ABHES for these programs

Homestead Schools, Inc. new branch location located in a premium office building, and you’ll feel like you’re entering a 5-star hotel with a large reception room, a concierge and a receptionist to welcome you. There are picnic tables downstairs at the entrance of the building if you wish to enjoy your lunch in the open sunny area. To top it all off, there’s a modern fully equipped gym to work out all your stress. We have spared no expense to make your education experience as pleasant as possible.
