Teaching Lab — Integrating GenAI into Writing Teaching: Exploring assignments and activities

Time

Wednesday, September 03 2025 from 1:10pm to 2:00pm

Location

Ross 212

This session, led by Angela Hakim & Jessica Jorgenson Borchert, will explore practical ways to engage Generative AI in your classes. They’ll share examples of integrating AI into course assignments and activities, along with resources such as sample activities and bibliographies for further reading.

Graduate students are especially welcome to attend—whether you’re teaching now or preparing to teach in the future, this is a great chance to pick up strategies, ideas, and resources you can carry into your own classrooms.

Looking forward to seeing you there!
