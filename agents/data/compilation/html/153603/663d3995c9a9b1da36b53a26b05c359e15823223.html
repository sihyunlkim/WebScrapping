Complaint Process

If you, a colleague, participant, or partner witnesses, experiences discrimination or harassment, regardless of who is alleged action is from, please be sure to report that behavior to an appropriate source including:

  • Supervisor or Supervisor's Supervisor;
  • University Human Resources;
  • The Office of Equal Opportunity; or
  • The United States Department of Agriculture

It is imperative that all discriminatory or harassing behavior be reported so that appropriate support is provided to the impacted party.
