Using Generative AI in the Workplace

This interactive workshop introduces participants to the fundamentals of generative artificial intelligence (AI) and explores its potential impact in today’s workplace. Through hands-on activities and real-world examples, participants will discover practical applications for generative AI and discuss important considerations for ethical and responsible use.

Participants will be able to:

  • Explain how generative AI can be integrated into current practice.
  • Evaluate the potential benefits and challenges of using generative AI.
  • Make informed decisions about when and how to use generative AI resources.

Course Information

  Time Requirement

This is a 2 hour face-to-face or virtual workshop. Alternative session lengths may be available upon request.
 

  Cost to Participants

$115 per person. Fee is waived for ISU Extension and Outreach campus or county-paid staff, elected council members, and programming volunteers (e.g. Master Gardener, 4-H) as well as Iowa State University faculty and staff. Questions or group rate inquiries should be directed to the course facilitator.
