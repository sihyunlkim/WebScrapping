Role and mission

The Office of the Senior Vice President and Provost, reporting to the President, is responsible for Iowa State University's land-grant academic mission in education, discovery, and service.

The office manages and supports:

  • assessment, accreditation, and continuous quality improvement in Iowa State's programs across 60 academic departments
  • planning, budgeting, policy, and efficiency initiatives throughout the division
  • management of faculty appointments, promotions, advancements, tenure decisions, and professional development programs
  • new and transfer student admissions, student financial aid, and the office of the University Registrar; new and continuing student programs, including New Student Orientation, Destination Iowa State, and commencement

Review the Division of Academic Affairs Strategic Plan to learn more about how we Educate, Innovate, and Serve.
