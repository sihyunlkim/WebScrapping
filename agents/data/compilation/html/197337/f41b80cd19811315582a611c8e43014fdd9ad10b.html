Our Company

Our school opened in 1959 with its origins being the hairdressing experience of my father-in-law. It has since continued to operate as a family owned cosmetology school among the beauty schools Westchester NY, offering programs on hairdressing and cosmetology classes. We are an accredited school that also offers cosmetology services, including hair styling, makeup, basic manicure/pedicure, waxing, and more.

We are not a big corporation and we have chosen to remain so over the past decades because we want to continue offering a friendly, warm, neighborhood-type of service to our students and customers. As one of the long-time cosmetology and hair schools Westchester NY, we will continue to provide the kind of personalized service that has earned us decades of loyalty from our customers.

Since we started, we have trained more than 3,000 people who went on to work as cosmetologists in some of the biggest salons or open their own salons in New York, California, Florida, and around the world. Others have chosen to become nail technicians, beauty consultants, platform artists, product representatives, trainers, makeup artists, and teachers. But they all have one thing in common – the acknowledgment of the completeness of the courses we are offering, which has made us one of the most reliable cosmetology schools Westchester NY.

Our 7,500 square feet of school area that boasts of four theory/lab rooms, administrative offices, a senior area, reception areas, storage areas, and a student lounge. The entire area is also designed to accommodate persons with disability.

Our Mission

Establish yourself as a beauty specialist by finishing a program here at Westchester School of Beauty Culture and learn from a school that has been around for over 50 years.

If you want a career in the beauty industry to make people look beautiful and feel good about themselves, finishing one or more of our courses here at Westchester School of Beauty, one of the longest-running cosmetology schools Westchester NY, can help put you on that career path. You can sign up for our courses and wait for our admission every second Monday of every month. You can study full-time, quarter-time or part-time with us with our flexible schedule.

Our most popular course is hairdressing/cosmetology among our beauty schools Westchester NY courses that include lectures and demos with hands-on experience at our on-site clinic that caters to the public. It can help you pass the State Board Test that you will need to get your license and finally start a career in the beauty industry. This course covers topics such as hair styling, hair cutting and shaping, hair analysis, hair coloring, shampoos and other treatments, hair and scalp disorder treatment, safety and health requirements, nail care, skin care, chemistry in cosmetology, chemical restructuring, and anatomy and physiology. You need to spend 1,040 hours of instruction, usually covering 9 to 13 months, depending on your schedule.

Our other course on esthetics covers topics such as makeup techniques, facial and body procedures, skin structure and functions, paramedical esthetic, bacteriology, and more. This course runs for 600 hours of instruction, usually covering around 5 to 10 months, based on your preferred schedule.

What makes our cosmetology and hair schools Westchester NY stand out is that we offer free lifetime employment counseling service to nudge you to the right direction in your career. Moreover, many of our graduates who have started their own salons usually get new employees from our graduates.

For our beauty clinic, we offer a discount to senior citizens from Monday through Wednesday. We also show how we treasure our veterans by giving them a free haircut.
