Hello, We're Glad You're Here.

What Moves Us

Are you looking for a career in the fast-paced and exciting beauty industry?

Do you get excited when you think about hair, skin and nails?

Have you always wanted to be a hairdresser, hair stylist, beautician or cosmetologist but didn’t know where to start?

Look no further. For more than 50 years, the Westchester School of Beauty Culture has been training students in the art and science of haircutting, coloring, facial esthetic services, makeup, nail artistry and nail extensions.

Who we are

A Quick History of The School

Westchester School of Beauty Culture has a reputation that’s second to none when it comes to training students to work in the “real world.” The school was established in 1960 in Lower Westchester by Michael P. Salamone to train students in the art and craft of Hairdressing, Esthetics and nail technology. Since then, the school has trained more than 3,000 people as cosmetologists. While some students are working in some of the leading salons in Westchester, New York, Florida, California and throughout the world, others have created their own vision by opening their own salon (or salons as the case may be).

What we do

Westchester Beauty School has trained thousands of people from New York City, Westchester, Putnam and Rockland counties, Connecticut and New Jersey to cut and style hair.

We offer day, night and Saturday hours. Our flexible full and part-time schedules let you continue working in your current job until you’re ready to begin your new career. With some dedication and hard work, you could be a professional cosmetologist in just under nine months.

Experience you can trust

Free Career Help

While success is up to you, we offer a free lifetime employment counseling service to point you in the right direction and help you get the job you’ve always wanted.

Many of our graduates have opened their own salons, while others have become award-winning stylists in New York, California, Atlanta, Florida and other exciting cities in the USA and around the world.

IT'S IN YOUR HANDS

If you’ve got the determination and willingness to learn, the Westchester School of Beauty Culture will turn your dreams into reality. Regardless of your education or job history, our flexible training programs will let you train as a cosmetologist during the day, or at night on Saturdays. You choose the hours that suit you.
