Faculty,

This helpguide provides links to resources and tutorials to assist you in your course delivery. This guide will be updated as new resources are made available. Pages labeled with  will open a new tab so you can always navigate back here.

Please know every effort is being made to support student learning. We have many resources and links to assist students and faculty.

  • Library and ASC staff (and peer tutors) are available on business days.
  • 24/7 Tutoring may be accessed via D2L courses (D2L navigation bar).
  • The ASC is available to do Writing Consultations for students.
  • Helpful tutorials are available for D2L help, using the catalog/databases, and more.

Please contact Academic Affairs or Learning Resources if you have questions about any of these resources or need assistance.

Have a resource you think other instructors would benefit from? We can add it to this guide!
