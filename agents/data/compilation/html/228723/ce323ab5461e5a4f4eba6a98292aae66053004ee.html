    Artificial Intelligence - Approved Tools

    Overview

    The Texas A&M University System published System Policy 29.01.05 - Artificial Intelligence on December 10, 2024. Section 6 of this policy states "AI must comply with all applicable data privacy and security laws; system policies, regulations and standards, and member rules and security controls." This means that artificial intelligence tools must be reviewed and approved by TAMU IT Security.

    A list of approved cloud services, including AI tools, is available at https://tools.it.tamu.edu/community/tools/reviewed-cloud-services.php. For questions regarding the use of any cloud service or AI tool not listed on that page, please email helpdesk@tamu.edu.
