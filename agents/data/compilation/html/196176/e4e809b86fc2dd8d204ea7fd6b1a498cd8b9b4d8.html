Accessibility

SUNY EIT Accessibility

The SUNY Electronic and Information Technology (EIT) Accessibility Policy (adopted June 2019) recognizes the importance of accessibility in the areas of electronic information tools and resources and is consistent with the SUNY commitment to diversity and inclusion. Developing out of this policy is the Final Report and Recommendations of the SUNY Electronic & Information Technology (EIT) Accessibility Committee, which provides guidelines and standards in the following areas:

  • Web Accessibility
  • Digital Content Accessibility
  • Classroom Accessibility
  • Library Accessibility
  • Procurement Accessibility.

At SUNY New Paltz, we have aligned our campus accessibility standards with the SUNY Policy and the Final Report to ensure that we provide the most accessible EIT services to our students, faculty, and staff, as well as to all members of the public who utilize our services or programming; you can view our campus policy for more information on our commitment.
