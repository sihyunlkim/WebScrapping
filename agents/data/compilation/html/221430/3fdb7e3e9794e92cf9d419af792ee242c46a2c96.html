    TCAT Crump Partners to offer Google Career Certificates

    TCAT Crump is excited to participate in the new Tennessee Board of Regents (TBR) and Google partnership that will help students earn Google Career Certificates in high-growth fields. Students will also be offered the companys new Google AI Essentials course, designed and taught by AI experts at Google to teach foundational skills in artificial intelligence technology. 

    (Pictured: Rob Magliaro, Google Workforce Partnerships Lead, and TBR Chancellor, Dr. Flora Tydings sign the partnership agreement while State Senator Dawn White of Murfreesboro and Motlow State Community College President, Dr. Michael Torrence observe.)
