Unlock the potential of AI in education! Join Tim Mousel for an engaging presentation, "From Concept to Chatbot: Enhancing Education with Custom AI Tools." Discover how to move beyond basic chatbot interactions and leverage advanced techniques to create truly impactful learning experiences. This session covers practical strategies for prompt engineering, designing effective AI-powered assignments, utilizing tools like Gemini, Claude, and Poe, and exploring real-world applications such as conversational debates, personalized tutoring, and accessible learning aids. Learn how to create, implement, and assess custom chatbots that will improve student engagement and save you time!
