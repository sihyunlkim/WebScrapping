Information for Prospective Students

Join the ecosystem of innovation and progress in health care at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai. Our facilities and renowned faculty provide a dynamic learning environment to pursue your degree and nurture your personal growth. Through science-based education, immersive training, and multidisciplinary research, we prepare students for careers where they can effect change in patient care, biotechnology, education, basic and clinical research, public health, management, and more.

The Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai's approach to education is rooted preparing students for success in today’s health care landscape. You will learn both abstract concepts and real-world applications to eliminate health care disparities, conduct legacy research, implement systemic change, and become a leader in your field.

Explore the resources below for insights on everyday life at the Icahn School of Medicine.

At the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, your experience is just as important as your studies. We encourage our students to take advantage of the unparalleled opportunities, activities, and resources here.

Positioned within one of the world's most celebrated health systems, the Mount Sinai Phillips School of Nursing develops aspiring nurses into healthcare leaders. Students at the Mount Sinai Phillips School of Nursing gain exposure to rare and complex cases that many nursing programs simply cannot offer, from pediatric metabolic disorders to advanced cardiac procedures at the renowned Fuster Heart Hospital.
