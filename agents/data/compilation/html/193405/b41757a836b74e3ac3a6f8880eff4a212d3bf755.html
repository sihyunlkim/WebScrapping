The AI and Emerging Technologies in Medicine multidisciplinary training area provides a diverse range of research avenues focused on innovative technologies in clinical applications and drug discovery. Scholars will learn under esteemed faculty and delve into the facets of machine learning and virtual reality simulation technologies, both geared towards enhancing clinical practices and revolutionizing the process of drug development.

Meet the Co-Directors

Ipek Ensari, PhD

Assistant Professor, Artificial Intelligence and Human Health

Dr. Ensari investigates mobile health (mHealth) and machine learning methods for complex patient-generated data toward improving chronic disease characterization and patient self-management.

View Profile
Alan C Seifert, PhD

Assistant Professor, Diagnostic, Molecular and Interventional Radiology

Dr. Seifert’s research centers on the physiologically relevant magnetic resonance imaging signals that are challenging to detect, and developing imaging methods that can quantify these signals.
