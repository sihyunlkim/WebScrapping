Center for Innovation in Teaching & Learning (CITL) hosts Fall Into AI 2025 at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

Posted on October 23, 2025October 23, 2025 by Shannon Bradley

The Center for Innovation in Teaching & Learning (CITL) is hosting the Fall Into AI at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Throughout this Fall series faculty and staff will have the opportunity to participate in hands-on workshops, guest talks, and conversations on how generative AI impacts the future of education and beyond. Make sure you subscribe to their newsletter to stay informed about all things generative AI.
