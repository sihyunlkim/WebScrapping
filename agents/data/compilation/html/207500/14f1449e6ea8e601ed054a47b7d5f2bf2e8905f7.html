Discover the Possibilities of Generative AI

  • Share This: Share Discover the Possibilities of Generative AI on FacebookShare Discover the Possibilities of Generative AI on LinkedInShare Discover the Possibilities of Generative AI on X

Instructor: Ashley Kennedy

Generative AI is transforming how we create, communicate, and collaborate—across industries and disciplines. In this quick-start course, Ashley Kennedy demystifies the world of generative AI, explaining how it works, how it’s trained, and how it’s reshaping everything from content creation to business strategy. Explore real-world examples of AI-generated text, images, video, and audio, and learn how to engage with these tools through thoughtful prompting and iteration. Ashley also unpacks key topics like multimodal AI, agentic systems, ethical considerations, and the evolving role of humans in an AI-driven world. Whether you’re looking to boost creativity, streamline your workflow, or simply stay informed, this course will help you understand the power—and responsibility—of working alongside AI.
