Powering Education

The IT Division is dedicated to empowering a thriving academic environment for students and faculty. Ensuring seamless access to technology resources, the foundation of your learning experience. This allows faculty to confidently deliver their courses, and students to actively engage in their studies. We support your success.

Technology for Healing

The IT Division plays an essential role in our mission to deliver the best possible healthcare. We achieve this by providing secure and reliable technology infrastructure that supports all aspects of patient care.
