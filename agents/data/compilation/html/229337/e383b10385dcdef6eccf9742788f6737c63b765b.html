General TTUHSC Policy Information

TAC 206 Required Resource and Informational Links

All official TTUHSC web sites and/or web pages must comply with the Texas Administrative Code § 206 - State Web Sites. TAC 206 requires that home pages and key entry points on a state agency web site provide the following links to State of Texas resources and agency information:

  • Texas home page
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (b)(1)(A)
  • Link Policy
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (b)(1)(B)
  • Statewide Search
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (b)(1)(C)
  • Privacy & Security Policy
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.72 (b)(2)(A)
  • Accessibility Policy
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.70 (b)(2)(B)
  • Agency Contact Information
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (b)(2)(C)
  • Description of the agency's Open Records/Public Information Act policy/procedures
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (b)(2)(D)
  • Compact with Texans
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (b)(2)(E)
  • Agency Home Page
    TAC Title 1 Part 10 Chapter § 206 Rule 206.5 (c)(2)

Other Footer Information
In September 2002, the Governor's Office announced the launch of a Texas Homeland Security web site which provides Texans with links to specialized homeland defense-related Internet resources operate by state agencies and the federal government. The Governor's office has requested that all state agency web sites link to the new Internet site.
