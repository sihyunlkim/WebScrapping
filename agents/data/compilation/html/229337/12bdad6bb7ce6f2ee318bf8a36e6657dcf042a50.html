Looking to improve your skills?

Texas Tech University offers a variety of free online training resources for faculty, staff, and students. To access trainings, follow the links below and log in using your eRaider account.

Trainings through Udemy

Udemy is an online learning platform that offers a wide variety of courses on many different topics. Here's a breakdown of what Udemy is all about:

  • Marketplace for learning: Think of it as an online marketplace where instructors create and sell courses, and students can enroll to learn new skills or improve existing ones.
  • Course variety: The course library is massive, with over 220,000 courses on pretty much any subject you can imagine, from programming and data science to business, arts, health, and even personal development.
  • Learning format: Courses are primarily delivered through video lectures, but instructors can also include downloadable resources like code samples, presentations, and PDFs to supplement the learning.
  • Learner benefits: Students can access the courses on their own schedule and pace, making it a flexible learning option. Upon completion, you can also earn a certificate of completion for each course.
