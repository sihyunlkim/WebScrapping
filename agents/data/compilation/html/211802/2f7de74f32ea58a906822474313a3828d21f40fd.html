About Conemaugh Medical Residency

The first medical training program at Conemaugh Memorial began in 1915 with three interns who were training for a one-year period. One of them, B.A. Braude, MD, was elected president of the Conemaugh Valley Memorial Hospital Medical Staff in 1938. While the original medical training program ended in 1975, others began. Today, Conemaugh Memorial residency programs include Emergency Medicine, Family Medicine, General Surgery, Internal Medicine, Pharm D, and Psychology, with preliminary year residencies in Internal Medicine and Surgery Residency programs.
