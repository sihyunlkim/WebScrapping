Teaching and Learning Excellence (TLE)

About Us

Teaching and Learning Excellence (TLE) is dedicated to enhancing the quality of instruction and fostering an engaging learning environment. We offer a range of services, including professional development workshops for faculty, instructional design support for all stakeholders on campus, and the integration of innovative teaching technologies. We support faculty members by providing resources and training to help them refine their teaching techniques and adapt to new educational trends. Students benefit indirectly through the improved quality of instruction and the enhanced learning experiences. Additionally, we collaborate with academic administrators to align teaching practices with the institution's strategic goals and accreditation requirements. By fostering a culture of continuous improvement in teaching and learning, our goal is to help ensure that Regis delivers a high-quality education that meets the evolving needs of our diverse students.

The Teaching and Learning Excellence Team looks forward to collaborating with you. We can help you with the following items:

  • How can I integrate learning technologies (e.g., Yellowdig, VoiceThread) into my course?
  • How do I embed or add videos to my course?
  • What are the best practices for inclusive pedagogy?
  • What are the best practices for designing accessible course materials?
  • What are some methods for enhancing student engagement in online courses?
  • What are the best tools to use for creating interactive content in my course?
