MANDY & DIANE LLC DBA

Mandalyn Academy

Mandalyn Academy Launched In 2001 When Mandalyn Freeland Realized The Possibility Of Creating An Exceptional Master Esthetics Program For Those Who Want A Successful Career In The Spa And Beauty Industry. In 2006, Diane Bitaraf Joined Forces With Mandalyn And They Began Building On The Core Values Of The Company And Identifying How They Could Ensure That Their Students Receive A High-Quality Education, Customer Service, And Become Experts In The Industry. Mandalyn And Diane Noticed What Had Been Done During Their Own Beauty School Experiences And That Of Other Schools Around The Country, But They Also Noticed What Wasn’t Being Done. They Committed To Creating A Structured, Yet Positive And Fun Atmosphere Where Students Can Become Confident And Successful Professionals.

Why Choose Mandalyn Academy?

At Mandalyn Academy, we believe education should prepare you for real success, not just a license. Our students gain the skills, confidence, and business knowledge needed to thrive in the beauty and wellness industry.

What Sets Us Apart:

  • Real clients who provide hands-on learning and valuable feedback
  • Real numbers that track performance, sales, and client progress
  • Real experience through advanced clinical and spa-based training
  • Advanced treatments that reflect real medspa services
  • Business education focused on entrepreneurship and career growth
  • Flexible career pathways designed for every stage of life
  • Long-term alumni support to guide graduates throughout their careers

Students graduate confident, skilled, and prepared for success in every professional setting.

How Admission Work

Contact admissions

Contact admissions by calling or texting 801-616-2339 or emailing
admissions@mandalynacademy.com. We’re happy to answer your initial questions.

Schedule a campus tour

A tour helps you get a feel for Mandalyn Academy, answer your questions,
and determine which program and schedule work best for you.

Complete the FAFSA

Complete the FAFSA if you plan to use financial aid. Visit https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa and use our school code: 041619. Reach out if you need assistance. Once submitted, we typically receive
your information within a few days and will contact you with award details.

Submit your application

Submit your application. Go to
https://mandalynacademy.com/program-info/ and complete the requested information. Pay the $50.00 application fee, which is included in your total tuition cost. We’ll be notified immediately
upon submission.

Complete your enrollment documents

Complete your enrollment documents. We’ll schedule a 30-minute appointment
to review final details, have you sign enrollment paperwork, and prepare you to start.

Meet your instructor

Meet your instructor. A few days before your start date, your instructor
will contact you to introduce themselves and provide any last-minute information.
