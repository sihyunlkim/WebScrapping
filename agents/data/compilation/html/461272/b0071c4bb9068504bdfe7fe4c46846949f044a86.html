Master Esthetics

Master Esthetician Program

1200 Hours | Day & Night Programs | Full-Time & Part-Time Options

At Mandalyn Academy, our Master Esthetician Program is designed for students seeking real-world experience, advanced hands-on training, and long-term career flexibility in the beauty, wellness, and medical aesthetics industries. This comprehensive 1200-hour program combines Basic Esthetics (Fundamentals) and Advanced Master Esthetics, providing students with the skills and confidence to graduate employable, career-ready, and business-minded. Financial aid is available for those who qualify.

Why Choose a Career in Master Esthetics?

The beauty and skincare industry continues to thrive, even during uncertain times. People will always invest in their confidence, appearance, and self-care. While aging is natural, the rate at which skin ages can be influenced by sun exposure, genetics, stress, and environmental damage. Master Estheticians are trained to slow visible aging, correct skin concerns, and promote long-term skin health through advanced treatments and personalized care.

This profession offers flexibility, which makes it an excellent choice for parents and individuals seeking a balance between work and family life. It is not uncommon for estheticians to create their schedule around their families and still earn a consistent, high income. For young professionals, esthetics provides a chance to build a rewarding career early. As a result, you can grow as a professional based on your skills, creativity, and relationships with clients instead of seniority.

A Master Esthetics license also expands your service options. Professionals can perform high-value beauty treatments and develop multiple specialties, which helps them:

Serve more clients

Increase retention

Maximize income per appointment

Build scalable, profitable businesses

Many graduates find success offering a variety of services such as facials, chemical peels, brow and lash treatments, and spray tanning within a single business.

More Than a Career: A Purpose-Driven Profession

Master Esthetics is about more than skincare. It is about helping people feel confident and comfortable in their own skin.

Master Estheticians:

Help clients manage acne, aging, pigmentation, and skin concerns

Restore self-confidence and emotional well-being

Deliver visible, measurable results

Build long-term, trusted relationships

This profession allows you to see the impact of your work every day. Watching someone leave your treatment room with renewed confidence is both meaningful and fulfilling.

Career Opportunities with a Master Esthetics License

Graduates of Mandalyn Academy’s Master Esthetician Program can build careers in:

  • Day spas and luxury spas
  • Medical spas and aesthetic clinics
  • Plastic surgery offices
  • Dermatology practices
  • Wellness and laser clinics

Entrepreneurial and Leadership Opportunities

  • Solo esthetician (studio or home-based)
  • Spa or med spa owner
  • Spa director or clinic manager
  • Instructor or school owner
  • Product brand representative or educator
  • Skincare line creator
  • Marketing and brand management roles in the beauty industry

This license provides flexibility, growth, and long-term career options in many areas of beauty and wellness.

Program Structure and Schedule Options

Our Master Esthetician Program is designed to fit your lifestyle while providing a complete, hands-on education. Whether you are balancing work, family, or other commitments, Mandalyn Academy offers flexible options to help you succeed while gaining real-world experience.

  • 1200 total hours
  • Day and evening classes available
  • Full-time and part-time schedules

Fundamentals (Basic Esthetics Curriculum – Included)

A strong foundation in Basic Esthetics sets the stage for the rest of the program, which covers essential skills and knowledge every skincare professional needs.

Fundamental training includes:

  • Skin anatomy and physiology
  • Chemistry and product safety
  • Facial massage and treatment room setup
  • Acne treatments and extractions
  • Anti-aging facials
  • Brow laminations
  • Spray tanning
  • Teeth whitening and tooth gems
  • Luxury back facials, hot stones, and aromatherapy
  • Makeup fundamentals
  • Business principles and career planning

This foundation prepares students for advanced treatments in medical aesthetics and professional skincare.

Master Esthetics – Advanced Practical Curriculum

Students progress into advanced training that aligns with Mandalyn Academy’s spa and medspa services. This ensures graduates are confident, skilled, and ready to enter the workforce.

Advanced Skin and Facial Treatments

  • Advanced chemical peels
  • Dermaplaning
  • Microneedling and RF Microneedling
  • Microdermabrasion
  • Customized corrective facials
  • Acne treatment protocols
  • Lymphatic massage
  • Gua sha, jade rollers, cold globes, and hot stones

Laser and Medical Aesthetics

  • Laser hair removal (multiple devices)
  • IPL (Intense Pulsed Light) therapy
  • Aerolase skin rejuvenation
  • Sublative skin resurfacing
  • Laser safety and contraindications
  • Pre- and post-treatment skin care
  • Fitzpatrick skin typing
  • Bloodborne pathogen certification

Body Treatments

  • Body wraps
  • Body sculpting
  • Back facials
  • Aroma Touch (dōTERRA)

Beauty Enhancement Services

  • Lash and brow laminations
  • Spray tanning
  • Teeth whitening
  • Tooth gems
  • Piercings

This advanced curriculum prepares students to perform high-demand, high-income services safely and effectively.

Business, Sales, and Professional Development

Mandalyn Academy emphasizes career-building as much as technical skill. Students learn how to develop sustainable, successful careers that fit their lifestyle goals.

Training includes:

  • Client consultations and treatment planning
  • Product knowledge and ingredient education
  • Retail and membership sales
  • Marketing and social media strategy
  • Resume building and mock interviews
  • Personal branding
  • Client retention and rebooking strategies

Students graduate with measurable performance records that include:

  • Product and service sales
  • Membership conversions
  • Client retention rates

These statistics help graduates stand out when applying for positions or starting their own business.

  • Client feedback and reviews

  • Academic performance

This recognition highlights dedication, skill, and consistency.

State Board Support and Reimbursement

Mandalyn Academy is committed to helping students pass their state board exams on the first attempt.

  • Extensive preparation is built into the curriculum
  • Students are encouraged to take their exams before graduation
  • Students who pass before completing the program receive exam reimbursement

This ensures students are fully supported and ready to begin their careers immediately after graduation.

Face Reality Acne Curriculum

Our program includes Face Reality Acne Education, a valuable addition for students interested in acne correction.

  • Complete acne education integrated into the curriculum
  • Graduates receive 50% off Face Reality certification (normally $800, discounted to $400)
  • Certification allows graduates to become Certified Acne Specialists

This credential helps professionals attract more clients and offer targeted, results-driven acne solutions.
