Vocational Programs

In addition to serving DeSoto, we also serve the Dallas-Fort Worth area!

Technical Education to Get You Working

The MT Training Center provides a wide variety of educational programs. Each of these programs are designed along career paths with employment as the end goal. We at MT Training center monitor not only employment trends, but also national targeted occupational demand lists to tailor our vocational programs to industry needs. Our focus is on you, the student, and we want to help you succeed whether youre starting a new job path or are looking to further your experience in your field. In addition to serving DeSoto, we also serve the Fort Worth area!

Vocational Training Courses:

CDL PREP Program

Program for the student ready to enroll that has their CDL Learners Permit.

Gunsmithing

Learn and master the art of firearms manufacturing and repair.

Truck Driving

Truck driving can be a great job and our trucking classes will prepare you to be a truck driver through 237 hours of instruction in truck driving skills, procedures, and regulations. Our truck driving school prepares our students for the road ahead.

Advanced CDL Training for the Entrepreneur

Go a step further in your truck driving career with our CDL education by learning how to establish a truck driving company and be a self-employed owner through CDL training.

Combination Welding

This is a beginner welding education and the objective is to train you in entry level welding so you can get welding.

Cosmetology

Learn those skills and competencies necessary to become employed in a variety of salon environments.

Class A Barber

Learn those skills and competencies necessary to become employed in a variety of salon environments.

Computer Training Courses:

Computerized Accounting

Gain the skills you need for a role in financial service industries and business accounting settings, including how to create, assess, and verify financial reports.

Business Office Specialist

For those looking to work in an office setting, this course prepares you in a variety of ways, including through word processing techniques, basic grammar, vocabulary, and phone skills.

Medical Front Office

Working in the front office of a medical facility requires certain skills and this course prepares you through hands on experience.

Veteran Benefits

MT Training Center is approved by the Texas Veteran Commission under the provision of Title 38, U.S. Code for VA educational benefits to train veteran and other eligible persons for approved programs and courses required for those programs.

Current programs approved for Veteran benefits are:

  • Business Office Specialist
  • Combination Welding
  • Computerized Accounting
  • Computer Numerical Controller (CNC) Machinist
  • Medical Billing and Coding
  • Medical Transcription
  • Truck Driving

Updated Financial Aid Programs

MT Training Center is approved to participate in the Federal Title IV, HEA Student Financial Assistance programs as a Proprietary Institution of Higher Education by the U.S. Department of Education. Current programs approved for Federal Title IV, HEA Financial Assistance are:

  • Advanced CDL Training for the Entrepreneur
  • Combination Welding
  • Computerized Accounting Program
  • Administrative Assistant Program
  • Computer Numerical Controller (CNC) Machinist Program

If you have more questions about any of our programs, or youd like to find out about signing up for a class, please call our office at 972-262-5395 and speak with a member of our team.
