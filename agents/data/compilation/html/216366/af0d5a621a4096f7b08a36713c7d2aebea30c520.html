Turnitin

Turnitin is a plagiarism tool that checks work for similarities and gives learners tips to improve their writing. The tool is integrated with Canvas, making it easy to incorporate into course assignments. Turnitin helps learners improve their writing skills and write with academic integrity.

Accessing Turnitin

Access Turnitin after creating an assignment within your course by selecting Turnitin from the plagiarism review panel.
