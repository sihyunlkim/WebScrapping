Technology Services for Thomas Jefferson University Students

JEFF IT

Document Type Handbook, Guide or Manual

Abstract

Most IT information is available, and questions can be answered, by visiting the jeffit.jefferson.edu web site and clicking on the Student box on the “Who Are You?” top banner. All student services and applications are listed there. If you can’t find an answer, call the Jeff IT Service Desk at 215 503-7600.
