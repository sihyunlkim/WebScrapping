Career & Education

At Washington Health System,a progressive community based health system, we utilizes the most advanced medical technology in our state-of-the-art facilities. Our two-hospital, award-winning health system has 2000+ employees,300+ physicians, 300 licensed beds and more than 40 off-site locations throughout three counties that service a population of over 250,000 people.

Our off-site locations include primary and specialty care physician practices, outpatient care facilities, diagnostic centers, theWHS Wilfred R. Cameron Wellness Center,Greenbriar Treatment Center, and theWHS Center for Recovery and Wellness. We are proud to offer the communitys only diplomaSchool of Nursingprogram as well as ourSchool of Radiologic Technologyprogram,Residency ProgramandFellowship Programs.

Since 1897 WHS has proudly maintained our community based focus and our commitment to provide and expand services in Washington, Greene and surrounding counties. Our mission is Great Patient Care. It is simple, to the point and EVERY employees and physicians day-to-day focus at WHS. We have a deeply rooted, community based board of directors who also supports this mission.
