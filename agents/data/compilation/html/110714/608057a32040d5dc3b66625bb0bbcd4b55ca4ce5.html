Generative AI Center

The Generative AI Center at UC Santa Cruz is a dynamic hub for research, collaboration, and education, uniting faculty and students across disciplines to explore the transformative potential of generative artificial intelligence. Drawing on the university’s culture of innovation and its proximity to Silicon Valley, the center is dedicated to advancing AI technologies that are both groundbreaking and socially responsible.

The center’s work focuses on driving innovation that leads to real-world impact, fostering collaboration across academia, industry, and the broader community, and ensuring that the development and use of AI are grounded in ethics, trust, and accessibility. By bringing together experts from computer science, engineering, the humanities, social sciences, and the arts, the Generative AI Center positions UC Santa Cruz at the forefront of responsible AI research and education.

Generative AI is reshaping the way humans interact with technology, moving beyond text-based systems toward multimodal tools that integrate language, vision, sound, and design. Faculty researchers at UC Santa Cruz are developing new models and applications that leverage this potential—from creating healthcare simulations and diagnostic tools to enhancing text-to-image generation, improving image recognition, and designing new programming languages for hardware.

The center also serves as a collaborative platform for developing ethical frameworks and policy guidance that address the complex challenges of this rapidly evolving field. By combining cutting-edge research with public education and outreach, the Generative AI Center reflects UC Santa Cruz’s mission as a public university: to innovate boldly, engage inclusively, and lead responsibly in shaping the future of artificial intelligence.
