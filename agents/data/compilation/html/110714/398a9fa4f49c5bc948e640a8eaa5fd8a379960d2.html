Student Code of Conduct (Policies and Regulations)

The Student Policies and Regulations Handbook, also referred to as the Student Code of Conduct, outlines the rights and responsibilities of students, faculty, staff, and others while on University property, in support of UC’s mission of teaching, research, and public service. It establishes University-wide standards to help maintain a safe, respectful, and productive environment while upholding freedom of expression and institutional integrity.
