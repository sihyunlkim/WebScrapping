IT Governance

Governance groups provide a framework for engagement, decision-making, accountability, and strategic alignment of information technology needs with campus priorities.

IT Governance Councils & Committees

IT Executive Governance

Provides strategic governance and oversight for all major technology initiatives and ensures alignment with the university’s mission.

Artificial Intelligence (AI) Council

Central body for guiding the university’s approach to Artificial Intelligence (AI) integration.

Digital Accessibility Council

Guiding the development, implementation, and ongoing operation of the UCSC IT Accessibility Policy Program.

IT Security Council

Strengthens the university’s information security by enforcing policy, supporting best practices, and fostering a security-conscious culture.

IT Systems and Data Governance

Brings together stakeholders to shape IT strategy, policy, and investments aligned with campus priorities.

Research Computing and Data Infrastructure

Provides the campus framework for institutional governance of shared research computing and data infrastructure.

Technology Enhanced Teaching and Learning

Aligns instructional technologies with campus priorities, providing guidance and strategic investment recommendations.
