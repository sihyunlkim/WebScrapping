Employer Services

Hire Skilled, Qualified Graduates from North American Trade Schools

At North American Trade Schools, our graduates are equipped with all-encompassing training to prepare them for the workforce. If your company is looking to hire professional-level employees, consider our excellent students and grads. Contact us using the form on this page.

Our mission: Service with excellence the needs of our community and the needs of our graduates by matching skills.

For over 50 years, our staff has worked to create reliable programs that produce the best-equipped workers in a variety of fields. To help them transition from coursework to career, we offer job placement services to our grads. These services are free of charge to both employers and grads. If youre ready to start hiring skilled workers in industries such as welding, truck driving, diesel technology, HRVAC, electrical technology, or industrial maintenance, check out North American Trade Schools.

Why Employers Hire NATS Graduates

Graduates of our trade programs have gone on to become very successful tradespeople and even owned their own businesses. We teach students to not only develop skills in their trade program but soft skills too. These include professionalism, positive attitudes, communication skills, and more.

Come Visit NATS!

If youd like to have a hiring event, host student interviews, or learn more about our job placement services, schedule a campus visit! Were always open to forming new employer connections, and wed be happy to put you in contact with our excellent students.
