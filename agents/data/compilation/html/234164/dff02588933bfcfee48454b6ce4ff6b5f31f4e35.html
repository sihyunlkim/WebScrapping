2025 1098-T Forms

Students may access their 1098-T tax form by following the steps below:

  1. Log in to My VUU
  2. Open the search bar
  3. Type “1098-T”
  4. Select the first link, “1098-T Report”
    1. My 1098-T Report
  5. Enter the tax year wish to view
  6. Click “Generate Report”

If you did not receive a 1098-T tax form, it typically indicates that there were not sufficient qualifying tuition charges or payments on your account during the applicable tax year for the form to be generated.  This may occur if tuition charges were fully covered by financial aid, scholarships, or other credits, or if there were no eligible charges or payments during that period.

For questions regarding how this information may affect your individual tax situation, please consult a qualified tax advisor.
