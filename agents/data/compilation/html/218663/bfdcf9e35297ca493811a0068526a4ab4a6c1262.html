About This Guide

The University Libraries offers this guide as a starting point for students, faculty, researchers, and staff to learn about AI, find out what's happening with AI on campus, discover AI-related resources and services provided by the Libraries, and more. 

Disclaimer

  • The University Libraries recognizes the areas of AI and Generative AI are currently in a state of rapid development and change. While we attempt to maintain currency with information provided, please appreciate there will likely be instances where our published information in this guide is out of date. Contact us and we'll do our best to update. 
     
  • While this guide presents information about, and actively uses, various AI tools, the University Libraries does not recommend or endorse any specific AI technology and/or tool(s). 
     
  • Be sure to review and understand the academic integrity and student conduct implications before choosing to use AI technologies and/or tools.
