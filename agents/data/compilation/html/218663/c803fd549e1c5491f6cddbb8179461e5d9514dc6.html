This office empowers Carolinians to be responsible citizens and uphold the core values of our academic community. We hold every Carolinian to a high standard of ethical behavior.

 

  • Code of Conduct

    Our Code of Conduct outlines students' responsibilities to themselves and the Carolina community.

  • Honor Code

    Our Honor Code describes the university's expectations for students' academic integrity.

  • Artificial Intelligence and Academic Integrity at USC

    Learn more about how this office handles violations of the Honor Code related to the use of artificial intelligence.

  • Student Organizations

    Our Student Organization Code of Conduct articulates the university’s procedure and due process protections for Student Organizations and reflects the standards they are expected to maintain.

  • Code of Conduct Violation Report

    This report should be completed if there is a concern that a student has violated a behavioral code (for example, alcohol or drug violations).

  • Honor Code Violation Report

    This report should be completed if there is a concern about academic dishonesty within the classroom. 

  • Administrative Conferences

    If you are alleged to be in violation of the university's Code of Conduct or Honor Code, you'll be given the chance to explain more about the incident during an administrative conference.

  • Carolina Judicial Council Hearings

    If you disagree with a decision made in your administrative conference, or you would prefer to have a panel of your peers decide your case outcomes, you can request a Carolina Judicial Council hearing.

  • Request a Presentation

    We are committed to providing proactive ways to address behavioral expectations both within and outside of the classroom. Please utilize this form to request a presentation from our office. 

Our Mission

The Office of Student Conduct and Academic Integrity and the Care Team process develop and enforce standards of conduct for students inside and outside of the classroom. This is achieved through clearly articulated and consistently administered standards which promote personal growth and preserve the rights and safety of the academic community.

 

Our Vision

The Office of Student Conduct and Academic Integrity and the Care Team process are committed to providing experiences, processes and resources that support the advancement of students’ principled decision making, responsible citizenship and adherence to the Carolinian Creed; and which contribute to an environment that supports the health and safety of members so that they may reach academic goals without unnecessary impediments. 
The Carolinian Creed » 

Our Core Functions

  1. Conduct administrative conferences/hearings and provide educational or developmental outcomes. 
  2. Provide educational outreach for individual and community awareness.
  3. Intervene when problematic behavior is identified.
