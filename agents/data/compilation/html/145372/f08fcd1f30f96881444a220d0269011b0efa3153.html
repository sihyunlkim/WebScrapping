At GU, we take seriously our role in empowering students for lives of character and service. That means holistic development of students. 

From making students feel safe on campus to career counseling and internship placement. From mental health counseling services, an on-campus health center, and multiple health and safety features to tutoring and testing, we are here to help our students thrive.
