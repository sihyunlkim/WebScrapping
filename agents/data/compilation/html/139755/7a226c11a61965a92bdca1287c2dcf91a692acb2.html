AI for the Real World

Tech AI is a Georgia Tech access point for artificial intelligence, connecting researchers, educators, and leaders to solve real-world problems. We build responsible, transparent AI, expand access to AI careers, accelerate research with powerful tools and data, and work with partners to apply AI where it can do the most good. At Tech AI, we believe AI should work for everyone.

Learn More

Connect with Tech AI

Whether you're an industry leader, researcher, educator, or student, we invite you to connect with Tech AI and be part of a growing community shaping the future of artificial intelligence. Partner with us to explore real-world applications, collaborate on cutting-edge research, or discover AI-driven solutions tailored to your goals. Let’s build the future of AI—together.

The Future of AI

In this video, Tech AI Director Pascal Van Hentenryck describes how artificial intelligence is reshaping our world and discusses the mission of Tech AI, Georgia Tech’s hub for AI research. He highlights the hub’s focus on impactful work and applying AI solutions to challenges beyond academia, driving meaningful change worldwide. Van Hentenryck is also the A. Russell Chandler III Chair and Professor in Georgia Tech's Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering and directs the NSF Artificial Intelligence Institute for Advances in Optimization (AI4OPT).
