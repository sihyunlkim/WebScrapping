Teaching and Learning in an Age of Artificial Intelligence

A series of workshops on the Pedagogy of using AI in the classroom

The incorporation of Al into pedagogy holds immense potential to enhance the teaching and learning experience for both students and faculty. CTL has been at the forefront of this educational frontier, organizing a series of workshops designed to empower faculty to explore the integration of Al into their teaching practices. Through these events, CTL seeks to inspire educators to harness the transformative power of Al and leverage its capabilities to foster innovative, creative, and engaging learning environments.
