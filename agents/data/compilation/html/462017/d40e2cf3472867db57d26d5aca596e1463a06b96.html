Kokomo Beauty & Cosmetology Courses

Enroll Now in Kokomo’s Premier Beauty and Cosmetology School
Summit Salon Academy Kokomo is now enrolling for all programs, including Cosmetology, Barbering, Esthetics, Massage Therapy, and Manicurist. Each course is designed to develop your technical skills, creativity, and understanding of the salon and spa business.

Our students learn with Pivot Point’s FUSE digital platform, using an iPad to access interactive lessons and real-world training tools.

Take the next step toward your career in beauty — contact Amanda in Admissions to learn more about our Kokomo beauty school programs and start dates.

Federal financial aid, scholarships, and payment plans are available for those who qualify.
Note: The 450-hour Manicurist program is not eligible for federal student aid.
