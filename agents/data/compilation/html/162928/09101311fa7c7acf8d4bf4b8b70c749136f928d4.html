Academic Integrity Policies

Responsible academic conduct is essential to the core values of The Johns Hopkins University. JHU students are expected to meet the highest standards of academic excellence and ethical conduct. Each of the divisions of the University maintains an academic integrity policy, and students, faculty, and staff are required to promptly report known or suspected violations of the policy.

The links below detail the academic integrity policies that apply to students and postdoctoral trainees for each division at the University. Cases of academic misconduct will be resolved using the relevant policies from the student's division of enrollment.
