Central Yeshiva Beth Joseph is an institution for advanced Talmudic scholarship in Airmont, New York. The yeshiva aims to produce senior Torah scholars and to raise each student to a higher level of his potential both in scholarship and character development. Another component of their mission is to provide the training needed to live a Torah life through the study of ethical texts, as well as providing students with appropriate role models drawn from both historical and contemporary personalities.

Notice of Nondiscriminatory Policy as to Students

Central Yeshiva Beth Joseph admits students of any race, color, national and ethnic origin to all the rights, privileges, programs, and activities generally accorded or made available to students at the school. It does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national and ethnic origin in administration of its educational policies, admissions policies, scholarship and loan programs, and athletic and other school-administered programs.
