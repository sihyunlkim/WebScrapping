There are 3 modules in this course

In Generative AI: Governance, Policy, and Emerging Regulation, youll discuss governance considerations for generative artificial intelligence (AI) systems deployed in an organization and explore data management practices, transparency methods, risk and impact assessments, and management approaches that ensure generative AI is developed and deployed responsibly. The course also provides an overview of the current generative AI policy and regulatory landscapes within the United States, European Union, and G7 countries. By exploring governance issues and the current regulatory landscape regarding AI, youll gain a deeper understanding of how to integrate, manage, and monitor AI within your organization.
