We invite you in as a guest to participate in hands-on instruction as our students learn the very best practices and techniques to deliver quality services at an affordable price.

    Service Disclaimers

     *Pricing is based on individual client needs, length, condition of hair, multiple colors used, etc. Final pricing will be quoted during the service consultation. Services include finish. Not all services available at all Institute locations. All services are performed by supervised students. Services are for ages 6+. Children under the age of 12 must be accompanied by an adult who is not receiving a service. **This charge is not subject to any offers or discounts. 

    SPA DISCLAIMER: Please plan to arrive 15 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment. Arriving late may interfere with your treatment time and may decrease the therapeutic effects for you. All reservations will end at their scheduled time so as not to delay the next guest. We understand that certain circumstances are unavoidable and may require cancellation. Out of respect for our students & staffs time, we request a 24-hour notice for any cancellation or reservation change.
