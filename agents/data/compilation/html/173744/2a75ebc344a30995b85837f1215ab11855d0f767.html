    Start your beauty journey at

    Aveda Arts & Sciences Institute
    Minneapolis

    Aveda Arts & Sciences Institute Minneapolis holds a special place in our hearts, as the very first Aveda Institute was founded right here. We offer cosmetology, esthiology (esthetics), advanced esthetics, and massage therapy programs, all integrating Aveda’s signature plant-based products and superb hands-on training. 

    about Aveda Arts Minneapolis

    Were located at the edge of the Marcy-Holmes neighborhood, just blocks away from Nicollet Island and a mile from the University of Minnesota Minneapolis campus. Our central location near the University and Central intersection offers easy access to many parts of the city, including historic Main Street.

    Located on multiple Metro Transit bus lines and within walking distance of a variety of local restaurants, lunch breaks and commuting are convenient and accessible. At Aveda Arts & Sciences Institute Minneapolis, youll learn beauty surrounded by beauty. The institute occupies a beautifully restored historic brick building, the Cataract Lodge. Built in 1926 in late Gothic Revival style for the Masons, the building was purchased in the 80s by Aveda founder Horst Rechelbacher for the very first Aveda Institute.

    The five floors contain elegant marble staircases lined with original stained-glass windows, two incredible ballrooms, a retail space, and even a domed meditation room with ceiling crystals. A building this inspiring could be where you attend beauty school as the next step in your career.

    More

    why choose Aveda Arts Minneapolis

    At this flagship campus of Aveda Institutes, our students come for far more than just skillbuilding. The students at Aveda Arts & Sciences Institute Minneapolis value our focus on wellnessboth personal and environmentaland rituals rooted in ancient beauty practices that still resonate today with a modern twist. 

    Beyond the classroom or clinic floor, students have the chance to participate in two fashion shows during the year: one for Breast Cancer Awareness and anotherfittingly for the Aveda brand that walks the talk on environment sustainabilitycelebrating Earth Month. Our active community also proudly participates in Minneapolis annual Pride parade and fest. 

    Its not just our physical building that has history, either. The proof points of our program are our noteworthy alumni. Among our distinguished Aveda Minneapolis graduates are beauty pros who have made it to the pinnacle of their fields, including the celebrity hairstylist Jonathan Van Ness; David Wagner, founder and CEO of Juut Salonspa; and Janell Geason, global artistic director and educator for Avedas makeup division.

    choose your passion

    Our Minneapolis campus offers many program options, all led by a team of Aveda instructors who will match your passion in an incredible educational experience. 

    Cosmetology

    For students seeking a broad base of knowledge in hair, makeup, skincare, manicuring, business skills, and more, Aveda Arts & Sciences Institute Minneapolis offers training in all aspects of cosmetology. We meet all state of Minnesota cosmetology standards, with the majority of training hours focused on hands-on work led by our experienced instructors. Our curriculum is delivered in true Aveda fashion, focusing on modern skills, Aveda products and techniques, and with a focus on you as a person, not as a number. 

    Our Cosmetology program graduates are prepared to work in Aveda salons and other settings. Because of the broad range of skills covered in the curriculum, those who receive an Aveda cosmetology training go on to many different careers: Some become business owners and entrepreneurs. Others work in fashion or specialized makeup and hair fields. With an Aveda education, the skys the limit.

    Esthiology

    Esthiology, also called esthetics or skincare, is a field with explosive growth in consumer demand. For those who care deeply about skin health and learning to provide a nurturing client experience, the Minneapolis campus Esthiology program may be a great fit. During your 600 hours of training, you’ll train to perform many facials, face and body waxes, and makeup applications using Aveda products and techniques emphasizing natural ingredients and wellness. 

    Upon graduation, youll have all the fundamentals to work in any type of spa as an esthetician or to start your own esthetician business. Esthetics graduates can also become managers, wellness professionals, waxers, and more.

    MORE ABOUT ESTHIOLOGY

    Esthiology / Advanced Practice Esthiology

    For those who want a deep skillset to perform both basic and advanced skin services and therapies, the Aveda Arts & Sciences Institute Minneapolis offers an exclusive Advanced Practice Esthiology program. Students in the basic Esthiology program may also choose to transfer into the Advanced Practice Esthiology program and have their prior training hours recognized.

    Our industry-leading esthetician instructors teach you how to perform Hydrodermabrasion, microneedling, microdermabrasion, dermaplaning, and chemical peels as well as electrical energy treatments. Graduates of this prestigious Advanced Practice Esthiology program are well-positioned to launch a career in traditional spas and salons as well as in medspas and clinical settings.

    Massage Therapy

    Our Massage Therapy program goes beyond the hands-on skills portion of massage to also teach business, wellness, and retail skills. You’ll use Avedas pure flower- and plant essence-based products to create our unique spa experience with Ayurvedic techniques. 

    Many of our massage therapy graduates work in traditional massage therapy businesses. Others may specialize in fields like sports massage or wellness, or start their own massage businesses.
