Ripon College believes in Midwest Residents.

Through our financial aid program, The Ripon Commitment, we offer two financial aid pathways that make a private education affordable and achievable for first-time undergraduate students from Wisconsin.

Pathway 2: Price Match. 

We will match the cost of UW-Madison’s tuition and fees for all Wisconsin students. 

At Ripon, we want students to make choices based on what’s best for them — not financial barriers. With our price match, you can earn a private school education at a competitive price. You won’t pay more than $12,186 per year!

Requirements:

  • Must be a Wisconsin resident
  • Incoming first-year student or transfer student
  • Must file a FAFSA
  • Must live on campus
  • After all aid has been applied, Ripon College tuition and fees will not be greater than UW-Madison tuition and fees of $12,186 for 2026.

Free Tuition and Fees.

Tuition and fees will be free for all Wisconsin students with a household Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) of $75,000 or less. This means you can focus on your future without financial stress. 

Requirements for Free Tuition and Fees:

  • Must be a Wisconsin resident
  • Incoming first-year students and transfer students
  • Household Adjusted Gross Income of $75,000 or less
  • Must file a FAFSA
  • Must live on campus
