    Application Management

    Log in as returning user or create an account as a first-time user to access your application.

    Returning users:

    If you are returning to complete your started application, you can login with the same email and password you used when you created your Applicant Account with Ripon College.
    • If you do not remember your password, you can do a password reset on the login page.
    • If you need assistance, contact the Office of Admissions at 920-748-8709 or email adminfo@ripon.edu.
    We hope you complete your application soon!

    Log in

    First-time users:

    When you "Create an Account" for the first time, you will be asked to select an application option. Read each description to determine your best application type:

    Undergraduate application:  This application is for first time college students, international students, and transfer students who are seeking a bachelor's degree.
    To be considered for admission, you must have completed high school through your junior year (high school seniors and international students), graduated from high school (non-traditional first time college student), or be attending another college or university (transfer students).
    Graduate application:  This application is for graduate-level students seeking a master's degree from Ripon College's new Master of Exercise Science program.
    To be considered for admission, you must have graduated from an undergraduate institution and have an undergraduate college degree. International applicants are not eligible for admission to the program based on requirements from the US Department of State.
