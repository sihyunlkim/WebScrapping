Connecting researchers across campus

The Artificial Intelligence and Informatics Consortium (AI2) facilitates collaboration across the many researchers at IU Indianapolis who are interested in advancing the use and impact of information systems on people, organizations, and society.

The Consortium seeks to connect these researchers to funding opportunities and a community of informatics scholars at the Indianapolis campus.
