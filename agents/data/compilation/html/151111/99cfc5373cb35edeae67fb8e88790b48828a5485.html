GenAI in Assessment Community of Practice

This is the application for the Generative Artificial Intelligence (GenAI) Community of Practice (CoP) offered by the Assessment Institute in Indianapolis.  The application contains three parts: (1) required attestations; (2) applicant information; and (3) questions:

  • What do you hope to gain from your involvement in the CoP?
  • What do you hope to contribute to the CoP?
  • Please briefly describe the context and scope in which your AI work is taking place (e.g., institution-wide, academic or support unit, department/program).
  • Please briefly describe your specific experience(s) with AI in professional context, including a self-assessment of your readiness for AI.
  • What are suggested topics you wish to learn about during the CoP?
  • What are potential pilot projects you wish to engage in during the CoP?
  • What are potential resources (speakers, publications, AI tools, etc.) you recommend the CoP consider consulting?
  • In thinking boldly, what is your ideal or optimal scenario for how AI benefits you and your work?

The application closes on Tuesday, July 8, 2025, at 11:59 p.m. (Eastern), and information about joining the CoP will be shared with applicants in early-August 2025. Should you have questions, please email planning@iu.edu and reference GenAI CoP in the subject line.

Colleagues not part of Indiana University (IU) will need to create an IU Guest account to access the GenAI CoP application. If you do not already have one, please create an IU Guest account.
