    Bachelor's degree in Artificial Intelligence

    Be part of the AI generation

    At a glance

    Format
    On-campus or online

    Start dates
    Fall, Spring, or Summer

    Process and cognitive automation may replace many traditional jobs, but they also create new opportunities. Earning a bachelor's degree in AI positions you for success as businesses and technologies continue to evolve. AI spans across platforms at enterprise scale, driving convenience, boosting productivity, and fueling innovation in fields like public safety and logistics. While others play catch-up, you can lead the way with our bachelor’s degree in Artificial Intelligence.

    • Apply
    • Visit campus

    What you'll learn

    Earning a Bachelor of Arts or Bachelor of Science in Artificial Intelligence prepares you to lead in a rapidly evolving field, where process and cognitive automation are reshaping industries. Throughout the program, you will focus on:

    • Human-centered computing: Learn how AI systems can be designed to work seamlessly with people, enhancing user experiences and solving real-world problems.
    • Computational process automation: Explore how AI can streamline processes, increase efficiency, and improve productivity across various sectors.
    • Societal impact of AI: Study the ethical, legal, and social implications of AI technologies and how they shape industries and communities.
    • Data science: Gain practical experience in data analysis and machine learning to apply AI in fields ranging from public safety to logistics.

    This degree equips you with the knowledge and skills to innovate and drive AI solutions at enterprise scale, positioning you for success as AI continues to transform businesses and technologies.

    Industry certification

    As part of your degree you'll earn the Rasa AI Assistant Developer and Cocohub certifications.

    • View the B.A. degree requirements
    • View the B.S. degree requirements

    Careers

    This degree prepares you for jobs in fields such as User Experience (UX) engineering, where AI technologies are integrated and evaluated for human interaction and compliance.

    Successful graduates will earn professional certification, and qualify for positions including:

    • Artificial Intelligence (AI) Architect
    • AI Bots Designer/Developer
    • AI UX researcher
    • Business Intelligence Analyst
    • Conversation Designer/Developer
    • Human Factors Engineer
    • Software Developer, Applications
