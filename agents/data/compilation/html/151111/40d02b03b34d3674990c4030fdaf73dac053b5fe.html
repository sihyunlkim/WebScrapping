Workshops

The CTL offers workshops on a wide range of topics to help faculty develop their teaching and technology skills.  These workshops usually last from one to three hours and are open to all IU Indianapolis faculty, staff, and graduate students.  Because seating is often limited, it is wise to register in advance. Visit the CTL events calendar to a see a listing of and to register for upcoming events.

If our scheduled workshops do not meet your needs, we would be happy to arrange a custom workshop for your department, school, or group.  These workshops can be tailored to your specific needs and interests. For both more information or to schedule a workshop, please contact the CTL at (317) 274-1300. Alternatively, you can fill out the following request form.

In order to submit a custom workshop request, you will need to login.
