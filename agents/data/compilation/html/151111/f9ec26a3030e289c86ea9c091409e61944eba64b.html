Other student procedures

IU Indianapolis has additional student regulations you should know.

Know your responsibilities

IU Indianapolis’ policies ensure that everyone follows university and campus regulations, procedures, and guidelines. Some of our policies are in place to protect the rights of students and guarantee access to a world-class education. Areas covered by our policies include grades, final exams, academic misconduct, immunizations, nondiscrimination, student privacy, veterans benefits, and Zachary’s law.
