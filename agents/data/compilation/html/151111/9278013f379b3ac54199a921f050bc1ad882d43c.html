About

At IU Indianapolis, the Provost's Office is dedicated to fostering student success by cultivating an environment that is recognized for excellence in education, research, and community service. We focus on strengthening academic programs and supporting faculty development to ensure a vibrant and innovative academic community.

Led by the provost and associate vice provosts, our team oversees a wide range of initiatives aimed at advancing our academic mission. As the university's chief academic officer, the provost is responsible for evaluating and enhancing academic policies and programs to uphold the highest standards of academic excellence.
