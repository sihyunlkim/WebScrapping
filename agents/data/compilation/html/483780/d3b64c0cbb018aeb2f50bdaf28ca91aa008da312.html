ABOUT THE UNIVERSITY

Shaping Tomorrow: Where Innovation Meets Education, and Dreams Take Flight

Washington University of Science and Technology (WUST) was founded with a deep commitment to transforming lives worldwide through highly effective quality education that seamlessly integrates academic excellence with practical skill development. Established by Engr. Abubokor Hanipan accomplished scholar, entrepreneur and leader in the global IT industryWUST was envisioned as a university offering higher education while equipping learners with the skills necessary to excel in todays competitive job market. At its core, WUST fosters a culture of lifelong learning, empowering individuals with the knowledge and capabilities needed to succeed at local, national, and global levels. This vision has been brought to life under the visionary leadership of its founding president, Dr. Hasan Karaburk, through the passionate contributions of highly skilled scholar-practitioner faculty, advanced and industry-relevant academic programs, and the integration of cutting-edge technology to deliver education effectively and efficiently, all while addressing the ever-evolving demands of the job market.
