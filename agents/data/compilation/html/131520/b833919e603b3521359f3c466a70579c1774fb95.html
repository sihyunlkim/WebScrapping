You’re invited to join Howard AI Chats: AI Policy Listening Session, an interactive, virtual conversation where you’ll learn from colleagues across Howard and share how AI is showing up in your own role. This session is designed to surface real experiences, insights, and questions that will inform upcoming AI guidance and resources at Howard. We hope you’ll join us for this important and engaging conversation!

 

Sponsored by the Howard AI Advisory Council  

 

Details 

  • Date: February 4, 2026 

  • Time: 12:00–1:30 PM 

  • Format: Virtual and interactive 

  • Register (required): https://howard.zoom.us/meeting/register/kI-jhC5yTimUAS0KBI6NtA 

 

The session will include facilitated small-group breakout discussions. No preparation is needed; just bring your perspective and curiosity!
