In this session, faculty who participated in the Spring 2025 Lumen Circles on generative AI and evidence-based teaching practices will share key insights on integrating AI into their teaching.

 

Through real-world examples and reflections, participants will explore how generative AI can support student engagement, personalize learning, and enhance instructional strategies. This session offers a unique opportunity to learn from peers actively applying AI tools in the classroom, grounded in evidence-based pedagogical approaches.
