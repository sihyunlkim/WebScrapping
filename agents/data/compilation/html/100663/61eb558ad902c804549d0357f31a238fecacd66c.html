How to write a GenAI policy for your research group Online

This session will critically assess the limitations of generative AI for professional writing. We will cover responsible use of GenAI including when to use it and how to properly disclose usage. The session will also provide a framework for you to develop a GenAI policy for your research group.

This session is sponsored by the UAB Libraries Office of Scholarly Communication as part of their Spring Seminar Series.
