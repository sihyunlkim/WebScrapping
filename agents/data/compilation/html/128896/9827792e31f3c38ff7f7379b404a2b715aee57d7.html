Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our campus locations, enrollment process, and program offerings.

Where are you located?

Cortiva Institute has seven locations nationwide:
  • Arlington, TX
  • Baltimore, MD
  • Crowell, CT
  • King of Prussia, PA
  • Maitland, FL
  • St. Petersburg, FL

How do I enroll?

Here are the simplified steps to enroll at Cortiva Institute 1. Inquire 2. Interview 3. Tour 4. Application 5. Financing 6. Enrollment 7. Start Day Take the first step & learn more cortiva.edu/request-info Or give us a call at 1-800-CORTIVA

What programs do you offer?

Cortiva Institute offers a variety of fast-track career training programs focused on professional careers in health, beauty, and wellness. Programs vary by location. Career Paths (Program Name Varies by State)
  • Massage Therapist
  • Esthetician (Skin Care Specialist)
  • Paramedical Esthetician
  • Makeup Artist & Esthetician
  • *NEW* Cosmetologist
  • *NEW* Nail Specialist

How can I contact you?

If youre interested in one of our programs or would like to see what program would best suit your career goals, please contact our Admissions Department. Fill out our contact form if you would like us to give you a call, text, or email. Or you are more than welcome to walk in or give us a call to get started: 1-800-CORTIVA

Do you offer financial aid?

The Financial Aid Department at Cortiva Institute provides financial aid to students who need it to pay tuition expenses at the school. The Financial Aid Department has established procedures that assure fair and consistent treatment of all applicants. Financial Aid is available for students who qualify.
