Career Path: Massage Therapist

Heal With The Power of Touch

Learn the skills and compassion needed to pursue a career in health & wellness. Gain hands-on training in various massage techniques and positively impact people’s lives every day!

Fast Track

Graduate and get licensed in less than a year!

Career Opportunities

A license in massage therapy opens a variety of career settings in the health and wellness industry, including:

  • Spa, Resorts, or Cruise Ships
  • Chiropractic or Plastic Surgery Offices
  • Gyms, Fitness and Athletic Centers
  • Rehabilitation Facilities
  • Physical Therapy Clinics
  • Pain Management Centers
  • Sporting Events for Athletes
  • Yoga Studios or Stretch Bars
  • Mobile Massage & more!

What can I expect?

Massage Therapy is its own art form that has the power to heal. In our program, you’ll learn how to reduce pressure and pain with your very own hands using a variety of massage techniques on different parts of the body:

  • Swedish Massage
  • Deep Tissue Massage
  • Sports and Event Massage
  • Massage for Special Populations
  • Seated (Chair) Massage
  • Hydrotherapy
  • Reflexology
  • Stretching & More!
