About AI Tools Library

Through a Perkins grant and in partnership with the Commonwealth, the SKILLS Collaborative at UofL tested a series of AI systems for Career and Technical Education (CTE) instructors. The CTE instructors and the UofL team identified these tools as useful ones for CTE teaching and learning environments.

 This listing is not an official endorsement or sponsorship of these tools.
