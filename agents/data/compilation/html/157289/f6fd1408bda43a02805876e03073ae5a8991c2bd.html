AI Education Tools

AI tools present a revolutionary opportunity in education. Today, students and teachers alike can leverage technology to increase access, improve outcomes and empower the next generation towards innovation. These are just a few tools among many currently available, with new ones being created every day.

Resources

ChatGPT is an integrated Large Language Model (LLM) that can assist in creating lesson plans and outlines based on a prompt.

MagicSchool creates interactive lessons, activities and resources. 

Eduaide connects K-12 teachers and students with resources to support effective learning

Perplexity connects users with cited information and resources from any topic based on a prompt.

Gemini is an integrated Large Language Model (LLM) that can use Google’s database to create lesson plans and outlines from a prompt.

TeachFX is an AI-driven tool that can provide real time or reflective feedback by processing the audio from a lesson to measure student engagement and interactions.

SlidesGo offers free presentation templates and AI assisted content generation for Google slides and Microsoft PowerPoint.

Prezi allows users to create dynamic, non-linear presentations with a zoomable canvas.

PopAI assists users in creating visually appealing presentations.

Synthesia creates AI voiceover presentations, bringing slides to life with an AI narrator.

Gamma creates and modifies slides using prompts, allows for highly specific inputs.

Presentations.ai create large presentations slides with minimal inputs or create slides from a file upload (PDF, MP4, etc.).

OwlLift creates dynamic in class activities from prompts and questions.

PopAI creates class activities from a file upload or prompting.

Curipod creates class activities in the form of interactive games.

Diffit finds resources and creates activities based on prompts, file upload (PDF, MP4, etc.) or a URL (web article, online video, etc.).

Parlay is an online platform for written and video discussions that uses AI to provide key insights and improve student engagement.

Khan Academy has a library of free courses, lessons and practice questions for all subjects.

Khanmigo - integrates ChatGPT to create a live individual tutor for students to utilize at a low cost.

OwlLift is a collaboration tool for group projects that can assist with teamwork and project management.

Magic School - supports group projects with collaboration tools and resource sharing.

CoGrader is an AI assessment tool to help grade and provide feedback on individual assignments.

EssayGrader is an automated grading system to evaluate written work and provide feedback.

Brisk Teaching provides grading tools and resources for educators to give detailed feedback to students.

TurnItIn is the most widely used plagiarism detection tool for originality and AI detection.

QuillBot is an AI specific plagiarism detection tool.

Scribbr is an AI specific plagiarism detection tool.

PlagiarismDetector - compares work to a wide database of sources and checks for AI use.

Quizizz easily create interactive quizzes and assessments for students from prompts, files, or URL. Watch our tutorial video

QuestionWell - generates high quality questions and assessments quickly from assisted prompting. Watch our tutorial video

QuizGecko creates questions and assessments based on prompts, files, URLs, or a topic.

PopAI - produces unique and engaging questions for assessments based on prompts or documents.

Brisk Teaching is a Chrome extension that can help generating questions and content for educators.

Magic School creates critical thinking questions from prompts, videos, URLs, and document uploads. Watch our tutorial video

Eightify quickly summarizes videos of any length, making it easier to grasp key points quickly.

NoteGPT summarizes YouTube videos from link to quickly extract key points and takeaways.

PDF.ai locates information based on a prompt within massive PDFs. It can summarize and provide key insights of the document.

PopAI - extracts key points and summarizes media (PDF, MP4, MP3).

Canva is a user-friendly graphic design program with photo editing and creating capabilities.  Watch our tutorial video

DALL-E is an AI model integrated with ChatGPT to generate images.

Adobe Firefly is a high quality text to image generator with the ability to modify from follow up prompts.

Leonardo is a standalone tool that creates digital art and images.

CrayonAI creates artistic images and illustrations.

Fliki is a text-to-video platform that convert articles and text into video.

VEED - is a ChatGPT plug in to create short form videos from prompts.

Invideo - is an online video editor that quickly creates professional videos.

Speechify is a text-to-speech tool with highly realistic voices.

Synthesia - is an AI video creation with integrated voiceover.

LOVO - is a text-to-speech with video sync and other integrated tools.

Grammarly is a writing assistant that improves grammar and gives style- and tone-specific feedback.

Jenny - is a writing assistant that provides help along the way, with an AI chat tool that asks questions as you research and write.

Gemini is an AI software that can help with editing, rephrasing, and tone.

ChatGPT creates and modifies text, gives feedback, and rewords text.

Diffit - rewords text to modify reading level, which is used for understanding text.

Brisk Teaching - modifies reading level for materials to a different age or grade level.

This listing is not an official endorsement or sponsorship of these tools.
