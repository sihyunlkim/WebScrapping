Join us for an engaging one-hour virtual workshop where participants will explore innovative approaches to using Generative AI tools in course design. Whether you are drafting your syllabus or creating supporting materials, this workshop offers practical insights and hands-on guidance to integrate AI in ways that align with your teaching goals. Explore strategies for using G-AI to streamline some of your course design processes. Discuss how to balance AI-generated content with your instructional voice and style. This interactive session on Microsoft Teams is ideal for educators looking to incorporate AI into their course development workflow, ensuring a blend of efficiency, creativity, and pedagogical integrity.
