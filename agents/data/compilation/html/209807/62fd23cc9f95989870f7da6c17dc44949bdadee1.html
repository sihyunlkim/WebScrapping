Provost and Executive Vice President for Academic Affairs Search

The Search Committee members extend their thanks to everyone who participated in the search for PSU's next Provost and Executive Vice President for Academic Affairs. Your engagement and feedback have played a vital role informing the Search Committee's recommendations to President Cudd. (Feedback forms closed Friday, February 13.) 

The individual selected to serve in this role will act as Portland State University's Chief Academic Officer and second-in-command to the President. The Provost will play a defining role in implementing the University’s new strategic plan, Future in Focus, and advancing PSU’s mission of inclusive student success, academic innovation, and positive public impact throughout our region. This leader will be charged with advancing academic excellence, empowering faculty, fostering research, supporting student learning, and overseeing the entire student experience, including admissions, enrollment management, student affairs, and advising.
