UC San Diego's Support of AI Services

UC San Diego is committed to harnessing Artificial Intelligence in a financially responsible and ethically sound manner.

Secure AI Solutions

UC San Diego is building an AI-enabled campus where students, faculty, and staff can confidently leverage trusted, secure, and responsible AI tools to enhance research, improve operations, and expand educational opportunities while protecting ethics, equity, and university values. Explore, access, and learn about AI services & resources across UC San Diego at the TritonAI site, your one-stop AI hub.

TritonGPT, our preferred Generative AI platform, is securely hosted at the San Diego Supercomputer Center. By leveraging a combination of open-source large language models (LLMs) and secure on-premise hosting, TritonGPT ensures that UC San Diego maintains full control over our data. Find out more about TritonGPT

Alongside TritonGPT, we are actively evaluating and supporting AI services integrated into our supported platforms. Our evaluation criteria reflect the University of California's comprehensive data protection and security policies. These policies encompass Electronic Information Security, Electronic Communication, Export Control, and FERPA standards.

TritonGPT

TritonGPT is a powerful tool that can help you navigate UC San Diego's policies, procedures, and campus life. It can provide you with information and resources related to campus service and more. It's like having a personal assistant who knows a lot about UC San Diego.

Use TritonGPT

AI Services from supported vendors

AI services for campus employees

Zoom AI Companion

Zoom AI Companion features include meeting transcription, summarization, Q&A, and more. Features need to be enabled via the Zoom web portal. Users are notified when a meeting is AI-enhanced.

Enable Zoom AI Companion

Microsoft Copilot with Data Protection

Copilot with Data Protection is a chatbot that provides a simple chat experience. It supports things like image generation, PDF summarization, content generation, and fact retrieval.

Use Copilot

Copilot for Microsoft 365

Copilot for Microsoft 365 is an AI-powered productivity tool that works alongside popular Microsoft 365 Apps such as Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, Teams, and more. This product is recharge and currently in a soft launch.

Find out more

Google Gemini & NotebookLM

Google Gemini is a multimodal AI model powering Google's AI chatbot and integrated across its products, capable of understanding and generating diverse data. NotebookLM is an AI-powered research and note-taking tool using Gemini to help users interact with their uploaded documents, offering summarization, Q&A, idea generation, and audio overviews.

Find out more
