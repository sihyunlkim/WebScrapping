Choose Integrity

At UC San Diego, fostering a culture of integrity is a collective responsibility. It's not just a set of rules; it's a commitment from each member of our community to stand up for what's right and speak out against cheating. We believe in the power of collective action to shape a campus environment where integrity thrives. 

Students, your voice matters. Don't let cheating go unchallenged. Silence only empowers a culture of dishonesty. Be courageous—stand up for what you know is right.

Faculty, you play an important role in creating classrooms of integrity. Actively foster an environment that encourages honest learning and respond to cheating. Your dedication to academic integrity helps shape the character of our community. 

Choosing integrity to empower a culture of integrity can look different for each member of our community. Whether you are a student or instructor, there are many options you can choose. If you’re interested in learning how you can choose integrity, check out our Top 10 Tips for Students or Top 10 Tips for Instructors.
