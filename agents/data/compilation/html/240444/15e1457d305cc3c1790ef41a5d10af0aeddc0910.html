About the Center

The Tommy G. Thompson Center on Public Leadership is a nonpartisan institute within the University of Wisconsin System. It was established in 2017 by act of the Wisconsin Legislature to facilitate research, teaching, outreach, and needed policy reforms regarding effective public leadership that improves the practice of American government.
