As artificial intelligence becomes increasingly woven into teaching, learning and research, questions about academic integrity are more timely and more complex than ever. Join students, faculty and the director of UNC's Office of Student Conduct for a thoughtful, balanced conversation about how AI is shaping academic work, where opportunities and risks lie, and how our shared values can guide responsible use. Audience questions and participation are encouraged.

 

This is an in‑person only panel taking place in the Wilson Library (Pleasants Family Assembly Room) and will include opportunities for Q&A, discussion, and networking.

 

This event is offered by the University Library and the Centre for Faculty Excellence. You can register through the library calendar.
