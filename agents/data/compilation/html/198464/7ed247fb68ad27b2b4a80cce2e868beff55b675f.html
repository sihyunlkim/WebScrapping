AI Prompt tools

There are AI tools designed specifically to help users generate prompts for other AI systems, enhancing their ability to produce high-quality outputs. Here are some of the most prominent and promising AI prompt tools currently available:

  • PromptPerfect
    Features: Optimizes and enhances prompts to improve AI model performance. Offers suggestions and modifications to maximize output quality.
    Use Cases: Improving the effectiveness of prompts for language models like GPT-3 and GPT-4, aiding in content generation and fine-tuning responses.
  • AIPRM (AI Prompt Manager)
    Features: Provides a browser extension to manage, organize, and share prompts for AI models like ChatGPT. Includes community-driven suggestions and prompt templates.
    Use Cases: Managing a collection of prompts, sharing best practices within a team, and improving AI-driven workflows.
  • Prompt Engineering (OpenAI)
    Features: Provides guidelines, examples, and best practices for crafting effective prompts for OpenAI models.
    Use Cases: Educational resource for developers and researchers looking to maximize the performance of AI models through effective prompt engineering.

​​​​​​AI Should be Used to Supplement and Improve Your Work, Not Replace It

  • Academic Integrity: Use AI tools ethically, ensuring that your work aligns with ECU's Student Code of Conduct which mandates honesty and integrity.
  • Supplementary Aid: Utilize AI as an aid, not a substitute, ensuring that you understand and contribute to the assignment.
  • Proper Attribution: Provide proper attribution for AI contributions to avoid plagiarism.
  • Instructor Consultation: Seek guidance from instructors on the acceptable use of AI tools.
  • Educational Objectives: Maintain the integrity and educational objectives of assignments by adhering to these guidelines.

AI Tools Getting Started

  • Claude (Ethical AI Use)
    Claude is an AI chatbot designed by Anthropic to prioritize ethical considerations and safe interactions, making it suitable for users concerned about the ethical implications of AI use. It offers both free and paid versions, with the paid version providing enhanced capabilities, such as advanced language understanding, more nuanced responses, and priority support. Claude is ideal for those seeking a conversational AI that focuses on responsible usage while assisting with tasks like content creation, research, and everyday queries; also good for coding. Free version with limited functionality and paid versions available.
  • FreedomGPT
    A privacy-focused AI chatbot that runs locally on your computer, providing faster response times and ensuring that your data is not shared with large tech companies. It is free to use and can be trained on your own data, allowing for a more personalized and secure user experience. Freedom GPT is suitable for users who prioritize data privacy while leveraging AI for tasks like content creation, coding assistance, and conversational interactions.
  • CHAT GPT
    ChatGPT, developed by OpenAI, is an AI language model that generates human-like text based on user prompts, useful for tasks such as drafting emails, writing code, and content creation. It has both free and paid versions; the free version offers basic features, while the paid ChatGPT Plus provides enhanced capabilities and priority access during peak times. Other GPTs include DALL·E for image generation, Codex for coding assistance, and Whisper for speech-to-text conversion.
  • Forefront (USE ChatGPT-4 for Free)
    provides free access to advanced language models like ChatGPT-4 and 3.5, along with other AI models. It allows users to create and use personas, generate images, and share content with colleagues. Forefront is ideal for users who need versatile AI tools for various tasks, including content creation, image generation, and collaborative projects. The platform offers both free and enhanced paid versions, with the paid version offering additional features and capabilities.
  • Google Gemini
    Gemini is Google's advanced AI model designed for a variety of tasks, including natural language understanding, translation, and summarization. It comes in both free and paid versions, with the paid version offering enhanced features and higher usage limits. Other capabilities of Gemini include image recognition, data analysis, and integration with Google's suite of tools for a comprehensive AI experience.
  • Microsoft Copilot
    Microsoft Copilot is an AI-powered assistant integrated into Microsoft 365 applications, enhancing productivity by providing intelligent suggestions and automating repetitive tasks. It comes in both free and paid versions, with the paid version offering more advanced features and greater capabilities. Other functionalities of Microsoft Copilot include generating content, summarizing documents, creating data visualizations, and integrating seamlessly with tools like Word, Excel, and PowerPoint.
  • AgentGPT
    AgentGPT is a browser-based tool that enables users to assemble, configure, and deploy autonomous AI agents that learn and perform tasks independently without requiring prompts. It offers a straightforward setup and user-friendly interface, making it accessible for both beginners and advanced users. This tool is ideal for automating complex workflows, enhancing productivity, and exploring advanced AI applications in a hands-on manner.
  • Jasper (Marketing)
    AgentGPT is a browser-based tool that enables users to assemble, configure, and deploy autonomous AI agents that learn and perform tasks independently without requiring prompts. It offers a straightforward setup and user-friendly interface, making it accessible for both beginners and advanced users. This tool is ideal for automating complex workflows, enhancing productivity, and exploring advanced AI applications in a hands-on manner.
  • INK (Digital Marketing)
    INK AI is an AI-powered writing and content optimization tool designed to help users create high-quality, SEO-friendly content efficiently. It offers both free and paid versions, with the paid version providing advanced features such as in-depth SEO recommendations, keyword optimization, and content performance analytics. INK AI assists with tasks like blog writing, website content, and marketing copy, aiming to improve search engine rankings and maximize audience engagement through AI-driven insights and suggestions.
  • Sudowrite (Creative Writing)
    Sudowrite is an AI writing assistant tailored for creative writers, offering tools to help with brainstorming, drafting, and editing stories. It features both free and paid versions, with the paid version providing advanced functionalities like enhanced story development, character creation assistance, and detailed feedback. Sudowrite is designed to boost creativity and productivity for novelists, screenwriters, and other storytellers by leveraging AI to generate ideas, overcome writer's block, and refine their writing.
  • Duck.ai
    Duck.ai is a free and private AI chat service launched by DuckDuckGo, a company known for its focus on online privacy

Writing AI Tools

  • Writefull
    Purpose: AI-powered assistant for academic and research writing.
    Features: Automated paraphrasing, abstract and title generation, copyediting.
    Training: Based on millions of scholarly journal articles.
    Versions: Free and paid, with the paid version offering advanced features.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers aiming to improve the quality and clarity of their academic writing.
  • Humata
    Purpose: AI tool for summarizing and simplifying complex technical papers.
    Features: Summarizes lengthy papers, explains complex content simply, and creates new writing based on your files.
    Versions: Both free and paid versions available.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers needing to quickly understand and work with complex academic and technical documents.
  • QuillBot
    Purpose: AI-powered writing tool for enhancing text.
    Features: Paraphrasing, grammar checking, citation generation, and explanation of corrections.
    Versions: Free and paid versions, with advanced features in the paid version.
    Ideal for: Students and writers looking to improve writing quality and learn from their mistakes.
  • JenniAI
    Purpose: AI research and writing assistant.
    Features: Sentence completion, tone and style creation, multi-style citations, paraphrasing, and rewriting.
    Versions: Both free and paid versions available.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers needing comprehensive support in writing and editing academic content.
  • PaperPal
    Purpose: AI tool for enhancing academic writing.
    Features: Real-time, subject-specific language suggestions to improve writing speed and quality.
    Versions: Both free and paid versions available.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers looking to write more effectively and efficiently in their specific academic fields.

Research AI Tools

  • Elicit: The AI Research Assistant:
    Purpose: Automates research workflows and assists with literature reviews.
    Features:
    Finds relevant papers without perfect keyword match.
    Summarizes takeaways specific to your research question.
    Extracts key information from scholarly papers.
    Aids in brainstorming, summarization, and text classification.
    Utility: Enhances research efficiency and effectiveness.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers conducting comprehensive literature reviews and academic research.
  • ChatPDF
    Purpose: AI tool for interacting with and understanding PDF documents.
    Features:
    Summarizes PDF content.
    Answers questions about the document.
    Provides key insights and explanations.
    Utility: Facilitates efficient comprehension and analysis of academic papers.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers needing to quickly understand and interact with academic PDFs.
  • Scholarcy
    Purpose: AI tool for summarizing and extracting key information from academic papers.
    Features:
    Condenses papers into referenced summaries.
    Highlights key claims, statistics, tables, and images.
    Provides quick navigation to important sections.
    Utility: Speeds up the process of understanding and reviewing academic papers.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers needing to quickly grasp core ideas and essential information from scholarly articles.
  • Consensus:
    Purpose: AI-driven search engine for extracting and distilling findings from scientific research.
    Features:
    Reads and interprets scientific papers.
    Extracts key results and findings.
    Provides summarized insights directly from research papers.
    Utility: Facilitates quick access to critical research findings.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers looking for key results from scientific literature without extensive reading.
  • Research Rabbit
    Purpose: AI-powered citation-based literature mapping tool.
    Features:
    Allows users to input seed papers to find related research.
    Visualizes networks of relevant papers.
    Helps discover connections and trends in the literature.
    Utility: Enhances the process of finding and understanding related academic work.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers looking to expand their literature review and explore interconnected research topics.
  • Explainpaper
    Purpose: AI-powered citation-based literature mapping tool.
    Features:
    Allows users to input seed papers to find related research.
    Visualizes networks of relevant papers.
    Helps discover connections and trends in the literature.
    Utility: Enhances the process of finding and understanding related academic work.
    Ideal for: Students and researchers looking to expand their literature review and explore interconnected research topics.
