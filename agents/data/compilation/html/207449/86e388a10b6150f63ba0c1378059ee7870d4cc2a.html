Get Involved 

Are you interested in learning more about Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its role in education? Join OCCC's AI Affinity Group to explore AI-related topics and discussions with fellow faculty and staff. 

The AI Affinity Group provides members:

  • A platform to share knowledge and AI tools.
  • Information about internal training opportunities from the Center for Learning & Teaching (CLT).
  • Discussion about the latest AI educational news and professional development opportunities.
  • Exploration of the potential applications of AI on campus.
  • A networking opportunity with colleagues who share similar interests.

Join Our Monthly Meetings on Microsoft Teams:

Faculty and staff interested in AI are invited to join any future meetings. Please contact the Center for Learning and Teaching or keep an eye on your OCCC email inbox for more information on joining the Teams channel and meeting times.

Copyright

What Are The Guidelines?

As of Summer 2025, laws regarding the use of AI in the creation of materials are still evolving. Additionally, most Generative AI Platforms use preexisting content on the internet to create the repositories they need to produce responses. There are several ongoing lawsuits which may impact whether or not content generated from these products fall under Fair Use or should be considered copyright infringement.
