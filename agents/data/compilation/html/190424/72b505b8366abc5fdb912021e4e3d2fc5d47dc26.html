    Grounded in the traditions of medicine and more than 125 years of teaching excellence, Weill Cornell Medical College prepares leaders who advance patient care in academic medicine and beyond. By embracing lifelong learning, leveraging partnerships, and harnessing health technology and AI, we equip our graduates to shape the future of medicine with purpose.
