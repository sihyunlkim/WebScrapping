Education

The WCM Institute of AI for Digital Health is committed to excellence in educational activities to advance medicine through the maintenance of scientific scholarship. Our director Dr. Fei Wang is offering the AI in Medicine class at Weill Cornell Medicine and the Machine Learning for Health class at Cornell Tech. We also host seminar series presented by eminent scholars in the field to present their cutting-edge research on AI for digital health. Our team members are invited to deliver presentations at other institutions and academic venues to disseminate our research.
