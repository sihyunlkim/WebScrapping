Institute of Artificial Intelligence for Digital Health

The mission of AIDH is to advance individualized health and healthcare with data-driven insights derived from applying artificial intelligence technologies. The institute will establish a community of experts across multiple disciplines in computational science and clinical medicine, facilitate collaborations, promote multi-disciplinary research, and engage in entrepreneurship and technology commercialization. The institute is housed within the Department of Population Health Sciences and will collaborate directly with faculty at Cornell Tech and the Cornell Ann S. Bowers College of Computing and Information Science.
