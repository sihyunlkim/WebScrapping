The talk will discuss the journey to a learning health system, where culture, informatics, incentives, and science are combined to consistently learn from and improve a health system’s outcomes. The advent of new capabilities from multimodal and generative AI presents a significant opportunity to accelerate these changes but also require investments and organization.
