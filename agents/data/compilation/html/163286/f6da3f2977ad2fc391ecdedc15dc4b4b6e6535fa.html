Resources

For Faculty, Staff & Students

UMD offers a wealth of resources and tools for using AI. UMD community members can make the best use of AI tools when they understand both the tools' benefits and limitations. Here we compile resources about tools themselves, how to use them, and some of the larger considerations around them.

Available Services

Tools

TerpAI

TerpAI is a powerful GenAI tool designed to simplify your interactions with technology and enhance your daily tasks. Acting as your AI Agent, thought partner, and educational resource, TerpAI helps you manage projects and research topics and get quick answers with ease. It's invaluable for students and educators, providing clarity on complex concepts and assisting with study guides and lesson plans. 

More about TerpAI

Tools

UMD Virtual Agent

The UMD Virtual Agent is a Generative AI (GenAI) chatbot. UMD Virtual Agents are an advanced type of artificial intelligence that uses machine learning, particularly natural language processing (NLP), to engage in human-like conversations. Unlike traditional rule-based chatbots, which rely on predefined scripts and responses, GenAI chatbots generate responses in real-time based on the context and content of the conversation. 

More on UMD Virtual Agent

President's Commission on Artificial Intelligence

The President's Commission on Artificial Intelligence (AI) was established in November 2023. This multidisciplinary commission is charged with exploring, evaluating, and providing recommendations on the responsible development, deployment, and ethical use of artificial intelligence (AI) technologies within the university community.
