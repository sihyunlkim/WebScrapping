Academic Integrity Seminar Instructions

The Code of Academic Integrity requires students wanting to petition to remove the "X" from their transcript complete an Academic Integrity Seminar. Students who are eligible to petition to remove their "X" will complete the sanction as originally stated in their sanction letter through the Office of Student Conduct. You should email AIseminar@umd.edu to obtain the AI Seminar materials.
