    CAIRE: Center for Artificial Intelligence Research and Education

    Empowering the Next Generation of AI Leaders

    CAIRE (Center for Artificial Intelligence Research and Education) was established by Dr. Sam Afsoos at the University of North America to advance the frontiers of artificial intelligence through cutting-edge research, innovative education, and real-world applications. Our mission is to equip students, professionals, and businesses with the knowledge and skills needed to harness the transformative power of AI.

    What We Do:

    • Research and Development: Pioneering AI technologies, including deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision.
    • Education and Training: Offering comprehensive AI and machine learning courses for college students, professionals, and researchers. We also provide specialized AI Executive Training Programs for corporate executives and industry leaders, empowering them to leverage AI for strategic growth and innovation in their organizations.
    • Industry Collaboration: Partnering with businesses to drive AI innovation and foster industry-ready talent.
    • Community Impact: Promoting responsible AI use through workshops, seminars, and thought leadership.

    Join us in shaping the future of artificial intelligence. Explore our programs and become part of a vibrant community of AI innovators.
