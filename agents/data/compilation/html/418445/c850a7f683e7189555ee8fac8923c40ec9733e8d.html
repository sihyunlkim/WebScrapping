Welcome

Welcome to the Jefferson County-Dubois Area Vocational-Technical School (Jeff Tech), a comprehensive career and technical high school and adult education program. We help our students attain the educational experiences, credentials, and training they need to succeed in a high-demand career upon graduation. While you’re visiting our online home, please explore the many reasons Jeff Tech is the school of choice. From our high school programs, where we help students prepare for careers while they work toward their diploma, to our adult education programs, helping adults become more successful in the workplace, our programs are second to none. 

A Message from Our Director

Greetings Jeff Tech family, 

I am pleased to welcome you to Jeff Tech. Our diverse student body is made up of high school students and adults who come from many walks of life, and we couldn’t be more proud of their accomplishments both during their time here at our school and after graduation. Our dedicated team of educators works hard every day to bring the best possible learning opportunities to our student body, and it shows. At Jeff Tech, we endeavor to meet the needs of our community by offering courses designed to provide skills for entry-level employment and opportunities for postsecondary education. Our mission is to ensure our students receive the opportunity for additional educational experiences, graduate with industry-recognized credentials, acquire training in emerging career trends, and attain placement in a high-demand career. We are grateful that each of you is a part of our family, and we look forward to the continued successes of all Jeff Tech students.

Sincerely,

Barry Fillman

Barry Fillman
Administrative Director
