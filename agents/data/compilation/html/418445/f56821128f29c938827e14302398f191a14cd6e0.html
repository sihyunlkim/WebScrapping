Administration & Staff

The Jeff Tech administrative team is available to assist you in such matters as enrollment, student and parent concerns, technology and business matters, post-secondary learning opportunities, as well as building use. Please feel free to contact our management team along with the rest of our staff members directly.

Click on the ‘send email’ link below a staff member’s name to send an email, or call (814) 653-8265 followed by the appropriate extension. We’re here to help!
