Teaching with AI

Event Date
Thursday August 15th 2024 - 9:00 AM
Location
Uptown Campus
Building
Howard-Tilton Memorial Library

 

Teaching with AI is on the minds of most faculty as we head into the fall term. This collection of workshops will offer faculty practical guidance on how to utilize AI to their advantage, engage students, and manage the potential negative impacts of AI on student learning. 

 

Thursday, August 15th | 8:30am to 1pm

 

RSVP to Attend

 

Rethinking Assessment and Accessibility in AI
(Lead by CELT Representative)

This workshop explores how AI is transforming the way we assess student learning, with a focus on promoting accessibility and inclusivity. We'll dive into the opportunities and challenges of AI’s use in assessment and uncover how to design effective strategies that promote critical thinking and ensure all students can demonstrate their knowledge and skills.

 

Learning Outcomes:

  • Identify the impact of AI on Assessment
  • Describe ways to create/recreate assessment for critical thinking
  • Discuss accessibility AI tools for effective teaching and learning.

 

 

 

 

 

Harnessing AI for Course Development – Create Your Digital Teaching Assistant
(Lead by ILC Representative)

 

Join us for an innovative and interactive workshop designed exclusively for Tulane University faculty! Discover the power of AI in course development by creating your very own digital Teaching Assistant. In this guided session, you will learn how to use AI to streamline and enhance your course design process. With our follow-along guide and a specially crafted Super Prompt, you will train an AI chatbot to specialize as a TA for your specific course.

 

This AI-powered TA will assist you with a wide range of tasks, including:

  • Creating detailed course outlines
  • Developing learning objectives and outcomes
  • Designing engaging quiz questions and assignments
  • Crafting in-class activities and discussion topics
  • Writing compelling course descriptions and introductions
  • And much more!

Empower yourself with the latest in AI technology to enhance your teaching and save time on course preparation. Don't miss this opportunity to revolutionize your approach to course development!

 

 

 

 

 

 

AI as Co-Professor: Analyzing Your Syllabus and Planning Your Course
(CAIDs Representative)

 

In this session, participants will learn how to leverage AI as a "co-professor" to enhance their course design and planning. Participants will explore AI-powered tools and strategies to analyze their syllabi and create teaching activities and materials in alignment with learning objectives.

 

Join us for an interactive workshop where you'll learn how to collaborate with AI to analyze your syllabus and plan your course! Bring your laptop and a syllabus!
