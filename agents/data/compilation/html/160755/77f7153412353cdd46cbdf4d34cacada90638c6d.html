AI: How it Works and How to Work with IT, a Practical Workshop for Students
Event Date
Fri, Oct 11 2024, 1 - 3pm
Howard-Tilton Memorial Library, B08
Master the use of the most powerful new AI tools through a hands-on workshop demonstrating how to use the latest versions of ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, and Meta AI to perform different tasks. Bring your computer and learn:
  • How to use the most important new AI tools
  • Which AI tools to use when
  • Enough about how AI works to understand how to use it well
  • Best practices for prompting
  • How to personalize AI tools to your preferences and use
The workshop is given by Professor Knud E. Berthelsen who has developed and teaches DATA 2150 - Artificial Intelligence Tools and AI workshops for Tulane faculty, staff, and students.

New Orleans, LA 70118
