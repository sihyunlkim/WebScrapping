ChatGPT: The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly

Date: 
November 13, 2024

Join us as the UC Merced Library presentsChatGPT: The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly!

"ChatGPT: The Good, The Bad, The Ugly" presents an exploration of generative AI technology, examining its benefits, drawbacks, and ethical considerations. Joe and Bronwen, librarians from UC Merced, engage students in critical discussions on the ethical implications of AI, motivated by a commitment to academic integrity and environmental stewardship.

This event is for the Honors Program and Living Learning Communities, but open to all. Bring a friend!
