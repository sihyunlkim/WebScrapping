Online Reference

The Collaborative Online Reference develop in Dewey University to achieve shared goals in technology and communications projects. COR is a web project that collect internet data, people collaborations, research, free books, electronics references, magazines and audiovisual content to sharing knowledge, learning and building a creative way to increase the academic community success.
