Institutional Advisory Board

At CCC...

At Cass Career Center, we believe in the power of collaboration to shape the future of Career and Technical Education (CTE). The CCC Institutional Advisory Board (IAB) is the heart of this mission—bringing together industry experts, passionate educators, student leaders, and dedicated community members to guide and grow the programs and support the overall direction of the organization to best empower students and transform lives.

What is the Institutional Advisory Board?

The Cass Career Center IAB is driven by a vision to help students thrive by gaining the skills they need for a competitive workforce. At its core, the board consists of diverse professionals, educators, students, and advocates who provide strategic guidance to ensure CCC continues to meet the evolving needs of both students and the community.

Objectives of the CCC IAB

The board's core mission focuses on three key pillars:

  • Shifting Perceptions: Changing how people view Career and Technical Education (CTE) by showcasing its importance in building a strong, skilled workforce. This includes highlighting success stories, illustrating how CTE prepares students for high-demand, well-paying careers, and breaking down outdated stereotypes that undervalue career and technical education. 
  • Expanding Outreach: Broadening awareness of CTE programs to engage new audiences, such as parents, educators, prospective students, and community leaders, while fostering relationships with industry partners. This involves creating targeted marketing campaigns, hosting events or workshops, and leveraging social media to connect with a wider audience that can support and benefit from CTE initiatives. 
  • Enhancing Workforce Development: Equipping students with the practical skills and knowledge they need to succeed in today’s job market. This includes aligning CTE curricula with industry standards, facilitating hands-on training opportunities, and forming partnerships with businesses to ensure students are prepared to meet real-world demands, ultimately bridging the gap between education and employment.

Why Join the CCC IAB?

Checklist to Become a Partner

Becoming a member of the CCC IAB means making a lasting difference in students' lives while supporting your community. Here's what we’re looking for in new members:

✔Industry Expertise: Share your knowledge and insights with students and staff.

✔Passion for CTE: Advocate for the importance of technical education in modern careers.

✔Community Engagement: Actively collaborate to create impactful opportunities for students.

✔Strategic Vision: Help guide the direction of CCC’s innovative initiatives.

Interested in driving change?

The CCC Institutional Advisory Board is seeking dedicated professionals to join its mission. Whether you're an industry leader or simply passionate about making a meaningful impact, your contributions can help shape the future of the next generation of skilled professionals. We’re always looking to expand our team and currently are seeking new members specifically in areas like Automotive, Welding, Fire Science/EMT, Marketing, and CJ/CSI.

To learn more, contact CCC Director Anne Hickman at anne.hickman@harrisonvilleschools.org to schedule a meeting.

Follow us!

Initiatives Driven by the IAB

  • Shifting Perceptions
  • Outreach Strategies
  • Workforce Development

Shifting Perceptions

  • Created a comprehensive CCC Career Pathways document to ensure clear communication of our programs and opportunities while promoting consistent messaging across all platforms.
  • Prioritizing the enhancement of CCC website content to clearly showcase and highlight our values, mission, and opportunities and strategically provide anticipated student outcomes, resulting in a stronger foundation for effective messaging.
  • Launched the Workforce Alliance Network to showcase the tangible benefits for businesses and organizations that invest their time and resources in partnerships with CCC, emphasizing positive outcomes and collaborative success.
  • Utilizing the expertise of CCC Student Ambassadors from each program area and all of our sending schools to support recruitment efforts. 

Outreach Strategies

  • Designed and implemented innovative outreach initiatives to engage our current community and attract prospective students.
  • Launched a campaign called "The Cass Experience," showcasing student perspectives at CCC to share their stories and highlight their unique experiences.
  • Added a student intern who's responsible for running our student-focused TikTok account. 

Workforce Development

  • Worked closely with IAB committee members to identify critical workforce development gaps and assess industry needs.
  • Creating more opportunities for students to attend networking events, such as Chamber of Commerce meetings and industry gatherings, helping them build professional connections.
  • Expanding service-learning projects that address real-world challenges, providing students with hands-on experience and sharpening problem-solving skills essential for today’s job market.
  • Expanding opportunities for summer programs, job shadowing, and internships, resulting in early workplace exposure, equipping them with valuable experience and better preparing them for future career success.
