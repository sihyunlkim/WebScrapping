Grants and Scholarships

Federal/State Grants

Federal Pell Grant

  • Award Amount: 2026-2027 award amounts have not been announced
  • Eligibility: Amount received depends on Expected Family Contribution (EFC)
  • Additional Information: Award Amount will be given to admitted students when award letter is created in May 
  • Deadline: Complete FAFSA by March 31

Access MO Grant

  • Award Amount: Maximum $1300 | Minimum $300 (subject to change)
  • Eligibility: Amount received depends on Expected Family Contribution (EFC)
  • Additional Information: Award Amount will be given to admitted students when award letter is created in May 
  • Deadline: Complete FAFSA by February 1

Missouri Fast Track Workforce Incentive Grant

  • Award Amount: Awards will be for any remaining tuition and fee costs not covered by other state and federal student aid programs. 
  • Eligibility: View eligibility requirements here 
  • Additional Information: Visit Financial Aid Office for more information
  • Deadline: 

Scholarships

A+ Scholarship

  • Award Amount: Tuition and Fee reimbursement
  • Eligibility: Student must graduate from qualified A+ school within the last two years. Fulfill all A+ requirements. 
  • Additional Information: Inform Financial Aid Office if you plan to use A+
  • Deadline: 

 Horatio Alger National Career & Tech Scholarship

  • Award Amount: Up to $2500
  • Eligibility: View requirements here 
  • Additional Information: 
  • Deadline: Application opens March 15  |  Application closes June 1 

Cass Community Health Foundation Scholarship

  • Award Amount: Scholarships range between $1,000 and $2,500 each and are for tuition only. 
  • Eligibility and Additional Information: contact the Adult Education Coordinator at Cass Career Center 
  • Deadline: March 1 

 Cass Regional Medical Center Scholarships - Opens Mid-November

  • Eligibility and Additional Information: contact the Adult Education Coordinator at Cass Career Center 

 Brave of Heart Scholarship

  • Award Amount: Up to $25,000 annually 
  • Eligibility: Be a child, spouse or domestic partner of a frontline healthcare worker who lost their life to a COVID-19-related cause. Additional requirements here 
  • Additional Information: 
  • Deadline: Application is closed. Pre-register here to receive information if application becomes available.
