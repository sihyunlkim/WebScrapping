Why Choose Penta?

Since 1965, Penta Career Center has shaped the careers of more than 30,000 professionals.  You, too, can benefit from Penta's career-technical training.
 
Penta provides a focused, career-oriented education for high school students who wish to achieve success and a competitive advantage in a chosen career, pursue additional certifications, attend two or four-year colleges, join the military, or journey wherever their paths may lead.  
 
Penta serves the following school districts:  Anthony Wayne, Benton-Carroll-Salem, Bowling Green, Eastwood, Elmwood, Genoa, Lake, Maumee, North Baltimore, Northwood, Otsego, Perryburg, Rossford, Springfield, Swanton, and Woodmore.
