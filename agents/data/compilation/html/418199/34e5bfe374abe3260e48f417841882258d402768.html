2016 Penta Academic Achievement Award Recipients

Congratulations to the following seniors who were recognized with the 2016 Academic Achievement Award. The students were honored during a special evening ceremony at Penta on April 21. Seniors who received the award maintained a 3.25 grade point average for the first three quarters of the current school year. Special thanks to Dr. Mike Bower, president of Owens Community College, for giving the keynote address and Toledo Building Services for sponsoring the award pins. Great job students!
