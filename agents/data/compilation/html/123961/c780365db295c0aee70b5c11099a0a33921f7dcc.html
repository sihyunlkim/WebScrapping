    The Office of Academic Integrity

    Mission

    Our mission is to foster an educational environment where integrity, learning, and academic excellence thrive.

    By actively engaging with students, staff, and faculty, we seek to cultivate a shared commitment to upholding the values of integrity and accountability within the university community. Through our educational programs and collaborative efforts, we emphasize the importance of academic honesty and contribute to the overall success and reputation of the university.
