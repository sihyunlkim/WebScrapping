COMING SOON: Diverse Learning Environments Survey for RVC Students!

    COMING SOON: Diverse Learning Environments Survey for RVC Students!

    Posted from Mon Mar 7, 2022 to Tue Mar 15, 2022.

    RVC Student Climate Survey
    RVC wants to hear from you because your voice matters! 

    The DEI Office will be implementing the Diverse Learning Environments (DLE) Survey on March 16 to RVC students who are at least 18 years-old and have completed at least 24 credit hours at RVC. Responses will help improve the student experience at RVC. 

    • First 400 students to participate will get a $10 gift card to the RVC Bookstore! 
    • Every one will be entered for a chance to win an HP 11.6" Chromebook!

    Check you RVC email on March 16 for the suvey link!

    Questions? Contact the Office of Diversity, Equity & Inclusion at RVC-DEI@RockValleyCollege.edu.
