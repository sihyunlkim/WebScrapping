News Release Request

News Release Request

We develop new releases for stories that have the best chance of attracting positive media coverage for our institution. To help us assist departments and faculty in sending news releasesto the media, we have provided the following news release form. In determining whether your announcement is newsworthy or not, please consider three main elements of news:

  • Relevance
  • Usefulness
  • Interest

Once you have submitted the form, the communications team will review and re-write, as necessary. We will then send back the release to you for final review prior to sending to the appropriate media outlets. Please note that communications will review to ensure that information submitted is newsworthy. If the information is not appropriate for a news release, we will work with you on other ways to promote your story through social media or internal communications.

 

Any questions, please contact Natasha Eidson at 580-349-1356 or e-mail natasha.eidson@opsu.edu

Photography Services

Photography Services

The Communications Department at Oklahoma Panhandle State University proudly offers exceptional photography services to capture and preserve cherished moments on campus. With a team of skilled photographers, state-of-the-art equipment, and a passion for storytelling through images, we aim to showcase the vibrant spirit and academic excellence of our university community. Whether it’s covering special events, campus life, academic achievements, or promotional materials, our photography services are dedicated to providing captivating visuals that reflect the essence of life at OPSU. From professional portraits to candid snapshots, we strive to deliver high-quality images that convey the university’s unique identity and foster a sense of pride among students, faculty, staff, and alumni.

Website Updates

Website Updates

At Oklahoma Panhandle State University, the Communications Department takes great pride in managing the university’s website with utmost dedication, ensuring it remains up-to-date and relevant for all its users. Recognizing the importance of accurate and timely information, the team diligently works to maintain the website’s content, keeping it aligned with the university’s dynamic events and developments. To facilitate streamlined updates and additions, the department encourages individuals and departments to utilize the Website Update Request form. This efficient form allows users to submit their requests for content updates or the creation of new webpages, guaranteeing that the website consistently reflects the university’s latest initiatives and offerings. By fostering this collaborative approach, the Communications Department aims to provide the university community and visitors with a user-friendly, informative, and engaging online experience.
