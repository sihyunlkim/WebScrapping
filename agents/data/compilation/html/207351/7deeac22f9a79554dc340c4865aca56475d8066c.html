Micro-Credentials

Micro-Credentials at OPSU

What are Micro-Credentials?

Micro-credentials are short, focused programs that allow learners to gain specific skills and earn digital badges that can be shared on resumes, LinkedIn, or with employers. OPSU micro-credentials are developed with input from industry partners to ensure real-world relevance. OPSU’s micro-credentials are developed with input from industry partners to ensure real-world relevance. All OPSU-issued badges are hosted on Credly as part of the OSRHE UpskillOK initiative.

Available Micro-Credentials

Accounting

Master foundational to advanced accounting skills using financial tools and software.

Learn More

Graphic Design: Cowboy Art

Hands-on digital design using Adobe Creative Suite and Western visual themes.

CLEET-aligned training in self-defense, scenario simulations, and emergency response.

Learn More

Alternative Teacher Certification

OSRHE-aligned support for alternative pathway candidates preparing for the Oklahoma teacher certification process.

Learn More

Science of Reading

Progressive reading instruction training focused on theory, diagnostics, and instructional application.

Learn More

Why Choose OPSU Micro-Credentials?

  • Earn digital badges to share with employers and on LinkedIn
  • Flexible, short-term programs designed to fit your schedule
  • Aligned with regional workforce needs
  • Affordable and stackable toward degrees
  • Badges issued through the OSRHE/OPSU collection on Credly
  • Program participation listed on UpskillOK.org as part of Oklahoma’s workforce development strategy
