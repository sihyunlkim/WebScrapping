About OPSU

Welcome to OPSU, where academic excellence meets community engagement. Established in 1909, OPSU offers diverse undergraduate programs that foster personalized learning experiences and prepare students for success in their chosen fields. Our vibrant campus, nestled in the heart of Goodwell, Oklahoma, offers a small-town charm and provides a supportive environment where students can academically and personally thrive.
