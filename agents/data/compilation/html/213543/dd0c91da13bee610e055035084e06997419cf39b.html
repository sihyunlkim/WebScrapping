Academic Integrity

On this website, you will find links to materials developed by faculty, the Student Senate, the Dean of Students Office, the Educational Policy committee, and Library and Technology Services. Please explore this information and use it to help promote the highest level of academic integrity at Lehigh.

Faculty are asked to engage students in discussions to assist students' understanding of the distinctions between appropriate and inappropriate actions. To aid in these discussions, we encourage faculty to use one or more of these vignettes during a class meeting early in the semester. The vignettes describe actual cases and invite discussion on how to reinforce academic integrity and avoid Code of Conduct violations.
