AI Policy

How We Use AI: eCornell Guidelines for Ethical Use of AI in Courses

At eCornell, our certificate programs blend personal connections with facilitators and peers with AI tools to enrich the learning experience.

Cornell Certificate programs offer a small cohort experience, fostering a supportive community and enabling meaningful interactions. Participants engage with peers and expert facilitators through discussions and live sessions. Programs include projects where participants apply concepts to their work and receive tailored feedback from facilitators. This approach sets eCornell apart, as most other certificate programs do not provide small cohort learning with instructor guidance.

eCornell is committed to innovation, integrating AI to enhance learning outcomes. AI complements human interaction, which is essential for providing guidance, fostering collaboration, and building meaningful connections.

When we use AI to support the course experience, we follow these principles:

Positive Impact
Our AI features are here to make your learning experience better and help you succeed. We aim to use AI in ways that benefit both students and society.

Transparency
You will always know when AI is being used in your courses. We believe in being clear and honest about how and why AI is part of your learning.

Safety, Security & Privacy
Keeping your information safe is our top priority. We make sure our AI tools protect your data and follow strict security standards.

Accountability & Feedback
Your feedback matters. If you have concerns or suggestions about AI features, you can share them with us. We thoroughly test every new feature to make sure it works well before it’s released.

Fairness & Inclusivity
Our AI tools are designed to be fair to everyone, no matter their background. We work hard to avoid bias and ensure our courses are based on trusted, expert knowledge.
