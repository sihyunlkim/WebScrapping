Greetings from the Cornell University campus in Upstate New York.

The Board of Directors of the Roper Center for Public Opinion Research adopted an Artificial Intelligence (AI) Policy on November 14, 2025, to serve as a supplement to the existing General Terms of Use.

The policy presents guidelines and requirements for the responsible use of artificial intelligence in research that utilizes data from the Roper archive and is designed to ensure compliance and maintain ethical standards when AI-related activities are employed.

Please take a moment to review the policy.

If you have any questions or need clarification, please send a message to roper-member@cornell.edu.  

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Happy Holidays!
