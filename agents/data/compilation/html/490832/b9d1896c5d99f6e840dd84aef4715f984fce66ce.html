Ross Education SMS Terms of Service
Description: Users who opt-in to Ross Education SMS messaging will receive communications from Ross Education Admissions via SMS for the purpose of providing information regarding enrolling into a healthcare career training program at a Ross Education campus.
How to Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to the short code. After you send the SMS message "STOP" to us, we will send you an SMS message to confirm that you have been unsubscribed. After this, you will no longer receive SMS messages from us.
Assistance: If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program you can reply with the keyword HELP for more assistance, or you can get help directly at social@rosseducation.edu.
Message Frequency: You will receive the first message five minutes after submission of your information. Message frequency will vary depending on further communications throughout the SMS conversation.
Cost: As always, message and data rates may apply for any messages sent to you from us and to us from you. If you have any questions about your text plan or data plan, it is best to contact your wireless provider.
Carrier Liability: Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.
