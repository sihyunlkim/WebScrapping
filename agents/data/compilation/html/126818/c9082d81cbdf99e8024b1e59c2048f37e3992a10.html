AI and Academic Integrity

Featuring Joseph Brown, Ph.D.

What does the future of higher education look like in a world where every student has access to powerful AI tools like ChatGPT and Microsoft Copilot? How can educators be more prepared to handle this rapidly evolving landscape? What should college students know before choosing to use AI tools as part of their coursework?

Topics: Artificial Intelligence, Technology, Ethics, Education
