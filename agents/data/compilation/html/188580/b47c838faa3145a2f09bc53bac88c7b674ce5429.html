Education

As one of the nation's oldest private medical schools, we take pride in offering an intimate, collegial environment that fosters humane values and genuine learning.

Programs

  • MD Program

    Educating the next generation of doctors since 1839.

  • Physician Assistant Studies

    Educating PAs since 1972, with experienced faculty and proven curriculum.

  • Nurse Anesthesiology

    Albany Medical College has been educating nurse anesthetists since 1951.

  • Alden March Bioethics Institute

    An internationally recognized leader in bioethics, offering an MS in Bioethics and more.

  • Biomedical Sciences

    Find the excitement of discovery at our four research centers.

  • Human Anatomy

    Providing graduate training and teaching experience in the anatomical sciences.

  • Clinical Investigation

    Training investigators in the techniques and processes used in clinical research.

Residencies & Fellowships

Our programs cover a spectrum of surgical, medical, and hospital disciplines. Residents and fellows enjoy an intimate learning environment with guidance from experienced faculty and access to the most advanced technology for specialty training and medical research. Find your match!
Learn More About Our Programs

Patient Safety and Clinical Competency Center

This unique facility provides the most advanced simulated clinical training to our residents, fellows, students, and medical providers. It allows students to learn and practice skills thoroughly and safely.
