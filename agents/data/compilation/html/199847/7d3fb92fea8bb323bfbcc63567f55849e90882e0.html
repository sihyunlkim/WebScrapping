Electronic Resources Policy

Wake Forest University’s licensed resources are only for the educational use of current Wake Forest University students, faculty, and staff, subject to the terms of the individual contracts and contract law. Use for commercial purposes or systematic downloading, printing, saving, or distribution (in any format) of substantial portions of any of these resources is prohibited.

For more information, see the University’s Policy on Responsible and Ethical Use of Computing Resources as well as the Copyright Policy, available from the Information Security Policies & Standards page.
