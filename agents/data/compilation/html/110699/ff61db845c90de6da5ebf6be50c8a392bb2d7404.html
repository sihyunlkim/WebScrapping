Overview

Many groups at UCSF and UC-Wide continue to develop and expand training around AI. Get started below and let us know of any to add. 

Professional Development and Individual Courses

UCSF offers several ways to go deeper into AI and Generative AI

  • Explore UC's SumTotal course bundle on AI. This includes classes such as the fundamentals of AI & Machine Learning: Metrics & Evaluation and Foundational Data Science Methods. As well as role specific training such as one on AI in Research Administration. 
  • Take advantage of UCSF's partnership with LinkedIn Learning to access the Generative AI course bundle to learn the core concepts of artificial intelligence and generative AI functionality.
  • Access O'Reilly content -- books and online self-paced courses on topics like machine learning, prompt engineering, computer vision and much more more through the UCSF Library. 
  • UCSF Library maintains a Data Science Education and Training portal where you can find a range of workshops and courses on AI fundamentals and beyond.
  • UCSF Library's Data Science and Open Scholarship team also teaches hands-on workshops on programming, machine learning, natural language processing and generative AI. You can find materials from past classes and check out the calendar to register for an upcoming session.  
  • Check out your colleagues recommendations on the UCSF GenAI community wiki list of crowd-sourced trainings. 
  • New training in responsible AI and big data research ethics
