Enhancing Teaching with Generative AI: Hands-on Strategies and Tips Online

Join us for an informative session designed to equip educators with the knowledge and tools to leverage generative AI capabilities for effective teaching. This event will introduce you to AI's fundamental principles and features, specifically focusing on Versa, and demonstrate how it can enhance teaching methods and improve student engagement.

Presenters:

Albert Dang, BA, MFA, Lead Design Strategist Versa AI Team, Education IT

Albert Lee, MADS, Research Solutions Leads Specialist, UCSF IT

Conan MacDougall, PharmD, MAS, Co-Vice Dean of PharmD Education and Associate Dean of Integrated Core Curriculum, School of Pharmacy

Alexa Tan, MA, Didactic Education Administrator, School of Pharmacy

Who Should Join?

The Symposium is intended for UCSF faculty and anyone supporting academic programs at UCSF.

Before You Attend:

To ensure you get the most out of this session, we encourage all attendees to sign up for a Versa account prior to the event. Versa is our interactive platform, which we will use during the workshop to demonstrate real-time applications of generative AI in education. By creating an account, you'll be able to follow along and actively participate in the demonstrations.

Steps to Get Started with Versa:

  1. Sign up for Versa: Please complete the sign-up form to register your interest in using Versa.

  2. Complete the Training: Once registered, you will find a 10-minute Introduction to the Versa training module. This training is crucial as it will help you understand the platform’s functionality and features, and we highly recommend completing it before the workshop to make the most of the session.

  3. Participate Actively: During the workshop, we will have several interactive segments where you can apply what you’ve learned directly in Versa. This hands-on experience is designed to enhance your understanding and confidence in using AI tools for education.
