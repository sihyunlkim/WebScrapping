Meeting the Moment:  Using AI for Teaching and Learning

Join us for the keynote session of the third annual Teaching and Learning Symposium

Register here.

Details

  • Type Event
  • Date August 26, 2024
  • Time 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Location
    Mission Bay Genetech Hall Byers Auditorium

About

Join us for the third annual Teaching and Learning Symposium, a four-day event hosted by the UCSF Library and UCSF Teaching Innovations Work Group. This symposium is a unique opportunity for faculty educators, technologists, and education staff to delve into the future of health education, explore innovative learning modalities, and exchange best practices. The sessions will cover a range of topics, including the use of generative AI in education, ed-tech tools, and strategies for adapting to rapid technological advancements. We will also emphasize the human-centered application of technology and digital literacy, ensuring that the human element remains at the forefront of educational innovation.

This hybrid event will feature one in-person session at Mission Bay and four virtual sessions held via Zoom. Don’t miss this unique opportunity to explore the future of education, bring innovative practices back to your classroom, and network with a community of like-minded peers. Register now to secure your spot and participate in this enriching experience. The Symposium is intended for UCSF faculty and anyone supporting academic programs at UCSF.
