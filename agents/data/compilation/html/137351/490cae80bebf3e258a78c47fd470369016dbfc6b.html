Generative AI Resources

This section contains information and resources to help you harness the power of generative AI at USF.

Explore this section to find resources that include AI-augmented learning and teaching, upskilling and training opportunities, tips and tricks for using generative AI effectively, and links to a community of practice for faculty members. You’ll also find tools and guidelines for safe computing, as well as external resources to help you use genAI tools.

Whether you’re new to generative AI or an experienced practitioner, our resources are designed to help you get the most out of this powerful technology.
