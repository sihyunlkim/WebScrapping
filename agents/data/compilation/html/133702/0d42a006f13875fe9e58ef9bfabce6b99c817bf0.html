FSCJ to Host a Town Hall Event on Artificial Intelligence in Higher Education

Jan 17, 2024, 14:21 PM

WHAT:           Florida State College at Jacksonville (FSCJ) will host an interactive Community Town Hall, “AI in Higher          Education: Possibilities and Perils,” featuring presidents from local colleges and universities. They will share insights into how their respective institutions are embracing the possibilities of AI while addressing the challenges it presents in higher education.

Panelists include:

  • Dr. John Avendano, President, FSCJ
  • Mr. Tim Cost, President, Jacksonville University
  • Dr. Moez Limayem, President, University of North Florida
  • Mr. Joe Pickens, J.D., President, St. Johns River State College

The guided discussion will be moderated by FSCJ Professor of Communications Dr. Audrey Antee and FSCJ Professor of Humanities Dr. Scott Cason. Audience members will have the opportunity to ask the panelists their questions as well.

Light refreshments will be provided to in-person attendees. A virtual option is also available.

Learn more and RSVP at https://www.fscj.edu/ai-fscj.

WHEN:           Wednesday, January 31, 2024
                        8:30-10 a.m.

WHERE:         FSCJ Advanced Technology Center, T140/141
                       401 W. State St.
                       Jacksonville, FL 32202

 

Florida State College at Jacksonville is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) to award associate and baccalaureate degrees. Florida State College at Jacksonville also may offer credentials such as certificates and diplomas at approved degree levels.
