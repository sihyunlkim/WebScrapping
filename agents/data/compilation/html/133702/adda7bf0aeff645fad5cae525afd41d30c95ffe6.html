The FSCJ Artificial Intelligence Business Academy developed in partnership with Intel’s Digital Readiness Program is a fast-track solution helping companies lead in the AI era. Gain practical skills to leverage AI with no prior technical background required. Courses are offered in a convenient modular design at your location on a customized schedule to meet your needs.

  • Drive AI innovation and adoption
  • Build foundational AI knowledge and skills
  • Accelerate operational efficiency
  • Promote responsible technology use

FSCJ’s A.I. Business Academy empowers organizations to develop the workforce with the latest AI technology tools and skills. The academy offers a future-ready investment in talent development whether you're upskilling your leadership team or preparing your organization for the next generation of digital transformation.

 

Course Description: This 8-hour course provides a comprehensive introduction to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML), designed for both managers and non-managers. Guides participants through the evolution of AI, its capabilities, and the emerging technologies shaping the future of industry. Participants will learn about the core principles of AI and machine learning, the different domains where AI is applied, and the tools available to integrate AI into their organizations, without the need for deep technical expertise. Two four-hour courses (8 hours)

Course Description: The course provides a comprehensive overview of AI fundamentals, including the history and evolution of AI, the role of data in building AI models, and an introduction to key AI and machine learning concepts such as TensorFlow, Scikit-learn and Natural Language Processing (NLP). 4 hours

Course Description: Prompt Engineering Essentials will provide skills to craft clear, specific, and well-structured prompts to unlock the full potential of Generative AI. Learn how to guide AI to understand your intentions, generate accurate and relevant responses, while enhancing productivity and creativity. Covers the core principles of prompt engineering and provides practical exercises with real-world examples. 4 hours

Course Description: Ethics and AI training equips business professionals with the knowledge and to navigate the complex ethical landscape of AI. Explore the key ethical dilemmas posed by AI, learn best practices for responsible AI development and deployment, and develop strategies for mitigating potential risks. 4 hours

 

The AI Business Academy is a specialty Workforce Education pathway provided through the FSCJ Baccalaureate, Career, and Technical Education programs.
