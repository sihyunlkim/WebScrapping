    Pathways at Edison State

    Take the lead with technology

    The Information Technology pathway offers an opportunity to gain expertise in various computer languages, learn to integrate processes effectively, acquire a strong foundation in computer science, or explore the exciting realms of web development and graphic design.

    Why pursue Information Technology at Edison State?

    • Exponential Growth  According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, web development employment is projected to grow about 13 percent from 2020 to 2030, faster than the average for all occupations.

    Explore Information Technology certificates

    The Information Technology pathway also offers technical certificates that students can obtain to increase their skills and advance in their careers.

      Technical Certificates (3037 credits)

      Edison State offers technical certificates within career program areas. Courses outlined in each certificate can lead to an Associate degree for the program sponsoring the certificate. Students generally receive certificates for the completion of 30-37 semester hours of coursework. A certificate can be completed in twelve months.

      Business Systems
      Computer Information Systems
      Database Specialist
      Interactive Media
      Networking
      Programming
      Systems Administration
      Web Development

      Short-Term Technical Certificates (Less than 30 Credits)

      Short-Term Technical Certificates give students the skills needed to enter a new career field or to advance in their current role and can be completed in under a year.
