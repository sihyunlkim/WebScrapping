Attendance & Grade Reporting

Reporting of Grades and Attendance
Below are the grade and attendance deadlines. Submissions must be done in MyESCC. Reporting windows close at 4:00 p.m. on the date indicated. Grades are viewable by students within 6 hours. Download a PDF of the Spring Reporting Schedule or view it below.

Attendance
Attendance must be academic attendance, which means direct participation/interaction between the instructor and student regarding academic matters.

No-Shows
Students who have no academic attendance by the second meeting of the class should be reported as a no-show in the Never Attended column. For online sections, students with no academic attendance by the second assignment/activity should be reported as a no-show in the Never Attended column.

Progress Checks
Submit an Academic Alert Form at weeks two (2) and four (4) for any student earning a 'D' or 'F' or who needs intervention. The Academic Alert form can be submitted anytime in addition to these checkpoints. 

Last Date of Attendance (LDA)
When a student drops or withdraws from a class, report the LDA. An LDA is also needed for a final grade of F, U, or IP. This is imperative for compliance with enrollment and financial aid regulations. Find instructions here.

Midterms
Midterm grades are a snapshot of the student's grade at the halfway point for classes that are 8 weeks or longerafter Week 4 in 8-week classes and after Week 8 in 16-week classes.

    Windows Remote Access Instructions
    Windows 7 Remote Access

    Use the following instructions to access Edison's network while off campus. If you need addition assistance, contact the IT Help Desk.

    1. Click Start
    2. Click All Programs
    3. Click Accessories
    4. Click Remote Desktop Connection
    5. Enter eccgateway2.edisonohio.edu for the computer name
    6. Click Connect
    7. You will then be asked to enter your Edison username and password
    8. This will connect you to the generic desktop and you will the have access to the network.
