      Employee Assistance Program

      All benefit eligible employees, household members, dependents in and away from home and parents/parents-in-law are eligible for the Employee Assistance Program (EAP) from Impact Solutions. The program provides unlimited phone consultation, 5 complimentary face-to-face counseling sessions per person per occurrence and numerous resources such as comprehensive work/life web self service tools.

      Employees currently eligible for benefits should have a member card which you will need to login. If you have lost your card or have never received one, please contact the Human Resources department.

      Reporting

      Violations of College Policy
      Employees should report to their direct supervisor or to the human resources department at 937.778.7832 or via the anonymous reporting line at www.edisonohio.ethicspoint.com.

      Violations of Ohio Ethics Laws
      Employees should submit a report via the anonymous reporting line at www.edisonohio.ethicspoint.com.

      Fraudulent Activity
      Employees should report via email to fraudohio@ohioauditor.gov or call the Fraud Hotline at 866.372.8364.

      Title IX
      Policy violations should be reported to the Title IX Coordinator at titleix@edisonohio.edu or 937.778.7872.

      Criminal Activity
      Employees should contact the Department of Public Safety at 937.778.7820.

      Strategic Key Results

      Key Result 1 | Sustain Positive Employee Engagement
      1.1  Create a culture that recognizes employees and celebrates accomplishment.
      1.2  Create a collaborative culture of measurement that advances accountability and innovation.
      1.3  Integrate Leadership learning opportunities to foster a culture in which employees are empowered to bring their best to work.

      Key Result 2 | Practice Fiscal Sustainability
      2.1  Establish a culture committed to continuously improving college policies, practices, processes, and systems to ensure long-term sustainability.
      2.2  Maximize both human and capital resources to leverage organizational effectiveness. 
      2.4  Create a culture of inquiry through regular data informed reviews and assessments that drive future planning.

      Key Result 5 | Vigorously Enhance Awareness of the Comprehensive Nature of Edison to all Stakeholders.
