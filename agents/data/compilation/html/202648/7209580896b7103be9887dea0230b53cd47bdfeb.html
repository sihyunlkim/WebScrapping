    20232026 Strategic Plan

    Taking Excellence and Innovation to the Next Level

    The 20232026 Strategic Plan provides tactical direction for Edison State and is guided by our vision, mission, values, and student learning outcomes. This plan is intended to be an integral part of the Edison State overall operations and daily initiatives. Outcomes presented here are the result of collaborative efforts from a host of stakeholders including alumni, students, staff, faculty, and community and business leaders from across our service region. You can review or download the Strategic Plan below.
