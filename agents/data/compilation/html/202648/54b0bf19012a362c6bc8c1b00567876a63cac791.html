We want you to succeed.

We work hard to maintain and expand resources that benefit you as a student. These resources range from the vast library assets and tutoring centerto the fitness and early childhood development centers. We hope these resources aid your success and help you make the most of your time at Edison State. You will find information below about services in place for you as an Edison State student. 

Please familiarize yourself with the Student Handbook. The Student Handbook has been compiled to answer many of the questions you may have, as well as explain Edison State policies and procedures. You will also find information on specific processes, from transferring credits to starting a club.

NOTE  The Student Handbook does not replace program-specific handbooks.

Quick links to more services and support.

    Charger Stations

    Charger Stations are available to all registered students and can be found at all Edison State locations during regular business hours. These small food pantries enable you to pick up snacks, toiletries, baby items, school supplies, and local resource information whenever needed. The Charger Station at the Piqua campus is located in Room 163, directly across from the Student Services suite. Charger Stations are in the student lounge area at the Troy, Greenville, and Eaton regional locations.

    Child Development Center

    The Edison State Child Development Center is a nationally accredited developmental program of care and education for children 3 years old through kindergarten. The program is open to the children in our local communities and the children of Edison State students, faculty, and staff.

    explore the Child Development Center
    Edison Alerts

    Edison State has partnered with Rave Mobile Safety to provide an emergency alert system called Edison Alerts. All current students, faculty, and staff are automatically enrolled in the program. Upon enrollment, you will receive an email notification from Edison Alerts, prompting you to complete the registration process. This email notification will be sent to your Edison State email account.

    Text or Voice Message Alerts Registered users are highly encouraged to log in to the Rave Mobile Safety site and add their cell phone number and any additional email addresses where they would like to receive Edison Alerts. You must complete the registration process to receive Edison Alerts by text, phone, or personal email.

    NOTE  Your cell phone provider may charge a per-text message fee to deliver emergency notifications to your phone.

    If deemed necessary by the Vice President of Administration and Finance, a campus-wide alert will be issued if a situation arises and causes a threat on or off campus. Students, faculty, staff, and other registered users will receive the warning through Edison Alerts. Depending upon the particular circumstances of the situation, especially in all situations that could pose an immediate threat to the community and individuals, a notice may also be posted on the College website homepage and social media channels.

    If you have questions or problems setting up your Edison Alerts, please contact the Department of Public Safety for assistance.

    Fitness Centers

    The Edison State Fitness Centers, both indoor and outdoor, are available to students, faculty, staff, and the community. In addition to a weight room, the indoor fitness center has an aerobics room.

    Student Resource Center

    The Student Resource Center is located on the Piqua campus, in Room 163, across from the Student Affairs Office. You can find resource information on local mental health care, child care, insurance, transportation, emergency housing, rental assistance, utility assistance, and more. The Charger Station is also located in this space at the Piqua campus. The Charger Stations have snacks, grab-and-go lunches, toiletries, baby items, and school supplies and are available to any registered student during regular business hours. Please send questions, requests for information, or item recommendations via email.
