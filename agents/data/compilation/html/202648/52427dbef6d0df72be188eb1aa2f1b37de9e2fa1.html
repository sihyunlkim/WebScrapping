Food & Beverages

Food and beverages can be found in the Market C area of the cafeteria at the Edison State Piqua campus. If you need a fast and free snack, visit a Charger Station found at all locations.

Student Wellness

The Student Wellness Center can help you with both physical and mental health concerns. We will help you navigate obstacles that may cause you undue stress or angst.
