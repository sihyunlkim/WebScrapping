Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare Seminar Series

Explore the transformative impact of Artificial Intelligence in healthcare with our Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare Seminar Series! Open to all Baylor faculty, staff and trainees, this eight-part series offers a chance to earn CME credits while gaining valuable insights into AI’s role in medicine. Attend all eight sessions to receive a certificate of completion—don’t miss this exciting educational opportunity!

Learners must be signed in to access each recorded session.

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare

Available to all.

AI in Education: Custom GPTs, Literature Review, and Curriculum Tools

Available only BCM Faculty, staff, and trainees.

Integrating AI into Healthcare Research

Available only BCM Faculty, staff, and trainees.

AI in Clinical Care: Practical Insights and Future Horizons

Available only BCM Faculty, staff, and trainees.

From Data to Decisions: Harnessing AI and Learning Health Systems

Available only BCM Faculty, staff, and trainees.

Navigating the Ethical Frontiers of AI in Healthcare: A Panel Discussion

Available to all.

AI in Action: Transforming Academic Medicine with Practical Tools

Available only BCM Faculty, staff, and trainees.

Shaping Tomorrow: Future Trends and Innovations in AI-Driven Healthcare
