About iPage Login

Upon successful login, you will be forwarded to your selected location.

By entering a User ID and Password, and clicking Login, the user acknowledges that:

  • S/he will abide by any requirements or restrictions Upstate may impose on system use.
  • It is strictly forbidden to allow any other individual to use his or her User ID and password.
  • Telecommunication costs incurred in accessing any databases through this system are at the user's own expense.
  • This access is to be used for Upstate related purposes only.
  • These access privileges may be withdrawn by Upstate at any time without prior notice.

Students & Upstate Staff With MyAccounts ID

Type your MyAccounts ID and Password into the form on the left and click Login.

Don't know your MyAccounts password? Please call the Computer Help Desk at 315 464-4115.

Medical Staff without MyAccounts ID

Type your Upstate Doctor Account ID and Password into the form on the left and click Login.

Don't know your password? Please call the Medical Staff Services at 315 464-5733.
