Library Questions and Answers

What research applications or tools are available to Upstate faculty, staff and students?

Answer

Beyond library resources, many resources such as REDCap, Figshare, TriNetX, SPSS, RStudio, MATLAB, and Tableau are available through IMT. Many of these can be found in the Software Center (PC) or Upstate Apps (Mac). To learn more, visit the Research Technology Core page.  

Please note that many of these tools are only available on Upstate-owned devices, and some may require approval from IMT. For more information, contact the Help Desk at helpdesk@upstate.edu.
