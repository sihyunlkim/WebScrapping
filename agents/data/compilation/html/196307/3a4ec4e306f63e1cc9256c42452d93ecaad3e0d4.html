SUNY Digital Repository (SDR)

In addition to SOAR, which houses scholarly works, Upstate utilizes the SUNY Digital Repository is for a range of creative, historical, and special collections.  Student creative and course-related works, newsletters, department work products, etc.  may be submitted to SDR so that they are openly available and discoverable in a Google search.

To add works or a new collection to the Digital Repository, please contact us at library@upstate.edu so that we can discuss your specific situation in greater detail.
