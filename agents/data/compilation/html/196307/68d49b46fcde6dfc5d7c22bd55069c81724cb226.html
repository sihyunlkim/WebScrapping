Medicine Resources

The library homepage provides access to the most comprehensive list of resources available through the Health Sciences Library at Upstate. However, this guide highlights items of interest to the field of medicine and programs in the College of Medicine.

Databases

Tools to search for peer reviewed articles, full text articles, trade publications, dissertations, clinical trials, reviews, videos, citations, ebooks, practice questions, and more.

Journals and Articles

Find peer reviewed journals by title and subject and discover ways to find full text articles faster using a browser extension and/or PMIDs and DOIs.

eBooks

Books available online.

Reserves and Textbooks

Reserves are items that are often used for classes and are available at the main desk and can be checked out for two hours or used during after hours. This page also includes some textbooks that are available online.

Point of Care Tools

Resources designed to quickly look up information when working with patients.

Mobile Apps

Configuration settings and download information for apps supported by the library: Browzine, UpToDate, Lexidrug, VisualDx, AccessMedicine, 5MinuteConsult, and Complete Anatomy.

Drug Information

Resources that contain information on drugs and related information such as dosing, warnings, toxicology, and patient education.

Calculators

Medical calculators to assist with dosing, diagnosis, and treatment.
