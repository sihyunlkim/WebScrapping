Training Course on Generative AI and ChatGPT for Academic Research

The IT and Cyber Security departments under the Applied Science Faculty are excited to announce a valuable professional development opportunity open to all department staff at Tishk International University.

Starting this Thursday, 28th November 2024, at 3:30 PM, we will launch a 10-week training course focused on Utilizing ChatGPT for Academic Research.

Training Course Overview

  • Duration: 10 Sessions (1 hour each)
  • Start Date: 28th November 2024 to 13th February 2025
  • Day, Time, and Venue: Every Thursday at 3:30 PM in TIU Main Building – Lab 206
  • Format: Each session is divided into theoretical learning followed by a practical application.

If you are interested, please register online here for the first session. Kindly ensure punctual attendance to make the most of this learning opportunity.

Please note that a new registration form with details for each week’s session will be shared with TIU staff via email accordingly.
