Library Mission Statement

Rolfing Library glorifies God and edifies the Trinity International University community by:

  • Providing access to quality resources

  • Fostering collaborative relationships in a warm and welcoming environment

  • Promoting lifelong learning

Library Values

Providing access to quality resources by:

  • Participating in resource sharing programs

  • Developing and maintaining collections

  • Ensuring accessibility of information for entire TIU community

 

Fostering collaborative relationships by:

  • Working across TIU departments and divisions

  • Facilitating cooperative learning

  • Modeling and empowering generous scholarship

 

Promoting lifelong learning by:

  • Enabling TIU community to enter into scholarly conversations

  • Equipping TIU community with strategies and tools for vocational effectiveness

  • Supporting TIU community beyond graduation

Privacy

Rolfing Memorial Library vigorously protects the confidentiality of all personally identifiable information about Library users. In accordance with the Illinois Library Records Confidentiality Act (75 ILCS 70/1) all borrowers' registration, circulation, and use records are confidential and may not be revealed to the public except by court order. 

Consortial Commitments

Rolfing is a member of CARLI, the Consortium of Academic and Research Libraries in Illinois and is committed to abiding by the CARLI membership agreement. Similarly, as an active I-Share participant, Rolfing cooperates with the I-Share agreement.
