Explore the Library's New Generative AI Resource Guide

We invite you to explore a new research guide from the Library:  Artificial Intelligence (Generative) Resources. This Guide will help you navigate the emerging field of generative artificial intelligence (GAI), which is a branch of AI that can create original content in various forms, such as text, media, code, and more. You may have already encountered some examples of GAI in popular tools like ChatGPT, Elicit, and Bard. The Guide provides an overview of these research tools, while also offering guidance on crafting effective prompts and citing the generated content properly to avoid plagiarism. In addition, the Guide explores the ethical implications of GAI, such as the potential for misinformation, bias, authorship, and copyright infringement. The Guide will also keep you updated on the latest trends and developments in GAI by featuring essential library databases, e-books, and a news feed. We hope you will find this resource helpful in your learning and research endeavors. If you have any questions or need help, please don’t hesitate to contact our librarians at reference@georgetown.edu.
