AI for Teaching and Learning Bootcamps

Join CNDLS for the ‘AI for Teaching and Learning’ Bootcamps, designed to help faculty explore how AI can enhance their teaching and research practice. These hybrid events provide hands-on experience with AI tools, frameworks for navigating ethical considerations, and effective strategies for integrating AI into teaching and student learning.

Building on years of AI literacy workshop exerience, the CNDLS AI Bootcamp offers faculty a structured space to experiment, collaborate, and develop concrete approaches for using AI in their classrooms. Participants will leave with practical applications to course and assignment, ready-to-use strategies, and a faculty learning cohort for ongoing support.

What You Will Learn

The focus of this intensive workshop will be to develop a shared frame of thought to help navigate the current landscape of higher education, while simultaneously building skills and testing tools.
