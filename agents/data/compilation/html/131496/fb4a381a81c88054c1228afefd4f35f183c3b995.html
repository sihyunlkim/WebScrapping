Artificial Intelligence

Whether you're passionate about interdisciplinary collaboration, pedagogical innovation, or AI ethics, there's a place for you to contribute to our ongoing efforts in fostering meaningful and sustainable AI integration at Georgetown.

Opportunities

Explore new programs and initiatives designed to enhance student learning and engagement by integrating AI-supported tools responsibly and creatively across different academic domains. Not sure which opportunity suits you best? See more details on our support options.

  • Teaching with AI

    Reflect on your pedagogy while reading our Teaching with AI teaching guide which shares examples from Georgetown faculty, as well as sample course policy language and rationale.

    Read the Teaching with AI guide

  • Trainings and consultations

    We can support your specific program needs by facilitating custom departmental workshops or host sessions for campus groups, ensuring that AI education is accessible and relevant across the university.

    Explore our options

  • CNDLS AI Lab

    A voluntary, supportive initiative designed to help departments develop literacy and fluency with AI tools, thoughtfully engage with AI in their teaching and research practices, as well as adapt their curriculum.

    Visit the CNDLS AI Lab site

AI Microcredential

This self-paced course supports Georgetown’s community in exploring how artificial intelligence intersects with research, learning, and everyday academic practice. Through eight short modules, you’ll gain hands-on experience, sharpen your critical thinking, and begin to shape your own intentional, ethical approach to AI.

Learn more about our AI microcredential

Resources across campus

  • AI Research Guide (Library)

    This guide is intended to help students, faculty, and staff at Georgetown University to navigate the quickly evolving terrain of generative AI. It provides an overview of popular AI research tools, offers guidance on crafting prompts in these tools, and points out key library databases and e-books on AI. It also supplies resources related to core AI ethical issues while laying out basic citation rules.

    Read the AI Research Guide open_in_new

  • AI, Analytics, and the Future of Work Initiative (MSB)

    The AI, Analytics, and the Future of Work Initiative addresses critical issues regarding the economic and social transformations brought by digital technology. Through research, convening, and education, the initiative informs business leaders and policy makers on how to devise solutions that help the most vulnerable members of society and foster the common good.

    Learn more open_in_new
