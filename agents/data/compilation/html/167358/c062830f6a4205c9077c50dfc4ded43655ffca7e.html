AI at Northeastern University

Transform Your Work with AI Tools and Support

Northeastern provides cutting-edge AI tools, training, and support to help faculty, staff, and students work smarter and achieve more. From enterprise-level access to comprehensive training, these resources make AI accessible and impactful for work across the university.

University-Supported AI Services

While AI is already in embedded in many of the university’s supported tools and software, featured enterprise-grade services maximize productivity and efficiencies for individuals and teams on a daily basis. All use of AI services must be consistent with the university’s AI Policy and the Appropriate Use Policy.

View Artificial Intelligence Services

Claude.ai

Claude is a powerful AI assistant built by Anthropic for creative work, advanced reasoning, visual analysis, and code generation. Claude for Education gives active faculty, staff, and students enterprise-level access through their Northeastern account.

Claude AI

Microsoft Copilot

Microsoft Copilot Chat comes with Northeastern’s Microsoft 365 license. This secure AI assistant provides insights from the web and references uploaded files and documents to help create content, summarize text and topics, and translate languages.

Microsoft 365 and Copilot

AI for Microsoft Teams

Teams Premium License adds AI capabilities to Microsoft Teams for faculty and staff who need advanced meeting tools. Features include AI-powered meeting notes and tasks while maintaining compliance with security protocols.

Request Teams Premium License

Quick Start for Departments

Get Your Team Using AI in Days, Not Months

Ready to transform how your department works? The AI Quick Start program provides operational leaders with a guide to helping their teams begin using AI immediately using proven strategies and real-world examples.

14-Day Adoption Plan:

  • Day 1: 30-minute team challenge
  • Days 2-7: Daily AI challenges and power sessions
  • Days 8-14: Buddy system and team showcase

Complete Resource Kit Includes:

  • AI Quick Start Guide for Department Leaders
  • AI Quick Sessions Leader Guide (20-minute training plans)
  • Week 2 Mini-Showcase Guide
  • AI Buddy System Leader Guide
  • Additional Resources with prompts and troubleshooting

Perfect for leaders who want their teams productive with AI immediately. Social, hands-on ideas keep the journey fun to help make it stick!
