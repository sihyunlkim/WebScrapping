Innovators transforming our world

Meet some of our experts who are building the future of human-AI collaboration.

Javed Aslam 

Inaugural Chief of Artificial Intelligence

A professor in the Khoury College of Computer Sciences, Javed Aslam’s research centers on AI and its applications, including machine learning, information retrieval, natural language processing, and healthcare. He also serves as the chief data science officer at CodaMetrix, an AI-healthcare company that uses AI tools to help clients automate medical coding processes.

Tina Eliassi-Rad

Professor of Computer Sciences

As a core faculty member at Northeastern’s Institute for Experiential AI, Tina Eliassi-Rad’s research focuses on the intersection of data mining, machine learning, and network science. Eliassi-Rad’s work has been applied to personalized search on the World Wide Web, fraud detection, mobile ad targeting, and ethics in machine learning.

Timothy W. Bickmore

Professor of Computer Sciences

Timothy Bickmore’s work concentrates on the relationships of human-computer interaction, natural language processing (dialogue systems), animation, and health/medical/behavioral informatics. He has spent much of his career studying how technology can be used to facilitate better medical outcomes for patients. He’s also director of the Relational Agents Group.
