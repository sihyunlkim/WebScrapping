What You Will Find Here

The links to the left detail Stony Brook University policy as it relates to information technology, specifically the use, access and security of IT systems on campus. The Division of Information Technology also concerns itself with supporting the University's obligation to protect the privacy of its student and personnel records by ensuring all personal data is kept confidential.

It is the responsibility of each student, faculty and staff member to familiarize themselves with these policies and adhere to them. Any questions or concerns regarding these policies may be directed to the Office of the Chief Information Officer at (631) 632-9085.
