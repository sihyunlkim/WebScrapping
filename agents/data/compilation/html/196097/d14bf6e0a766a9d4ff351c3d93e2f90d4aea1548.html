AI Tools

A current directory of AI tools offered by DoIT for smarter meetings, ensuring academic integrity, organizing your thoughts, or simplifying your workflow

HIPPA Data Considerations

The following tools may not be used with HIPAA data.

 

Copilot

Interact with CoPilot Chat using web data.  If you are looking to integrate Copilot with Office 365, your department will need to purchase a license for you for $216.00/year (subject to change).  Details about the different Copilot licenses are available and updated regularly on Microsoft's website. Before you ask your department to purchase a license, see if you are able to perform your desired task by signing into Microsoft 365 CoPilot App as features change frequently.

 

Chat with CoPilot
Gemini

Our Google Apps for Education license provides access to all to chat with Gemini at no additional cost. Faculty/Staff seeking the ability to use within Gmail, Docs, Vids and other Google Apps, your department will need to purchase a Google AI Pro license from DoIT for $192.24/year (subject to change). To learn more about the differences in functionality, review Google's Gemini Subscription page.
