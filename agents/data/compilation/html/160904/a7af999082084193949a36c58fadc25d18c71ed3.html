About Xavier

“Xavier University is where leaders come from. No matter what the obstacles, Xavier alumni rise to the top. It's the Xavier way."

  • MISSION
  • DISTINCTION
  • RANKINGS

The ultimate purpose of the University is to contribute to the promotion of a more just and humane society by preparing its students to assume roles of leadership and service in a global society.

FIND OUT MORE

Xavier is ranked among the top HBCUs nationwide and recognized as a national leader in STEM and health sciences. Xavier is a leader in educating African American students who graduate from medical school, and its College of Pharmacy is among the country’s top producers of African American pharmacists.

FIND OUT MORE

Xavier University of Louisiana is a private, coeducational, liberal arts college with the distinction of being the only historically Black Roman Catholic institution of higher education in the United States.
