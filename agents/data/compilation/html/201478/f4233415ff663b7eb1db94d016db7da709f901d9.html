Career Tech Education (CTE)  programs provide students with practical, hands-on experience in their chosen career field. This hands-on learning approach helps students better understand the concepts they are learning and reinforces their understanding of the material.

CTE programs are designed to prepare students for the workforce. Students who complete a CTE program are equipped with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in their chosen career field.

CTE programs often have partnerships with local businesses and industry professionals. These connections give students access to real-world experiences and opportunities to network with professionals in their desired career field.

CTE programs are often more cost-effective than traditional four-year college programs. Students can complete their education in a shorter amount of time and at a lower cost, making CTE a great option for those who want to enter the workforce quickly. 

CTE programs offer a wide range of career options to choose from. From healthcare to technology to skilled trades, there is a CTE program for almost every career interest!

WHY CAREER TECH?

At Buckeye Hills, we provide hands-on training for in-demand jobs, so our students can get to work and use their skills to change the world.
