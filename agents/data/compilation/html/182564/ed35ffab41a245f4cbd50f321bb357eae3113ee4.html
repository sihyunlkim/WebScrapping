Accessibility Training

As part of NSHE and WNC’s commitment to fostering an inclusive and accessible work environment, we are pleased to announce mandatory accessibility training for all WNC employees. This training is designed to equip each of us with information and resources to ensure that WNC information, courses, facilities/physical spaces and communications are accessible to everyone, including those with disabilities.
