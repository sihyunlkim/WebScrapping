Welcome to the Center for Teaching Excellence

 

 

Western Nevada College’s Center for Teaching Excellence is an online hub that supports outstanding teaching!  WNC does this by showcasing pedagogical best practices, fostering strong community building, and engaging instructors in a fun, collaborative environment.

 

The Wildcat Connection

 

Producers Josh Fleming and Ryan Johnson sit down with Geology professor Winnie Kortemeier in the newest episode of the Wildcat Connection. Winnie talks about her passion for geology, her recent sabatical work, and the importance of the individual learning experience WNC professors offer to their students.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

TEACHING RESOURCES

Let’s explore together!  Many teaching practices can be learned through workshops and articles; however, there is great value in getting one-on-one support. Our consultations are a place to discuss one’s teaching style, classroom goals, and interactions in a safe and supportive environment.  Consultations are available with CTE staff members on campus or online via zoom.

Here are some examples of the types of support we offer:

  • Course Design, Development, and Delivery
  • Instructional Strategies
  • Instructional Technology
  • Student Engagement
  • Time Management
  • Online and Hybrid Teaching
  • And Many more.

We are here to support your individual needs!
