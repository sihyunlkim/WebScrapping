Program Review & Assessment

  1. Home
  2. Institutional Research
  3. Program Review & Assessment

All administrative areas and academic programs at Western Nevada College create annual plans and assessment reports to ensure that programs are contributing to student success, institutional effectiveness, and mission fulfillment. Academic programs additionally conduct five-year program reviews that allow for a multi-year overview of student learning outcome achievement, program successes, and opportunities for change and growth.
