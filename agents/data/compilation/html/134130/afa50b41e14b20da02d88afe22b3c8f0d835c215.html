How ChatGPT Can Support Your College Research

Understand a topic
Explain key terms, theories, and background in simple language.

Explain complex ideas
Break down difficult concepts in step-by-step terms.

Summarize material
Turn long articles, reports, or papers into bullet points or concise abstracts.

Brainstorm ideas
Suggest research questions, angles, and keywords.

Plan your research
Help outline steps like finding sources and creating timelines.

Edit your writing
Improve grammar, structure, and clarity in your drafts.

Format citations
Show how to cite in APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.

Learn research methods
Explain methods like surveys or statistical analysis.

üéØ Tips for Using ChatGPT Effectively

  • Be specific: Ask focused questions.
  • Use it early: Let it help you brainstorm and plan.
  • Ask for examples: Like outlines or citations.
  • Fact-check everything: Use library databases.
  • Review critically: Don’t copy/paste — reflect and rephrase.
