Pioneering a Comprehensive Approach to AI Education

Suddenly, AI seems to be everywhere in our lives, and in our work. Our job is to prepare students for this reality, even as it evolves.

Scientists and scholars have been using machine learning, deep learning, and other forms of artificial intelligence for decades. Yet in higher education, AI remained specialized. The University of Florida has pioneered the effort to integrate AI education in every major and every college. AI Across the Curriculum is our initiative and our ethos.

An Initiative Beyond One University

As proud as we are of our efforts, we are just beginning. We hope that what we have learned about building a comprehensive approach to AI education can serve as the foundation for other universities. As we build on each other’s work, we strengthen higher education and empower our graduates.

AI to Serve Human Needs

AI is rapidly transforming the economy, from engineering to business to education, agriculture, and the arts. This takes shape as personalized medicine, harvesting robots, self-driving cars, and much more. UF’s university-wide initiative seeks to infuse AI into education and research across the natural sciences, social sciences, and humanities. We want to expand human-centered design so that AI serves humanity and the planet.

UF research aims to thwart crimes against cats

The University of Florida’s Veterinary Forensic Sciences Laboratory identifies causes of death in free-roaming cats and investigates animal cruelty. In short: They fight crimes against cats. One researcher is now using AI to help identify patterns that determine whether a human was involved in a cat’s death.

UF researchers identify mental health effects of AI-driven job insecurity

As AI rapidly moves into the workplace, many workers are feeling uneasy. Two University of Florida researchers have developed a new clinical model that could help health care providers recognize and treat the stress and disruption that come with it.

Artificial Intelligence for Higher Education Teaching Excellence

A brand-new, faculty-focused training series just launched to help University of Florida instructors gain a deeper understanding of artificial intelligence. The “AI Adventure” series explores the transformative potential of AI, how AI systems work, ethical implications for AI in education, and ways AI can be applied to their own teaching.
