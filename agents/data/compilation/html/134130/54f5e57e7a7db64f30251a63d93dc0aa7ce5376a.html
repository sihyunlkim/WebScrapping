Teaching AI, Teaching with AI

UF offers a range of opportunities for faculty to develop and hone their AI teaching and research expertise.

Artificial intelligence is a broad term that covers a range of applications in almost every field. Many faculty have used some form of AI in their research and teaching for years; many others are discovering how AI has begun to reshape work in their own disciplines.
