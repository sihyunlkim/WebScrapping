Year of Focus 2025-2026

  • Share an idea
  • Leave a comment

The Year of Focus is an annual initiative that highlights a single question or challenge, engaging the Wayne State University community through a variety of events and discussions. For 2025-26, the theme, "AI in Action," will explore the innovative technologies reshaping our world and the opportunities and questions they raise. This year’s goal is to deeper understand the future of intelligence and engage in the complexities of new technology and its impact on the human experience.

  • Association of Research Libraries

    The Association of Research Libraries
    The Association of Research Libraries – a nonprofit membership organization of major research libraries and archives in the United States and Canada – champions research libraries and archives, develops visionary leaders, and shapes policy for the equitable advancement of knowledge and information. 

    ARL/CNI AI Scenarios: AI-Influenced Futures
    To imagine a future and explore the range of uncertainty and possibilities associated with AI in the research and knowledge ecosystem, the Association of Research Libraries (ARL) and the Coalition for Networked Information (CNI) created four scenarios framed around two critical uncertainties: societal intentionality of AI process and design, and the societal adaptation of AI.
