    Student Services

    The mission of Student Services is to support each student throughout their academic career from the point of enrollment to the moment of graduation. We work closely with each student to ensure they reach their academic goals and will be successful upon completion of their program. Our team strives to foster the development of all students by providing support services, academic advising, and student life activities.

    Our main goal is to promote educational excellence and a global perspective that will help students achieve their personal and academic aspirations.     Registrar

    The Office of Registrar at Westcliff University monitors and supports student progress by providing services to students, faculty, academic and administrative departments, alumni, and the public.

    The Office of Registrar ensures the protection and confidentiality of academic records under the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA). The University is legally and ethically obligated to safeguard the confidentiality of any information contained in these records, and hence, University personnel only have access to the specific information required in the performance of their job function.
