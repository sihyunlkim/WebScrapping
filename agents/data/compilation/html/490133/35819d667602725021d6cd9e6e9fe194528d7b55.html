The ARC:
Need Support with your Academics? Want to meet with an ARC Specialist?
The Academic Resource Center (ARC) strives towards the success of Westcliff students by providing quality learning assistance and academic support, both online and in-person. The ARC provides students the opportunity to meet with a Writing or Content Specialist, use a computer to work on assignments, and to simply have a quiet space to study. 
 
ON CAMPUS AND ONLINE LINK, WITH HOURS: https://westcliff.mywconline.com (*new users will need to create an account using their Westcliff email)
 
 We encourage all students to make appointments or email the arc@westcliff.edu with any academic related questions.

The IDEA Zone is a place where students, staff, and faculty can provide instant feedback, suggestions, or complements on any aspect of Westcliff life. Simply complete the form, and it is automatically transmitted to the appropriate people.
