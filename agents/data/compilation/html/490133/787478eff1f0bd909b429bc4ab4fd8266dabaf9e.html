Technology Center

Westcliff’s Technology Center prepares students and graduates with relevant and cutting-edge technologies for job opportunities and advancement. The Technology Center focuses on the development of special programs at Westcliff that provide students and graduates with an understanding of current and emerging technologies in their respective fields.

Our most recent developments made in collaboration with the Technology Center included the introduction of the following:

    • Full Stack Coding Bootcamp

  • AR/VR Mobile Game Design Certificate Program [Launching in 2024]

Upcoming Initiatives

Our Emerging Technology Committee focuses on researching innovative technologies relevant to teaching and learning and integrates them into the Westcliff curriculum. With our ongoing research and development, the university will continue to develop new innovative technology-focused programs to meet the demands of tomorrow.

Contact Us

If you’re interested in learning more about our upcoming initiatives and new programs, please email our Emerging Technology Committee at dzhou@westcliff.edu.
