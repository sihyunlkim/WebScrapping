Create an Autonomous Enterprise with AI

May 30, 11:00 am - 12:00 pm

  • « Beach Day
  • Westcliff University Commencement 2024 »

Join Doug Shannon in an insightful talk on the future of AI, Generative AI (GenAI), and Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), exploring how these technologies pave the way for the Autonomous Enterprise. Delve into the evolving landscape of technology and discover the trajectory toward a future where autonomy plays a central role. Doug will share perspectives on auditing GenAI alongside automation and provide valuable insights during a Q&A session. Gain a comprehensive understanding of the transformative journey ahead and the integration of these advancements in the business realm.

Doug Shannon is an accomplished leader in intelligent automation with more than two decades of experience in IT. He has a proven track record in building self-healing automation processes and has been recognized as one of the top 50 intelligent automation leaders globally. Currently, Doug serves as a Gartner Peer Community Ambassador, a council member for VOCAL, and a member of the Theia Institute, which is a cybersecurity & AI ethics think tank, based in Washington D.C.
