Campus Leadership

Achieving excellence together

Penn State Shenango's leadership team works to implement transformative experiences for students, employees and our community. Guided by Penn State's strategic plan and the campus's local mission, our campus directors strive to facilitate excellence every day. 

In addition to Penn State Shenango's team of directors, the campus collaborates with local community leaders through the Penn State Shenango Advisory Board.
