Academics and Advising Center

Update: Penn State Shenango is not currently enrolling new students. Current students can access academic advising and degree planning through campus services.

Penn State Shenango’s Academics and Advising team is passionate about supporting students as they develop solid academic plans, prepare for internship and job searches, and succeed in a competitive world. Housing Academic Advising, the Division of Undergraduate Studies, and the University Registrar in one place, the AAC suite is the perfect place to plan your Penn State story.

Academic Advising

Our dedicated team of advisers help students achieve their fullest academic potential by encouraging them to make informed decisions based on their interests, abilities, values, and goals. Advisers help students by providing guidance and information about course selection, academic planning, degrees, and Penn State requirements and policies.

SHARC Learning Center

The Penn State Shenango Academic Resource Center (SHARC) is staffed with professional and peer tutors who are excited to help you succeed in a variety of classes through tutoring, workshops and study groups.

University Registrar

Shenango’s registration office provides enrollment and registration services, maintains academic records and transcripts, coordinates commencement ceremonies, and manages policies in relation to student records.
