AI Governance and Advisory Bodies

University bodies examining AI abound. The below groups represent a selection of those most focused on AI governance at the campus and system-wide level, or that have put forth reports or recommendations on AI.

  • Academic Senate

    • Committee on Teaching (COT)
  • Other
