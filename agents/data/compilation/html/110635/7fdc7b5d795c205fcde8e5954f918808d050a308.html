For Instructors

AI is increasingly relevant to how instructors plan their courses, find materials, assess student work, and guide students on the ethical uses of AI.

UC Berkeley has resources to support instructors in all of these areas. Use this page to find out more!

As an instructor:

The Teaching section of this site can assist you with:

  • Guiding students in the ethics of using AI in coursework 
  • Assessment methods in the age of AI
  • AI tools that can help you in preparing and conducting your classes
  • Locating training 

The Guidance section of this site can assist you with:

  • Privacy protection and risk associated with using AI in teaching and grading

The Community section of this site can assist you with:

  • Connecting with other instructors on AI-related topics
  • Finding a campus committee to serve on, relating to AI

The Research section of this site can assist you with:

  • Identifying researchers and their work regarding AI 

The Tools & Services section of this site can assist you with:

  • Identifying appropriate and available AI tools for use in teaching or research
