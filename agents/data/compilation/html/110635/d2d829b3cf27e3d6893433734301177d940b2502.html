AI Governing & Advisory Bodies

University bodies examining AI abound. The below groups represent a selection of those most focused on AI governance at the campus and system-wide level. This listing is not comprehensive. 

UC Berkeley

  • Provost's Council on AI (PAC-AI)
  • AI Risk Subcommittee of the Compliance and Enterprise Risk Committee (CERC-AIR), sponsored by PAC-AI
  • Academic Senate Committee on Computing & Information Technology (CIT)
  • Data Governance Committee (DGC)
  • Technology Foundations Committee (TFC)

University of California

  • UC AI Governance and Transparency (website)
  • UCOP: President's Council on AI
  • UC Academic Senate: University Committee on Academic Computing & Communications(link is external)
