AIC Achieves Accreditation

THE AMERICAN ISLAMIC COLLEGE (AIC): RENEWED, RESILIENT, RISING, RIGOROUS, DIVERSE, INCLUSIVE, PROUD…AND ACCREDITED!

  • The FIRST Muslim Institution of Higher Education in America
  • Now ONE OF JUST TWO Independent Muslim colleges in America that have achieved academic accreditation on their own merits
  • a mission and vision that welcomes everyone, includes social justice, and resonates with our time
  • accredited through the Higher Learning Commission (HLC)
  • small classes, personalized teaching and mentoring
  • world class faculty who teach, travel, mentor, research, publish, and play pivotal roles in society
  • students, staff, and faculty from diverse cultural, religious, and socio-economic backgrounds (we keep it real)
  • engaged alumni who stand ready to help and advise
  • a close, caring, inclusive community
  • community partners doing world-changing work in Chicago, across the country, and all over the globe
  • Accredited BA, MA, and MDIV degree programs, with new degree and certificate programs on the horizon and in the works
  • Both in person and HLC-authorized online options for individual courses and whole degree programs
