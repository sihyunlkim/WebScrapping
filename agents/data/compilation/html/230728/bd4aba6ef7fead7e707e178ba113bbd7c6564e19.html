AI Tools

Utah State University has a variety of AI tools available for faculty, staff, and students to utilize.

Microsoft Copilot with Data Protection Available
Available for any current USU student, faculty, or staff.

Copilot provides AI chat for the web with access to modern models at no additional cost. Commercial data protection will be enabled when eligible users are signed in with their school account. This means user and organizational data are protected, chat prompts and responses in Copilot are not saved, Microsoft has no eyes-on access to them, and they aren’t used to train the underlying large language models.

More information is available from Microsoft.
Access Microsoft Copilot with Data Protection

Zoom AI Companion Available
Available for any current USU student, faculty, or staff.

The Zoom AI Companion creates automatic meeting summaries and lets participants ask the AI questions about the meeting. It can also supplement recordings and whiteboard content creation. To enable the Zoom AI Companion, update to the latest version of Zoom, login to your USU Zoom account and configure your preferences in the AI companion tab of your Zoom Settings. 

More information is available from Zoom.
Access Zoom AI Companion

Box AI Available
Available for any current USU student, faculty, or staff.

Box AI is available via the box.usu.edu website interface. It allows you to extract insights, analyze content, and maintain security of any set of documents stored in box without needing to download and reupload to another AI tool.  Free to use, secure, and no usage limitations.

More information about this tool is available from USU and Box
Review USU Box AI Documentation

Google Gemini Available
Available for any current USU student, faculty, or staff.

You must be logged in using your USU.EDU email address to ensure that chat data remains secure and is not used to train models. Google may impose certain usage limitations.

More information about this tool is available from Google.
Access Google Gemini

Google NotebookLM Available
Available for any current USU student, faculty, or staff member with your @usu.edu email address. Upload and utilization limits may apply.

Upload your documents, or share specific URLs, to help you understand, summarize, and learn from your selected content.

More information about this tool is available from Google NotebookLM.
Access Google NotebookLM

Microsoft Copilot for Microsoft 365 Upon Request
This is available as a purchased add-on per A# account for a cost of $30/month (annual commitment) using a USU index number. During the annual commitment period, departments can request a transfer to a different A#.

This tool embeds AI functions into common Microsoft 365 apps, including Excel, Word, Outlook, Powerpoint, and Teams. Microsoft does not currently include this in any education-wide licensing program and is therefore only available for account by account purchase.

An introduction and overview of features are available from Microsoft.
Request Access to Copilot for Microsoft 365
