Policies and Forms

Read the Code of Conduct for Computer Science Classes.

Forms you need to change your major, petition for a late add, block directory information, request waivers, and make other requests can be accessed through USU's Office of the Registrar.

Graduate Student Forms

  • Fill out the student annual review form and submit it to your major professor via this ServiceNow link. Your major professor will also fill out a review form and submit it via ServiceNow. This review will be saved to your student files.
  • Supervisory Committee Approval Form (SCAF): MS/CS Plan A, MS/CS Plan B, MS/DS Plan A, and PhD/CS
  • Program of Study (POS): MS/CS Plan A, MS/CS Plan B, MS/DS Plan A, MCS, and PhD/CS
  • Master's Thesis/Project Proposal Approval (TPA): MS/CS Plan A, MS/CS Plan B, MS/DS Plan A
  • Application for Candidacy (ACDD): PhD/CS
  • Appointment for Examination (AFE): MS/CS Plan A, MS/CS Plan B, MS/DS Plan A, and PhD/CS
  • Thesis/Dissertation Format and Style (F&S) and Electronic Publication Approval: MS/CS Plan A, MS/DS Plan A, and PhD/CS
  • Authorship & Copyright (A&C): MS/CS Plan A, MS/DS Plan A, and PhD/CS
  • Title Pages for Master's Plan A and Doctoral Dissertations: MS/CS Plan A, MS/DS Plan A, and PhD/CS
  • Plan B Report/Creative Project Submission: MS/CS Plan B
  • IRB-approved Protocol Closeout: If your thesis/report/dissertation requires IRB approval.
  • Embargo Request: If you/your advisor would like to restrict electronic release of your thesis/dissertation.
  • Graduation Check Request: MS/CS Plan A, MS/CS Plan B, MS/DS Plan A, MCS, and PhD/CS
