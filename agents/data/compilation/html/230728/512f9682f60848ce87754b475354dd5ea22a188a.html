Data Science & Artificial Intelligence Center

A nexus for faculty, students, and external stakeholders interested in data science, machine learning, and AI.

The goal of the Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DSAI) Center is to be a hub at USU for students, faculty, staff, and external stakeholders interested in data science, machine learning, and AI. Our priorities include fostering faculty collaborations across campus, connecting industry partners with faculty, staff, and students, and becoming a resource in data science and AI research for the state of Utah.
