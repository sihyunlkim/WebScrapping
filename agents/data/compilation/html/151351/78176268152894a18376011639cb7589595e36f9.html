The Academic Experience

See where your interests can take you

The IU academic experience isn't just about what you learn, it's about what you uncover in yourself. Fuel your interests and discover new ones with top-ranked academics, real-world experiences, and a community that will support you every step of the way. 

Explore our degrees and majors

At IU, you don’t follow a single path–you build one.

Explore flexible academic pathways beyond the traditional major and minor. Tailor your academic journey with interdisciplinary and advanced study options designed to launch your career. 

Dual degrees and double majors

Earn two separate degrees by combining fields like journalism + biology, or pursue two majors within one degree, such as finance + accounting.

Individualized major program

Create a multidisciplinary major that fit your unique interests.

Accelerated Master's Program

Earn your bachelor's and master's degrees in five years or less.

Research and creative activity

Study abroad

Choose among 380+ programs that help you explore a culture, earn credit, and get experience.

Internships

Get valuable experience and connections, hone your skills, and see if you’re on the right career path.

Hutton Honors College and honors programs

Our most accomplished undergraduates are invited to enter the Hutton Honors College, which offers small seminars, events with special visitors to campus, scholarships, and grants for activities like research and international travel. Other IU schools and departments have their own honors programs.
