Leadership & Administration

Leadership on the flagship campus

Our chancellor

Dr. David A. Reingold leads IU Bloomington with a focus on student success, academic excellence, and civic engagement. A sociologist and seasoned academic leader, he brings decades of experience in public policy, research, and faculty development to the university's flagship campus.

University-wide leadership

President Pam Whitten works with students, faculty, and staff to build on IU’s reputation as a top research university, advancing the evolving needs of our state and world. The Office of the President oversees IU’s seven campuses and two regional centers, which include more than 25,000 faculty and staff and more than 90,000 students.
