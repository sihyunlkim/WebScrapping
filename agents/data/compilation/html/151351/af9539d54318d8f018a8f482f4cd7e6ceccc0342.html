Faculty & Staff Resources

Find what you’re looking for

We’re proud of our faculty and staff. You’re part of a nearly 12,000-strong crew of people who support the educational and research missions of the university and make the campus a great place to study and work. Check out some of the resources we offer to make your life a little easier. We’ve highlighted some of the university’s most popular services below.

Try One.IU

Looking for something that’s not here? Check out One.IU, your one-stop shop for many IU services.

Whether you’re faculty or staff, these resources are for you

Human Resources

Learn about your medical and dental benefits, retirement plans, FMLA, and much more.

Protect IU

Be prepared with tips from Protect IU. You’ll also find information about IU-Notify, which keeps you up to date on local emergencies and weather situations.

Parking Operations

Need to get to campus? Here you’ll find information about parking permits, alternative transportation, and motorist assistance.

University Information Technology Services

Get help with technology. Change your passphrase, get software, learn how to use a new software application, request classroom technology, and more.

Recreational Sports

You know what they say about all work and no play. Have fun and get fit by becoming a member of Recreational Sports.

IU Today

Find out what’s going on around campus. IU Today delivers news that’s relevant to faculty and staff.

Employee perks

Need to buy a car? Time to replace your mobile phone? Find deals and discounts for faculty and staff at local businesses and franchises.

Americans with Disabilities Act

Need accommodations for a disability? Here’s what you need to know. We’re committed to maintaining an inclusive, accessible environment.

Child care

Looking for on-campus child care? Early Childhood Education Services provides year-round, high-quality care and education.

University Policies

Wondering if you can bring your dog to work? Not sure of IU’s policy about carrying firearms? Check the University Policies site first.

IU Mail Services

Got mail? Find out about sending and receiving mail at your campus office.

Fleet Services

Need a car for university business? Start at the Fleet Services.

Purchasing

Find everything you need to know about buying items for university use.

Stop Sexual Violence

We’re committed to leading the fight against sexual violence. Find out how you can get involved.

Tobacco cessation

IU Bloomington, like all university property, is tobacco free. Get help with quitting tobacco products.

Lifelong Learning

Learn a new subject—for fun or for professional development—through IU Lifelong Learning.

Work-Life Balance

Find out about support services available to help you find balance in your life.

Additional faculty resources

Office of the Provost and Executive Vice President

Learn about the Office of the Provost and Executive Vice President’s role in ensuring the outstanding quality of our faculty and student body.

Office of the Vice Provost for Faculty and Academic Affairs

No matter where you are in your career, you can develop it further with help from the Office of the Vice Provost for Faculty and Academic Affairs.

Research at IU

Are you writing a proposal or looking for funding, or do you need help developing a research idea? IU’s research offices can help.

Bloomington Faculty Council

Get involved. Learn about the Bloomington Faculty Council’s authority over faculty governance and how it affects you.

Center for Innovative Teaching and Learning

Contact CITL for individual consultations, workshops, programs, and funding in support of teaching and learning.

Classroom Technology Services

Get help with technology in the classroom or have your lecture recorded.

Still haven’t found what you’re looking for?

Check with your school or department to find the answers to your questions.

Additional staff resources

Bloomington Professional Council

Interested in knowing what goes on behind the scenes? The Bloomington Professional Council works to improve policies and practices that enhance your quality of life.

University Controller

Looking for information about filling out a timesheet or about payroll or taxes? The Office of the University Controller can help.
