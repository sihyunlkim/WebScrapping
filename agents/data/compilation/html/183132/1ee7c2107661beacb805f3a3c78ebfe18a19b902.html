Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make an appointment with a tutor?

You can make an appointment with a tutor in Navigate. Or email MCCTutoring@ccsnh.edu or call 603-206-8150.

How can I find out which books I need for my classes?

You can search the MCC Bookstore website. Be sure to have your class schedule available as you will need the course and section numbers to be sure you are getting the correct books.

Where can I study with my kids?

Kids are welcome anywhere, but if you prefer a room with a door that closes, try our Family Study.

How do I print?
Printing from a Campus Computer

All MCC registered students have the ability to print in B/W or Color in the Library/Learning Commons and in the open Student Lab located on the upper floor of the Student Center, above the MPR, near the Student Senate and CAB offices. All registered students are automatically provided a printing stipend.

When printing using one of the MCC Student Computers in these two areas, simply open your document within the appropriate application (word, excel, browser, canvas, etc), navigate to the print function within the application, provide your EasyLogin Username and Password when prompted and the cost of the print job will be deducted from your printing stipend.

When prompted to authenticate, use your EasyLogin Username and Password.

Wireless Printing

Wireless printing is now available at MCC, but is limited to PDF documents only.   

  • Go to https://print.ccsnh.edu/ & log in with your EasyLogin username and password.
  • Click on Web Print.
  • Click on Submit a Job.
  • Scroll down and select the MCC printer you wish to print to (search for MCC)
  • Upload documents & complete

For more info visit the IT Printing Guide

I've followed the instructions for off-campus access, and it still doesn't work. I can't get into Canvas. Help!

Try clearing your cache (go to history and look for clear cache or try these shortcuts: on a Microsoft OS computer, simultaneously pressing CTRL+SHIFT+DELETE on a Mac, simultaneously pressing CMD+SHIFT+DELETE. You may also try closing and reopening your internet browser, using a private browser session, or using a different browser. If those don’t work, then contact MCC help desk by email or calling 603-206-8080.

I'm a nursing major. How do I find articles by nurse authors?

 

There is no single way, but here are some tips (the video above shows tips 1 and 2, step-by-step):

1. You can add 

AND nurs*

to your keyword search in any of our databases or MCC Library OneSearch. This will look for some form of the word nurse in the title, abstract, and subject tags of the articles.

2. You can choose "advanced search" in EBSCO databases (including OneSearch, the PICO search box, and CINAHL) and find options to check off for nursing. In CINAHL you can choose nursing under Journal Subset and you can check "Any author is a nurse," a box just above the Journal Subset box. In other databases you can choose Nursing and Allied Health as a discipline. 

Please ask a librarian if you need help with these strategies.

Also, see our Nursing and PICO research guides.

Please make appointment with a tutor in Navigate. You can also have a paper reviewed through Brainfuse online tutoring. Brainfuse is free for students.

  1. Sign in to Canvas
  2. Go to any course
  3. Click on 24/7 Tutoring in the left hand side menu. 
My EasyLogin isn't working. I can't get into a database. What do I do?

If your EasyLogin is not working for Canvas, SIS, and your college email or you do not remember your password: 

  • Change your password yourself by going to the Apps Portal, scrolling down to below the blue Sign In button and clicking on Need Help Signing In? Follow the prompts to reset your password. 
  • If you are not successful, visit the IT Helpdesk located in the Library Learning Commons, call the Helpdesk at (603) 206-8080 or email mccitsupport@ccsnh.edu.

If your EasyLogin works for Canvas, SIS and your college email and you are only having a problem accessing library resources:

  • Try clearing your "cache" in your browser (ctrl-shift-del on windows -- command-shift-del on mac) OR try a different browser
    • If those commands don't work look for instructions at https://clear-my-cache.com    
    • Pro tip: you might have to close the browser after you clear your cache and reopen)
    • This can sometimes help with Canvas access errors, too
    • If you take classes at more than one NH community college, try using a different browser for each library
  • Try waiting a few minutes and trying again.
  • If that doesn't help, please contact the library at 603-206-8150 or mcclibrary@ccsnh.edu.
    • Please take a screen shot  or photo of the error you are getting to help identify the problem.
    • Also let us know if you're registered for classes at any other NH community college
  • You can also chat with a librarian 24/7 and we'll do our best to send you PDFs of articles if you are on a tight deadline. Click the Chat With Us button on this page.
