Want to Get Better with
Your DAW?

Get THE training NEEDED to start a career in music, film, live sound, broadcast and game audio.

Explore Ableton Live for Music Production

Ableton Live is a powerful DAW known for its clip-based workflow. At CRAS, you’ll be introduced to its unique approach to MIDI and audio production, widely used by songwriters and producers. Gain hands-on experience and expand your skills with a different style of music creation.

Train in Pro Tools
for Recording & Mixing

Pro Tools is the industry standard for recording, editing, and mixing. At CRAS, you’ll train hands-on in real studios, learning how to record, mix, and produce like a pro. Plus, Pro Tools comes included in your CRAS Laptop Recording Package, so you can practice anywhere.

Learn Logic Pro X
for Music Production

Logic Pro X is used at CRAS to teach essential DAW concepts, including digital audio recording, MIDI sequencing, effect plugins, and routing. Gain hands-on experience while building a strong foundation in music production. Plus, Logic Pro X is included in your CRAS Laptop Recording Package.

Learn Wwise
for Game Audio Production

Wwise is an essential tool for interactive audio in gaming. At CRAS, you’ll explore how it’s used for adaptive music, sound effects, and immersive experiences. Gain hands-on training in implementing sound into game engines and prepare for a career in game audio.
