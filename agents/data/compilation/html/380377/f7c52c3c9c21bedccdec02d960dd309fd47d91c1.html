Privacy Policy

Northwest Indian College Foundation Privacy Policy

Who is Northwest Indian College Foundation

Our website address is: https://foundation.nwic.edu/

Information analytics collected and why it is collected.

Because we value your privacy and patronage, Northwest Indian College Foundation wants to address any privacy concerns you may have. This Privacy Policy outlines information that this website may collect and how that information may be used. When visitors submit a form on the site, we collect the data shown in the contact form(s), and the visitors IP address and browser user agent string to help spam detection (Google Analytics, reCAPTCHA, etc.).

This website does not collect personal identifiable information about visitors (e.g., name, city, state, telephone number, or email address) UNLESS you have submitted it. If you do not want your personal data collected, please do not submit it. Information you submit may be used to offer or provide products or services to you, communicate with you for other business purposes.

When visiting this Website, we may collect technical but not personal identifiable data of computers and browsers visiting the site, and this information may be shared with others. Examples of this type of information include the Internet browser version and settings, the type of computer operating system and the referring domain name.

Links

This website may contain links to other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or the content of third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not cover the collection of information by those other companies and Websites. Although we encourage third parties to provide their own privacy policies, we are not responsible for their activities, such as how they handle the information they collect online.

Contacting You

Information you submit on the website and through other sources may be used to tell you about events and donation opportunities that might interest you, or to invite you to participate in surveys, or other events. E-mail messages will provide a method by which you may opt-out of future mailings. To indicate a preference to not receive e-mails or other communications, please send a message to foundation@nwic.edu.

On occasion, we use your identification information to validate your identity. As for usage tracking data, we primarily use that information to operate and improve our Website, and to further our marketing efforts.

Cookies

If you visit our login page, we will set a temporary cookie to determine if your browser accepts cookies. This cookie contains no personal data and is discarded when you close your browser.

Embedded content from other websites

News articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website. These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

Who we share your data with

As a general rule, we do not share your information with unaffiliated third parties. We may, however, share your information with our own service providers (DonorView, MailChimp, PayPal, etc.) to the extent necessary to fulfill your requests. We may also share your information with our affiliates, but we will not share it with unaffiliated third parties for direct marketing purposes. We reserve the right to disclose your information under limited circumstances, such as to cooperate with law enforcement or judicial authorities as required by law or legal process, to protect our own legal rights, or to protect the public good.

Information and data security

Whenever you visit, and/or login to websites on the internet, you run the risk that the information you give out will fall into the wrong hands. Although we implement and maintain reasonable security procedures and practices to safeguard your privacy, we cannot guarantee the security of any information you provide to us. We accept no responsibility or liability for the activities of third parties.

Updating Our Privacy Policy

Northwest Indian College Foundation, from time to time, change our Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting our updated Privacy Policy on our Website and inserting a new Effective Date.

Contacting Northwest Indian College Foundation

If you would like to send us a request as indicated above, or if you have any other questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us by email at foundation@nwic.edu.

Effective Date:

This Privacy Policy is effective as of June 20, 2023.
