Certification

Certification Mandatory?

Yes

Licensing Info

Successful completion of RPR (NCRA) or CVR (NVRA) is required prior to testing for state certification or work experience or successful completion of a California-Recognized Court Reporting School.

CA allows for reciprocity with the National Verbatim Reporters Association (NVRA) CVR and CVR-S certification. If a hopeful licensee has either certification, they are not required to take the Board’s skills test, only the two written tests – English and Professional Practice – for licensure.
