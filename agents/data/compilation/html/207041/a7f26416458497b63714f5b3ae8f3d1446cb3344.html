Mathematics - Data Science

College of Health and Sciences

Bachelor of Science in Mathematics
Data Science Concentration

Turn data into knowledge. Drive informed decisions.

Offered through ECU’s College of Health and Sciences, the Data Science concentration equips students with the computational, mathematical, and analytical skills needed to excel in a data-driven world. Students learn to collect, process, and interpret complex data sets and communicate actionable insights across industries.

Program Highlights:

  • Training in data management, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling

  • Emphasis on real-world problem solving and clear data communication

  • Prepares students for careers in data analytics, research, finance, healthcare, tech, and more

  • Strong foundation for graduate study in data science, statistics, or applied mathematics
