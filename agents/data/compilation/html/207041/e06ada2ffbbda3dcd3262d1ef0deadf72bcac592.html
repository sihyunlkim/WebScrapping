Accreditation

East Central University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission, a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education.

  • Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP)

    The Harland C. Stonecipher School of Business is accredited by The Accreditation Council for Business and Programs (ACBSP).

  • National Educational Leadership Preparation

    The M.Ed. in Educational Leadership program is aligned with National Educational Leadership Preparation (NELP) standards.

  • Society of Health and Physical Educators

    The M.Ed. in Sports Administration program is aligned with Society of Health and Physical Educators (SHAPE) standards and North American Society for Sports Management (NASSM) standards.
