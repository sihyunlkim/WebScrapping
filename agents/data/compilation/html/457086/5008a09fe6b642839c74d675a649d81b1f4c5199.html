HOW TO APPLY FOR FINANCIAL AID

It is strongly recommended for applicants to seek the assistance of the financial aid office when filling out FAFSA

You must complete and submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid(FAFSASM) to apply for federal student aid and most state and college aid. FAFSA on the WebSM is the quickest and easiest method of applying. To go to FAFSA website click here 

FSA ID

The FSA ID as your electronic passport to federal student aid online.

Students, parents, and borrowers are required to use an FSA ID, made up of a username and password, to access certain U.S. Department of Education websites. Your FSA ID is used to confirm your identity when accessing your financial aid information and electronically signing your federal student aid documents.

You can create an FSA ID now by clicking here.
