Academic Affairs Strategic Plan

Academic Affairs, in partnership with the other divisions, commits itself to the ULM Strategic Plan as the University continues to change lives for the betterment of the citizens of our region. The faculty, staff, and administrators will align divisional, collegiate, school, and program offerings, policies, and processes to fulfill ULM’s mission and vision.

In concert with this effort, the Provost's Office is developing the Academic Affairs Strategic Plan. Find out more about the plan here.
