FAQs

Student Support
How do I change my term?
If an applicant wants to change the term (semester) they applied for, they will need to complete a new application.This does not apply to part-of-term changes, such as changing from Spring 1 to Spring 2, but it does apply to changes from one term to another.* Applicants who currently have an application in process (not yet accepted) will be required to complete a new application and also pay an additional application fee. Therefore, it is important that applicants be confident with when they plan on starting. Students who have been accepted will be required to submit a new application to change their term but willnotneed to pay another application fee. These studentsmust work in coordination with Admissions to let them know their intentions so Admissions can waive the application fee for the second application. This will only be allowed one time any subsequent term changes made by the student will require a new application and another application fee. *Students who need to change their start date within the same term they applied for (such as changing from 2024 Spring 1 to 2024 Spring 2) must reach out to Admissions to request that change.

Do online program(s) utilize an e-portfolio system? If so, do students have to pay for this at the beginning of a program?

ULM currently uses LiveText, a web-based subscription platform that students typically use to submit one project or assignment per online course. Students must pay for this subscription when they sign up for the portfolio; the price breakdown is below:

  • Standard: $115 Five-year accounts (graduate programs)
  • Standard with FEM: $133 Five-year accounts (undergraduate programs)
  • Renewal for one year: $30
M.Ed. Educational Leadership Online Program
Can the internship hours/experiences for the M.Ed. Educational Leadership online program be completed any time?
Internship courses need to be completed during the fall and spring terms.
Can all field hours/experiences be completed at the student's current schoolor is it a requirement to seek experiences at various schools?
Field hours can be completed at the student's current school. If the student chooses to complete hours at a different location, they must sign a memorandum of understanding (MOU) with the school-based mentor.
Ed.D. Curriculum & Instruction Online Program
How does the dissertation in the Ed.D. online program work? Is it embedded into the program, so students begin working on it after a certain number of courses?
Students are given the option to write a dissertation or three research articles, and they must make their decision before the comprehensive exam.
What is the process for deciding on a dissertation option, and will the two groups be distinguished?
The candidate discusses dissertation options with their major professor before comprehensive exams to arrive at a decision. There will be no differentiation based on dissertation options.
Do students have the option to defend their paper(s) virtually?
Students who choose either option are required to use a traditional dissertation defense.
