Help for students

Having trouble with your new laptop?

Weve had reports of the new Lenovo laptops screen going black or flickering black. We feel this is due to drivers being out of date. The laptops were imaged in the factory back in early June so drivers are now over two months old. Please take some time (even if you havent had issues) to update the drivers. Heres how:

  1. Plug your laptop into power (very important step)
  2. Start an app called Lenovo Commercial Vantage
  3. Click on Check for updates
  4. Once all the updates have completed you should restart the laptop

Additionally, if your computer ever gets stuck with a black screen, there is a small pin hole on the bottom. You can poke a pin, tack, etc. in that hole and it will reset (shut off) the laptop. Then run the lenovo updates. (updated 8/27/2025)

Coursera

Coursera access is now available at no cost. All U of M students, faculty, and staff have free access to a range of courses and specializations in Coursera. Access U of M Coursera content through Coursera for Minnesota as well as over 3,800 online courses and 400 specializations from other institutions through the Coursera for Minnesota Consortium, all at no cost. Simply log in with your U of M credentials (using the "Log in with your organization" link). Email Fritz Vandover with any questions about how to access Coursera content to supplement your U of M courses.
