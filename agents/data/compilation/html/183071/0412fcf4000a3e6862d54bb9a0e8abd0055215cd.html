Earn Your UNH Degree in Manchester

We are the University of New Hampshire in the heart of Manchester, offering the world-class UNH degree in the state’s business hub. Here, students learn from—and through—real-world experience, which is why 93% of UNH Manchester graduates are employed or enrolled in further education within six months of graduation. Whether you want to learn in person or online, UNH Manchester offers a flexible, affordable education that prepares you for the future you want.

PROGRAMS OF STUDY

UNH Manchester delivers career-driven undergraduate and graduate programs in today’s high-demand fields. With small class sizes and a focus on industry experience, our majors don’t just prepare you for the world—they empower you to change it.

Admissions


You’ve got big plans for your future. We’ve got the world-class academics, distinguished faculty and industry connections to get you there. If you’re ready to take the next step, applying is easy. Just follow the steps below.

Undergraduate
  

  1. Submit an application by April 1 for fall 2021. First-year students apply online via the Common App and transfer students submit the UNH Transfer Form or the NH Transfer Form if transferring from a community college.
  2. Send in your official high school transcript and/or GED, and official college transcript(s) if applicable.
  3. Submit one letter of recommendation (optional for transfer students).
  4. If you’re a New Hampshire resident, submit the Residency Statement.
  5. Optional: Submit official SAT and/or ACT scores.

Graduate
 

  1. Submit an online application. Deadlines vary by program.
  2. Send in your official college transcript(s).
  3. Submit your GRE and/or GMAT scores, if required by the program.
  4. If you’re a New Hampshire resident, submit the Residency Statement.
