Academic Support

Each student has unique academic goals. We're here to help you achieve yours.

Whether you're looking for a little guidance or ongoing support, we offer a range of academic resources designed to meet you where you are and help you get where you want to go. 

Knack Tutoring

UNH partners with Knack, a Peer-to-Peer tutoring platform, to provide all undergraduate and graduate students in Durham and Manchester with access to verified UNH tutors who have successfully completed your course. All courses are covered, and where there are no peer tutors available, there’s an optional link for an on-demand professional tutor. Knack peer tutoring is free for our students!

expand all

You can sign up for Knack Tutoring at unh.joinknack.com and complete your profile. Once you finish signing up, you can look for a tutor. You will be able to meet virtually or in-person, based on your preference. For assistance, you can review help guides here.

You can apply to be a Knack tutor once you’ve completed your first semester at UNH Manchester. You can learn more here

Connors Writing Center

The Connors Writing Center offers one-on-one writing conferences and asynchronous collaboration for current UNH students (undergraduate and graduate). They work with writers from all disciplines on many different kinds of writing. Students can select whether they want in-person (Durham), virtual (synchronous), or written (asynchronous) feedback on their writing.

The CWC in-person appointments are held on the Durham campus but are available to any current UNH student.

Learn more about the Connors Writing Center here.

Library: UNH Manchester librarians can help with your research. Visit cps.unh.edu/library to access services, search for academic sources, or contact us at 603-641-4173 / unhm.library@unh.edu.

Faculty Office Hours

Faculty office hours are designated times when your professors are available to meet with students. You can check your course syllabus for scheduled office hours or ask your instructor.

It's a chance to ask questions, clarify course material, get feedback on assignments, and build a professional relationship with your instructors. These conversations can help you succeed academically and may even lead to future opportunities like research, mentorship, or recommendation letters.

Support doesn’t stop here!

Success isn’t just about what happens in the classroom—it’s about your whole well-being. At UNH Manchester, we’re here to support you beyond academics with wellness resources like counseling and food assistance, plus essential student services including academic advising and career support. Whatever you need to thrive, we’re ready to help you navigate every part of your college experience.
