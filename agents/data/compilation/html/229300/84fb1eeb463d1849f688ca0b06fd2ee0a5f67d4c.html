Research Focus

We are in the midst of the Artificial Intelligence (AI) revolution that is transforming every major industry, including healthcare. Our students and researchers with a focus on this domain develop and use advanced computational methods and tools to significantly improve patient care, disease prevention, and biomedical discovery.

Collaborate With Us

Categories

The following categories represent the key research thrusts or major application domains of Artificial Intelligence at UTHealth Houston. They are not necessarily formal administrative divisions but rather reflect the primary areas where our faculty and research groups focus their AI efforts (e.g., Clinical NLP, Predictive Modeling, Health Data Privacy, Bioinformatics & Genomics, Medical Imaging Analysis, etc.). They help organize the breadth of AI research conducted at UTHealth Houston.

Featured Customized Models

These models are primarily developed by faculty, postdoctoral fellows, and graduate students within UTHealth Houston's McWilliams School of Biomedical Informatics (MSBMI). Their purpose is to leverage artificial intelligence to accelerate biomedical discovery, improve clinical decision-making, and enhance patient outcomes.

Transforming Clinical Text into Analytical Insight

Represents our strength in processing and understanding complex clinical text data from electronic health records, which is foundational for many clinical AI applications. 

Med-BERT

Transforming Cancer Research with AI

Showcases our application of advanced AI in high-impact disease areas like oncology or genomics, demonstrating translational potential. 

CancerGPT

Empowering Biologists with AI-Driven Protein Interaction Networks

Helping biologists automate genetic data analysis by generating protein interaction networks and searching scientific literature.
