Training Overview

These trainings will build fundamental awareness and enhance the understanding and utilization of AI skills in research, education/teaching, clinical healthcare, and the arts/humanities.

Sessions

  • October 1 - Session One: Fundamental Information
  • October 8 - Session Two: AI Application in Teaching & Education
  • October 15 - Session Three: AI Application in Clinical Practice
  • October 22 - Session Four: AI Application in Research
  • October 29 - Session Five: AI In Administrative Duties
  • November 6 - Session Six: AI as a Co-creator in Visual, Musical, Performance Arts and the Humanities

All UTHealth Houston faculty are invited to register for any session you wish to attend.

Register Now

This training is hosted by the Office of Faculty Affairs and Development, in Collaboration with the McWilliams School of Biomedical Informatics and the Office of the Associate Vice President, IT User Experience and Support.
