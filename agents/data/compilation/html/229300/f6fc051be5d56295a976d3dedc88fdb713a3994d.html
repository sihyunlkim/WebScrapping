Education

  • AI Hub Faculty Training Program

Advancing healthcare through artificial intelligence begins with education and training.

At UTHealth Houston, we are committed to equipping our students, healthcare professionals, and faculty with the necessary knowledge and hands-on experience to harness AI for research, clinical applications, and operational improvements. Through AI literacy programs, workshops, and hands-on training, we empower the next generation of specialists to shape the future of AI in healthcare.

Explore our curated selection of learning resources, including LinkedIn Learning and Microsoft Learn, tailored to support AI literacy and competency development. Whether you're a beginner looking to understand the fundamentals or an expert aiming to apply the latest models for generative AI, UTHealth Houston offers opportunities to expand your expertise and stay at the forefront of AI-driven innovation in healthcare.
