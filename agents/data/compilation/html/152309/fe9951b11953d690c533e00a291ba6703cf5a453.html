Gain a Strong Hair Styling Foundation at Roger's Academy of Hair Design

We're a hair stylist school that's been serving Evansville, IN since 1982

Roger and Mary Hayden founded Roger's Academy of Hair Design in Evansville, IN back in 1982. They owned and operated three successful salons over 16 years!

While operating their salons, Roger and Mary found themselves re-training many of their new hires. They realized that there was a need for a school that could train salon-ready hair stylists, so they made that the goal of Roger's Academy. Now, 35 years later, Roger's Academy is one of the largest cosmetology schools in the area.

Roger's Academy is family-owned and operated. We offer cosmetology and barbering programs at our two campuses. Choose our hair academy and impress your future employer with your hair styling skills.

Why pick us?

When you select our beauty school, you can count on getting a high-quality cosmetology education. You'll get:

  • Hands-on experience
  • Lessons taught by professionals in the industry
  • Experience with up-to-date techniques

Apply to our programs today to learn from some of the best.
