Frequently Asked Questions

Contact Us

Q: How much is the program?

A: Total: $22,900 (includes cost of kit $3,600 and registration fee $100). This cost is applicable to both Cosmetology and Barbering.

Q: What are the average cost of living components for our student population?

A: Living Expense - Food: $323/month; Housing for students living off campus: $804/month; Housing for Dependent students living at home: $403/month
Miscellaneous Personal Expenses: $384/month
Transportation Expenses: $437/month
(updated May 2024)

Q: How long is the program?

A: Both Barbering and Cosmetology are 1500 clock hour programs, which takes about one year to complete on a 30 hour per week schedule.

Q: What is the school schedule?

A: Our full time schedule is Tues-Sat, 8:30am-3pm. Our part time schedule is Tues-Sat, 8:30am-12:30pm. Our campus is open until 5 pm, so it is possible to obtain 40 hours per week in attendance.

Q: Do you offer financial aid?

A: YES! Financial aid is available to those who qualify. We are accredited, which enables our students to use the FAFSA to apply for Student Aid. Our Federal school code is 016593. Visit the website for FAFSA here for more info.

Q: Does your school offer scholarships?

A: YES! 1. We currently offer a High School Graduate Scholarship for up to $2,000. It requires an applicant to complete a scholarship application, to have graduated within the current year, and to have chosen Roger's Academy directly out of high school (i.e. no other college attendance prior). 2. We also currently offer a good attendance scholarship in the amount of $1,000. It is obtained by good attendance at our school. If criteria is met scholarship is awarded upon completion of our program.

Q: How do I enroll?

A: An application may be submitted through our website, or call the admissions office to schedule a tour/enrollment appointment.

Q: How long does it take to enroll?

A: Not long at all! Our Admissions specialists are super helpful through the whole process! A student could be officially enrolled within a day or so!
