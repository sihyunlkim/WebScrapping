What Does Student Conduct & Academic Integrity Programs (SCAIP) Do?

We support a respectful, safe community by enforcing student conduct policies, reviewing misconduct cases, and assigning educational sanctions where necessary. We educate students about their rights, hold them accountable for their responsibilities, and support their ethical growth. Learn more about SCAIP's mission and meet our staff by visiting the Contact Us page.
