Workshop #3: Canvas Essentials - Elevate Your Course!

Speaker: Dr. Linda Lemus

Title: Canvas Essentials: Effective Course Design.

Description: Master Canvas for engaging learning. Learn to streamline navigation, simplify grading, track attendance, and enhance collaboration.

Topics: Modules, Gradebook, Attendance, Google Docs.

Target: All faculty.

Date/Time: April 8, 2025, 2:00 PM PDT (Zoom)

 

 

 

Workshop #4: Canvas Quizzes - Classic vs. New

Speaker: Dr. Karthikeyan Chandrasegaran

Title: Demystifying Canvas Quizzes.

Description: Explore New Quizzes' analytics for improved assessment.

Target: Canvas users seeking New Quizzes expertise.

Date/Time: April 15, 2025, 2:00 PM PDT (Zoom)
