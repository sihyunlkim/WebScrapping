It is a clean, private, and comfortable space with a lock, electrical outlet, a chair, and a table, a sink with running water, a refrigerator, and paper towels. Those seeking to arrange for access to the lactation room, should present their current CSI ID to the Office of Diversity & Compliance, 1A-205. Keys for the room will be issued through that Office.
